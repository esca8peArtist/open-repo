**Verified contact methods**:
- Web contact form: https://nilc.org/about-us/contact-us/

**Contact strategy**: Use the web form as the primary channel. Address to "NILC team" and request it be forwarded to their technology or security team. The staff page may reveal a named contact in communications or technology if you want to personalize further.

**Confidence in contact**: HIGH — web form is current, `info@nilc.org` confirmed by published sources.

## Suggested Send Order and Timeline

**Day 1 — Send to all 5 simultaneously** (or within the same session):

There is no meaningful reason to stagger these sends. All five organizations are peers, not competing for the same information, and none will be confused or put off by learning others received it. Simultaneous send is the most efficient path.

Suggested order if you prefer sequential (prioritized by potential reach):

1. **CLINIC** — highest downstream reach (400+ affiliates). If CLINIC distributes this to affiliates, it multiplies every other outreach you do.
2. **NILC** — credibility anchor for the legal community. A NILC endorsement will carry weight with other legal aid orgs.
3. **RAICES** — direct client-facing Texas-based, high enforcement exposure.
4. **ILRC** — resource distribution infrastructure; may publish this as a resource.
5. **NLG** — security-culture-aware; will understand and spread within their membership.

**Day 6 (5 business days later)**: Check for responses. If none, send one follow-up to each. The follow-up note: "Following up on the security guide I shared last week — wanted to make sure it arrived. Happy to answer any questions about any section."

**Day 11 (10 business days)**: If still no response, no further follow-up is needed. The information is distributed; the decision is theirs. Do not chase.

**After responses arrive**: Offer to create a simplified plain-language handout (Part 0 + Tier 1 checklist, one to two pages, Spanish and English) for community-facing distribution. DISTRIBUTION_CHECKLIST.md identifies this as the appropriate collateral for Tier 1B community-based organizations.
