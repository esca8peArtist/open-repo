### Action 4 — Germination Tray (if not already started)
**When**: Today, May 5 — this is the last viable date
**Time**: 15 minutes
**Why it matters**: Cluster A shoot needs 9-10-day-old seedling sprouts as props. May 5 start
produces sprouts by May 14-15. May 10 start produces sprouts too young for May 10 shoot.

If the tray was already started April 30 or May 1: no action needed.
If not yet started: basil or lettuce seeds in any tray with potting mix, moistened, covered.
Place in the warmest available spot (top of refrigerator, near a heating vent).

**What happens if you miss this**: Use grocery-store bean sprout tray as a prop substitute,
or swap 2 Cluster A products (Seed Saving Manual, Zone Calendar) to Cluster D/E stock compositing.
This is documented as Risk 2 in TRACK_B_PRODUCTION_PIPELINE.md Section 6.

## Section 4: Risk Mitigation

### If the May 10-11 shoot cannot happen
- Reschedule to May 17-18. Same format, same props, same shot list.
- Impact: Phase 4 launch shifts to June 6 instead of May 30. Email automation and
  Cluster D/E images still go live May 30.
- Worst-case launch: June 15, 2026 (documented in TRACK_B_PRODUCTION_PIPELINE.md Section 6).
- Do not delay zone card builds or Kit automation for the shoot delay — those are independent.

### If the @seedwarden handle is unavailable on a platform
- Fallback order: @seedwarden.co, @seedwarden.seeds, @seedwarden_guides
- Use the same handle on all three platforms. If primary is taken on one but available on others,
  prioritize cross-platform consistency over the exact primary handle.
- Record actual handles in WORKLOG.md.

### If Kit account setup is delayed past May 22
- First subscribers can be manually welcomed with a direct email.
- Zone cards can be attached as email attachments to a manual Gmail send temporarily.
- This is a 1-3 day workaround, not a long-term solution.

### If social account creation is delayed past May 7
- Prioritize: Instagram and Pinterest (highest ROI for Seedwarden's content type).
- TikTok can launch 1-2 weeks later without impacting the May 30 target.
- Delay does not block zone card builds or Kit automation.

### If Etsy account remains unverified at launch
- Track B is designed to be independent of Etsy status.
- All CTAs point to the Kit landing page ("link in bio to get your zone card").
- No Track B content needs to be changed when Etsy goes live — update bio links only.
