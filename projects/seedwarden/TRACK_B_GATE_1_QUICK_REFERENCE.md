**Create 15 tags** — Kit > Subscribers > Tags > "Create a tag":

Interest tags (name exactly): `seed-saver` `forager` `food-preserver` `homesteader` `medicinal-herbs` `vip-buyer` `phase-1-buyer`

**Landing page** (25 min):
1. Kit > Landing Pages > "Create a landing page" > use "Simple" or "Clean" template
2. Add zone selector field (dropdown: Zones 3–10)
3. Form action: subscribe + apply zone tag
4. Publish page > copy URL
5. Add URL to Instagram bio link, TikTok bio link, Pinterest website field

preview text, CTA buttons, UTM parameters, and delay settings.
Build order: Email 1 (immediate) > Email 2 (2 days) > Email 3 (3 days) > Email 4 (2 days) > Email 5 (3 days)

3. Wait 1 min — confirm Email 2 is NOT sent immediately (delay logic active)
All 3 must pass before activating sequence
