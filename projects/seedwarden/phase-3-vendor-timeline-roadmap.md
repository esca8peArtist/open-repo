## Core Logic: Why Outreach Starts May 22, Not May 30

The May 30 user decision determines which vendor confirmations to activate — but it does not determine which vendors to contact. All three vendor categories (Goldenseal sourcing, peer reviewers, Canva designers) can receive outreach on May 22 without knowing the final scope:

- Goldenseal RFQs are path-agnostic: getting live specimen and dried root quotes in hand by May 27 informs the May 30 path decision rather than depending on it.
- Peer reviewer invitations are scope-agnostic: a reviewer confirming interest in the Women's Health bundle on May 28 remains valid whether the scope is 3 bundles or 5 bundles.
- Designer quotes are scope-switchable: the designer brief templates request quotes for both the 3-bundle and 5-bundle scope separately, so the user can select the appropriate tier on May 30.

Every day of delay past May 22 compresses the response window before the June 1 confirmation deadline. A May 30 send date (the decision date itself) leaves only two business days for supplier responses before the June 8 zero-float Goldenseal order deadline. Outreach on May 22 provides six business days of response window.

## Contingency Paths by Vendor Category

### If No Goldenseal Supplier Confirms Cultivated Stock by June 1

Activate Path 2 immediately. Do not wait past June 1 to make this call.

Actions: Email media@ncbg.unc.edu and media@mobot.org on June 1. Begin Wikimedia Commons *Hydrastis canadensis* CC-BY-SA image search. Log the three best candidates in PHOTO_ATTRIBUTION_LOG.md by June 7. The Path 2 timeline has zero float from this point — the June 21 photography lock date requires attribution logging to be complete by then.

### If No Peer Reviewer Confirms by June 8

Activate Reviewer Variant H (AHG directory general outreach) immediately. Post a brief request in any AHG member community or newsletter you have access to. A general community post reaches a wider pool than targeted individual outreach and often produces faster responses from members with immediate availability. Frame the ask as: "Paid peer review opportunity for a practitioner-grade medicinal herbs educational guide series launching June 22. 45-minute review, $150 honorarium. AHG credential required. Must confirm by June 12."

If no reviewer confirms by June 12, proceed with the launch fallback language from PEER_REVIEWER_RECRUITMENT_STRATEGY.md: "This guide is designed to meet RH (AHG) practitioner standards. We are actively seeking RH peer reviewer confirmation for a forthcoming listing update." Do not delay the June 22 launch for a reviewer confirmation — the launch date is hard.

### If Finalist Designer Cannot Confirm July 3 Delivery

Send the designer brief to Priority 3 contacts (fiverr.com/etsy_digital_design, wellness_brand_canva) immediately. At the same time, begin Phase 3 cover design in Canva using Phase 2 templates as the base — the self-execution path (12.5 hours) is a viable backup and can run in parallel with designer outreach without committing either path. The self-execution path should be actively developed through June 8 if no designer has confirmed; after June 8, the writing sprint begins and the 12.5-hour design load needs to be offloaded if sprint pace is above 4 hours/day.

### If Option B Writer Cannot Confirm by June 1

Default to Option A or C. Log the decision in WORKLOG.md. The writer-not-hired contingency does not require any vendor action beyond closing the open writer search. Option A (5-bundle solo) proceeds with the full 56–66 hour writing load; Option C (3-bundle solo) proceeds with 36–44 hours. The designer confirmation and reviewer confirmation are unaffected by the writer decision.
