# Bundle Canva Design Templates — Q3 Medicinal Herbs

**What this document is**: Copy-paste-ready Canva design briefs for all 5 Q3 medicinal herb bundles. Each brief specifies: (1) brand compliance colors/fonts/layout, (2) asset dimensions and placement, (3) copy/headline placement, (4) user upload instructions, (5) expected output format for Etsy/Shopify.

**How to use**: Open this document alongside Canva Pro. For each bundle, copy the design specification into a Canva project, then upload user-provided photography and adjust text layers per final review approval. Expected time: 10-15 minutes per bundle (one-time setup).

## SEEDWARDEN BRAND STANDARDS (All Bundles)

### Color Palette (Verified Seedwarden Hex Codes)
- **Primary Green**: #6B8E6F (sage green — used for headers, callouts, accent bars)
- **Cream/Off-White**: #F5F1EB (background, light text areas)
- **Copper Accent**: #B17661 (links, buttons, section dividers)
- **Dark Text**: #2C3E3C (body copy, deep charcoal)
- **Light Accent**: #D9C8B8 (subtle background tints, watermarks)

### Typography (All Bundles)
- **Headers** (H1/H2): Montserrat, Bold, 48–60 pt (color: Sage Green #6B8E6F)
- **Subheaders** (H3): Montserrat, SemiBold, 32–36 pt (color: Copper Accent #B17661)
- **Body Copy**: Georgia, Regular, 14–18 pt (color: Dark Text #2C3E3C)
- **Callout Boxes**: Georgia Italic, 16 pt (color: Sage Green #6B8E6F on Cream background)
- **Small Text** (metadata, disclaimers): Georgia, 10–12 pt (color: Medium Gray #666)

### Layout Standards (All Bundles)
- **Page Dimensions**: 8.5" × 11" (standard PDF letter size)
- **Margins**: 0.75" left/right, 1" top/bottom
- **Column Structure**: Single column with sidebar space (3" sidebar for botanical illustration or callout)
- **Image Placement**: 
  - **Hero image**: Top 3" (full width, 8.5" × 3")
  - **Body content**: Middle 5" (text with optional 2-3 small inline images)
  - **Footer**: Bottom 1" (page number, copyright, Seedwarden branding)

### Botanical Illustration Style (All Bundles)
- **Hero Images**: Professional botanical illustration or high-quality nature photography
- **Inline Images**: Small (1–1.5" square) black-and-white botanical line drawings or accent photos
- **Image Quality**: 300 DPI minimum (for print-ready PDF export)
- **Attribution Placement**: Small text bottom-right of images (6 pt, gray)

## Troubleshooting & FAQs

### Q: Can I use different hero images for each bundle?
**A**: Yes. The specifications above provide primary recommendations, but alternatives are listed for each bundle. Ensure the chosen image aligns with the bundle's therapeutic category (respiratory images for Respiratory bundle, etc.). All images must be 8.5" × 3" and high-quality (min. 1,500 × 3,200 pixels).

### Q: What if I don't have three inline botanical images?
**A**: You have three options: (1) Use single large image and omit inline images; (2) Use Seedwarden's Wikimedia Commons image links (provided in PHOTO_ATTRIBUTION_LOG.md); (3) Source your own from Wikimedia Commons or iNaturalist with proper CC licensing. Inline images are optional but recommended for visual interest.

### Q: Can I adjust the price from the specifications above?
**A**: No. Prices are set based on Phase 2 market research and competitor analysis. Deviations require user decision and must be documented in BUNDLE_SUPPLY_SOURCING_AND_COSTING.md.

### Q: What about Practitioner 10-Pack pricing?
**A**: Current recommendation is 3x the bundle price (e.g., $22 bundle → $66 for 10-pack). User may adjust. Typical range: $60–$75 for bundles in this price tier. Confirm pricing before finalizing listings.

### Q: Can I include QR codes linking to Etsy/Shopify?
**A**: Yes. Add optional QR code to footer area (0.5" × 0.5", bottom right). Links to product URL on primary sales platform.

### Q: What file format should I use for uploading to Etsy/Shopify?
**A**: **PDF for product listing download** (customer receives guide as downloadable PDF after purchase). **JPG or PNG for thumbnail image** (1:1 square crop of hero section for Etsy listing preview). Both formats can be generated from Canva export.
