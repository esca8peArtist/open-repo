## Risk Mitigation (CRITICAL)

### If You Fall Behind

**Day 7 catch-up target**: You should have 24,500–28,000 words written by end of Week 1.

- **If at 20,000 words** (3 days behind): Drop 1 bundle from simultaneous launch. Move Immunity + Digestive to Phase 3b (August). Switch to Path C2 mentally. Adjust spreadsheet.
- **If at 15,000 words** (5 days behind): Same as above, but more aggressive. Drop Sleep to Phase 3b as well. Revert to Path C2 (3 bundles). This is your pause point.
- **Recovery is NOT possible after Day 7 without scope cut.**

### Revision Under Pressure

Because you have ~2 days to revise 80,000+ words:

- **Revision approach**: 
  - Read-through for flow: 1 pass, 2–3 hours (trust your first draft)
  - Spell/grammar: Use Grammarly plugin (30 min)
  - Medicinal accuracy: Spot-check highest-risk dosages only (ginger, vitex, other prescription-interact species). 2 hours.
  - Ship it. Perfection is the enemy of done.

- **Post-launch revision**: You can still fix typos after July 9 (Etsy allows edit). Plan 1 hour post-launch for fixes.

### Context Switching Overhead

Working 12–13 hours/day means:

- 5–7 hours writing (deep focus)
- 1–2 hours platform setup (Canva, Kit, Etsy) — do this in morning or evening, **not** during writing peak (10am–4pm)
- 1 hour admin (email, messages, breaks)

**Recommendation**: Batch your non-writing work. Days 1–4 of each week: mornings are platform days (6–8am), afternoons/evenings are writing days (8am–8pm). Days 5–7: pure writing 24 hours except meals/sleep.

**Advantage**: 5 bundles live on July 9 (vs. staggered July 1 / 5 / 9)

## Decision Checkpoint: Do You Really Want This?

**Before committing to Path A2, ask yourself**:

1. **Can I write 12+ hours/day for 22 consecutive days?** 
   - Yes, and I've done similar sprints before → Path A2 is viable
   - Maybe, but I've never done it → Path C2 is safer
   - No → Path B2 (hire contractor) or Path C2 (3 bundles)

2. **Do I care more about speed or quality?**
   - Speed is critical (market window) → Path A2
   - Quality is critical (brand reputation) → Path C2
   - Balanced → Path B2 (you write 3, contractor writes 2)

3. **What's my fallback if I'm 5 days behind by July 1?**
   - I'll drop 2 bundles and switch to C2 → Path A2 with mental escape hatch
   - I won't know what to do → Don't choose A2

**Recommended**: If you're not 100% confident, choose **Path C2** (recommended) or **Path B2** (with contractor hire).
