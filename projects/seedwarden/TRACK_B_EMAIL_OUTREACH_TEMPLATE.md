**What the Zone Cards are**

Seedwarden just completed a free set of Zone Quick-Start Cards for all 8 USDA hardiness zones (Zones 3–10). Each card is a single-page printable PDF with:

- **Frost dates and season length** specific to your zone — last frost, first frost, growing season days

- **Storage and preservation timing** — matched to each zone's actual harvest window, not a generic guide that ignores whether you have 95 growing days or 280
- **Heirloom variety spotlight** — regionally tested, open-pollinated varieties with days-to-maturity and growing notes, selected for each zone's specific climate

The cards are completely free. No email required, direct PDF download. They are built for herbalists, practitioners, students, wildcrafters, and home growers who want a one-page regional reference they can print, post in their growing space, and share with their community.

**Who these are for**

- **Herbalists and practitioners** who want to align harvest timing with their zone's actual growing calendar
- **Students and apprentices** who need a one-page zone reference for field identification and plant timing
- **Wildcrafters and foragers** who track seasonal windows by region, not by generic month-by-month guides
- **Home growers and gardeners** who want zone-specific crop and variety recommendations without sorting through generic catalogs
