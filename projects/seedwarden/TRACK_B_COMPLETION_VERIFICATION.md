## Appendix: Common Issues & Resolutions

### Issue 1: WordPress slow to set up (Gate 1)
- **Symptom**: Installation taking >2h; themes not loading
- **Fix**: Restart WordPress container; clear browser cache; try different theme
- **Timeline impact**: Can recover with 2–3h additional time; no launch delay if resolved by May 18

### Issue 2: Images larger than expected (Gate 2)
- **Symptom**: Hero images 2–5MB; guide page load >5s
- **Fix**: Batch optimize all images at once (free tool: TinyPNG or Squoosh). Takes 1–2h.
- **Timeline impact**: 4h buffer in Gate 2; can recover; no launch delay if resolved by May 25

### Issue 3: Social scheduling platform limits (Gate 3)
- **Symptom**: Instagram/TikTok only accept 10 scheduled posts; need to queue 20–25
- **Fix**: Use third-party scheduler (Buffer, Later, SocialBee) which batches uploads without platform limits
- **Timeline impact**: May delay Gate 3 to May 28–29, but launch May 30 still possible

### Issue 4: SEO metadata incomplete (Gate 2)
- **Symptom**: Yoast reports 8/12 guides have "Red" readability; titles missing keywords
- **Fix**: Batch-fix using Yoast's checklist; 1–2h to complete
- **Timeline impact**: Can recover; acceptable if resolved by May 28 (SEO improves post-launch anyway)

### Issue 5: Social video quality poor (Gate 3)
- **Symptom**: TikTok/Reels videos have bad lighting, audio, or editing
- **Fix**: Reshoot 2–3 videos (1h per video); use phone + natural light
- **Timeline impact**: Gate 3 timeline tight; may need to reduce from 25→20 TikTok videos to make deadline
