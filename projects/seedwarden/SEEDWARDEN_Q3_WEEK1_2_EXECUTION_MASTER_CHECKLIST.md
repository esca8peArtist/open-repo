## Week 1-2 Contingency Incident Playbooks

### Incident 1: Email Delivery Failure
**Symptoms**: 0% open rate by Jun 30 12:00 UTC (6+ hours after send)

**Root Cause Check**:
1. Check spam folder (Gmail: check spam, promotions tabs)
2. Check Kit sent status (Kit.com dashboard → Broadcasts → Women's Health Day 1)
3. Verify email list not empty (Kit.com dashboard → Subscribers)

**Recovery Steps**:
- [ ] If spam issue: Mark as "not spam" in Gmail; request Kit support for ISP whitelisting
- [ ] If list empty: Manually add test subscriber + resend test email
- [ ] If Kit error: Switch to Mailchimp free tier (has pre-integration) or MailerLite
- [ ] Resend Day 1 email by Jun 30 18:00 UTC (catch Window 2 of day shift)

**Escalation**: If email delivery unresolved by Jun 30 18:00 UTC → switch to manual email + pause Kit automation until resolved

---

### Incident 2: Etsy Listing Not Searchable
**Symptoms**: 0 Etsy impressions by Jun 30 18:00 UTC; search "women's health bundle" returns no listing

**Root Cause Check**:
1. Verify listing status: Etsy dashboard → Active listings
2. Verify tags: 13-15 relevant tags present (see Q3_ETSY_LISTING_TEMPLATES.md)
3. Verify title includes primary keyword: "Women's Health" in title

**Recovery Steps**:
- [ ] Edit listing title to include full phrase (add "Medicinal Herbs" or "Herbal Bundle" if missing)
- [ ] Add 2-3 missing tags (high-volume long-tail keywords like "women's wellness", "herbal medicine bundle")
- [ ] Republish listing
- [ ] Search again: should appear within 1-4 hours

**Escalation**: If listing still not searchable by Jul 1 12:00 UTC → contact Etsy support (may be category/maturity filter)

---

### Incident 3: Negative Feedback or Complaint
**Symptoms**: Etsy review with <3 stars OR social media negative comment

**Response Protocol**:
1. **Within 2 hours**: Respond publicly (Etsy review → reply section, Instagram → reply to comment)
   - Acknowledge concern
   - Offer solution (refund, replacement, clarification)
   - Provide email contact for private resolution
   
2. **Do NOT**:
   - Delete complaint (makes it worse; users remember deletion)
   - Get defensive (appears unprofessional)
   - Promise things you can't deliver

3. **Escalation** (if customer unresolved after 24h):
   - Offer refund + free replacement
   - Provide direct email/phone contact
   - Document in "Customer Resolution Log" for Phase 3 analysis

**Example Response Template** (copy-paste, customize):
> Thank you for your feedback. We take quality seriously and want to make this right. [If dosage/quality concern]: Our bundles are [sourced from X / prepared by Y]. [If request]: We'd be happy to [refund/exchange/clarify]. Please email [support@seedwarden] with your order number and we'll resolve this within 24 hours.

### Incident 4: Zero Orders After 48 Hours
**Symptoms**: 0 Etsy orders through Jul 1 18:00 UTC despite 20+ impressions

**Root Cause Check**:
1. Check product price: Is $35 (Women's Health) competitive? (Target: $30-40 range)
2. Check product photos: Are they professional / appealing?
3. Check reviews: Are there any existing reviews (social proof)?
4. Check shipping cost: Is shipping visible + reasonable?

**Recovery Steps**:
- [ ] Lower price 10% ($35 → $31.50) for 48-hour flash sale + announce on social
- [ ] Add new product photos (if current ones low quality)
- [ ] Add testimonial or "as seen in X" blurb to listing description
- [ ] Repost on social with new angle ("only 48-hour flash sale pricing")

**Escalation**: If still 0 orders by Jul 6 → shift to free sample + email funnel strategy (market testing phase)

---

- [ ] Contact contractor immediately: "Photos needed urgently; can you send by [time]?"

**Escalation**: If photos not received by Jul 6 12:00 UTC → proceed with Option B or C above (do NOT delay launch past Jul 6; Week 3 is dependent)
