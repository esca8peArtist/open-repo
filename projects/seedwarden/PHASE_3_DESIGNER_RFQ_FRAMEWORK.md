# Phase 3 Designer RFQ Framework
## Scope-Specific Email Variants — May 30 Decision Package

**Prepared**: May 22, 2026
**Send timing**: May 30 (immediately after scope decision) or as soon as vendor responses confirm designer availability
**Quote due from designers**: June 3, 2026 (3 days post-decision)
**Contract-by deadline**: June 8, 2026 (aligned with pre-sprint setup calendar)
**Design delivery deadline**: July 3, 2026 (design lock; hard date)

---

## Part 1: RFQ Framework — How to Use These Variants

**Step 1**: On May 30, confirm scope decision (C2 = 3-bundle; A2/B2 = 5-bundle).

**Step 2**: Select the matching RFQ variant (Section 2 or Section 3).

**Step 3**: Send to Priority 1 designers from the candidate roster (phase-3-vendor-negotiation-templates.md, Section 4). Send to 3–4 designers simultaneously to create competitive pressure.

**Step 4**: Receive quotes by June 3. Evaluate per the scoring criteria in Part 4.

**Step 5**: Accept quote and send contract by June 8.

**Budget guard rails**:
- 3-bundle scope: budget ceiling $840 total (6 deliverables at $140/each max)
- 5-bundle scope: budget ceiling $1,400 total (10 deliverables at $140/each max)
- Per-deliverable floor: $45 (below this, portfolio quality is unlikely to meet the standard)
- If all quotes exceed ceiling: activate self-design fallback (Canva, 19.3 hrs embedded in sprint)

## Part 3: 5-Bundle RFQ Variant (Option A or B Scope)

**Use when**: Option A2 or B2 is selected on May 30.

---

**To**: [Designer email or platform message]
**Subject**: Quote request — Medicinal Herbs Bundle Covers and Zone Cards (5 bundles, Canva, July 3 deadline)

---

Hello [Designer Name],

I found your work through [Etsy/Fiverr] and your portfolio is a strong match for a project I am putting together. I am requesting a competitive quote for a botanical wellness guide design project with a firm July 3, 2026 deadline.

**Project Summary**

I produce Seedwarden — a practitioner-grade medicinal herbs guide series for credentialed herbalists and clinical buyers. The Phase 3 series covers five herb bundles: Women's Health, Respiratory Health, Sleep and Nervines, Immunity Support, and Digestive Support. Each bundle is a PDF digital guide sold on Etsy.

I need a designer to produce cover images and zone card layouts for all five bundles.

**Scope — Full Five-Bundle Set (10 Deliverables)**

Each bundle requires two deliverables:

1. Bundle cover image (2400x2400px square — Etsy primary listing image)
2. Zone card layout (8.5x11in portrait, one-page quick reference for PDF insert)

Total: 5 covers + 5 zone cards = 10 deliverables.

**Brand Kit (Provided on Acceptance)**

I will share the full Canva brand kit including:

- Phase 3 color palette:
  - Deep Burgundy #8B3E3E (Women's Health, Immunity headers)
  - Sage Green #6B8E6F (Respiratory, Digestive headers)
  - Muted Lavender #9B8BA0 (Sleep header)
  - Apothecary Gold #D4AF37 (accent bar, all bundles)
  - Clinical Cream #F9F5F0 (background, all bundles)
  - Dark Charcoal #2C2C2C (body text, all bundles)
- Typography: Playfair Display Bold (headers), Lato Regular (body), Cormorant Garamond Italic (botanical names)
- Seedwarden logo PNG (transparent background)
- Cover layout wireframe (header 25% / hero image 60% / footer 15%)
- Zone card content and structure brief

**Cover Specifications**

- Canvas: 2400x2400px square
- Each bundle has its own header color (as listed above)
- Hero image area: botanical photograph or illustration (I provide the source images)
- Text elements I will supply: bundle title, five species names (common + Latin), "Practitioner-Grade Guide" label
- Footer: Seedwarden wordmark + "Educational Guide" line

**Zone Card Specifications**

- Canvas: 8.5x11in portrait, 300 DPI, PDF output
- One-page quick reference layout with: species name (Latin + common), habitat zone, key uses (3–5 bullets), contraindication summary (1–2 lines), photo thumbnail placeholder
- Color consistent with corresponding bundle cover
- Clean table or card layout — no decorative flourishes; this is a clinical reference card

**Required File Deliverables**

- Covers: PNG at 2400x2400px, sRGB color space
- Zone cards: PDF at 300 DPI, letter size, sRGB
- Editable Canva share link for each design file (for minor post-delivery revisions)

**Revision Policy**

One round of minor revisions included per deliverable (color correction, text adjustment, image swap). Structural redesign beyond first round is billed separately at an agreed rate.

**Timeline**

- Project start: June 8, 2026 (on contract acceptance)
- First drafts: June 20, 2026 (for review before sprint start June 22)
- Final delivery: July 3, 2026 (hard deadline — design lock date)

**Quote Request**

Please quote separately:
- Option A: Full five-bundle set (10 deliverables)
- Option B: Three-bundle set (6 deliverables: Women's Health + Respiratory + Sleep only)

Include your proposed delivery timeline for each option and confirm whether July 3 is achievable for your current schedule.

Please include 2–3 examples of your botanical, wellness, or herbal product design work relevant to this project.

Send your quote to [email] by June 3, 2026.

Thank you for your time — I look forward to seeing your quote.

[Name / Seedwarden]

## Part 5: Quote Evaluation Scoring Criteria

Score each designer on five criteria (1–5 scale each; 25 points maximum). Select the highest scorer above the budget ceiling threshold.

| Criterion | Weight | Score (1–5) | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Portfolio style match | 5 points | ___ | Does existing work already show apothecary, botanical, or herbalist aesthetic? 5 = perfect match; 1 = unrelated |
| Price within budget ceiling | 5 points | ___ | 5-bundle at or below $1,400; 3-bundle at or below $840 = 5 pts. Over ceiling by 10% = 3 pts. Over 20% = 1 pt |
| Timeline feasibility | 5 points | ___ | Explicitly confirms July 3 delivery with first drafts by June 20 = 5 pts. Confirms July 3 only = 3 pts. Unclear = 1 pt |
| Canva proficiency | 5 points | ___ | Offers editable Canva share link = 5 pts. Offers editable file in another format = 3 pts. Export-only = 1 pt |
| Communication quality | 5 points | ___ | Responds within 24 hours, asks clarifying questions, demonstrates project understanding = 5 pts |

**Minimum score to proceed**: 17/25. Below 17, request a portfolio example before proceeding.

**Tie-breaker**: Portfolio style match. A designer whose existing work already looks like the Seedwarden brand will require fewer revision cycles.

**Do not hire** a designer who:
- Cannot provide 2+ relevant portfolio examples
- Does not confirm July 3 in writing
- Quotes below $45/deliverable (quality signal)
- Has fewer than 20 completed reviews on platform

## Part 7: Self-Design Fallback Protocol

Activate if: all designer quotes exceed budget ceiling, or no designer confirms July 3 delivery by June 5.

**Self-design timeline (Canva, embedded in sprint)**:

| Task | Sprint Day | Hours | Float |
|---|---|---|---|
| Brand Kit load + palette test | Pre-sprint June 21 | 0.5 | Zero — must complete |
| WH Cover | D2 June 23 | 1.2 | 4 days |
| Respiratory Cover | D3 June 24 | 1.2 | 4 days |
| Sleep Cover | D9 June 30 | 1.2 | 3 days |
| WH Zone Card | D7 June 28 | 0.8 | 4 days |
| Resp Zone Card | D10 July 1 | 0.8 | 4 days |
| Sleep Zone Card | D11 July 2 | 0.8 | 4 days |
| Consistency review + export test | D16 July 7 | 0.5 | 2 days |
| **Total self-design hours (3-bundle)** | | **7.0 hrs** | |

Self-design at 3-bundle scope adds 7 hours to the sprint but eliminates the $450–$840 design cost. Under Option C with 44–48 writing hours, 7 additional design hours is manageable (total sprint writing + design = 55 hrs, well within the 93.9-hour Option C sprint budget).

**Self-design quality note**: The cover quality under Canva self-design is launch-viable for the practitioner buyer segment. The practitioner purchase decision is driven by clinical content depth, not cover aesthetics. A clean Canva cover using the Phase 3 brand system will perform adequately. Commission a Priority 1 designer for v1.1 covers post-launch if self-design covers show lower click-through in Etsy analytics.
