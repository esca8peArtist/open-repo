## Blocker 1: Three Tag Corrections Required

**What it is**: Three Etsy listings have tag sets that need corrections before upload. These are not editorial changes — they are specific keyword replacements to fix character-count violations and improve keyword match precision.

**Time to resolve**: 15 minutes (copy-paste from this document into Etsy draft listings)

**Why it matters**: Etsy tags have a 20-character maximum per tag. Tags exceeding this limit are silently truncated, causing the listing to rank for a shorter and often unrelated keyword. Uploading with incorrect tags wastes the listing's ranking potential from Day 1.

## Blocker 2: Etsy Account Verification

**What it is**: The Etsy seller account needs to be active and verified before any listings can go live. This means: account not suspended or under review, Etsy Payments connected and not pending identity verification.

**Time to resolve**: 10 minutes of user action + 24–48 hours for Etsy's verification process

**Why it matters**: Even if all 21 product listings are uploaded in Draft status, they cannot be published (made visible to buyers) until Etsy has verified the seller account and connected Etsy Payments. Attempting to publish before verification results in listings that appear live on your side but are invisible to buyers.

---

### Step 1: Check current account status (2 minutes)

Log into Etsy at etsy.com > click your profile icon > Sell on Etsy > Shop Manager.

Look for any of these banners:
- "Your shop is under review"
- "Identity verification required"
- "Complete your shop setup"
- "Connect Etsy Payments to start selling"

**If no banner is visible**: your account may already be verified. Proceed to Step 3 (verification test).

**If any banner is visible**: proceed to Step 2.

---

### Step 2: Complete verification (5–10 minutes)

**For "Identity verification required"**:
- Click the banner or go to: Etsy > Account Settings > Security > Identity Verification
- Provide a government-issued ID (driver's license or passport)
- Etsy's verification is typically completed in 1–5 business days
- You will receive an email when verification is complete

**For "Connect Etsy Payments"**:
- Go to: Etsy > Shop Manager > Finances > Payment Settings
- Click "Set up Etsy Payments"
- Enter your bank account information (routing number + account number)
- Confirm your tax information (SSN or EIN for US sellers)
- Etsy Payments setup typically completes within 1–2 business days

**For "Complete your shop setup"**:
- Go to: Etsy > Shop Manager > Settings > General Settings
- Ensure all required fields are filled: shop name, shop policies, billing information
- Click Save after each section

---

### Step 3: Verification test (3 minutes)

After completing Step 2 (or if no banners were visible), verify the account is active:

- Navigate to your Etsy shop public URL: etsy.com/shop/[your-shop-name]
- If the shop page loads with your listings visible to an incognito browser: account is active
- If the page shows "This shop is currently unavailable" or a redirect to the shop setup page: verification is still pending

---

### Step 4: If verification is still pending after 5 business days

Contact Etsy support directly:
- URL: help.etsy.com > Contact Us > "Shop verification" topic
- Provide: your shop name, the date you submitted documents, the email associated with your account
- Reference that you have products ready to publish and need to understand the timeline
- Etsy support typically responds within 24–48 hours

**If verification cannot be resolved before May 24 (Option B contingency)**:
- Phase 2 (Track B) launches independently on May 24 — no Etsy dependency
- Phase 1 listings remain in Draft and can be published the same day Etsy verification completes
- Alternative: Gumroad (gumroad.com) as a temporary sales channel — 15-minute setup, 10% transaction fee vs. Etsy's 6.5%
- See `TRACK_A_CONTINGENCY_LAUNCH_PLAN.md` Section 5, Option B for the full contingency plan
