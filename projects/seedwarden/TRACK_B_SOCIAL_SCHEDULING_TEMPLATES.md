# Track B Social Scheduling Templates
## Launch Week: May 30 – June 5, 2026

**How to use this file**: Work through each day in order. For each post:
1. Copy the caption text into Buffer/Later's caption field
2. Copy the hashtags into the hashtag field (some platforms keep them separate)
3. Select the image file at the path listed under "Asset"
4. Set the schedule time listed
5. Add the Etsy link or Kit landing page URL where noted

**When to execute**: May 19 (after social accounts are live from Gate 1). Buffer/Later
scheduling does not require Etsy listings to be live yet — the links can be added once
listings are activated on May 30.

**Buffer vs. Later**: Both support Instagram, TikTok, and Pinterest scheduling. Buffer's
free tier supports 3 connected channels and 10 scheduled posts per channel. Later's free tier
supports 1 channel. If scheduling all platforms in one session, use Buffer or upgrade to Later's
Starter plan ($18/mo) for launch week only.

**TikTok native upload rule**: TikTok posts cannot be published via Buffer/Later on the free tier.
Buffer/Later will notify you at the scheduled time to upload manually. Do not use Instagram's
"Share to TikTok" cross-post feature — TikTok suppresses algorithmically distributed cross-posts.
Upload the same video file directly from your phone's camera roll.
