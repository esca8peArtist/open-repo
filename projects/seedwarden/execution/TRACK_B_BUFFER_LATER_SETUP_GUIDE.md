## Part 2: Brand Voice Guidelines by Platform

### Instagram Brand Voice

**Tone**: Conversational, educational, warm
**Audience**: Backyard gardeners, homesteaders, food sovereignty learners
**Post types**: Origin story (Day 1 Reel), carousel guides, carousel tips, educational reels
**Content focus**: Before-and-after visuals, variety stories, seed-saving tips, harvest insights

**Visual style**: 
- Mixed: mockup product shots + real hands/gardens
- Color palette: Deep Forest Green (#143b28), Warm Cream (#F5EDD6), sage tones
- Always include a hook in first 3 seconds (text overlay or spoken)

**Engagement expectations**:
- Respond to comments within 24 hours
- Captions 100–300 words (long-form storytelling)
- Hashtags: 15–30 relevant tags per post
- Posting frequency: 3–4x per week during launch month

### TikTok Brand Voice

**Tone**: Authentic, slightly more casual than Instagram, educational
**Audience**: Younger homesteaders, Gen Z gardeners, foraging enthusiasts
**Post types**: 15–30 second educational clips, before-and-after visuals, quick tips, trend-adjacent content

**TikTok-specific rules**:
- NEVER cross-post from Instagram — always upload native from camera roll
- Hook viewers in first 2 seconds (text overlay or trending audio optional)
- Use trending sounds but only if they match the topic (not forced)
- Hashtags: 3–5 per video (TikTok algorithm rewards specificity over volume)
- Call-to-action: "Link in bio" with Kit landing page URL

**Posting frequency**: 2–3x per week minimum to signal activity

### Pinterest Brand Voice

**Tone**: Inspirational, practical, lifestyle-focused
**Audience**: DIY gardeners, homesteaders, food preservers, gift-buyers researching topics
**Post types**: Static pins, carousel pins (5-slide templates ready in Canva)
**Content focus**: Product guides, regional growing plans, livestock care, preservation methods

**Visual style**:
- 1000x1500px tall pins (best for Pinterest algorithm)
- Product image + text overlay (template ready in pin-template-specs.md)
- Color: Deep Forest Green header/footer, Warm Cream body, product image centered
- Text readable at thumbnail size (font size minimum 18px)

**Engagement expectations**:
- Pins drive traffic to Etsy listings (goal: click-through to purchase)
- Destination link: Always point to specific Etsy product listing, never generic shop URL
- Pin description: 200–300 characters with long-tail keywords and hashtags
- Posting frequency: 3–5 pins per week (Pinterest reward consistency + fresh content)

## Troubleshooting Quick Reference

**Instagram connection fails**:
- Confirm Seedwarden account is Business type (not personal)
- Confirm email is wanka95@gmail.com
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting in Buffer/Later settings

**TikTok not available in Buffer/Later**:
- TikTok scheduling support varies by platform version
- Fallback: Use TikTok's native scheduler (5 minutes on May 30 morning)
- See TRACK_B_DAY_1_CONTENT_PRODUCTION_STAGING.md Piece 2 for native scheduler steps

**Pinterest board won't appear in scheduling UI**:
- Create board at pinterest.com first (Settings > Create board)
- Refresh Buffer/Later page
- Re-authorize if needed (Dashboard > Channel Settings > Reauthorize)

**Posts scheduled but not posting at scheduled time**:
- Confirm channel is in "active" state (not paused)
- Check time zone setting in Buffer/Later (should match your local time)
- Verify Etsy shop is live and listings are not in draft state
