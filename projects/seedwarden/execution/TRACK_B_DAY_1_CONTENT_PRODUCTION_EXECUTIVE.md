## Part 6: Risk Mitigations

### Risk: Mockup Image File Not Found

**If**: Mockup PNG file is missing from `projects/seedwarden/mockups/` directory

**Mitigation**:
1. Check WORKLOG.md for list of all 63 mockup files (provided by session 1152)
2. Search in sibling directories: `assets/`, `mockups/`, `products/`
3. If file is truly missing, substitute with the nearest related product mockup
4. Log the substitution in WORKLOG.md under "Day 1 Content Notes"

**Fallback visual**: If specific mockup is unavailable, use ANY mockup from the Seedwarden
product line (they all show the same "before and after" concept). The specific product is
less important than demonstrating the concept.

---

### Risk: Instagram Reel Fails to Render at Scheduled Time

**If**: May 30 at 09:00 — Reel is scheduled but doesn't post

**Mitigation**:
1. Check Instagram account status (not suspended, login works)
2. Verify Reel file is not corrupted: re-upload to Buffer/Later
3. Try uploading directly to Instagram (native upload) instead of scheduler
4. If native upload works but scheduler doesn't, note issue in WORKLOG.md

**Fallback**: Post manually May 30 morning (5 minutes, direct Instagram upload).

---

### Risk: TikTok Upload Platform Unavailable

**If**: TikTok app/website is down or blocked on May 30 morning

**Mitigation**:
1. Use VPN if TikTok is regionally blocked
2. Try web (tiktok.com) instead of mobile app or vice versa
3. Wait up to 2 hours for service restoration
4. If unavailable beyond 11:00am local time, post later that day (TikTok algorithm doesn't
   penalize same-day delayed posts as long as they're still within May 30)

**Fallback**: Post May 31 morning (same video, one day delayed).

---

### Risk: Pinterest Pin Text Is Unreadable at Thumbnail Size

**If**: May 27 QA check — text is too small or blurry at 100% zoom in Canva

**Mitigation**:
1. Verify Canva design is set to actual dimensions (1000x1500px, not zoomed out)
2. Zoom to 100% (View > Zoom > 100%)
3. If text is still small, increase font size:
   - Zone 3 (product name): minimum 36px Playfair Display
   - Zone 4 (description): minimum 22px Lato
   - Zone 5 (URL): minimum 18px Lato
4. Re-export and re-test

**Fallback**: Simplify text in Zone 4 (description) to 1 line instead of 2, reducing
character count and forcing larger font size.

---

### Risk: Etsy Listings Are Not Yet Live

**If**: May 30 morning — Etsy listings are still in draft state

**Mitigation**:
1. Activate all Etsy listings by May 29 EOD (confirm in Etsy Shop Manager)
2. Update Pinterest pin destination URLs if listing URLs changed
3. If a listing is not ready, substitute a live listing instead

**Fallback**: Delay Day 1 Pinterest pins until all Etsy listings are live (push to May 31
or June 1). Instagram and TikTok can proceed (they don't link directly to Etsy).

---

### Risk: Kit Landing Page URL Is Not Confirmed

**If**: May 30 morning — Kit landing page is not yet live

**Mitigation**:
1. Instagram/TikTok bio links can remain empty initially (just say "Link in bio coming soon")
2. Update bios as soon as Kit page is confirmed live (May 27–29)
3. Email subscribers will know to look for link anyway (from email subject line and CTA)

**Fallback**: Use Etsy shop URL (seedwarden.etsy.com) instead of Kit landing page.
This is suboptimal for email capture but keeps all CTAs live.

---

### Risk: Time Zone Mismatch on Scheduled Posts

**If**: May 30 — Posts are scheduled for wrong time (e.g., 9pm instead of 9am)

**Mitigation**:
1. Confirm your local time zone in Buffer/Later settings BEFORE scheduling
2. Double-check each post's scheduled time before confirming
3. Use 24-hour format (09:00 = 9am, 21:00 = 9pm) to avoid AM/PM confusion
4. Screenshot each scheduled post showing the date/time

**Fallback**: Manually post any misdated content on May 30 at the correct local time.
