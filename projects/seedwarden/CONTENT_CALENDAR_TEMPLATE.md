## Content Mix Ratios — Definition

| Category | Definition | Expected Content Form |
|---|---|---|
| Educational (30%) | Teaches a specific, verifiable skill or fact. Dandelion ID features, seed saving technique, leaf arrangement rule. No vague wellness language. | TikTok video, Instagram Carousel, Pinterest Educational Hook Pin |
| Community / Brand (30%) | Builds relationship with existing audience. Customer stories, behind-the-scenes of guide production, "what we learned" updates, creator presence. | Instagram Reel (talking head), TikTok talking head, Instagram Story |
| Promotional (20%) | Direct product spotlight. Product name, price, features, link. No apology for selling. 1-in-5 posts maximum. | Instagram Carousel (product), Pinterest Product Mockup Pin, Reel scroll-through |
| Behind-the-Scenes (20%) | Process content: photo shoot setup, field location, notebook sketches, production day. Differentiated from "Community" by subject — BTS is about the work, Community is about the people. | TikTok short, Instagram Story, Instagram Reel |

## Part 2: Hashtag Sets

Hashtag strategy follows the 3–5 hashtag rule for Instagram (confirmed post-December 2025 algorithm).
TikTok: 3–6 hashtags. Pinterest: hashtags in pin description, no hard limit but 8–12 is optimal.

### Set A — TikTok Core

Use on all TikTok posts. Rotate the third hashtag based on content type (see sub-sets below).

**Educational content**:
```
#wildedibles #foraging #plantidentification
```
**Brand/Community content**:
```
#foraging #homesteading #seedwarden
```
**Seed saving content**:
```
#seedsaving #heirloomseeds #homesteading
```
**Seasonal / urgency content**:
```
#foraging #wildedibles #seasonalforaging
```
**Food sovereignty content**:
```
#foodsovereignty #homesteading #selfsufficiency
```

### Set B — Instagram Core

Use on all Instagram posts. Add one content-specific hashtag from the rotation list below.

**Base (use on every post)**:
```
#foraging #seedwarden
```
**+ content-specific (rotate)**:
- Educational plant ID posts: add `#wildedibles`
- Seed saving posts: add `#seedsaving`
- Homestead / seasonal posts: add `#homesteading`
- Product promotion posts: add `#etsydigitaldownload`
- Zone-specific posts: add `#growyourown`

**Maximum 5 hashtags total on any Instagram post.**

### Set B-promo — Instagram Promotional Posts

Use specifically when the post is direct product promotion:
```
#etsydigitaldownload #foraging #seedwarden
```
Optional fourth: `#wildedibles` or `#homesteading` based on which product is featured.

### Set C — Pinterest Pin Description Hashtags

Pinterest hashtags appear at the end of pin descriptions. Use 8–12 per pin.

**Set C-product (Product Mockup Pin)**:
```
#[product-specific keyword] #growyourown #homesteading #[zone-keyword if applicable]
#seedwarden #heirloomseeds #digitaldownload #instantdownload
```
Product-specific keywords: `#survivalgraden` / `#wildedibles` / `#seedsaving` / `#nativeplants`
Zone keywords: `#zone5` / `#zone7` / `#midwestgarden` — add when the product is zone-specific.

**Set C-edu (Educational Hook Pin)**:
```
#[topic keyword] #foraging #plantidentification #growyourown
#homesteading #seedsaving #[zone keyword] #fieldguide
```

### Brand Hashtag Policy

- `#seedwarden` — use on every Instagram and TikTok post without exception. This builds the brand
  hashtag over time. Do not use on Pinterest (Pinterest hashtags serve search, not community).
- `#foraging` and `#homesteading` — primary reach hashtags; use on most posts.
- `#etsydigitaldownload` — promotional posts only; not on educational content.

## Part 3: Posting Time Rationale

Times are calibrated for Central Time Zone (Zone 5 Midwest primary audience) based on
typical engagement patterns for educational content in this niche.

| Platform | Optimal Window | Why |
|---|---|---|
| TikTok | 6:00–8:00am CT | Morning commute + breakfast consumption window; educational content performs well before work day |
| Instagram Reels | 8:00–10:00am CT | Peak Instagram Reels distribution window; algorithm favors early engagement |
| Instagram Carousels/Static | 9:00–11:00am CT | Saves and shares (higher-value actions) concentrated mid-morning for this content type |
| Pinterest | 8:00am–1:00pm CT | Pinterest is used during planning sessions; morning-to-midday aligns with homesteader decision-making window |

**Avoid**: 12:00–2:00pm CT (lunch noise, algorithm deprioritizes), 9:00pm+ CT (engagement exists
but save rate drops; educational content saves are the primary value signal on this platform).

## Part 5: Behind-the-Scenes Content Prompts

BTS content requires no scripting — these are prompts to capture during natural work sessions.
Target: one BTS piece per week (Weeks 2–4). Can be 30-second TikTok or Instagram Story series.

| Week | BTS Opportunity | What to Capture |
|---|---|---|
| Week 2 | Field location scout or walk | 30 seconds of footage: location, what species are present, what you're looking for |
| Week 3 | Guide content review or annotation | Notebook, printed draft, red-pen corrections; narrate one thing you changed |
| Week 4 | Print or packaging review | Guide in hand, compare digital vs. print, note tactile quality observation |
| Any week | Canva template building | Screen capture of design in progress; narrate one design decision |
| Any week | iNaturalist or USDA lookup | Show the research process; explain why you verify species names from two sources |

BTS content performs best when it shows a specific decision being made, not a vague "process."
"I changed this caption because buyers asked..." outperforms "here's my workspace."
