# Co-Marketing Partnership Opportunities
## Seedwarden Phase 3–4

**Prepared**: May 21, 2026
**Partnership activation window**: June 1 – July 13, 2026
**Revenue impact focus**: Reduce customer acquisition cost (CAC) from organic-only baseline; generate cross-audience exposure to qualified buyers already purchasing complementary wellness products.

---

## 1. Framework: What Makes a Good Co-Marketing Partner

The right co-marketing partner for Seedwarden Phase 3 satisfies three conditions simultaneously:

1. **Audience overlap without product overlap** — Their customers are interested in medicinal herbs, plant-based wellness, or clinical herbalism but are not already purchasing digital field guides from Seedwarden.
2. **Trust transfer** — Their endorsement transfers credibility. A nutritionist recommending Seedwarden's Women's Health guide is a more credible signal than a paid ad to the same audience.
3. **Logistics simplicity** — The partnership can be executed with a short email, a swap of promotional posts, or a coupon code. No contracts, no co-branded product development, no revenue splits requiring accounting overhead.

For Phase 3 (June 22 launch), all partnerships should be executable by a single person in under two hours of setup. Complex revenue-split or co-branded product arrangements belong in Phase 4 planning.

**Partnership value scoring** (1–5 for each dimension):
- **Audience fit** — How closely does their audience match Seedwarden's buyer profile?
- **Trust transfer** — Does their endorsement carry credibility weight for the herbalism practitioner market?
- **Friction to activate** — How hard is it to start the conversation and get a yes?
- **Revenue potential** — What is a realistic monthly revenue contribution if the partnership is active?

## 5. Bundle Co-Marketing Opportunities

These are product-adjacent bundles where Seedwarden guides are packaged with a complementary physical or digital product from a partner brand. No inventory is required from Seedwarden (guides are digital). Revenue split negotiated per bundle.

| Bundle Concept | Partner Type | Seedwarden Contribution | Partner Contribution | Split Model | Complexity | Priority |
|---------------|-------------|------------------------|---------------------|-------------|------------|----------|
| **Practitioner Herb Library + Seed Starter Kit** | Strictly Medicinal Seeds or similar seed company | Women's Health + Immunity PDF guides | Curated seed packet (5–7 medicinal herb seeds for cultivation) | Seedwarden guides at MSRP; partner seeds at MSRP; no split required — separate purchases co-promoted | Low | High (Phase 3) |
| **Sleep Support Bundle** | Herbal tea company (not Mountain Rose) | Sleep PDF guide | Sample-size dried Valerian + Passionflower tea | Revenue split: 50/50 on bundle sale OR independent co-promotion | Medium | Medium (Phase 3) |
| **Women's Herbal Wellness Kit** | Herbalism school alumni community | Women's Health PDF guide | School-branded herb identification card or botanical print | No revenue split — co-brand for mutual visibility only | Low | Medium (Phase 3) |
| **Clinical Reference Library + Skincare Guide** | Phase 4 Herbal Skincare (Seedwarden internal) | Women's Health + Respiratory PDF guides | Herbal Skincare guide (Phase 4 product) | Internal — no external split required | Low | High (Phase 4) |
| **Herb Grower's Digital Toolkit** | Etsy sellers: seed envelopes, botanical prints, planting guides | Cultivation sections across all bundles | Complementary digital product (botanical illustration set or zone planting chart) | Etsy Bundle feature OR simple cross-shop promotion | Low | Medium (Phase 3–4) |

**Implementation note for Etsy bundle cross-promotion**: Etsy does not natively support cross-seller bundles (items from two different shops cannot be sold as a single transaction). The workaround is a shared promotional campaign — both shops run a simultaneous discount and link to each other in listing descriptions or social posts. No revenue split required; each seller keeps their own revenue. This is the zero-friction model for Phase 3.
