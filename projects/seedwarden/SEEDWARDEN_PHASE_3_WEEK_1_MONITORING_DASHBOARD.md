# Seedwarden Phase 3 Week 1-2 Monitoring Dashboard

**Purpose**: Real-time tracking of email, social, and contractor metrics during Week 1-2 execution (June 29 – July 13, 2026). This dashboard converts abstract targets into actionable daily tallies. When a metric crosses a threshold, the response is documented.

**Update cadence**:
- **Morning brief** (8:00 AM ET): Log any overnight email/social events
- **Mid-day check** (12:00 PM ET): Email delivery rate + open rate at 3-hour mark
- **Evening review** (6:00 PM ET): Final daily tally + next-day prep

**How to use**: Copy each day's section, fill in actuals, and compare to GREEN / YELLOW / RED thresholds. When a metric triggers YELLOW or RED, execute the response step from PHASE_3_WEEK_1_2_ALERT_THRESHOLDS.md.
