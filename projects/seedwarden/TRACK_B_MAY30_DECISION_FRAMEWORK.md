3. Etsy seasonality supports the 3-bundle sequence: Women's Health (practitioner intent year-round), Respiratory (buyers research in summer for fall/winter), Sleep (July burnout peak). All three have strong July demand signals.

4. The November–December holiday review accumulation window is fully preserved. Both the 3-bundle and 5-bundle paths get all listings live before October, which is the effective accumulation window start for the cold/flu season peak.
