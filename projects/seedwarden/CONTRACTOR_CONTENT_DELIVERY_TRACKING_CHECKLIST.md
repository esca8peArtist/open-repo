# Contractor Content Delivery Tracking Checklist — Week 1-2

**PURPOSE**: Deterministic tracking of 6 contractor deliverables during peak acquisition period (Women's Health + Respiratory launches). Every deliverable has a due date, a quality gate, an escalation rule, and a payment trigger. No judgment calls — follow the rules mechanically.

**CONTRACTORS & TRACKS**:
- **Photography Track**: 1 photographer (15 images total: 5 per bundle × 3 sessions)
- **Writing Track**: 3 writers (Respiratory, Immunity, Digestive copy)
- **Specialist Track**: 1 habitat specialist (ecosystem sections)

**SUCCESS CRITERIA**:
- All deliverables received on or before due date
- All deliverables pass quality gates (no revisions needed)
- All milestones paid within 48 hours of approval
- Escalation rules applied mechanically (3+ days late = AT RISK, 5+ days = fallback activation)
