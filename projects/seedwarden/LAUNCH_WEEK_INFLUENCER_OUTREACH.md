# Seedwarden Launch Week Influencer Outreach
## May 30 – June 5, 2026

**Objective**: Seed awareness of Seedwarden's launch with 50+ micro-influencers across four content categories (foraging/wild edibles, homesteading/growing, prepper/survival, permaculture/sustainable living). Goal is not viral amplification — it is qualified introductions: getting the product in front of people who have audiences that will actually buy.

**Outreach offer**: 1–2 relevant digital guides (free, instant download access) in exchange for an honest mention or review. No payment. 15% affiliate code for their audience if they want to promote further. No exclusivity, no posting requirement.

**Why this offer converts**: Micro-influencers in this niche are content-hungry — they need to post something genuinely useful 3–6 times per week. A well-produced, research-backed field guide is content they can build around. The offer costs Seedwarden $0 (digital delivery) and generates content with direct product placement in front of qualified buyers.

**Contact window**: Contact all Tier 1 (highest priority) influencers by May 23. Contact Tier 2 by May 26. Leave May 27–29 for follow-up on non-replies. Target first posts landing May 29–June 2.

## Launch Week Amplification Sequence: May 30 – June 5

### Pre-Launch (May 19–29): Contact Tier 1

**May 19–23**: Contact Tier 1 foraging accounts (#1–17) by email where email is findable; by DM where it is not. Use Template A. Send access to Wild Edibles Quick Reference via Google Drive shared link. Customize each message to reference one specific piece of their content.

**May 24–26**: Contact Tier 1 homesteading accounts (#18–32) using Template B. Send Seed Saving Field Manual + Zone Card access. Note outreach in the tracking log (see Metrics section below).

**May 27–29**: Contact Tier 1 prepper accounts (#33–43) using Template C. Send Wild Edibles + Hunting Manual bundle access. Follow up on non-replies from May 19–23 with one brief DM: "Sent you a note about Seedwarden guides — did it land in your inbox?"

**May 29**: Contact Tier 2 accounts (#44–55). These are larger accounts — pitch is the same but acknowledge their scale briefly: "I know your following is large, so this is just a free copy to look at — no posting requirement or expectation." Manage your own expectations: Tier 2 response rate is 5–15% vs. 20–35% for Tier 1 micro-accounts.

**Weekly email sign-up targets**:
- Week 1 (May 30–June 5): 25–50 sign-ups from all sources combined
- Week 2 (June 6–12): 40–70 sign-ups (influencer content begins compounding)
- Week 3–4 (June 13–26): 60–100/week (organic social momentum established)

**Launch week social engagement targets**:
- TikTok completion rate: 60%+ average across Week 1 posts
- Instagram Reel saves: 20+ per Reel by Day 7
- Pinterest impressions: 500+ by June 5 (new accounts start low; this signals indexing success)
- Kit landing page conversion rate: 15%+ of visitors (if below 10%, test alternate headline)

**Influencer post performance targets** (if any Tier 1 posts during launch week):
- Minimum acceptable: 1 influencer post featuring Seedwarden during May 30–June 5
- Strong: 3–5 influencer posts during launch week
- Exceptional: 1 influencer post generating 50+ Kit sign-ups or 10+ Etsy views directly

**Affiliate code tracking**: Each influencer code (e.g., CHAOTICFORAGER15) used at Etsy checkout is directly attributable. Aggregate weekly. Any influencer whose code generates 3+ purchases in the first 30 days is worth a follow-up: offer an upgraded commission rate (20%) or a collaborative content project.

**Kill criteria**: If an outreach batch of 10 contacts generates zero responses after 7 days, review the template and offer. Common failure modes: pitch is too generic (not personalized), guide offer is mismatched to their niche, DM went to filtered message requests. Adjust before next batch.
