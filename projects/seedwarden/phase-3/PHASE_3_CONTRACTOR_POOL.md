## Licensing Terms Reference

All contractor engagements require these licensing terms at minimum. Include in initial outreach and confirm before deposit payment.

| Term | Requirement | Acceptable Minimum |
|---|---|---|
| Usage rights | Commercial use, print and digital | Non-exclusive commercial license |
| Attribution | Attribution not required in guide body | Seedwarden brand, not contractor name |
| Exclusivity | Non-exclusive preferred | Non-exclusive; exclusive if needed and priced accordingly |
| IP assignment | All work for hire, Seedwarden retains rights | Work-for-hire clause in contract |
| Derivative works | Seedwarden may modify/adapt content | Must be confirmed in writing |
| Term | Perpetual for Seedwarden use | Minimum 5-year license if not perpetual |
