## Screening Rubric Quick Reference

### Photographer Track (50 points)

| Criterion | Max Points | Quick Scoring |
|---|---|---|
| Flat-lay portfolio confirmed | 20 | 20=dedicated flat-lay; 14=mixed portfolio with flat-lay; 6=product but no flat-lay; 0=no product work |
| Image quality / resolution | 15 | 15=clearly 2000px+, clean; 10=good exposure, resolution unclear; 3=quality issues |
| July 1–8 availability confirmed | 10 | 10=explicit; 6=probable; 3=not yet; 0=unavailable |
| Commercial license confirmed | 5 | 5=confirmed in writing; 2=willing to negotiate; 0=refuses |

**Threshold**: 35+ proceed to bid/discovery; 30–34 borderline; below 30 decline.
