# Seedwarden Phase 2 — Social Media Setup Guide

## Overview

Seedwarden Phase 2 launch (May 30) requires Instagram, TikTok, and Pinterest business accounts. This guide walks you through account creation, verification, business setup, and content policy compliance for each platform.

**Timeline**: 14 days (May 16-29) before launch on May 30  
**Effort**: 4-6 hours total setup (can split into 2-3 sessions)  
**Cost**: $0-30/month for business features (optional upgrades)

| Platform | Setup time | Cost | Features needed | Compliance notes |

| **Instagram** | 45 min | Free | Shopping tags, Reels, cross-post to Facebook | None special |

## Part 5: Content Moderation & Community Management

### Comment/DM Response Policy

**Respond to all DMs within 24 hours**:
```
Q: "Is this plant edible?"
A: "I can't ID plants from photos alone. Here's how to identify: [link to guide]. 
    If unsure, consult a local expert or use multiple sources before consuming."

Q: "Can this cure [disease]?"
A: "I provide foraging information, not medical advice. Always consult a doctor 
    for health concerns. These are food plants, not medicine."

Q: "Where can I find X?"
A: "X grows in [region/habitat]. Here's how to identify and safely harvest: [guide link]."
```

### Flagging Dangerous Content

If someone posts dangerous foraging advice (e.g., "eat this poisonous plant"):
1. **Don't engage** (no argument/correction in comments)
2. **Report to platform** (especially TikTok/Instagram for safety violations)
3. **Optional**: Pin safe version of that guide in your replies

### Banned Topics (Don't Engage)

- "This herb cures cancer" discussions
- "Foraging in [private property]" tips without permission disclaimer
- Controlled substance identification (cannabis, poppies) in non-legal jurisdictions

## Part 7: Legal & Compliance Notes

### Plant Identification Accuracy

**Critical**: Misidentification can be dangerous. Before publishing any guide:
- ✅ Cross-reference 3+ authoritative sources (Peterson, Flora manuals, university extension)
- ✅ Include at least 2 toxic look-alikes
- ✅ Include clear ID markers ("Distinctive smell", "Hairy stem", "Serrated edges")
- ✅ Include habitat/season info
- ✅ Test your ID with local experts if possible

### Foraging Safety Disclaimers

Add to every guide:
```
"⚠️  Foraging Safety: Never eat wild plants unless you can positively identify them. 
When in doubt, do not consume. Consult multiple sources before harvesting. Some people 
have allergies — do a small test first if new to a plant. Forage only where permitted; 
always get landowner permission on private property."
```

### Controlled Substances

**Cannabis** (legal in some states):
- ❌ Don't post in non-legal states (violates platform policy)
- ✅ Safe to post in legal states with clear "legal only where permitted" disclaimer
- Check TikTok/Instagram/Pinterest cannabis policies for your region

**Poppies** (ornamental vs drug):
- ✅ OK to post about ornamental poppies (California poppy, Oriental poppy)
- ❌ Don't post about opium poppies or drug-production methods
- Use clear botanical names to avoid confusion

**Regulated plants** (endangered or harvest-restricted):
- ✅ OK to post about identification/biology
- ✅ Must include: "Protected in [state]. Do not harvest."
- Examples: Wild ginseng (regulated in most states), truffles (controlled in EU)

### Affiliate Links & Sponsorships

If you link to Amazon/Etsy product listings:
- ✅ Clearly disclose: "This post contains affiliate links" (Pinterest requires this)
- ✅ Only recommend products you've personally tested
- ❌ Don't misrepresent affiliate interest as unbiased review

If brands approach you for sponsorship:
- ✅ Clearly label: "Sponsored" or "Ad" (FTC + platform requirement)
- ✅ Only promote products/services you actually believe in
- ❌ Don't accept sponsorship that conflicts with your mission

## Part 8: Troubleshooting & FAQ

### "My TikTok is getting no views"

**Common causes & fixes**:
1. **Not using trending sounds**: TikTok prioritizes posts with trending audio. Use TikTok's "Discover" → "Sounds" to see trending audio.
2. **Text too small**: Text overlay must be readable at 1-inch mobile screen size
3. **Boring first 3 seconds**: TikTok stops playing if users swipe away immediately. Hook viewers in first second.
4. **Wrong hashtags or none at all**: Use 5-10 hashtags + let algorithm push you (#FYP = for you page)

**Quick fix**: Record a new video with trending sound from TikTok, large text, and a question hook ("Can you ID this plant?"). Post as Reel (Instagram) for better reach.

### "Instagram reach is low, nobody seeing my posts"

**Common causes**:
1. **Not using Reels**: Instagram heavily prioritizes Reels over static posts
2. **Posting at wrong time**: Post 6-10am or 6-9pm (peak engagement times)
3. **No hashtags or using banned ones**: Use 15-30 hashtags; avoid over-aggressive ones
4. **Engagement too low**: Engage with 20-30 other accounts daily (comment, like) to boost visibility

**Quick fix**: Repost your best photo as a Reel (with trend music), include 20-30 relevant hashtags, respond to every comment within 1 hour.

### "Pinterest pins not getting clicks"

**Common causes**:
1. **Weak pin title/description**: Add keywords ("plant identification", "foraging guide")
2. **Ugly thumbnail**: Bright, large text; high-contrast colors; readable at small size
3. **Low-SEO link**: Link to obscure URL. Use guide title in URL if possible (e.g., /garlic-mustard-guide)
4. **Too few pins**: Need 30-50 pins to start getting algorithmic reach; requires 2-3 weeks

**Quick fix**: Redesign top 5 pins with better titles and keywords. Create 20 more pins over next week. Review analytics → repin your top-performing pins every 2-3 weeks.

### "Account getting flagged for spam"

**Common causes**:
1. **Cross-posting identical content**: Each platform wants unique content tailored to that audience
2. **Too many links**: Don't link from every post. Mix educational content with guides.
3. **Engagement pods/bots**: Avoid any services promising "instant followers" — they violate platform policies
4. **Using bots to auto-comment**: Every comment and like should be genuine

**Quick fix**: 
- Vary your content (mix guide links with educational-only posts, behind-scenes, community content)
- Respond to comments and DMs manually
- Don't use third-party engagement tools
