
## Paid Promotion Threshold

If Day 14 Gist views are between 150–300 and organic growth has plateaued, consider a targeted paid test:

- **Budget**: $20–50 (one-time test, not ongoing spend)
- **Platform**: Instagram or Pinterest (both have gardening and homesteading interest categories)
- **Targeting**: Interests — gardening, heirloom seeds, herbalism, homesteading; geography — US, by zone if possible
- **Goal**: Drive 50+ additional downloads to establish whether paid traffic converts
- **Decision**: If paid test produces > 20 downloads at under $1.50 per download, consider a sustained $50–100/month promotion budget for Track B
