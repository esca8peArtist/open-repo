6 hours if working continuously both days.

**Output**: 8 zone card PDFs, exported, named, saved locally, uploaded to Google Drive,
sharing set to "Anyone with the link," direct download links generated and tested.

without Google Drive download links. Gate 3 Kit setup starts May 27. If zone cards are not

### Block 6: Google Drive Upload and Link Generation (11:30–12:00, 30 min)

This step makes the zone cards accessible via email. All 8 PDFs must be on Drive before
Gate 3 (Kit) can be completed.

**Steps**:
1. In Google Drive (logged into wanka95@gmail.com), create a new folder: `Seedwarden Zone Cards`
2. Upload all 8 PDF files into this folder
3. For each file, right-click > Share > "Anyone with the link" > "Viewer" > Copy link
   That link is in the format `https://drive.google.com/file/d/[FILE_ID]/view`
4. Convert each link to a direct download link:
   Replace `/file/d/[FILE_ID]/view` with `/uc?export=download&id=[FILE_ID]`
   Result: `https://drive.google.com/uc?export=download&id=[FILE_ID]`
5. In an incognito window, paste each direct download link into the browser address bar
6. Confirm: the PDF downloads immediately without prompting for Google account sign-in
   If any link shows "Request access" or opens in a Google Drive viewer: the sharing
   setting is wrong. Re-check the sharing permission on that specific file.
7. Log all 8 working download links in WORKLOG.md under the heading "Kit Zone Card File URLs"

**Why the `/uc?export=download&id=` format**: The standard Google Drive share URL opens
a viewer in Gmail. Kit Email 1 must trigger an immediate PDF download, not a viewer page.
The `/uc?export=download` format forces immediate download. This is required — Kit Email 1
built with the wrong URL format will fail the 3-test protocol on May 29.
