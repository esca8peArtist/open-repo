## Section 1: Decision Matrix

### Decision 1 — Sprint Scope

**Question**: Which bundles execute in the June 22–July 13 sprint?

**Context**: Writing time is the binding constraint. All five bundles require 56–66 adjusted hours solo. The three-bundle path (Women's Health, Respiratory, Sleep) requires 36–44 adjusted hours and preserves 3–5 hours per bundle for editing — below this threshold, quality degrades measurably. The practitioner tier requires a minimum of three bundles live, so three bundles is the minimum scope that unlocks the $47/month practitioner revenue stream.

| | Option A — Conservative | Option B — Minimal | **Option C — Balanced (RECOMMENDED)** |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Bundles in sprint** | 5 bundles (all) | 2 bundles (Women's Health + Respiratory) | **3 bundles (Women's Health + Respiratory + Sleep)** |
| **Writing hours** | 56–66 hrs (solo, compressed) | ~30 hrs | **36–44 hrs** |
| **First upload** | June 29 | June 29 | **June 29** |
| **Sprint end** | Aug 2 (all 5 complete) | July 7 | **July 13** |
| **Editing time per bundle** | 2–2.5 hrs (compressed) | 5 hrs each | **5 hrs each** |
| **Practitioner tier eligible** | Yes, immediately (July) | No (2-bundle minimum not met) | **Yes, end-of-July** |
| **Complexity** | High | Low | **Medium** |
| **Daily writing load** | 5+ hrs/day (Jun 22–Aug 2) | 4 hrs/day (Jun 22–Jul 7) | **3–4 hrs/day (Jun 22–Jul 13)** |

**Pros and cons by option**:

Option A (5-bundle, all): Full product line ready at one launch moment. Practitioner tier launches with complete offering. Con: 5+ hrs/day writing with no buffer across 42 days. Any overrun on bundle 3 or 4 cascades into all subsequent bundles. Quality variance is high at this pace. Risk: high likelihood of at least one bundle requiring a rewrite pass, pushing Digestive or Immunity to September.

Option B (2-bundle, minimal): Lowest execution risk; fastest path to first revenue. Con: practitioner tier is unavailable with only 2 bundles, capping steady-state revenue at the core tier only. Customer satisfaction risk if buyers ask "where is the Sleep bundle?" and no timeline is available. Option B is the right choice only if an unexpected time constraint emerges before June 22.

Option C (3-bundle, balanced): Three bundles cover the three highest Etsy search-intent categories (Women's Health, Respiratory, Sleep/nervines) and align directly with July demand signals (practitioner research, summer burnout, fall/winter respiratory prep). Writing load of 36–44 hours over 22 days is 1.6–2 hours per day on average, with natural writing days at 4 hours and rest days built in. Editing time of 5 hours per bundle is preserved. Immunity and Digestive defer to July 20 and August 3 at zero revenue cost to the June 22–July 13 window.

**Recommendation: Option C.**

Option C is the only path that simultaneously achieves June 22 launch, preserves editorial quality, and unlocks the practitioner tier by end of July. The revenue gap versus Option A is modest in the first 90 days (see Section 2) and closes entirely by October when all five bundles are live regardless of which sprint option is selected.

**Goldenseal drops off the critical path under Option C**: Goldenseal is the Immunity bundle (deferred to July 20). Women's Health and Respiratory launch with no Goldenseal dependency. The June 1 supplier deadline becomes a quality upgrade option rather than a hard launch gate — this is the single most important risk reduction of Option C.

At 50 core buyers/month and 20% practitioner conversion: 10 practitioners/month is a realistic mid-range estimate.

The practitioner tier is the primary revenue lever that separates the $347/month floor from the $1,215+ ceiling. Option C unlocks it. Option B does not.
