## Option A — Conservative

**When to choose**: Phase 2 launch shows forager cohort below 15%, OR total conversion below 0.5%, OR fewer than 10 first-day sales. (This would represent a material reversal from current gate-cleared status and warrants caution.)

### What launches June 22–July 13

Women's Health, Respiratory Health, and Sleep and Nervines only. These three bundles represent the highest practitioner search intent and the clearest seasonal alignment (cold/flu season and summer burnout peak). Immunity and Digestive defer to August–September.

### Timeline

| Date | Milestone |
|---|---|
| June 22 | Sprint begins. Write Women's Health (Days 1–3) |
| June 24 | Begin Respiratory Health writing |
| June 27 | Women's Health complete, PDF export |
| June 29 | Women's Health upload live on Etsy |
| July 3 | Respiratory complete, PDF export |
| July 6–7 | Respiratory upload live |
| July 9 | Sleep and Nervines complete |
| July 13 | Sleep upload live. Sprint ends. |
| August 3 | Begin Immunity writing |
| August 17 | Digestive upload live. Full 5-bundle catalog complete. |

### Resource requirements

- Writing hours: 37–43 adjusted hours (3 bundles) across 22-day sprint
- Daily intensity: 3–4 hours/day average, no days above 5 hours
- Design hours: 7.5 hours (3 covers + 3 zone cards; runs parallel to writing)
- Photography: pre-sprint track unchanged; indoor studio only

### Risk level

Very low. Writing capacity is comfortable. No contingency required unless pace drops below 1.5 hours/day. The only risk is the Immunity bundle missing the November cold/flu accumulation window — it uploads August 17 instead of July 20, losing 4 weeks of review accumulation before peak season.

### Revenue impact

Conservative month 3 target: $900–$1,100/month gross. Immunity and Digestive in September contribute incrementally. Full steady-state catalog ($2,000–$3,500/month) begins approximately November.

## Option C — Aggressive

**When to choose**: Phase 2 overperforms materially. 50+ first-day sales, OR 1.5%+ conversion rate on three or more products, OR forager cohort above 35%. This signals a demand level that justifies compressing the writing schedule and absorbing additional labor cost to match supply to demand faster.

### What changes from Standard

A second writer (trusted herbalist writer) handles Respiratory and Sleep bundles. This creates two parallel writing tracks, cutting total writing duration from 22 days to approximately 14 days while maintaining quality. Design and photography remain with Writer A (the user).

### Timeline

| Date | Milestone |
|---|---|
| June 1 | Brief sent to Writer B: content outline + sourcing guide + FTC quick reference |
| June 8 | Writer B confirms acceptance; questions resolved before sprint |
| June 22 | Sprint begins. Writer A: Women's Health + Immunity + Digestive. Writer B: Respiratory + Sleep |
| July 1 | All 5 writing tracks complete (14-day writing window for both writers) |
| July 5 | All 5 PDFs export-ready |
| July 7 | Women's Health upload live (stagger adjusted slightly to match 7-day spacing from July 7 first upload) |
| July 13 | Respiratory upload live |
| July 20 | Sleep upload live |
| July 27 | Immunity upload live |
| August 10 | Digestive upload live |

**Note**: The upload sequence shifts by approximately 8 days vs. Standard because early writing completion creates no advantage on Etsy's algorithmic window — each listing still needs 7-day minimum spacing. The benefit is float (all writing done before uploads begin, allowing review and revision passes) and reduced Week 1 daily intensity.

### Resource requirements

- Writer A hours: 40–44 adjusted hours (Women's Health + Immunity + Digestive)
- Writer B hours: 26–30 adjusted hours (Respiratory + Sleep)
- Writer B cost: $650–$1,050 at $25–35/hour
- Design hours: 12.5 hours (Writer A only; runs parallel)
- Brief deadline: June 1 — send content outline + sourcing guide to Writer B

### Risk level

Low. Writing buffer eliminates Week 1 intensity risk entirely. The only new risk is Writer B quality variance — their FTC framing must be reviewed against Appendix A of `phase-3-medicinal-herbs-critical-path.md` before PDF export. Budget 3–4 additional hours for a quality pass on Writer B's bundles.

### Revenue impact

Aggressive month 3 target: $2,800–$3,800/month gross. Revenue trajectory is identical to Standard because upload dates are staggered identically. The financial upside is a 14-day writing buffer that allows a higher-quality first-pass review, which improves Etsy review accumulation through fewer early buyer complaints.

## Option D — Focused Single-Cohort

**When to choose**: One cohort dominates Phase 2 purchases at 45%+ share. This creates a reordering opportunity — front-loading the 2–3 bundles most relevant to the dominant cohort maximizes early review accumulation in the category most likely to convert.

### What changes by dominant cohort

**If forager cohort above 45%**: Upload order becomes Digestive → Women's Health → Respiratory → Sleep → Immunity. Digestive's Dandelion cross-sell hook is the highest-value content for a forager-dominant audience. Upload June 22 (1-day expedited write for Women's Health cover), followed by Digestive June 29.

**If homesteader cohort above 45%**: Upload order becomes Women's Health → Digestive → Sleep → Respiratory → Immunity. Homesteaders are project-incremental — starting with the most accessible species (Calendula, Lavender, Dandelion in Women's Health and Digestive) matches their purchase progression.

**If prepper cohort above 45%**: Upload order becomes Immunity → Respiratory → Women's Health → Sleep → Digestive. Immunity's Goldenseal and Echinacea content and Respiratory's Elderberry align with the prepper audience's supplement-focused search terms.

### Standard cohort case (forager dominant, the most likely scenario given current 21.3% baseline)

| Date | Milestone |
|---|---|
| June 22 | Sprint begins. Women's Health writing (Days 1–3) |
| June 25 | Begin Digestive writing (cross-sell forager hook priority) |
| June 29 | Women's Health upload live |
| July 1 | Digestive complete |
| July 6–7 | Digestive upload live (in Respiratory's usual slot — the forager audience for Digestive benefits from the earlier upload window) |
| July 9 | Respiratory complete |
| July 13 | Respiratory upload live |
| July 16 | Sleep complete |
| July 20 | Sleep upload live |
| July 27 | Immunity upload live |

### Resource requirements

Identical to Option B (Standard). The only change is the writing order — not the writing volume. No second writer required. Design adapts cover sequence to match new upload order.

### Risk level

Low-Medium. Writing order shifts, which changes which bundles are in Week 1 (higher intensity). Digestive replaces Respiratory in Week 1, which is actually lower risk: Digestive has two shared-species sections (Calendula, Lemon Balm) from later bundles, reducing first-week writing load by approximately 4 hours. The Dandelion section (900 words) is the Week 1 writing peak.

### Revenue impact

Focused month 3 target: $2,200–$3,200/month gross. The forager cohort cross-sell flywheel activates earlier — buyers of the Digestive bundle (via Dandelion cross-sell from Wild Edibles Quick Reference) become the first medicinal herb cohort, generating early-cohort email list tags for follow-up. This slightly improves LTV trajectory for the highest-converting buyer segment.

## What Does Not Change Across Any Option

Regardless of which option you select, the following are constant:

**Hard deadlines that do not move**

- June 8: Goldenseal order placed OR Wikimedia CC photo path confirmed. Zero float. No recovery if missed.
- June 15: Elderberry order placed. Mountain Rose Herbs dried herbs order placed.
- June 21: Canva Phase 3 palette loaded (15 minutes). Pre-sprint photography complete.
- June 22: Sprint begins — all options start on this date.
- June 29: Women's Health upload — first Etsy algorithmic window opens. Any option that delays this loses the forager practitioner search window in early July.

**Pre-sprint actions (June 1–21) identical across all options**

The pre-sprint work is the same regardless of scope choice: supplier outreach May 20, Goldenseal decision by June 8, photography May 26–June 21, Canva palette loaded June 21. None of these actions require knowing the final option — they are unconditionally necessary.

**Upload spacing (7-day minimum) is a constraint, not a variable**

Each listing needs 7 days minimum spacing to maximize Etsy's algorithmic discovery window for "newest" listings. Compressing spacing to 3–4 days is not recommended regardless of how aggressively the writing sprint completes.

**Minimum viable launch across all options**

If writing pace falls below plan in any option: Women's Health + Sleep + Respiratory are the minimum viable launch set. These three bundles cover the highest practitioner search intent, the highest visual appeal (Passionflower thumbnail), and the cold/flu season demand ramp. Immunity and Digestive defer to August–September without material revenue impact at this stage.
