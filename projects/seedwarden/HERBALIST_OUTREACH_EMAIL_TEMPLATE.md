# Herbalist Outreach Email Template
## Zone Cards Launch — Community Leader Outreach

**Use this template for**: Direct outreach to community leaders who can amplify Zone Cards to their audiences. Not for cold lists — for individuals and moderators who manage active communities.

**Personalization hooks** are provided for each audience type. Insert the one relevant to your recipient. The rest of the template is ready to send as written.

## Word Count Note

The base template (without personalization hook) runs approximately 280 words. With a personalization hook inserted, it runs 340–380 words. The target range is 300–400 words — do not pad beyond this. Shorter is better when personalization is not needed.

---

## Sending Notes

- **Pre-launch framing (May 28–29)**: Use the first subject line group. The "preview before launch" framing gives recipients a reason to act now rather than wait.
- **Launch day (May 30)**: Switch to the second subject line group. The cards are now live — the message becomes an announcement rather than a preview.
- **AHG chapters**: Contact chapter coordinators, not individual members directly. Let the coordinator decide whether to circulate to their list.
- **Reddit moderators**: This template is for modmail. Adapt length downward for Reddit (Reddit modmail works best under 200 words — use the opening paragraph and the moderator personalization hook only).
- **Do not send the same email twice**: If a contact does not respond after May 30, one follow-up is appropriate. After that, move on.
