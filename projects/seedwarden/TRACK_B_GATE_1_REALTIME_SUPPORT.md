### Instagram Troubleshooting Flowchart

**"Username is not available" / username taken**
- Try backups in order: `seedwarden.co` → `seedwarden.seeds` → `seedwarden_guides` → `seedwarden_co` → `seedwarden_farm`
- Claim the first available one and write it down
- Use that exact username on TikTok and Pinterest too
- Do not spend more than 3 minutes on username selection — the backup variants are all acceptable

**"Email already in use"**
- You may have a dormant Instagram account on that email
- Click "Log in instead" and use "Forgot password?" to recover it
- If you cannot recover the existing account, use the Gmail plus-alias pattern: `wanka95+instagram@gmail.com` (Gmail delivers it to your inbox, Instagram sees it as a unique address)
- Note the alternate email in your password manager entry

**"Logo upload fails" or photo does not appear**
- Check file format: Instagram accepts JPG and PNG — `seedwarden_logo_1.png` is PNG and should work
- Check file size: Instagram has no stated hard limit but files over 8 MB occasionally timeout — the logo file is small and should not hit this
- If upload spins and fails: try a different browser, or switch from desktop to mobile (or vice versa)
- If still failing after one retry: skip the photo upload for now, proceed to TikTok and Pinterest, then return and batch-upload the logo to all three platforms at the end

**"Email verification link not arriving"**
- Check spam/junk folder first
- Wait 2 minutes — Gmail occasionally delays by 1–2 minutes
- Click "Resend email" if the option appears
- If not arriving after 5 minutes: check that `wanka95@gmail.com` is correctly entered (no typos)
- Last resort: use the SMS verification option if your phone number is available

**"Account creation blocked" or CAPTCHA loop**
- Instagram occasionally blocks rapid signups from a new IP
- Try a different browser (Chrome → Firefox, or vice versa)
- Clear cookies in the current browser and try again
- If still blocked: try from your phone on mobile data (different IP than your WiFi)
- If age verification is requested: enter a valid birthdate — do not use 2025 or 2026

- You may have a prior TikTok account on that email
- Tap "Sign in" and use "Forgot password?" to recover it
- If you cannot recover it: use `wanka95+tiktok@gmail.com` as the account email (Gmail delivers to same inbox)
- Note the alternate email in your password manager

**"Email verification not arriving"**
- Check spam/junk folder first — TikTok verification emails sometimes land in spam
- Wait 3 minutes — TikTok code delivery can be slower than Instagram
- Tap "Resend code" if available
- If email is still not arriving after 5 minutes: use your phone number instead (TikTok supports SMS verification as an alternative to email)
- If you prefer not to use your personal phone number: use a Google Voice number if you have one

**"Phone number required" during signup**
- TikTok sometimes requires phone verification for new accounts, especially in certain regions
- This is a one-time step for account security, not an ongoing requirement
- Enter your mobile number, receive the SMS code, enter it, and continue
- Your phone number is not displayed publicly on your profile

**"Logo upload fails" / photo won't save**
- TikTok accepts JPG and PNG up to approximately 50 MB — the logo file is well within limits
- If the upload spins: try from a stronger WiFi connection (mobile photo uploads are sensitive to connection quality)
- If still failing: save the logo to your phone's camera roll first (not just downloaded to Files), then retry the upload from the camera roll directly
- Alternative: skip the profile photo for now, complete bio setup, then retry logo upload before moving to Pinterest

**"Video upload prompt on first login"**
- TikTok sometimes prompts new users to upload a video immediately after account creation
- Tap "Skip" or close the prompt — you do not need to post video today
- Account setup does not require any content to be posted

**"Account creation too fast" / rate limit error**
- Wait 5 minutes, then try again — this is a bot-detection timeout that clears quickly
- Do not attempt the same email multiple times in rapid succession

**Content rule (important for all future TikTok use)**
Never use Instagram's built-in "Share to TikTok" or "Cross-post" feature. Always upload video files directly to TikTok as a native upload. Cross-posted content is suppressed by TikTok's algorithm and reaches significantly fewer viewers.

### Pinterest Troubleshooting Flowchart

**"Email already in use"**
- You may have a personal Pinterest account on that email
- Log into that account and convert it to Business (Settings > Convert to business) rather than creating a new account
- If you cannot access the existing account: use "Forgot password?" to recover it
- If you do not want to reuse a personal account: use `wanka95+pinterest@gmail.com` as the signup email and note it down

**"Username taken"**
- Try backups in order: `seedwarden.co` → `seedwarden.seeds` → `seedwarden_guides` → `seedwarden_co` → `seedwarden_farm`
- Pinterest usernames cannot contain dots — if `seedwarden.co` fails, use `seedwarden_co`
- Use whatever backup you claimed on Instagram and TikTok (match across all three platforms)

**"Logo upload fails" or size error**
- Pinterest profile photos accept JPG and PNG
- Pinterest documentation recommends a minimum of 165×165 px for profile photos — the logo file exceeds this minimum
- If upload fails with a size error: the logo PNG file is small and should not hit Pinterest's limits; try downloading the logo file again from the project directory and re-uploading
- If PNG fails: open the logo in any image viewer, export or save as JPG, and try the JPG version
- Pinterest logo sizing for pins and boards (not profile photos) has different requirements — those apply later when you create pins, not during account setup today

**"Email verification loop" / cannot proceed past email verification**
- Pinterest occasionally sends a second verification email after signup and blocks access until it is confirmed
- Check your inbox for an email from Pinterest with subject "Confirm your email"
- Click the link in that email and then return to pinterest.com — the account should now be accessible
- If the email is not arriving: check spam folder, wait 2 minutes, then use the "Resend" option on the verification screen

**"Your account looks suspicious" / bot detection**
- Pinterest flags new accounts that move through setup too quickly
- Click "Verify this is you" — this usually presents a photo recognition challenge (select all images with [object])
- Complete the challenge and continue
- If prompted for phone verification: entering your phone number resolves this permanently; it is not displayed on your profile

**"Category selection required" — which to choose**
- If Pinterest requires a category for your Business account: select "Gardening & Outdoor Living" or "DIY & Crafts" as the closest match
- "Online retailer" covers the business model accurately if that option appears
- Category affects Pinterest's internal recommendations, not your public profile — any reasonable selection is fine
