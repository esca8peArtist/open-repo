# Phase 2 Pre-Launch Email Templates
## May 9–29, 2026 — Four Pre-Launch Broadcasts

**Purpose**: These four emails warm the existing Phase 1 subscriber list during the 21 days before
Phase 2 launches. They are not part of the automated welcome sequence — they are one-time broadcast
sends to confirmed subscribers who are already on the list. Each one is paste-ready.

**Send to**: "All Confirmed Subscribers" (Kit segment) — includes Phase 1 buyers who were tagged
`purchased` and any organic sign-ups collected since the Kit landing page went live.

**What these emails are NOT**:
- Not the May 30 launch broadcast (that is in `phase-2-kit-broadcast-copy.md`)
- Not the automated welcome sequence (that is in `marketing/email-and-launch-plan.md`)
- Not the post-purchase Campaign 1 sequence (that is in `phase-2-campaign-copy-drafts.md`)

**Send decision**: These pre-launch emails are optional — if the list is small (<25 subscribers),
sending all four may feel over-communicative. In that case, send only Email B (May 20 countdown)
and Email D (May 29 reminder). Skip A and C.

## EMAIL A — Phase 2 Teaser Announcement
**Send date**: May 12–14, 2026 (any day works; avoid weekends)
**Audience**: All confirmed subscribers
**Subject**: Something new is coming May 30

---

**SUBJECT LINE:**
Something new is coming May 30

**PREVIEW TEXT:**
Seven new zones. Zone Quick-Start Cards. The full catalog expansion. Here's what's happening.

**BODY:**

Hi {{ subscriber.first_name }},

A quick note before the official launch.

On May 30, I'm releasing Phase 2 of the Seedwarden catalog. Here's what that means for you:

**Seven new zones.** The catalog now covers Zones 3 through 10 — the full continental US and southern Canada. If you've been in a zone that wasn't fully represented, that changes May 30.

**Zone Quick-Start Cards.** Every zone gets a free one-page field card with your frost dates, the plants worth prioritizing this month, and the two or three things worth doing right now in your region. Cards are free for subscribers — yours ships automatically when Phase 2 goes live.

**More guides.** Wild edibles, medicinal herbs, seed saving, food preservation, native plants — the catalog expanded significantly. Some of these were on the list since Phase 1. They're built now.

If you've been waiting for a specific zone or category to show up, check on May 30.

— Anya
Seedwarden

*P.S. As a Phase 1 subscriber, you'll get early access to the launch coupon when it goes out May 30. Nothing to do now — just watch for that email.*

**KIT SCHEDULING PATH:**
Kit > Broadcasts > New Broadcast > Subject: "What Phase 1 buyers are saying" >
Audience: All Confirmed Subscribers > Schedule: May 24 or 25, 9am local time

**EDITORIAL NOTE**: Authentic quotes outperform polished testimonials. Short and specific beats long
and effusive. If a buyer wrote "this is the only zone-specific foraging guide I've found" — use
that exact phrasing, do not clean it up. Reader trust comes from the rawness of real feedback.
