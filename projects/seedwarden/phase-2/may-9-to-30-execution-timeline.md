### May 17 (PDF Export + Google Drive Upload)

| Task | Time | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Export all 8 zone card PDFs from Canva (PDF Print, Flatten PDF checked) | 20 min | Naming: `zone-[N]-quick-start-card.pdf` |
| Upload all 8 PDFs to Google Drive | 10 min | One folder: "Seedwarden Zone Cards" |
| Set each file to "Anyone with the link can view" | 5 min | Required for automated delivery |
| Convert each share URL to download format: change `/view?usp=sharing` to `/uc?export=download&id=[FILE_ID]` | 10 min | This is the most fragile step — wrong format = silent failure |
| Test all 8 download URLs in incognito browser | 10 min | Non-negotiable; confirm each starts a PDF download |
| Record all 8 Kit download URLs in TRACK_B_CANVA_SETUP_AND_EXPORT_GUIDE.md zone card table | 10 min | Reference for Kit Email 1 build |

**CRITICAL**: The URL format `https://drive.google.com/uc?export=download&id=[ID]` is the only
format that delivers a PDF download to Kit subscribers. Google's default share URL opens a
viewer page — subscribers cannot download from it. Test in incognito; if the browser opens a
viewer instead of downloading, the URL format is wrong.
