# Week 1-2 Social Engagement Monitoring Dashboard

**PURPOSE**: Real-time tracking of LinkedIn, Instagram, and YouTube performance during peak acquisition period (June 29-July 13). Daily metrics enable rapid content pivots if engagement misses thresholds. All data is copy-paste into Google Sheets or CSV spreadsheet.

**HOW TO USE**: 
1. **Daily update**: Log metrics each day at same time (recommended 5:00 PM ET / 21:00 UTC for 24-hour post performance)
2. **Alert thresholds**: Platform-specific (LinkedIn 8%, Instagram 5%, YouTube 500+ views)
3. **Weekly aggregation**: Rows "Week 1 Total" and "Week 2 Total" auto-sum
4. **Decision point**: July 15 decision on Week 3 content strategy based on Week 1-2 performance

**ENGAGEMENT % CALCULATION**: (Likes + Comments + Saves + Shares) ÷ Reach × 100 = Engagement %

**IMPORTANT**: Track reach from each platform (LinkedIn shows impressions, Instagram shows reach, YouTube shows views). Engagement % varies by platform — compare Week 1 to Week 2 on SAME platform, not across platforms.

## ALERT THRESHOLDS & ESCALATION

**YELLOW ALERT TRIGGERED IF**:
- LinkedIn post <8% engagement: Review subject line, consider different format (thread, quote, case study)
- Instagram post <5% engagement: Review photo quality, caption clarity, hashtags, posting time
- YouTube post <500 views (Week 1) or <1,000 views (Week 2): Consider thumbnail design, title keywords, video length

**RED ALERT TRIGGERED IF**:
- LinkedIn post <5% engagement: Immediate review + repost with different hook within 48 hours
- Instagram post <2% engagement: Immediate review + consider repost or story follow-up
- YouTube video <200 views after 48h: Review title, description, tags, and consider deletion if technical issue

**ESCALATION ACTIONS**:
1. Identify root cause: Is it content quality (post type, message), timing (wrong hour?), or audience size (not enough reach)?
2. Adjust next post: If educational posts underperform, shift to testimonial. If morning posts flop, try afternoon. If reach is low, boost with paid promotion (if budget available).
3. Document learning: Add to PHASE_3_SOCIAL_MEDIA_PERFORMANCE_INSIGHTS.md for Phase 4 reference.
