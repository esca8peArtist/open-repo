**Status**: Ready — use when Phase 2 photography is complete or substantially underway
**Prepared**: April 27, 2026

## What Phase 3 Needs From Phase 2

Phase 3 social media (TikTok, Instagram, Pinterest) is asset-dependent. The Phase 3 Social Media Growth Strategy (phase-3-social-media-growth-strategy.md) is researched and execution-ready, but it requires a content bank before the first post goes live. That content bank is built primarily from Phase 2 photography output.

The connection is not incidental — Phase 2 lifestyle photos are shot once and serve three roles simultaneously: Etsy slots 4–5, TikTok/Instagram B-roll and stills, and Pinterest pin backgrounds. This triple use means the same 2–3 hours of shooting time funds weeks of social media content. Phase 3 cannot reasonably launch without this foundation, and it should not launch before 30 days of Phase 1 Etsy data establishes which products to feature.

This document defines how to move from a completed Phase 2 photography session to a launch-ready Phase 3 content bank, and how to maintain the content pipeline from there.

## How Phase 2 Photos Feed Into Phase 3 Platforms

### TikTok

TikTok video content for Seedwarden follows a 21–60 second format: hook, demonstration, result. Phase 2 lifestyle photos serve as B-roll stills — brief 1–2 second cuts between spoken segments that give the eye somewhere to rest and reinforce the product being discussed.

**Direct use of Phase 2 photos in TikTok videos:**

The simplest integration is a cut from talking-head footage to a still of the relevant lifestyle photo, held for 1.5–2 seconds, then back to speaking. This does not require video of the product — a photo cut-in is standard practice for product-focused educational content and is indistinguishable from video on a 60-second timeline.

Example: A video titled "Why your seeds keep dying" would:
1. Open with a 3-second hook: "This one mistake kills 80% of seed-starting attempts" (talking head).
2. Cut to the flat-lay lifestyle photo of the Apartment Seed Starting Kit on a wooden surface with a germination tray — held 2 seconds.
3. Return to explanation of the mistake.
4. Cut to the Seed Saving Field Manual lifestyle photo showing the guide open near seed envelopes — held 2 seconds.
5. Close with: "The full seed-starting sequence is in the guide — link in bio."

In this structure, two Phase 2 photos directly generate approximately 8 seconds of watchable content within a 45-second video. No additional video production is needed for those segments.

**Content calendar integration (from phase-3-content-calendar-template.md):**
Phase 2 photos should be tagged by product cluster and added to the content calendar's "asset bank" column. When building each week's content, cross-reference which products have lifestyle photos available and prioritize those for "product feature" and "educational" post types.

The 35% educational / 20% product feature / 15% values / 15% behind-the-scenes content mix from the content calendar template maps directly onto Phase 2 photo availability: educational content can use any relevant lifestyle photo as B-roll; product feature posts use the composited Etsy-ready image; behind-the-scenes posts use unedited raw photos from the shooting session (intentionally informal).

### Instagram

Phase 2 photos serve three distinct Instagram formats:

**Reels:** Same structure as TikTok — 15–45 seconds, lifestyle photos as cut-in stills. Re-export the TikTok video without the TikTok watermark (use CapCut or the original export file before posting to TikTok). Instagram Reels and TikTok can share 80–90% of the same content with minor format adjustments.

**Carousels:** This is where Phase 2 photos differentiate from TikTok content. A 6–8 slide Instagram carousel can use multiple Phase 2 photos from the same cluster to tell a coherent visual story. Example: "6 things to preserve before winter" would use 6 different Cluster C lifestyle photos (Harvest Preservation, Fermented Harvest, Grow Your Own Hot Sauce, plus props-only shots from the same session). Each slide is one food item with a text overlay naming it. The final slide is the product image (Etsy-ready composite) with a call to action.

This carousel format requires no additional photography if all Cluster C photos are complete — it assembles existing Phase 2 assets into a new format.

**Static posts:** Phase 2 lifestyle photos can be posted as static images with educational captions and minimal text overlay. These perform below Reels for reach but above average for saves and comments — the engagement type that builds community. Post static lifestyle images during off-peak times when you would not otherwise post a Reel.

**Stories:** Unedited, informal versions of Phase 2 photos — including "behind the scenes" shots of the photo session itself (props laid out, setup angles, outtakes) — are appropriate for Stories. Stories are where audience relationship is built; showing the process of creating the catalog is authentic content that stock photography cannot replicate. One shooting session generates approximately 10–15 Story-appropriate candid shots alongside the final portfolio.

### Pinterest

Phase 2 photos are the single most valuable asset for Pinterest. Pinterest is a visual search engine; the image is the primary element that determines whether a pin gets clicked, and a lifestyle photo performs significantly better than a product mockup alone.

**Pin structure using Phase 2 photos:**

Each Phase 2 lifestyle photo should generate a minimum of 3 Pinterest pins:

- **Pin variant 1 — Product focused:** Lifestyle photo as background, product title and price as text overlay ("Seed Saving Field Manual — $14"), direct link to Etsy listing. This is the standard commercial pin.
- **Pin variant 2 — Benefit focused:** Same photo, different overlay text that leads with a search-intent phrase rather than a product name ("Save Your Own Seeds: Complete Guide"). Targets a broader keyword while linking to the same listing.
- **Pin variant 3 — Educational hook:** Same photo, overlay text that teases a piece of content ("7 seeds worth saving before fall"). This pin links to the Etsy listing description, which contains the full educational content, or to a future blog post if one exists. This format trades immediate CTR for longer pin lifespan — educational pins accumulate repins for months.

For 21 products × 3 pin variants = 63 pins minimum. Creating all 63 in Canva takes approximately 3–4 hours in a single batch session (15-minute setup, 3–4 minutes per pin with template). Use Canva's "apply to all pages" function with a template: paste the photo background, change the text overlay, export. This is a repeatable batch workflow.

**Board structure for Phase 2-driven pins:**

Pin variant 1 and 2 go to product-specific boards:
- "Seed Saving Guides & Resources"
- "Food Preservation Printables"
- "Container Gardening Plans"
- "Urban Homesteading Downloads"
- "Survival Garden Planning"
- "Wild Foraging Guides"

Pin variant 3 (educational) also goes to broader topic boards:
- "Homesteading Tips & Skills"
- "Grow Your Own Food"
- "Food Independence & Sovereignty"

Posting cadence: 2–3 fresh pins per day via Tailwind (or manually), spread across boards. The 63 minimum pins, distributed at 2 per day, covers 31 days of Pinterest content from a single Phase 2 shooting session.

Same structure, rotating to Cluster C (food preservation) and Cluster D (survival/livestock) products. If any Phase 1 Etsy reviews exist, screenshot and use in Instagram carousel or TikTok. Continue rotating through all 21 products, each getting at least one TikTok mention and one Pinterest pin series within the first 30 days.

## The Single Most Important Handoff: Raw Photo Archive

Phase 2 produces two categories of photos: Etsy-ready composites (processed, cropped, branded) and raw lifestyle photos (unedited, uncropped, full-context images from the shooting session). Both serve Phase 3.

The raw photos matter for Phase 3 in ways that the Etsy-ready images do not:
- They contain natural context and background that the square-cropped Etsy images lose
- They can be cropped to vertical (9:16) for TikTok and Instagram, or horizontal (16:9) for YouTube thumbnails
- They include behind-the-scenes content (in-progress setup, outtakes, props arrangements) that feeds Stories
- They can be recomposed as different pin variants with new text overlays without returning to the original background

**Required action before Phase 3 launch:** Archive all raw Phase 2 photos to a dedicated folder separate from the Etsy-ready composites.

Recommended structure:
```
projects/seedwarden/marketing/lifestyle-photos/
├── stock/               [raw stock downloads, Week 1]
├── raw/                 [unedited physical photos, Week 2]
│   ├── cluster-a/
│   ├── cluster-b/
│   └── cluster-c/
└── etsy-ready/          [final 2400×2400px composites, all products]
```

The `raw/` folder is the Phase 3 content bank. Every time a social post needs a lifestyle visual, the first stop is `raw/` — not the Etsy-ready folder, which has already been cropped and optimized for Etsy's square format and may not compose well for 9:16 vertical or 2:3 Pinterest formats.
