# Phase 3 Contractor Onboarding Integration Checklist

**Purpose**: Map contractor hiring (Items 10–11 COMPLETE from exploration queue) to operational marketing execution. Ensures that as contractors are onboarded, they are immediately integrated into marketing workflows (email sequences, social credit, photo/content usage).

**Success criteria**: Every contractor knows:
1. When their work appears in marketing materials
2. How they're credited (portfolio links, byline, etc.)
3. How to provide feedback on marketing placement
4. When to expect testimonial/appreciation communications

---

## PHASE 1 — Contractor Hired (Offer Accepted, Contract Signed)

**Timeline**: June 24–28, 2026 (Item 11 completion window)

### Contractor Communication — Hand Them the Dashboard

**When**: Within 24 hours of contract signature

**Send to contractor**:
1. Email with PDF attachments:
   - PHASE_3_CONTRACTOR_SUCCESS_DASHBOARD.md (their row on the 6-week grid)
   - PHASE_3_LAUNCH_CALENDAR.md (when bundles launch, when their deliverables are needed)
   - PHASE_3_LAUNCH_MARKETING_CALENDAR.md (what marketing happens each week)

2. In the email body, personalize:
   - "Your photography will appear in the [BUNDLE NAMES] bundles launching [DATES]"
   - "You'll be credited as: [PHOTOGRAPHER NAME] — [PORTFOLIO LINK/INSTAGRAM]"
   - "This email explains the full sprint timeline and what to expect each week"
   - "Questions about timeline or integration? Reply here."

**Template email to contractor**:

---

Hi [NAME],

Welcome to the Phase 3 sprint. I'm sending you three documents that map out exactly how your work integrates into the bundles and marketing timeline:

**2. PHASE_3_LAUNCH_CALENDAR.md**

Timeline for you specifically:

- Session 2 due: [DATE] for [BUNDLE] (launches [DATE])
- Session 3 due: [DATE] for [BUNDLE] (launches [DATE])

**3. PHASE_3_LAUNCH_MARKETING_CALENDAR.md**

## PHASE 3 — Weekly Check-In (Weeks 1–4, Every Monday)

**Timeline**: July 1 – July 26, 2026

### Contractor Communication — Check-In Template with Marketing Notes

**Template addition** (to standard weekly check-in):

In the standard check-in email, add one line at the end:

> **Marketing angle for this week**: [BUNDLE] launches on [DATE]. If you see social posts using your work, you'll see credit as [YOUR PORTFOLIO LINK]. Let me know if you'd prefer different formatting for any credits.

### Social Media Crediting Protocol

**During weeks when contractor's work appears**:
- [ ] Before posting with contractor's content, send a preview DM/message showing how they're credited
- [ ] Example: "Your photo will appear in tomorrow's post as 'Photography by [Name] — [LINK to portfolio]'. Does that format work for you?"
- [ ] Contractor has 4 hours to request formatting change (rare, but allows for control)

**Post goes live**:
- [ ] Tag contractor account in comment if posting to Instagram/LinkedIn (if they want public credit)
- [ ] Share the post with contractor via DM/email (so they see how their work appeared)

### Email Sequence Preparation

**When contractor's work is part of an email**:
- [ ] Send draft of email to contractor 24 hours before send (if they want to see it)
- [ ] Template: "Hi [Name], your [photos/writing/expertise] is featured in an email launching tomorrow. Here's the draft: [attach PDF or link]. Let me know if you want any adjustments to how your work is presented."
- [ ] Contractor has 4 hours to request changes (rare — most are happy to see their work in marketing)

## PHASE 4 — Final Handoff (Weeks 5–6)

**Timeline**: July 27 – August 2, 2026

### Contractor Communication — Handoff Checklist + Marketing Recap

**When**: Before releasing final payment (Milestone 4)

**Send contractor**:

Use template PHASE_3_CONTRACTOR_COMMUNICATION_TEMPLATES.md (Template 4 — Final Handoff Checklist), and add one section:

> **Marketing Recap**
>
> Your work has appeared in:
> - [LIST bundles and launch dates]
> - [NUMBER] social media posts (LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube) with your credit
> - [NUMBER] email sequences to our [X] subscriber list
> - [OPTIONAL: Kit.com listing if applicable]
>
> View posts here: [LINK to social profile or marketing calendar]
>
> We'll be sending your post-launch appreciation email within 7 days of [final bundle] launch date. That email will include launch metrics and a request for testimonial (no pressure — but we'd love to hear about your experience).

### Testimonial Request Preparation

**For photographers**:
- Prepare 2–3 questions for testimonial email:
  - "What was the most interesting aspect of working on this project?" (learn about their experience)
  - "How do you think the photos captured the botanical detail and Seedwarden's brand?" (get them to reflect on quality)
  - "Would you be interested in Phase 4 (Sleep deepening, Rare Natives)?" (gauge Phase 4 interest)

**For writers**:
- Prepare 2–3 questions:
  - "What was the hardest herb to research?" (identify challenging species for Phase 4)
  - "How did the FTC compliance framework affect your writing process?" (evaluate feedback loop)
  - "Would you be willing to be first-contact for Phase 4?" (Phase 4 interest)

**For habitat specialists**:
- Prepare 2–3 questions:
  - "Which regional accuracy note took the most research?" (identify depth of work)
  - "Any species you found missing from the scope?" (identify Phase 4 gaps)
  - "Would Phase 4 interest you?" (Phase 4 interest)

## PHASE 5 — Post-Launch Appreciation Sequence (Week 7+)

**Timeline**: August 3–17, 2026

### Contractor Communication — Three-Email Appreciation Sequence

**Email 1 — Day 1 Post-Launch: Praise and Metrics**

**When to send**: Within 24 hours of their bundle going live

**Template**: PHASE_3_CONTRACTOR_COMMUNICATION_TEMPLATES.md (Template 5 — Payment + Testimonial)

Use this template and customize:

> **Launch Metrics**
>
> [BUNDLE NAME] went live on Etsy on [DATE] at [TIME]. Here's what's happening so far:
> - Listing views: [NUMBER] in first 24 hours
> - Pre-launch email reached [NUMBER] subscribers (opened by [%])
> - Social posts featuring your work reached [NUMBER] impressions
> - [OPTIONAL: First sale/download already happening]
>
> Your [photography/writing/expertise] made a measurable difference. Specific praise for one thing they did well:
> - Photographer: "The flat-lay on [surface] with [detail] has been the highest-engagement image across all posts."
> - Writer: "Your [section/tone/research depth] is exactly what differentiates this guide from everything else."
> - Specialist: "Your [region/species notes] gives growers confidence in what they're growing."

**Email 2 — Day 7 Post-Launch: Testimonial Request + Phase 4 Opener**

**When to send**: 7 days after their bundle goes live

**Template**: Use the customized version of Template 5, add testimonial questions:

> **A quick ask — testimonial**
>
> If you're willing, I'd love a brief note (2–4 sentences) about your experience working on this project. Specific questions that would help:
> 1. [Customized question for their track — see prep section above]
> 2. [Customized question #2]
> 3. [Customized question #3]
>
> These stay in my contractor roster notes, not public. No format required; even a few sentences by email works.
>
> **Future collaboration — Phase 4**
>
> Seedwarden Phase 4 (planning now, execution fall 2026) includes [brief description — Sleep deepening, Rare Natives series, etc.]. You would be my first-contact for [their track] if you're available and interested.
>
> I'll reach out before opening general outreach. No pressure — if your plate is full, I understand.

## PHOTO/CONTENT USAGE RIGHTS & ATTRIBUTION

### Photography Attribution Protocol

**During marketing (emails + social)**:
- Format: "Photography by [Photographer Name] — [LINK to portfolio / Instagram / website]"
- Example: "Photography by Sarah Chen — [instagram.com/sarahchenphotos](http://instagram.com/sarahchenphotos)"

**In bundle PDF** (if contractor's photos are used):
- Add to front matter: "Botanical photography by [Name]. View more at [LINK]."
- Example: "Botanical photography by Sarah Chen. View more at sarahchenphotos.com"

**In Etsy listing**:
- Add to product description: "Photography by [Name]"
- Example: "Professional botanical photography by Sarah Chen (@sarahchenphotos)"

### Writing/Content Attribution Protocol

**In bundle PDF**:
- Byline on title page: "Written by [Writer Name]"
- Habitat notes (if specialist contributed): "Habitat and regional accuracy reviewed by [Specialist Name]"

**In email/social**:
- Optional: "Written by [Writer Name]" in email footer or social caption
- Example: "Today's bundle was written by Rebecca Lexa, author of The Everyday Naturalist"

## Contingency — Contractor Dropouts or Delays

**If contractor misses delivery date**:
- [ ] Activate PHASE_3_CRITICAL_PATH_ANALYSIS_JUNE22_JULY13.md contingency (Wikimedia CC images for photos, self-write for copy)
- [ ] Notify other contractors: "[CONTRACTOR] is running behind. We're activating the [CONTINGENCY]. No impact to your timeline."
- [ ] Email audience: "[BUNDLE] will launch [+1 day] instead. Here's why [transparent reason]. Coming tomorrow."

**If contractor's work is used as contingency fallback**:
- [ ] Still credit them: "Fallback photography sourced from Wikimedia Commons" (no personal credit needed, but honesty required)
- [ ] Do NOT credit original contractor who dropped
- [ ] Document in WORKLOG.md
