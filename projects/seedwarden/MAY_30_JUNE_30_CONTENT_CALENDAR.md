# 30-Day Social Media Content Calendar
## May 30 – June 30, 2026

**How to use this calendar**:
- "IG" = Instagram (Reel unless marked Carousel or Story)
- "TT" = TikTok (native upload, not cross-post — separate file or same video file uploaded directly)
- "PIN" = Pinterest (static pin, 1000×1500px JPEG unless marked otherwise)
- Captions, hashtags, and scripts are in `phase-2-social-content-calendar-60day.md` for Weeks 1–4
- Post copy cross-referenced by Day number from that calendar

**Platform cadence targets**:
- Instagram: 4 posts/week (2 Reels + 1 Carousel + 1 Story highlight or static)
- TikTok: 3 posts/week (every IG Reel natively re-uploaded + 1 TikTok-specific)
- Pinterest: 7 pins/week (product pins and educational pins, batched and scheduled)

**Brand voice**: Warm, direct, slightly political but never preachy. Urban-centered, not
aspirational homestead. Every post either teaches something practical, shares a
values-aligned perspective, or shows the product in a real context.

## Platform-Specific Optimization Notes

### Instagram
- Post Reels to Reels tab (not Feed), or use Instagram's "Share to Feed" toggle to crosspost.
- Best posting times (2026 data from PHASE_2_SOCIAL_GROWTH_STRATEGY.md): 9–11am and 7–9pm
  in audience's local time zone. Set Buffer to 9am or 7pm.
- Stories: post 3–5 Stories any day you publish a Reel to extend engagement window.
- Hashtags: use 3–5 per post (Instagram recommendation as of December 2024). Use specific,
  niche hashtags — not #gardening (1B+ posts). Use: #heirloomseeds, #wildedibles,
  #foraging, #foodsovereignty, #urbanfarming (vary per post relevance).
- Carousel saves signal: if a Carousel gets saves >5% of reach, repeat the format.

### TikTok
- Upload natively — never use "cross-post from Instagram." TikTok suppresses cross-posted
  content. Save the Instagram Reel file (1080×1920px MP4) and upload it directly to TikTok.
- Captions: shorter than Instagram. 1–2 sentences + 3 hashtags max. TikTok shows first line
  only before "read more."
- Hashtags for TikTok: #foraging, #wildplants, #heirloomseeds, #foodie, #homesteading —
  TikTok hashtag relevance differs from Instagram; forager niche is stronger on TikTok.
- Best posting times: 7–9am and 7–9pm. Consistent daily posting is more important on TikTok
  than specific times.
- TikTok-specific angle: more raw, faster-cut, more direct-to-camera. Instagram Reels can
  be slightly more polished. TikTok rewards authenticity over production value.

### Pinterest
- Batch-schedule in one session: use Pinterest's native scheduler or Buffer. Schedule 2–3
  pins per day with minimum 2-hour gaps. Never publish more than 4 in a day.
- Pin descriptions: 150–300 words. Include: main keyword in first sentence, zone reference
  where relevant, direct CTA to "link in bio" or Etsy listing URL.
- Board structure: create separate boards for "Heirloom Seeds," "Wild Edibles + Foraging,"
  "Food Preservation," "Zone Growing Guides" — pins perform better when organized by topic.
- Educational pins outperform product pins for saves and reach on Pinterest. Product pins
  drive clicks. Mix is 3:1 educational to product in early months.
- Rich Pins (if domain claimed): enable on Etsy shop URL for automatic price/title pulls.
