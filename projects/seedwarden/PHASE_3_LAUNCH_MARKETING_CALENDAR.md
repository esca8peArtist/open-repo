## Risk Mitigation & Contingencies

### If email open rate is below 25% by week 1:
- [ ] Check deliverability in Kit (is email reaching inboxes?)
- [ ] Review subject line; consider A/B test on next bundle
- [ ] Segment audience; send promotional version separately from educational version
- [ ] Audit list health (any unsubscribes indicating poor fit?)

### If social engagement is below target:
- [ ] Check hashtag reach (are #herbalism, #medicinalherbs trending?)
- [ ] Review optimal posting time (9am ET may not be optimal for your audience)
- [ ] Increase video content (YouTube Shorts showing better ROI than static posts)
- [ ] Engage with comments immediately (algorithm rewards fast response)

### If contractor delays cause bundle upload delay:
- [ ] Activate contingency documented in PHASE_3_CRITICAL_PATH_ANALYSIS_JUNE22_JULY13.md (Wikimedia CC images + self-shoot fallback)
- [ ] Email audience: "Bundle [name] launching [+1 day]" — transparency > silence
- [ ] Post on Instagram: "Coming tomorrow instead of today" — audience engagement continues

### If Etsy link breaks or product doesn't show up:
- [ ] Check Etsy backend (is draft published, not stuck in draft?)
- [ ] Verify bundle title matches promotional materials
- [ ] If broken, send follow-up email: "Bundle link fixed" with corrected URL
