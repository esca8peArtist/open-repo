# Phase 3 Medicinal Herbs — Scope Decision Analysis

**Prepared**: May 22, 2026
**Decision deadline**: May 30, 2026 (by end of Phase 1 launch day)
**Execution window**: June 22 – August 3, 2026
**Gate status**: BOTH CLEARED — no re-check required before choosing an option

---

## Executive Summary

Option C (3-bundle solo: Women's Health, Respiratory, Sleep) is the correct default choice for a May 30 decision made without live Phase 1 sales data. It delivers $2,400–$2,900 in 90-day revenue, unlocks the practitioner tier July 15, requires zero out-of-pocket writing cost, and carries 4 sprint float days against a 36–44 hour writing commitment. The $745 90-day revenue gap versus Option A closes by September when Immunity and Digestive go live under any plan. Option B (outsourced) does not recover its $1,600–$2,000 writer cost within the calendar year under conservative sales assumptions and introduces a June 1 writer-confirmation deadline with no recovery path if missed. Choose Option C unless Phase 1 day-one data shows 50+ unit sales or 1.5%+ conversion on three or more products — in which case, Option A or B become defensible based on the trigger rules in Section 6.

## 4. Outsourcing Analysis — Option B

### Cost Estimate

Option B as described in `PHASE_3_OPTION_ANALYSIS.md` (and `PHASE_3_SECOND_WRITER_CANDIDATE_PROFILES.md`) assigns three bundles to Writer B: Respiratory, Immunity, and Digestive. Total scope for a qualified herbalist writer: 20–25 adjusted hours per bundle at market rates.

| Writer Cost Variable | Low Estimate | High Estimate |
|---|---|---|
| Generalist freelance writer (Upwork/Fiverr) | $29–$45/hr | 45–55 hrs total = $1,305–$2,475 |
| Subject-matter herbalist writer (niche rate 2–3× generalist) | $60–$90/hr | 45–55 hrs total = $2,700–$4,950 |
| Flat-rate negotiated scope (3-bundle sprint) | $1,400 | $2,000 |
| **Realistic range for a credentialed herbalist writer** | **$1,600** | **$2,000** |

A generalist writer at $29–$45/hr cannot produce the contraindication panels and FTC-compliant sourcing framing that the practitioner buyer expects. The writer must be a credentialed herbalist — which commands 2–3x generalist rates. Market rates from PayScale (2026 average $29.37/hr; experienced specialist $60–$100/hr) confirm this range. Expect $1,600–$2,000 as the realistic flat-rate floor for a qualified writer completing three bundles in a 22-day sprint.

### Timeline Impact of Outsourcing

The outsourcing path requires: writer confirmed by June 1, briefed with the content outline and FTC Quick Reference; draft review turnaround by July 1; quality pass (3–4 additional hours) before July 5 PDF export. The June 1 deadline has zero float — if no writer is confirmed by that date, the entire Option B path defaults to Option C. This creates a hard binary: either the writer is locked before June 1, or the option does not exist.

### The Only Case Where Outsourcing Pays

Option B generates a positive ROI only if the user's freed writing hours (the 26–30 hours saved vs. Option A) are invested in activities with a demonstrably higher return than the $1,600–$2,000 writer cost — specifically: practitioner outreach (AHG Symposium August 14–16 is the primary target), affiliate partner activation, or B2B direct sales to herbalist supply stores. If those saved hours are spent on lower-leverage tasks, outsourcing produces a $700–$1,900 net loss versus Options A or C.

## 6. Final Recommendation and Decision Rules

### Default Recommendation: Option C

Option C is the correct choice unless Phase 1 day-one data (May 30, 2026) establishes a materially stronger signal. It delivers:

- The three highest-demand, lowest-competition bundles first
- $2,400–$2,900 in 90-day revenue at 87% gross margin
- 4 sprint float days against a manageable 36–44 hour writing commitment
- Zero writer hiring risk, zero June 1 dependency
- Full practitioner tier ($120–$150/bundle) unlocked July 15
- Lowest sunk-cost exposure ($1,471 maximum)

### Decision Rules (Read on May 30 with Phase 1 data in hand)

**Choose Option C if any of the following are true on May 30:**
- Phase 1 conversion rate is below 1.5%
- Phase 1 day-one unit sales are below 30
- Writing availability for June 22 is less than 5 hours/day (confirmed)
- No writer has been identified or contacted (Option B is not available)
- Phase 1 data is unavailable or inconclusive at time of decision

**Choose Option A if ALL of the following are true on May 30:**
- Phase 1 conversion rate is 1.5%–3% on at least two products
- Phase 1 day-one unit sales are 30–50
- Writing availability is confirmed at 5+ hours/day June 22 – July 5
- No writer candidate has been identified (Option B is not viable)

**Choose Option B if ALL of the following are true on May 30:**
- Phase 1 conversion rate is above 3% OR day-one sales exceed 50 units
- A qualified herbalist writer has been identified and is confirmed available for a June 1 briefing
- The user's freed writing hours (26–30 hours) have a specific allocation to practitioner outreach, affiliate setup, or B2B store outreach — not general overhead
- Out-of-pocket budget of $1,600–$2,000 for writer is confirmed

**Regardless of option chosen:**
- June 8 Goldenseal path must be decided (live specimen order OR Wikimedia CC confirmed)
- June 8 AHG peer reviewer outreach must begin
- June 15 Elderberry order and Mountain Rose Herbs herbs order must be placed
- June 21 Canva palette must be loaded (auto-locks if not confirmed by June 15)

### Summary Decision Table

| Phase 1 Day-One Signal | Option | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Below 30 sales OR below 1.5% conversion | **C** | Demand signal is insufficient to justify 56+ hour sprint commitment |
| 30–50 sales OR 1.5–3% conversion, writer not available | **A** | Standard performance; solo sprint is viable; no hiring complexity |
| 30–50 sales OR 1.5–3% conversion, writer confirmed by June 1 | **A or B** | Writer must be confirmed AND freed hours must have a clear high-leverage allocation |
| 50+ sales OR 3%+ conversion AND writer confirmed | **B** | Demand overperformance justifies writer cost; freed hours go to outreach |
| One cohort above 45% of purchases (forager/homesteader/prepper dominant) | **C or A with reordered upload sequence** | See `phase-3-scope-decision-matrix.md` Option D reorder table |
