## Bottom Line Up Front

Ten combinations exist. Six are viable. The matrix below quantifies each. **C2 is the recommended default**: 3-bundle scope with Wikimedia CC Goldenseal path. It carries the lowest operational risk, zero schedule float consumed before sprint start, and the highest probability of hitting all June 22–July 13 milestones without reactive pivots. Vendor quote data received May 28–30 may shift the designer scope decision (Canva vs. outsource) but does not change the scope or Goldenseal path recommendation.

## Part 1: Combination Reference Key

The 10 combinations are formed by crossing three scope options against two Goldenseal paths, with an orthogonal Canva designer decision that applies to all combinations.

**Scope Options**:
- **A** — All 5 bundles, single writer (Women's Health + Respiratory + Sleep + Immunity + Digestive)
- **B** — All 5 bundles, second writer for Immunity + Digestive (hire confirmed by June 1)
- **C** — 3-bundle focus, single writer (Women's Health + Respiratory + Sleep; Immunity and Digestive post-sprint)

**Goldenseal Paths**:
- **1** — Live specimen order from Prairie Moon or Strictly Medicinal (hard deadline: June 8)
- **2** — Wikimedia Commons CC-BY-SA + NC Botanical Garden supplemental (no hard deadline)

**Canva Designer Scope** (applies orthogonally to all A/B/C combinations):
- **5-bundle designer scope** — quote and contract for all 10 deliverables (5 covers + 5 zone cards)
- **3-bundle designer scope** — quote and contract for 6 deliverables (3 covers + 3 zone cards)

This produces the primary 6 named combinations (A1, A2, B1, B2, C1, C2) plus 4 non-viable or dominated combinations noted below.

Each combination is assessed across seven dimensions:

7. **Risk profile** — Bear probability, primary failure mode
