|---|---|---|---|
| Women's Health | $22 | $19.66 | 5 species + conservation content |
| Immunity Support | $22 | $19.66 | CITES sidebar + 4 species |

| Digestive Support | $20 | $17.85 | 4 species standard |
| 5-Bundle Library (consumer) | $85 | ~$73 | 18% discount vs. individual |
| Practitioner 10-packs | $120–130 | ~$105–114 | License premium |

### Q4 Pricing Rationale

Q4 pricing carries a seasonal premium above Q3 for three documented reasons:

1. Gift market price tolerance is systematically higher than self-purchase. Buyers purchasing for others are willing to pay 15–25% more for equivalent content when framed as a curated gift rather than a personal purchase.
2. Seasonal urgency creates perceived time value. A "Winter Protocol" carries implicit urgency that an evergreen guide does not. Buyers who postponed a summer purchase act on seasonal relevance.
3. Q4 bundles contain incremental new content (seasonal framing, dosing calendars, protocol sections) that justifies a content-based premium beyond the Q3 base.

### Q4 Price Points

| Bundle | Q4 Price | Premium Over Q3 Comparable | Net/Sale |
|---|---|---|---|
| Q4-A Winter Immunity Defender | $30 | +$8 vs. Immunity ($22) | ~$26.30 |
| Q4-B Holiday Stress Relief | $28 | +$8 vs. Sleep ($20) | ~$24.60 |
| Q4-C Winter Digestive + Holiday Eating | $30 | +$10 vs. Digestive ($20) | ~$26.30 |
| Q4-D Gift-Giver's Collection (2-guide set) | $38 | +$6 vs. buying pair separately | ~$33.25 |
| Q4-E Winter Apothecary Practitioner | $35 consumer / $165 10-pack | Premium tier | ~$30.50 / ~$144 |

**Q4 All-Season Bundle (new upsell)**: A "Complete Year of Wellness" collection listing all 5 Q3 bundles + Q4-A + Q4-B = 7 guides. Priced at $120 (saves ~$58 vs. individual, 32% off). This is a high-consideration purchase that requires a dedicated Kit email touch to convert. Do not launch until at least Q4-A and Q4-B are live. Target launch: November 15.

**Gift packaging digital upcharge**: The Q4-D listing includes a printable gift card PDF and a "for the giver" intro letter. These add zero production cost and are not separately priced; they are included in the $38 Gift-Giver's Collection price. Do not offer gift wrapping as a separate paid add-on — the simplicity of "everything included in the download" is the gift product's USP.

## Part 3: Kit Email Automation Updates for Seasonal Sends

The existing Kit (Convertkit / kit.com) sequence was designed for the Q3 launch (4 sends, June 22 – July 13). Q4 requires a parallel seasonal sequence that does not disrupt Q3 buyer nurture flows.

### Q4 Email Architecture

**New segment tag**: Create a "Q4 Winter Wellness" tag in Kit for all subscribers who open the October 1 email. This allows Q4-specific follow-up without emailing the full list for every Q4 send.

**Do not alter Q3 sequences**: Q3 buyers are mid-nurture (Weeks 1–12 post-purchase). Insert Q4 sends as broadcast emails (one-time sends), not automated sequence additions. Automated sequences are reserved for onboarding.

**Timing rule**: No more than 2 email sends in any 7-day window across Q3 nurture + Q4 broadcast. If a Q3 automated send falls in the same week as a planned Q4 broadcast, delay the Q4 broadcast by 3–4 days.

| Nov 1 (gifts) | `[Q4-C_LINK]`, `[Q4-D_LINK]` | Oct 31 |
