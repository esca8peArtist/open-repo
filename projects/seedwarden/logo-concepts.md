# Seedwarden — Logo Concepts

Five distinct logo directions. Each includes a visual concept, color palette, typography direction, and an AI image generation prompt you can use with Midjourney, DALL-E, or Adobe Firefly.

---

## Concept 1: The Keeper

**Concept:** A pair of cupped hands holding a small collection of seeds. Simple, human, warm. Communicates stewardship without being heavy-handed. The most immediately legible option for a general audience.

**Style:** Woodcut / linocut illustration. Bold lines, slight texture, no gradients. Feels handmade and intentional.

**Color palette:**
- Primary: Deep forest green `#2D5016`
- Secondary: Warm cream / off-white `#F5EDD6`
- Accent: Burnt sienna `#A0522D`

**Typography:** Wordmark "SEEDWARDEN" in a sturdy serif (Playfair Display, or similar) — small caps, slightly tracked out. Sits below the mark.

**Best for:** Etsy shop icon, product covers, stamps. Timeless. Works in single color.

**AI prompt:**
> Woodcut style illustration of two open hands cradling a small pile of heirloom seeds, minimal detail, bold black lines on cream background, no text, square format, linocut aesthetic, warm earthy tones

---

## Concept 2: The Vault

**Concept:** A stylized seed inside a simple geometric shield or vault shape. Communicates protection and preservation directly — the "warden" aspect of the name. Clean, modern, ownable as a standalone mark.

**Style:** Flat geometric / minimal. No texture. Could work as an app icon or embossed seal.

**Color palette:**
- Primary: Slate blue-green `#3D6B61`
- Secondary: Off-white `#F8F4EC`
- Accent: Gold / ochre `#C9A84C`

**Typography:** "SEEDWARDEN" in a clean geometric sans (Futura, Montserrat, or similar) — all caps, tightly spaced. The modern contrast to a slightly traditional mark.

**Best for:** Brands that want to feel trustworthy and slightly institutional. Strong at small sizes (favicon, embroidered patch).

**AI prompt:**
> Minimal flat vector logo of a single heirloom seed silhouette centered inside a simple geometric shield shape, clean lines, no texture, flat design, slate green and gold color palette, no text, square format

---

## Concept 3: The Branching Seed

**Concept:** A seed that is simultaneously sprouting and branching into a small root system below and a stem above — the full cycle of life in a single contained mark. Circular or oval composition.

**Style:** Fine-line botanical illustration. Delicate, detailed, intentional. Feels like something from an old seed catalog or herbarium print.

**Color palette:**
- Primary: Deep ink green `#1A3A2A`
- Secondary: Parchment `#EDE0C4`
- Accent: Sage `#8FA882`

**Typography:** "SEEDWARDEN" in a thin, elegant serif (Cormorant Garamond or similar). Could wrap around the circular mark or sit beneath it.

**Best for:** Products that want to feel premium and craft-oriented. Especially strong on printed PDF covers and packaging.

**AI prompt:**
> Fine line botanical illustration of a single seed with delicate roots growing downward and a small sprout growing upward, symmetrical, contained within an oval border, parchment background, dark green ink, herbarium print style, no text, no color fill, just linework

---

## Concept 4: The Commons

**Concept:** Several different seed shapes arranged in a loose circle or community cluster — no single seed at the center, all equal. Communicates diversity, community, and the commons. Subtly political without a word.

**Style:** Hand-drawn / folk art. Slightly imperfect, warm, accessible. Like something you'd find on a community bulletin board or a zine cover.

**Color palette:**
- Primary: Terracotta `#C4603B`
- Secondary: Cream `#FAF0DC`
- Accent: Olive `#6B7B3A`

**Typography:** "SEEDWARDEN" in a friendly hand-lettered or humanist sans (Nunito, or an actual hand-lettered wordmark). Lowercase feels right here.

**Best for:** Appeals to the community organizer, the activist, the zine reader. Strong fit for the Anti-Catalog and Seed Swap products in particular.

**AI prompt:**
> Hand-drawn folk art style illustration of seven different heirloom seed shapes (tomato, bean, pepper, squash, sunflower, corn, pea) arranged in a loose circle, slightly imperfect lines, warm terracotta and olive color palette on cream background, no text, round composition, zine aesthetic

---

## Concept 5: The Sentinel

**Concept:** A single tall sunflower or wheat stalk — standing upright, bold, almost architectural. Simple silhouette. Conveys the "warden" idea through posture and presence rather than literal imagery.

**Style:** Bold graphic / poster art. High contrast. Feels like a WPA poster or a protest print — strong, confident, unapologetic.

**Color palette:**
- Primary: Near-black `#1C1C1C`
- Secondary: Golden yellow `#E8B84B`
- Accent: Off-white `#F5F0E8`

**Typography:** "SEEDWARDEN" in a bold condensed sans (Oswald, or similar) — all caps, stacked or horizontal. Punchy.

**Best for:** Strong brand presence on social media, tote bags, stickers. The most visually assertive option. Stands out in an Etsy marketplace full of soft botanical aesthetics.

**AI prompt:**
> Bold graphic poster art style illustration of a single tall sunflower silhouette, high contrast, flat black and golden yellow on off-white background, WPA poster aesthetic, strong clean lines, no text, portrait format, powerful and simple

---

## Recommendation

If forced to pick one to start: **Concept 1 (The Keeper)** or **Concept 3 (The Branching Seed)**.

- Concept 1 is the most universally readable and communicates the brand's human side immediately.
- Concept 3 is the most premium-feeling and will look best on PDF product covers.

Both can be created cheaply via Fiverr (search "woodcut logo" or "botanical illustration logo") or generated with Midjourney using the prompts above and then cleaned up in Canva or Illustrator.

## Additional Prompts (Geometric / Modern Direction)

These were developed based on a preference for simpler, more geometric and modern styling. Ready to paste directly into DALL-E 3, Imagen, or Adobe Firefly. For best results append: `"vector art style, flat design, no gradients, logo design"`.

---

### Prompt 1: Three Plant Silhouettes

> Minimal flat vector logo, three plant silhouettes in a row on a single horizontal baseline, stark white on deep forest green background. Left plant: a pine tree reduced to pure geometry — single vertical trunk, symmetrical horizontal lines decreasing in length toward the top. Center plant: a sunflower reduced to a perfect circle head on a single straight stem with two symmetric leaves. Right plant: a wheat stalk — single vertical stem with small oval grain clusters along the top third. All three silhouettes same height, equal spacing, no detail, no shading, no gradients. Clean modern botanical icon set. Square format, centered composition, logo-ready.

---

### Prompt 2: Seed with Geometric Background

> Flat vector logo, foreground/background composition. Background: fine parallel diagonal lines running from upper-right to lower-left at 45 degrees, medium spacing, color is a muted mid-tone — not white, not black, something warm. The diagonal lines occupy the full square but fade in opacity toward the center. Foreground: a single large heirloom tomato seed shape (oval, slightly pointed at both ends) centered and elevated, rendered in deep solid color with a clean 3px outline in contrasting tone. The seed is large enough that it visually interrupts and dominates the diagonal lines behind it. No text. Minimal, modern, graphic. Flat design, no drop shadow, no gradients, square format.

---

### Prompt 3: Geometric Cross-Section

> Minimal flat vector logo inspired by a tree ring cross-section. Three to four concentric rings centered in the frame, clean even spacing, rendered as outlines only — no fill. At the exact center: a small seed shape (teardrop or oval) in solid color as a filled silhouette. Color palette: warm cream background, dark olive green rings, terracotta seed. The rings suggest growth, time, and rootedness without being literal. No text, no gradients, no texture. Logo-ready, square format, modern and geometric.

---

### Prompt 4: Letterform Integration

> Minimal flat vector wordmark logo. The word SEEDWARDEN in clean geometric sans-serif, all caps, wide letter-spacing. The two E's in SEED are modified: their three horizontal bars are replaced by three thin horizontal lines that extend slightly past the letterform and terminate in small upward-pointing sprout tips, as if the letters are growing. Every other letter is standard. Color: near-black lettering on off-white, with the sprout tips in a single accent color (sage green or terracotta). No icons, no frames. The logo is the typography. Modern, restrained, clever without being cute. Horizontal format.

---

### Prompt 5: Seed Grid

> Minimal flat vector logo. A perfect 3x3 grid of nine identical seed shapes (small ovals, slightly pointed ends), evenly spaced, arranged like a planting diagram viewed from above. Eight of the nine seeds are the same muted color. One seed — positioned slightly off-center, not in the middle square — is a bright contrasting accent color, as if it has just germinated or been selected. No outlines, no borders, no text. Deep navy background, cream seeds, single terracotta accent seed. Square format, centered composition, modern and geometric.

## Getting a Final Logo

**Option A — AI generation (free/fast):**
Use the prompt for your chosen concept in Midjourney, DALL-E 3, or Adobe Firefly. Generate several variations, pick the best, clean up in Canva.

**Option B — Fiverr (cheap, higher quality):**
Search "heirloom seed logo" or "botanical woodcut logo" on Fiverr. Budget $30-80 for a solid result. Share this brief with the designer.

**Option C — Canva (DIY):**
Canva has botanical and seed illustration elements built in. Can assemble a clean logo in 30 minutes using their free library.
