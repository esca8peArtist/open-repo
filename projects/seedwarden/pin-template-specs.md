# Pinterest / Instagram Pin Template Specifications

**What this document is**: Complete design specifications for 5 reusable pin templates. Each template is specified with exact dimensions, colors, typography, layout zones, and content guidance. A designer or the Canva Brand Kit can implement these from this document alone — no further creative briefing required.

**What this document is not**: A set of mockups. These are specifications, not finished designs. Implement in Canva using the Brand Kit configured in CANVA_EXECUTION_PLAYBOOK.md Section 1.2.

**Template summary**:
1. Product Mockup Pin — commercial intent, drives direct Etsy click
2. Educational Hook Pin — discovery intent, drives saves and profile follows
3. Lifestyle Flat-Lay Pin — emotional connection, drives both saves and clicks
4. Values / Perspective Pin — brand differentiation, drives follows
5. Carousel Pin Cover — for multi-image carousel pins on both Pinterest and Instagram

All 5 templates are designed for dual use: Pinterest (2:3 ratio, 1000×1500px) and Instagram feed (4:5 ratio, 1080×1350px). The core design works for both; only the crop changes.

## Template 1 — Product Mockup Pin

**Purpose**: Drives direct Etsy purchase intent. This is the "I want to buy this" pin. Should appear clearly as a product listing, not as generic content.

**When to use**: For each of the 21 products — one primary product pin per product. Creates the 21-pin base layer of the Pinterest catalog.

**Dimensions**: 1000×1500px (Pinterest); crop to 1080×1350px for Instagram

---

### Layout Specification (vertical zones, top to bottom)

**Zone 1 — Header band** (0–200px from top, full width)
- Background: Deep Forest Green (#143b28)
- Content: "SEEDWARDEN" in Cormorant Garamond Italic, 28px, Warm Cream (#F5EDD6), centered
- Purpose: brand identification at first glance, even before reading the product name

**Zone 2 — Product image** (200–1050px, full bleed)
- Content: The product's Etsy slot 4 lifestyle photo (full-bleed to the left and right edges, no padding)
- Overlay: A semi-transparent Warm Cream (#F5EDD6 at 15% opacity) vignette around the bottom 200px of this zone — creates the fade into Zone 3 without a hard line
- No text in this zone. The lifestyle photo speaks.

**Zone 3 — Product name** (1050–1200px)
- Background: Warm Cream (#F5EDD6) or Parchment (#EDE0C4)
- Content: Product name in Playfair Display Bold, 36px, Deep Forest Green (#143b28), centered
- If product name is two lines, reduce to 30px — never let the name overflow this zone

**Zone 4 — Description + CTA** (1200–1380px)
- Background: Warm Cream or Parchment (matching Zone 3)
- Content line 1: One-sentence product description, Lato Regular, 22px, Deep Ink Green (#1A3A2A), centered
- Content line 2: "PDF Download — Instant Access" in Cormorant Garamond Italic, 18px, Sage (#8FA882)
- Content line 3 (optional): Price in Lato Bold, 24px, Burnt Sienna (#A0522D) — e.g., "$14" — only include if the product price is a conversion signal ($20+ or a clear value, like $5 impulse pricing)

**Zone 5 — Footer band** (1380–1500px)
- Background: Deep Forest Green (#143b28) or Deep Ink Green (#1A3A2A)
- Content: "seedwarden.etsy.com" in Lato Regular, 18px, Warm Cream (#F5EDD6), centered — this doubles as the Etsy store CTA
- Below the URL (optional): a thin Sage (#8FA882) horizontal rule, then "Free food sovereignty guides at link in bio" in Cormorant Garamond, 14px, Sage — this seeds the lead magnet concept without requiring a separate pin

---

### Copy Guidelines for Product Mockup Pins

**Product name**: Use the exact Etsy listing title, shortened if over 50 characters. Example: "Seed Saving Field Manual" (not "Seed Saving Field Manual — Open-Pollinated & Heirloom Guide PDF").

**Description line**: The single most compelling thing a buyer would want to know. Focus on outcome, not content. Examples:
- "Every seed saving method from harvest to storage in one reference." (Seed Saving Field Manual)
- "5 container garden layouts for apartments and balconies." (Container Growing Blueprint Pack)
- "Identify, harvest, and preserve 18 wild edibles." (Wild Edibles Quick Reference — Phase 3)
- "Plan your region's best survival garden — zone-by-zone layout and timing." (Survival Garden Regional Plans)

**What not to include**: Do not include bullet points, sub-headings, or long descriptions in the image. Long copy on a pin reduces the visual impact of the lifestyle photo and creates a cluttered look. Pinterest saves are driven by the image — the description field in the pin itself (not the image) handles long text.

---

### Product Pin — Pinterest Description Text

This text goes in the Pinterest pin description field (not the image). Canva does not control this — write it separately in the Pinterest upload screen or scheduling tool.

**Format**: 150–300 characters (Pinterest displays the first 150 before truncation in the feed).

**Template**:
[One-sentence benefit statement]. [Content specifics — 2–3 key features]. PDF instant download — link to [product name] at seedwarden.etsy.com. #[topic] #[topic] #[topic] #seedwarden

**Example — Seed Saving Field Manual**:
Save seeds from any food crop with the right technique for each plant type. Covers tomatoes, peppers, beans, squash, and 12 more vegetables. PDF instant download at seedwarden.etsy.com. #seedsaving #heirloomseeds #growyourown #foodsovereignty #gardening

## Template 2 — Educational Hook Pin

**Purpose**: Discovery intent — drives saves, follows, and eventual product exploration. This is the highest-volume template — build more of these than product pins because they compound over time. Educational pins are pinned by other users, which creates distribution without any additional work.

**When to use**: For any content that teaches something useful without requiring a product purchase. Topics drawn from the existing guides, but the pin itself is free educational value. Aim for 2–3 educational pins per product cluster.

**Dimensions**: 1000×1500px (Pinterest); crop to 1080×1350px for Instagram

---

### Layout Specification

**Zone 1 — Full-bleed background** (0–1500px)
- Option A: Lifestyle photo from the relevant cluster (full bleed, slightly darkened — overlay a Deep Ink Green (#1A3A2A) layer at 45–55% opacity over the photo to make text readable)
- Option B: Flat Parchment (#EDE0C4) or Warm Cream (#F5EDD6) background with a botanical illustration or simple graphic element
- Option A creates more visual energy; Option B creates cleaner, more Pinterest-native look. Use Option A for imagery-heavy topics (foraging, preservation), Option B for list/tip content.

**Zone 2 — Hook text block** (centered vertically in the pin, or positioned upper-third for Option A)
- Enclosing shape: A rounded rectangle in Warm Cream (#F5EDD6) at 90–95% opacity, padded 24px on all sides. This creates a legible text panel over any background.
- Header text: The hook question or number in Playfair Display Bold, 44–52px, Deep Forest Green (#143b28)
- Keep to 1–2 lines maximum. A hook that runs 3+ lines is too long for pin format.
- Supporting text (optional): 2–4 lines of Lato Regular, 20–22px, Deep Ink Green (#1A3A2A) — list items or brief explanation

**Zone 3 — Category label** (bottom 200px)
- Thin Sage (#8FA882) rule across full width
- Below rule: "SEEDWARDEN — [CATEGORY]" — e.g., "SEEDWARDEN — SEED SAVING" — in Cormorant Garamond Italic, 16px, Sage
- This labels the pin's category for the user and brands it simultaneously

---

### Hook Text Templates by Topic

**Seed saving and heirloom**:
- "Which seeds can you actually save from your garden?"

**Values and food sovereignty**:

## Template 3 — Lifestyle Flat-Lay Pin

**Purpose**: Emotional connection — shows the product in a real-world context, answers "is this for someone like me?" Bridges the gap between educational content and direct product purchase intent.

**When to use**: When the slot 4 lifestyle photo from the Etsy shoot is strong enough to stand alone as the full-bleed image. This is the simplest template — the photo does most of the work.

**Dimensions**: 1000×1500px (Pinterest); crop to 1080×1350px for Instagram

---

### Layout Specification

**Background**: The lifestyle flat-lay photo (Etsy slot 4 image), cropped to 1000×1500px from the original 2400×2400px square. The most visually strong quadrant of the flat-lay usually fits the 2:3 vertical format well — typically the center or upper-center of the overhead shot.

**Overlay elements** (minimal — the photo should dominate):

Top band — product name:
- A Warm Cream (#F5EDD6) bar, full width, 70px tall, at 90% opacity, positioned 60px from the top
- Product name in Playfair Display Bold, 26–30px, Deep Forest Green (#143b28), centered in the bar
- This is the only text in the upper portion of the pin

Bottom panel — CTA:
- A Deep Forest Green (#143b28) bar, full width, 120px tall, at 95% opacity, positioned at the very bottom
- Line 1: The core benefit in Lato Regular, 20px, Warm Cream (#F5EDD6), centered — 1 line maximum
- Line 2: "Instant PDF download — seedwarden.etsy.com" in Cormorant Garamond Italic, 16px, Sage (#8FA882), centered

**What not to add**: No bullet points, no additional text, no watermarks beyond what is specified above. The lifestyle photo's power is in its simplicity — adding more text elements reduces the visual impact.

---

### Flat-Lay Pin Cropping Guide

The lifestyle photos were shot at 2400×2400px (1:1). To produce a 1000×1500px pin from the same image:

1. Open the 2400×2400px image in Canva
2. Set canvas to 1000×1500px
3. Place the image — it will overhang on left and right
4. Pan the image left-right to find the strongest 2:3 crop from the flat-lay
5. In most flat-lay overhead shots: the product is in center, so the 2:3 crop from the center of the 1:1 frame works. If the product is off-center, pan toward the product.
6. Apply Zone 1 product name bar and Zone 5 CTA bar as overlays
7. Export at 1000×1500px, JPEG quality 85%

**For Instagram 4:5**: Set canvas to 1080×1350px. Use the same center crop logic.

## Template 4 — Values / Perspective Pin

**Purpose**: Brand differentiation. Seedwarden's political identity (food sovereignty, anti-corporate agriculture, practical self-reliance) is a conversion driver for the specific audience that finds those values compelling. This template surfaces that voice in pin format. These pins drive follows more than clicks — they build an audience that will buy repeatedly, not just once.

**When to use**: For content in the "values/political" content pillar (target 15% of content mix). Use sparingly — 1–2 per 10 product pins. These are not promotional; they are brand character.

**Dimensions**: 1000×1500px (Pinterest); crop to 1080×1350px for Instagram

---

### Layout Specification

**Background**: Flat Parchment (#EDE0C4) or Deep Forest Green (#143b28) — no photo background for this template. The text is the content.

**Upper third** (0–500px):
- A botanical illustration or minimal line drawing — a seed, a plant form, a mason jar — in Sage (#8FA882), centered. This is the visual anchor. Use public domain botanical illustrations from Wikimedia Commons (search "[plant name] botanical illustration" — many are CC0 or public domain).
- The illustration should be simple and on-brand, not complex. A clean line drawing of dandelion, a single seed, or a mason jar is sufficient.

**Middle third** (500–1000px):
- The statement in Playfair Display Bold, 42–50px, leading color (Deep Forest Green on Parchment background; Warm Cream on Green background), centered, with tight line spacing
- Maximum 2–3 lines. This is a headline, not a paragraph.
- Below the statement: a thin Sage rule, then a 1-sentence elaboration in Lato Regular, 20px — 1 line only

**Lower third** (1000–1500px):
- The source or context in Cormorant Garamond Italic, 18px, centered — "From the Seedwarden shop" or a specific product name if the statement is derived from guide content
- Seedwarden branding: "SEEDWARDEN" in Lato Bold, 14px, all caps, centered — bottom 60px, in the lighter color (Warm Cream or Sage depending on background)

## Template 5 — Carousel Pin Cover

**Purpose**: The cover slide for a multi-image carousel pin on Pinterest (up to 5 images) or an Instagram carousel. The cover must communicate the full value of the carousel at a glance — if the cover is weak, nobody swipes.

**When to use**: For educational carousel content (the "5 types of home preservation" or "which seeds can you save" format). Should be built whenever a new educational carousel is produced — the cover slide is a dedicated design, not just the first photo.

**Dimensions**: 1000×1500px (Pinterest carousel); 1080×1350px (Instagram carousel — note: Instagram carousels can be 1:1 or 4:5, but 4:5 gives more feed presence)

---

### Layout Specification

**Background**: Option A (Parchment/Cream with graphic elements) or Option B (lifestyle photo with overlay). Both work; choose based on whether the carousel topic is visual (Option B) or conceptual (Option A).

**The number or hook** (dominant element, upper 60% of the pin):
- The number or key question in Playfair Display Bold, 120–140px, Deep Forest Green (#143b28)
- Below the number/hook: the topic in Playfair Display Regular, 36px, Deep Ink Green (#1A3A2A) — 1–2 lines
- Example: "3" (large) / "Types of Home Food Preservation" (below, smaller)
- Example: "5" (large) / "Vegetables to Grow in Any Container" (below, smaller)

**Subtext / value proposition** (lower 30% of the pin, above the footer):
- "Swipe to learn" in Lato Regular, 20px, Sage (#8FA882) with a right-pointing chevron glyph ("→") — this tells Pinterest/Instagram users that there is more content to engage with
- 1 supporting line: what they'll know after the carousel, in Lato Regular, 18px, Deep Ink Green

**Footer band** (bottom 80px):
- Deep Forest Green (#143b28) background
- "SEEDWARDEN" in Lato Bold, 16px, Warm Cream (#F5EDD6) — centered
- If the carousel links to a specific product, add the product name in Cormorant Garamond Italic, 14px, Sage

---

### Carousel Inner Slide Template (slides 2–5)

The inner slides of a carousel should be consistent with the cover but lighter — they are content, not hooks.

**Inner slide layout**:
- Background: Parchment (#EDE0C4) or Warm Cream (#F5EDD6) — flat, no photo (keeps reading easy)
- Slide number (top left): the slide number (2, 3, 4, 5) in Cormorant Garamond Italic, 20px, Sage (#8FA882) — small, not prominent
- Main content: the slide topic in Playfair Display Bold, 32–36px, Deep Forest Green, centered — 1–2 lines
- Supporting content: 2–4 lines of Lato Regular, 20px, Deep Ink Green (#1A3A2A), left-aligned with 40px left margin
- Optional: a small botanical icon or line element in Sage as a divider between the heading and the supporting text
- Footer (every slide): thin Sage rule + "SEEDWARDEN" in Lato 12px, Sage — bottom 40px

## Canva Implementation Workflow

### One-time setup (do once):

1. Configure Brand Kit with all 6 hex colors and 3 font pairings (see CANVA_EXECUTION_PLAYBOOK.md Section 1.2)
2. Build each of the 5 template designs at 1000×1500px, name them clearly ("Product Pin Template," "Educational Hook Pin Template," etc.)
3. Save each as a Canva template (click "Share" > "Template" in Canva, or simply name the design file and duplicate it each time — Canva Free does not have a formal template library, but duplicating from a master design achieves the same result)

### Per-pin production workflow:

For a product pin (Template 1):
1. Duplicate the Product Pin Template design
2. Replace the Zone 2 lifestyle photo with the new product's slot 4 image
3. Update Zone 3 product name
4. Update Zone 4 description line
5. Optionally update Zone 4 price if applicable
6. Export 1000×1500px JPEG for Pinterest
7. Change canvas to 1080×1350px, adjust crop, export for Instagram

Estimated time per pin once templates are configured: 4–7 minutes.

### Batch production target:

With 5 templates and 21 products, the full Phase 2 pin library is:
- 21 product pins (Template 1) — one per product
- 8–10 educational pins (Template 2) — 1–2 per cluster
- 10 lifestyle flat-lay pins (Template 3) — for top 10 products by revenue priority
- 4–5 values pins (Template 4) — evergreen, rotate monthly
- 4–6 carousel pin covers (Template 5) — one per major educational carousel

**Total pins to produce**: approximately 50–60 for a complete Phase 2 Pinterest library.
**Estimated production time** with configured templates: 5–7 hours for the full batch.

This batch build is referenced in phase-2-social-content-calendar-60day.md Days 55–60 (content bank build). Produce all 50–60 pins in one batch session, then schedule them over 8–10 weeks using Pinterest's native scheduler or a scheduling tool.
