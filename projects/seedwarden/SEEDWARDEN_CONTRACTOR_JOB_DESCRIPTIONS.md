- Format: JPEG, high quality (under 20 MB per file)
- White balance: consistent within each bundle set; warm 5000–5500K preferred
- File naming: `[bundle-slug]-[type]-[nn].jpg` (e.g., `respiratory-flatlay-01.jpg`, `sleep-lifestyle-02.jpg`)
