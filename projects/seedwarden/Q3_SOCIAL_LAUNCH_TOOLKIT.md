**File specifications**:
- Format: Instagram (1080×1350px for feed posts, 1080×1080px for carousel)
- Export: High-res JPG (RGB, 72dpi for web)
- Font suggestions: Montserrat (headers), Open Sans (body) — warm, readable herbalism brand feel
- Photography style: High-quality botanical close-ups + lifestyle composition
- Mood: Educational + luxury + approachable (not clinical, not trendy)

- Bundle guides (supply PDFs of Women's Health draft, Respiratory outline, etc.)

**Approval process**:
1. Designer creates 5 draft graphics
2. Review + provide feedback (focus on herb photography quality, text readability, CTA prominence)
