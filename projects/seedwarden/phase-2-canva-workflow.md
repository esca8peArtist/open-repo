# Phase 2 Canva Workflow

**Purpose**: A single document covering every Canva build in Phase 2. Read this with Canva open. No decisions to make — only specifications to follow. Organized in the order you build things.

**Total Canva time estimate**: 19-24 hours across the Phase 2 window
- Brand Kit setup: 30 min (once, done first)
- Zone 5 master card: 2.5-3 hrs
- Zones 6, 3, 4, 7, 8, 9, 10 (7 cards): 5-6 hrs (35-45 min each)
- Lifestyle compositing (21 products × 2 = 42 images): 4-5 hrs
- Pinterest pins (15 pins): 3-4 hrs
- Instagram carousel (Day 3, 5 slides): 60-90 min
- Social graphics (launch week): 2-3 hrs

## Part 1: Brand Kit Setup (One-Time, 30 Minutes — Do First)

## Part 2: Zone Card Builds (8 Cards — Highest Priority)

### Build Order (do not change this order)

| Order | Zone | Band Color (hex) | Duplicate From | Time Estimate |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 — master | Zone 5 | #2D5016 Temperate | New US Letter canvas | 2.5-3 hrs |
| 2 | Zone 6 | #2D5016 Temperate | Zone 5 master | 35 min |
| 3 | Zone 3 | #3D6B8A Cool | Zone 5 master | 45 min |
| 4 | Zone 4 | #3D6B8A Cool | Zone 3 | 40 min |
| 5 | Zone 7 | #C9943A Warm | Zone 5 master | 45 min |
| 6 | Zone 8 | #C9943A Warm | Zone 7 | 40 min |
| 7 | Zone 9 | #A0522D Hot | Zone 5 master | 45 min |
| 8 | Zone 10 | #A0522D Hot | Zone 9 | 40 min |

**Why this order**: Each zone-band color group is built sequentially (duplicate within the same color group). If you duplicate Zone 5 (green band) and forget to change the band to blue before populating Zone 3 content, you ship a Zone 3 card with the wrong visual indicator. This order makes that error impossible.

**Rule**: When starting any zone after Zone 5, immediately change the zone band color as the first edit — before any content changes.

### Zone 5 Master Card — Session 1A + 1B

**Canvas setup**:
- New design > Custom dimensions: 1200px wide × 900px tall
- File name (Canva project): `SW-Zone5-Master`
- Project folder: Seedwarden Phase 2

**Session 1A — Layout Only (90 min, no zone content yet)**

Build the structural layout on a blank canvas with placeholder text. Do not add zone-specific content until Session 1B.

Layout structure (top to bottom):

1. **Header zone band** (top): Rectangle element, full canvas width (1200px), 80px tall, positioned at the very top. Fill color: #2D5016 (Temperate). Add text inside: "Zone 5 Quick-Start Card" — Playfair Display, 28px, Warm Cream (#F5EDD6), horizontally centered.

2. **Seedwarden logo**: Upload logo PNG from Brand Kit. Position in header, right-aligned, 50px tall, vertically centered in the band. If the logo is white/light, it will be visible against the green band. If it is dark, use a white version or a text element "Seedwarden" instead.

3. **Main body area**: Three-column layout, separated by 12px gutters.
   - **Column 1 (left) — Plant Calendar**: Title "Plant This Month" in Playfair Display 16px, Deep Forest Green. List 4-6 crop entries in Lato 13px. Each entry: crop name (bold) + one-line action ("Start indoors," "Direct sow," "Transplant"). Left column width: 360px.
   - **Column 2 (center) — Spotlight Plants**: Title "Zone 5 Variety Spotlight" in Playfair Display 16px, Deep Forest Green. 2-3 variety entries with: variety name (Cormorant Garamond 15px, Deep Ink Green, bold), one-sentence description (Lato 12px, standard weight). Center column width: 420px.
   - **Column 3 (right) — Zone Tips + CTA**: Title "Zone 5 Growing Notes" in Playfair Display 16px, Deep Forest Green. 2-3 bullet points of zone-specific tips in Lato 12px. Below tips: CTA box — Deep Forest Green rectangle, 200px wide × 50px tall, text "Get more zone guides" in Lato 14px Warm Cream. CTA box bottom-aligned within the column. Right column width: 360px.

4. **Variety Spotlight Band** (optional, between body and footer): Thin horizontal rule or 30px-tall band, Parchment (#EDE0C4) background, no text in this first build — just a visual separator.

5. **Footer bar**: Rectangle element, full canvas width, 50px tall, positioned at bottom. Deep Forest Green (#143b28) background. Left-aligned text (Lato 11px, Warm Cream): "[ETSY-URL]". Right-aligned text (Lato 11px, Warm Cream): "[KIT-URL]". Use the exact bracket placeholder text — do NOT add live URLs until May 21 QA sweep.

Run a visual check: at 50% zoom, confirm the three columns are balanced and readable. Print-preview the layout by zooming to 10% — the zone band color and zone number must be visible even at thumbnail size.

**Session 1B — Populate Zone 5 Content (60 min)**

Open CANVA_ZONE_CARD_BATCH_WORKFLOW.md > Zone 5 section. Copy each text block into the corresponding field:

- Header band: "Zone 5 Quick-Start Card" (already set)
- Column 1 (Plant Calendar): copy Zone 5 "Plant This Month" crop list
- Column 2 (Variety Spotlight): copy Zone 5 variety names and descriptions
- Column 3 (Zone Tips): copy Zone 5 growing notes bullets
- Footer: keep [ETSY-URL] and [KIT-URL] placeholders

After populating content, run the Zone 5 pre-export checklist:
- [ ] Zone number "5" appears in the header band text
- [ ] All three columns have content — no placeholder text visible except footer brackets
- [ ] Footer brackets are exactly "[ETSY-URL]" and "[KIT-URL]" (you will replace these May 21)
- [ ] No text overflows its column boundary (zoom to 100% and scroll)
- [ ] Logo visible in header band

Export PDF test: File > Download > PDF Print > Flatten PDF: Yes. Open the exported PDF in any viewer. Confirm: (1) all fonts rendered correctly (no substitution to serif/sans mismatch), (2) no clipped or overflowing text, (3) footer bar is fully visible at the bottom edge.

Save as master template — do not duplicate yet. The Zone 5 master is the source for all subsequent zones.

### Building Subsequent Zone Cards (Zones 6, 3, 4, 7, 8, 9, 10)

For each subsequent zone, follow this exact 6-step sequence:

1. Open the Canva project for the source card (see "Duplicate From" column in the build order table)
2. Canva > File > "Make a copy" — name the new file `SW-Zone[N]-Card`
3. **FIRST EDIT: change the zone band color** to the correct hex code for this zone (before any text changes)
4. Update the header band text to the correct zone number (e.g., "Zone 3 Quick-Start Card")
5. Open CANVA_ZONE_CARD_BATCH_WORKFLOW.md > [Zone N] section. Substitute all zone-specific content:
   - Column 1: replace Zone 5 crop list with Zone N crop list
   - Column 2: replace Zone 5 variety spotlight with Zone N varieties
   - Column 3: replace Zone 5 growing notes with Zone N notes
   - Footer: keep [ETSY-URL] and [KIT-URL] brackets unchanged
6. Run the per-card checklist and export PDF test before considering the card "done"

**Per-card checklist (run for every zone)**:
- [ ] Zone number correct in header band text
- [ ] Zone band color matches the build order table
- [ ] No Zone 5 content in any column (most common error — scan every text element)
- [ ] City references updated to zone-appropriate cities
- [ ] Footer: exactly "[ETSY-URL]" and "[KIT-URL]" brackets (no live URLs yet, no partial URLs)
- [ ] PDF export test passes (no font substitution, no text overflow)

## Part 3: Lifestyle Image Compositing (42 Images — Etsy Slots 4 and 5)

### What This Is

After the photo shoot (May 10-13), each lifestyle JPEG needs a light Canva compositing step: add the Deep Forest Green brand strip at the bottom with the Seedwarden wordmark. This creates visual consistency across all 21 listings when buyers browse the shop.

The compositing does NOT add the product mockup on top of the lifestyle photo. The lifestyle photo IS the product (it shows the printed guide in a scene). The compositing only adds the brand strip.

### SW-Master-FlatLay Template Build (May 14)

1. New Canva design: 2400×2400px (custom dimensions)
2. Upload the best Cluster A flat-lay photo as the full-canvas background layer
3. Add brand strip: Rectangle element, full canvas width (2400px), 80px tall, positioned at the very bottom of the canvas. Fill: Deep Forest Green (#143b28).
4. Add text in brand strip: "Seedwarden" in Playfair Display, 22px, Warm Cream (#F5EDD6), centered horizontally, vertically centered in the strip.
5. Optional product name overlay: lower-right corner, product name in Playfair Display 16px, Deep Forest Green at 60% opacity, positioned above the brand strip.
6. Save as `SW-Master-FlatLay` in the Seedwarden Phase 2 folder. This is the compositing template.

### Per-Product Compositing Workflow

For each of the 42 product images (21 products × 2 slots):

1. Duplicate `SW-Master-FlatLay` in Canva
2. Rename the duplicate to: `[product-slug]-slot4` or `[product-slug]-slot5`
3. Click the background layer > "Replace" > upload the corresponding lifestyle JPEG from `/etsy-ready/`
4. Update the optional product name text overlay with the correct product name
5. Export: File > Download > JPEG > Quality: High
6. Rename the downloaded file: `[product-slug]-slot4.jpg` or `[product-slug]-slot5.jpg`
7. Save to `/projects/seedwarden/marketing/lifestyle-photos/etsy-ready/`

**Batch compositing order** (by Etsy upload priority):

| Priority | Cluster | Products | Target Completion |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | D — Stock composited | 5 products (survival garden, hunting manual, livestock manual, meat preservation, native plants) | May 15 |
| 2 | C — Physical kitchen | 3 products (fermented harvest, harvest preservation, hot sauce) | May 15-16 |
| 3 | B — Physical urban | 4 products (container blueprint, apartment growing, plant catalog, seed swap kit) | May 17 |
| 4 | A — Physical flat-lay | 8 products (all seed/garden products) | May 18-20 |

**Cluster D+E compositing (stock-composite method)**: These 5 products use stock backgrounds from `assets/stock-raw/` with the product mockup overlaid. The process is slightly different:
1. New Canva design: 2400×2400px
2. Upload stock background image (e.g., `survival-garden-regional-plans-slot4.jpg` from `assets/stock-raw/`)
3. Upload product mockup from `mockups/` directory
4. Position mockup at 50-60% of canvas width, offset to one side (not centered)
5. Apply drop shadow to mockup: Offset shadow, 25% opacity, blur 10px, X +3, Y +5
6. Add brand strip as above
7. Export and rename as `[product-slug]-slot4.jpg`

## Part 4: Pinterest Pin Builds (15 Pins for Launch Week)

### Canvas Size

New Canva design: 1000px wide × 1500px tall (2:3 ratio, Pinterest standard)

### 5 Pin Templates (Build One of Each First, Then Duplicate for Remaining Products)

All 5 templates are specified in full in `pin-template-specs.md`. Summary:

**Template 1 — Product Feature Pin**
- Background: lifestyle flat-lay image (1000×1500px crop from the 2400×2400 JPEG)
- Overlay: Deep Forest Green rectangle at top of pin, 200px tall, 80% opacity
- Title text: product name in Playfair Display 32px, Warm Cream, in the green overlay
- Subtitle: one-sentence benefit in Lato 14px, Parchment
- Brand strip at bottom: 60px, Deep Forest Green, "Seedwarden" centered in Lato 18px Warm Cream

**Template 2 — Educational/Quote Pin**
- Background: Warm Cream (#F5EDD6) solid
- Large quote or fact: Playfair Display 36px, Deep Forest Green, centered, occupying top 60% of canvas
- Attribution: Cormorant Garamond 18px italic, Deep Ink Green, below the quote
- Lifestyle image thumbnail: bottom 35% of canvas, no border
- Brand strip: bottom 60px, Deep Forest Green

**Template 3 — Zone Card Preview Pin**
- Background: exported zone card image (screenshot or PDF page as image)
- Overlay badge: "Free Zone Guide" in Cormorant Garamond 24px, Burnt Sienna, positioned upper-right
- CTA text: "Get yours at the link" in Lato 16px, white, positioned in lower overlay band
- Brand strip: bottom 60px, Deep Forest Green

**Template 4 — Lifestyle Context Pin**
- Full-canvas contextual lifestyle image (from slot 5 — hands-use shots)
- Title overlay at top: semi-transparent Deep Forest Green bar, product name in Playfair Display 28px Warm Cream
- No bottom strip beyond a minimal "Seedwarden" text in lower-right at Lato 12px

**Template 5 — Seasonal/How-To Pin**
- Background: Parchment (#EDE0C4) solid
- Numbered list layout: 3-4 steps, each with Playfair Display 24px heading + Lato 14px step text
- Lifestyle image in right 40% of canvas (vertical strip), bordered with 4px Deep Forest Green line
- Header: "Zone [X] — [Month] Tasks" in Cormorant Garamond 30px

### Pin Export Settings

1. File > Download
2. File type: **JPEG** (not PNG — JPEG is smaller and loads faster on Pinterest)
3. Quality: High (90% or maximum available)
4. Compression: Low

### Pin Naming Convention

```
[product-slug]-pin-[template-type].jpg
```

Examples:
```
seed-saving-field-manual-pin-product.jpg
companion-planting-chart-pin-educational.jpg
zone-5-quick-start-card-pin-preview.jpg
harvest-preservation-field-manual-pin-lifestyle.jpg
```

**Save location**: `projects/seedwarden/marketing/lifestyle-photos/pins/`

### Scheduling

- Schedule using Pinterest native scheduler (Publish > Schedule within a pin)
- Time slots: Tuesday through Friday, 8pm-11pm local time
- Minimum 2 hours between pins from the same account in one day
- Launch week (May 31-June 5): 3-4 pins per day, all pre-scheduled by May 29

Full slide copy with exact text is in `phase-2-social-content-calendar-60day.md` Day 3.

### Carousel Export Settings

1. File > Download
2. File type: **PNG** (higher quality than JPEG for graphics and text-heavy slides)
3. Export all pages: Yes (Canva will download a ZIP with all 5 slides as separate files)
4. Unzip and rename each file:

```
carousel-day3-slide1.png
carousel-day3-slide2.png
carousel-day3-slide3.png
carousel-day3-slide4.png
carousel-day3-slide5.png
```

### Instagram Upload

1. Instagram > Create post > Multi-image icon (select multiple images)
2. Add all 5 slides in order (Canva exports them numbered)
3. Cover photo = Slide 1 (the full-green cover)
4. Caption: from `phase-2-social-content-calendar-60day.md` Day 3

## Part 6: Launch Week Social Graphics

### Instagram Launch Post (May 30, 2pm)

- Size: 1080×1080px JPEG (square format for feed)
- Content: top lifestyle flat-lay image from Cluster A (the strongest shot, chosen during May 22-25 cull session)
- Add: Deep Forest Green brand strip at bottom (80px), "Seedwarden" in Playfair Display Warm Cream, centered
- No additional text overlay on the image — caption carries the copy
- Export: JPEG, high quality

### TikTok Launch Video (May 30, 2pm)

- If video footage is available: Export 9:16 (1080×1920px) MP4 from phone or Canva
- If no video footage: In Canva, create a video design (1080×1920px), add the lifestyle flat-lay as the background, apply Canva's "Ken Burns" pan-and-zoom animation, export as MP4
- The Ken Burns animation makes a static image look like video in a TikTok context. It is not ideal but it functions — TikTok algorithms do prefer native video, so film actual footage if at all possible

### Pinterest Launch Pins (May 30, 3:30pm)

- Build 3 launch pins, one per flagship product from the launch email
- Use Template 1 (Product Feature Pin) for each
- Schedule all 3 to go live at staggered times: 3:30pm, 5:30pm, 7:30pm (2-hour gaps)
