**Launch date**: May 30 unchanged.

### Trigger 2: Photo Shoot Slips to May 17-18 AND Something Else Also Slips

**Condition**: Shoot moves to May 17-18 AND one other item (zone cards, Kit setup) is behind.

**Response**: Launch May 30 with partial Etsy image library. Clusters D and E (stock-composited images, already staged) go live May 30. Clusters A, B, C lifestyle photos are added as a post-launch update within 7 days.

**What launches May 30**: Email automation (fully independent), social posts, 10 Etsy listings with stock compositing in slots 4-5, 11 Etsy listings with existing 3-image stacks.

**Revenue impact**: Negligible. Existing 3-image stacks are functional. The launch broadcast reaches the subscriber list; conversions come from the email sequence, not from lifestyle images.

---

### Trigger 3: Zone Cards Running Over Estimate

**Condition**: Canva time is running 2.5-3 hours per card instead of 60-90 minutes.

**Response**: Minimum viable launch path is 4 zone cards (Zones 5, 6, 7, 8 — highest US population density). These four zones cover the majority of Seedwarden's buyer geography. Zones 3, 4, 9, 10 are built and delivered during the first week of June.

**Kit email handling**: Build Email 1 with a placeholder variant for zones 3, 4, 9, 10 that says "Your zone card is being built — it will arrive by June 7." This is already documented as an acceptable minimum in the zone card production spec.

---

### Trigger 4: Kit Account Setup Delayed Past May 22

**Condition**: Kit account creation or email sequence build is not complete by May 22.

**Response**:
- Day 22-23: If only account creation is complete but sequence is not built, new subscribers receive a manual Gmail welcome with zone card attached as an email attachment.
- This is a 1-3 day workaround. Build the full sequence before any subscriber reaches Day 5 of the sequence.
- If Kit is still incomplete on May 28: launch with manual Gmail as the email channel. Email the launch broadcast from the Seedwarden Gmail. This is not the preferred path but does not block the Etsy or social launches.

---

### Trigger 5: Phase 1 Etsy Store Not Yet Live on May 30

**Condition**: Etsy account or listing approval delays push Phase 1 past May 30.

**Response**: Track B launches as planned. All CTAs in the email and social posts point to the Kit landing page, not to Etsy listings. When Etsy goes live (regardless of date), update social bio links to include the Etsy store URL. Email sequence links will need UTM-tagged Etsy URLs added at that point.

**What does NOT need to change**: Every element of the May 30 launch except the Etsy step at 10am. The email and social tracks are entirely Etsy-independent.
