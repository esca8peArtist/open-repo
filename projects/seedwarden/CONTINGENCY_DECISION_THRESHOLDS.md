## Day 3 — June 2, 2026

### Primary Decision: Track A Holdout Influencers (Optional Add)

**Decision question**: Should the Track A holdout influencer cohort be activated based on Track B early performance?

Track A is currently blocked on separate user actions. However, a subset of influencers from the Track A list were classified as "Track A holdout" — meaning they were not approached in the initial Track B May 28–30 outreach because their audience fit better with Track A's Etsy/paid product framing than the free Zone Card distribution play. If Track B launches with strong early traction (Day 3 threshold met), activating these holdouts during the launch window is a low-cost amplification opportunity.

**Track A holdout influencer profile**: Contacts who:
- Focus on paid product recommendations rather than free resource distribution
- Have herbalism educator audiences that expect curated, vetted recommendations rather than raw community resources
- Would be a better match for: "I'd like to compensate you / set up an affiliate link" rather than "here's a free resource your community can download"

**Decision gate**:

| Day 3 Gist views | Reddit upvotes | Decision |
|-----------------|---------------|---------|
| < 30 views | < 10 upvotes | Hold. Do not activate Track A holdouts. Diagnose Track B distribution before expanding to a new channel. |
| 30–70 views | 10–25 upvotes | Optional. Track B is functioning but not at breakthrough pace. Activating 1–2 Track A holdouts who are easiest to reach (email already drafted) is acceptable but not urgent. |
| > 70 views | > 25 upvotes | YES — activate. Strong Day 3 signal means the product is resonating. Adding Track A holdouts now amplifies an active wave. Prepare affiliate terms. |
| Any count, but 1+ influencer organically shared | Regardless of view count | YES — activate immediately. Organic sharing is the clearest product-market signal. |

## Day 7 — June 6, 2026

### Primary Decision: Full Success / Moderate / Low Response Matrix

**Decision question**: What does the first-week baseline tell us about Track B's distribution trajectory and Phase 2 scope?

#### Scenario 1: Full Success

**Thresholds**: Gist views > 200 AND Reddit upvotes > 50 AND Instagram reach > 500 AND at least 3 organic shares by others

**Decision**: Phase 2 scope expansion.

Actions:
1. Build the Etsy paid bundle listing immediately: all 8 zone cards + cover page + zone-selection guide. Suggested price: $5–9.
2. Begin email list building: add a Kit/ConvertKit subscribe form to the Gist description or a landing page. The organic sharing momentum means an email capture layer is now worth the setup friction.
3. Activate Phase 2 content wave: begin planning zone-specific deep-dive posts (one per zone, over the following two weeks). These are distinct from the June 1–7 calendar already planned.
4. If 5+ influencer contacts have responded: pursue the 2–3 highest-engagement contacts for formal affiliate partnerships. Prioritize contacts with newsletters (Sabrena Gwin / AHG route, John Gallagher / LearningHerbs) because newsletter mentions produce sustained click traffic rather than single-day spikes.

**Revenue projections at full success**:
- Week 2 Etsy listing at $7/bundle: even 10 sales = $70. If 3 affiliate partners each refer 5 sales: 15 additional sales = $105. Week 2 total ~$175. Low absolute revenue, but the distribution momentum de-risks Phase 3 investment significantly.

---

#### Scenario 2: Moderate Response

**Thresholds**: Gist views 100–200 AND Reddit upvotes 25–50 AND Instagram reach 200–500 AND 1–2 organic shares

**Decision**: Maintain current cadence with minor adjustments.

Actions:
1. Continue June 1–7 social calendar as planned (no changes).
2. Build Etsy listing on a relaxed timeline (target June 13, not June 7). Moderate response still validates the paid bundle, just not at full-launch urgency.
3. Send one follow-up to the 2–3 highest-engagement influencer contacts from the pre-launch outreach. Subject: "Quick follow-up — zone cards got [X] downloads in week 1, thought you might like to know."
4. No paid promotion yet. Day 14 checkpoint will confirm whether a paid $20–50 test is warranted.

---

#### Scenario 3: Low Response

**Thresholds**: Gist views < 100 AND/OR Reddit upvotes < 25 AND/OR Instagram reach < 200 AND 0 organic shares

**Decision**: Rapid-response adjustment before investing further.

**Diagnose the distribution gap first** (this takes 15 minutes, do it before taking any action):

| Hypothesis | Check | If true |
|------------|-------|---------|
| Reddit posts were removed or filtered | Check your Reddit post history for removal notices | Repost as image posts with personal framing |
| Gist URL was not inserted in social posts | Check actual published post captions for the URL | Edit captions; add new Story with corrected link |
| Instagram reach is low because account is new | Check Instagram account age and follower count | This is expected for a new account; organic reach on new accounts is consistently below 200 until the account has 50+ posts |
| Outreach emails were not sent | Check email sent folder | Send immediately — this is the highest-priority action if unsent |
| Gist is not the right distribution format for this audience | Check whether views are proportionally low across all platforms (not just Gist) | Consider switching to a Gumroad free product or Etsy free listing — search-discoverable platforms may outperform link-sharing platforms for this audience |

**Rapid-response adjustments (in priority order)**:
1. If outreach emails not sent: send all Tier 1 and Tier 2 contacts immediately. This is the most common reason for low early traction.
2. If Reddit distribution failed: post in r/foraging (750K+ members, not yet reached if r/vegetablegardening was primary) with image post format.
3. If social posts had placeholder URL: correct immediately. Add an Instagram Story with corrected link.
4. If all above were correctly executed: do not panic. Week-1 results for new product accounts are almost always below projections. Proceed with June 1–7 content calendar as planned. Day 14 will have a more meaningful baseline.
