## General Customization Guide

### Personalization merge fields (use these to customize each template)

```
[CONTACT_NAME]              → "Sabrena" or "Susan" (first name for warmth)
[ORGANIZATION]              → "AHG," "United Plant Savers," "Chestnut School"
[ZONE_GUIDES_ANGLE]         → Core value prop customized per audience
[AUDIENCE_TYPE]             → "RH practitioners," "students," "conservation-focused herbalists"
[CALL_TO_ACTION]            → "newsletter feature," "affiliate partnership," "podcast appearance"
[REGION_EMPHASIS]           → "Appalachian," "Pacific Northwest," "Southeast" (per contact location)
[COMMISSION_RATE]%          → 15–25% (customize per budget)
[LANDING_PAGE_URL]          → Final URL (to be determined, e.g., seedwarden.co/zones)
```

### Tone guidance per template

| Template | Tone | Formality | Length |
|----------|------|-----------|--------|
| A: AHG/RH Practitioners | Professional, collaborative | Medium | 300 words |
| B: Conservation (UpS) | Mission-aligned, values-driven | Slightly formal | 280 words |
| C: Affiliate/Creator | Business-casual, partnership-focused | Medium | 320 words |
| D: School Directors | Educational, community-focused | Medium | 300 words |
| E: Reddit/Discord | Friendly, transparent | Casual | 100–150 words |

### Subject line best practices

**Effective subject lines**:
- Include value prop (e.g., "practitioner-grade," "zone-specific," "FGV-featured")
- Mention the recipient's community (e.g., "for RH practitioners," "for your students")
- Create urgency without pressure (e.g., "May 30 launch," "early partnership opportunity")

**Avoid**:
- "Check this out" (too vague)
- ALL CAPS (too aggressive)
- Multiple question marks (feels unsure)
- Overly promotional language

## Follow-Up Protocol

### If no response after 48 hours:

**Follow-up email subject**: "[Follow-up] Zone guides partnership — quick question"

**Body** (keep brief):

"Hi [NAME], just checking in on the email I sent [DATE]. No pressure, but if you're interested in featuring the guides, we're on a May 30–June 1 timeline. Happy to answer any questions or provide a preview. Let me know!"

---

### If positive response:

**Confirm partnership terms** (be specific):
- Newsletter feature: Confirm date and any copy edits
- Affiliate link: Send tracking URL and commission confirmation
- Podcast: Confirm date/time and talking points
- Social share: Provide graphics and custom caption

**Provide assets immediately**:
- PDF preview or landing page link
- Pre-written email copy (ready to send to their audience)
- Social media graphics (if applicable)
- Talking points or podcast intro script

---

### If conditional response ("Sounds good, but..."):

**Address objection directly**:
- Budget: Explain affiliate commission structure or offer alternative partnership (newsletter-only, non-monetized)
- Audience fit: Provide specific examples of why this matches their community
- Timing: Offer flexibility (May 30 launch OR June 1 feature, your choice)
- Format: Offer alternatives (single zone vs. all 8, email vs. social, etc.)

**Respond within 24 hours** to keep momentum.

## Email Sending Checklist

Before hitting send on each email:

- [ ] **Personalization**: Used first name, organization name, and specific audience details
- [ ] **Subject line**: Clear, value-focused, mentions audience or partnership type
- [ ] **Tone**: Matches template guidelines (professional but warm)
- [ ] **Length**: Not more than 300–350 words (respect recipient's time)
- [ ] **Call-to-action**: Clear (newsletter feature, affiliate link, podcast appearance, etc.)
- [ ] **No typos**: Proofread (especially names and URLs)
- [ ] **Signature**: Includes your name, title, and email
- [ ] **Timing**: Sending May 27–28 (allows response before May 30 launch)
- [ ] **Follow-up plan**: Know when/how you'll follow up if no response
