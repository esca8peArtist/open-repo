## Section 3: Canva Designer Brief Template

Use this brief when requesting quotes from Etsy or Fiverr herbalist designers. Send to 3–5 designers May 27–29 to have quotes in hand before the May 30 user decision on scope.

The brief is scope-agnostic: it covers the full five-bundle asset set (Option A/B) by default, with a clear note that the final scope (3 vs. 5 bundles) may adjust the deliverable count. This lets designers quote accurately without requiring the scope decision first.

### Designer Brief — Phase 3 Medicinal Herbs Bundle Visual Assets

**Project**: Seedwarden Medicinal Herbs Series — Phase 3 Cover Designs and Zone Cards
**Brand**: Seedwarden (established Canva brand kit; full brand kit shared on project acceptance)
**Launch date**: June 22–July 13, 2026 (staggered uploads)
**Design deadline**: July 3, 2026 (all assets delivered; hard date)
**Quote due**: May 29, 2026
**Contact**: [Your email]

---

**Project Overview**

Seedwarden is a practitioner-grade botanical education brand selling digital guides on Etsy. Phase 3 is the medicinal herbs series. I need a designer to produce cover images and zone card infographic layouts for five medicinal herb bundles. The covers are the primary Etsy listing image (2400x2400px square). The zone cards are a one-page quick reference insert in each bundle PDF.

**Scope — Full Five-Bundle (may reduce to three bundles depending on scope decision by May 30)**

The five bundles are: Women's Health, Respiratory Health, Sleep and Nervines, Immunity Support, and Digestive Support. Each bundle requires:

- One bundle cover image (2400x2400px, Etsy primary listing image)
- One zone card layout (8.5x11in or A4, one-page quick reference for PDF insert)

Full scope: 5 covers + 5 zone cards = 10 deliverables.
Reduced scope (if 3-bundle only): 3 covers + 3 zone cards = 6 deliverables.

**Brand Kit (to be shared on project acceptance)**

- Primary palette (Phase 3 Herbalist/Apothecary):
  - Deep Burgundy #8B3E3E (Women's Health, Immunity header color)
  - Sage Green #6B8E6F (Respiratory, Digestive header color)
  - Muted Lavender #9B8BA0 (Sleep bundle header color)
  - Apothecary Gold #D4AF37 (accent bar, all bundles)
  - Clinical Cream #F9F5F0 (background, all bundles)
  - Dark Charcoal #2C2C2C (body text, all bundles)
- Heading font: Playfair Display Bold (free in Canva)
- Body font: Lato Regular (free in Canva)
- Accent font: Cormorant Garamond Italic (free in Canva)
- Logo: Seedwarden wordmark PNG (transparent background; provided on acceptance)

**Cover Design Specifications**

- Canvas size: 2400x2400px (square; Etsy primary image format)
- Layout: header block (top 25%) with bundle name in Playfair Display Bold; hero image (center 50%) — botanical illustration or clean herb photography; footer (bottom 25%) with Seedwarden logo and tagline
- Hero images: I will provide the botanical source images (Wikimedia CC or licensed photos); designer composites them into the layout
- Text elements on each cover: bundle name, five species listed (common + Latin), "Practitioner-Grade Guide" label, Seedwarden wordmark
- Each bundle has its own header color per the brand kit above — no two covers share an identical header color

**Zone Card Specifications**

- Canvas size: 8.5x11in (letter), intended as a PDF insert page at 150–300 DPI
- Layout: one-page quick reference; content includes species name (Latin + common), habitat zone, key uses (3–5 bullet points), contraindication summary (1–2 lines), photo thumbnail placeholder
- Brand colors consistent with corresponding bundle cover
- Clean table or card layout — no decorative flourishes; this is a clinical reference card

**Required Files on Delivery**

- All covers: PNG at 2400x2400px, sRGB color space
- All zone cards: PDF at 300 DPI, letter size, sRGB
- Editable Canva link shared to my Canva account (for minor revisions post-delivery)

**Revision Policy**

- One round of minor revisions included per deliverable (color correction, text adjustment, image swap)
- Structural redesign requests beyond one revision round are billed separately

**Quote Request**

Please quote the following separately so I can select scope:
- Option A: Full five-bundle set (10 deliverables)
- Option B: Three-bundle set (6 deliverables, Women's Health + Respiratory + Sleep)

Include your delivery timeline for each scope option, confirming July 3 deadline is achievable. Please also include two or three examples of your previous botanical, wellness, or herbal product design work.

Send your quote to [email] by May 29.
