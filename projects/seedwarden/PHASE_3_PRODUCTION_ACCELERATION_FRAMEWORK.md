# Phase 3 Production Acceleration Framework

**Prepared**: May 31, 2026  
**Purpose**: Enable Phase 3 Medicinal Herbs launch in 4–6 weeks from Phase 2 data arrival — cutting the current 8–10 week baseline estimate by 35–50%. This is achieved through three mechanisms: parallel workstream execution during the Phase 2 observation window (May 30–June 15), decision-agnostic pre-staging of production assets, and template reuse across bundle content.

**June 1 review status**: All five Phase 3 production optimization files staged. Orchestrator runs decision tree June 20 upon data arrival. Production begins June 21.

---

## 1. Phase 2 → Phase 3 Decision Trigger

**Data gates**: Three Phase 2 metrics determine Phase 3 launch scope. All three are observable in Etsy analytics and Kit (email platform) within 15 days of launch.

| Metric | Observation source | Measurement method |
|---|---|---|
| Conversion rate | Etsy Shop Manager → Traffic | Views-to-orders ratio on Phase 2 bundles |
| Forager cohort composition | Kit email list → tag analysis | % of subscribers tagged "forager" vs. "herbalist" vs. "practitioner" |
| Revenue baseline | Etsy Finances → Monthly earnings | Cumulative gross revenue, days 1–15 post-launch |

**Gate thresholds** (full detail in `PHASE_3_RAPID_DECISION_TREE.md`):

- Conversion > 1.5% AND cohort > 20% AND revenue > $200 = Phase 3 proceeds (any scope)
- Conversion > 3% AND cohort > 30% AND revenue > $500 = Full quad-bundle scope
- Conversion < 1.5% OR revenue < $200 = Defer Phase 3; maintain Track A Etsy organic + reassess August

**Data arrival window**: June 15–20 (15–20 days post-launch). Orchestrator runs decision tree June 20. If all three green signals are present, production begins June 21.

**The framework is decision-agnostic**: All pre-staging work described in Sections 3–5 is valid regardless of which scenario the data selects. Nothing done during the pre-staging window is wasted if Phase 3 is deferred.

## 2. Three Phase 3 Launch Scenarios

### Scenario A — Women's Health Solo

**Trigger condition**: Conversion 1.5–2.5%, cohort 20–29%, revenue $200–$499

**Bundle composition**:
- Women's Health bundle (Lavender, Black Cohosh, Calendula, Chamomile, Red Raspberry Leaf) — 5 herbs, ~10,000 words
- Practitioner 10-pack add-on for Women's Health only

**Production scope**:
- 1 guide (authoring + design)
- 1 Etsy listing set (5 photos, 1 bundle description, 1 10-pack listing)
- 1 photographer brief executed
- 1 Kit landing page + 2-email post-purchase sequence
- 1 Pinterest board + 6 social posts

**Launch timeline from decision**: 4 weeks (June 21 decision → July 19 upload)

**Cost model**: $180–$260 total production cost (no second author required at this scope)

---

### Scenario B — Women's Health + Respiratory

**Trigger condition**: Conversion 2.5–3%, cohort 25–34%, revenue $400–$699

**Bundle composition**:
- Women's Health bundle (5 herbs) + Respiratory bundle (Elderberry, Echinacea purpurea, Mullein, Thyme, Elecampane) — 10 herbs total, ~19,000 words
- Practitioner 10-pack for both bundles
- Cross-bundle landing page ("Herbal Home Apothecary: Women's Health + Respiratory")

**Production scope**:
- 2 guides (authoring: ~38–44 hours total with template reuse)
- 2 Etsy listing sets + 1 cross-bundle bundle listing
- Shared photography session (10 herbs, 2-day shoot)
- 2 Kit landing pages + shared 4-email post-purchase sequence
- Synchronized upload: Women's Health Week 1, Respiratory Week 3

**Launch timeline from decision**: 5–6 weeks (June 21 decision → July 26–August 2 for second bundle)

**Cost model**: $350–$480 total production cost (second author optional at this scope — see Section 6)

### Scenario C — Full Quad-Bundle

**Trigger condition**: Conversion > 3%, cohort > 30%, revenue > $500

**Bundle composition**:

**Launch timeline from decision**: 8 weeks (June 21 decision → August 16 full upload complete)

## 6. Author Hiring Framework

This section adapts the systems-resilience `AUTHOR_HIRING_RUNBOOK.md` pattern for product content authors rather than domain research authors. The hiring process structure is identical; the qualification criteria differ.

### When to Activate

Activate only if June 20 data confirms Scenario B or C. Pre-stage the shortlist during the Phase 2 observation window (Section 4, Parallel Track 4) but send no outreach until June 20.

### Candidate Profile — Product Content Author

**Voice requirements** (different from research authors):
- Must demonstrate direct-instruction style: "Harvest the second-year spike in July" not "It has been observed that..."
- Must show comfort with botanical terminology without over-relying on Latin binomials in body text
- Must demonstrate FTC-aware framing: evidence cited with study description, not health claims
- Must be capable of writing contraindications accurately (herb-drug interactions, pregnancy cautions)

**Knowledge requirements**:
- Herbalism background: practitioner, forager, or home-grower experience (not academic botany)
- Familiarity with at least two of the five bundle categories (Women's Health, Respiratory, Sleep, Immunity, Digestive)
- Can write from both cultivation and preparation angles (not just preparation)

**Sourcing channels** (in order of preference):
1. American Herbalists Guild member directory (ahg.herbalist.org/member-directory) — practitioner voice confirmed
2. United Plant Savers author list (unitedplantsavers.org) — conservation framing alignment
3. Upwork with filter "herbalist" + "botanical" writing samples requested
4. Herbalist Instagram accounts with "Guide" or "Field Notes" in bio — direct DM outreach

### Qualification Gates

Identical structure to `AUTHOR_HIRING_RUNBOOK.md` with adapted criteria:

**Gate 1 — Writing voice** (15 minutes):
- Review candidate sample against Women's Health bundle introduction standard in `PHASE_3_BUNDLE_MASTER_TEMPLATE.md`
- Pass: Direct, warm, practically-oriented voice with appropriate botanical terminology
- Fail: Academic hedging, generic wellness copy, no evidence of herb-specific knowledge

**Gate 2 — Content accuracy signal** (20 minutes):
- Ask candidate: "Describe how you would handle the Black Cohosh estrogenic activity controversy in a guide written for home herbalists"
- Pass: Acknowledges scientific debate, recommends avoiding during pregnancy without over-alarming, cites traditional use alongside modern research
- Fail: Either dismisses the controversy entirely or catastrophizes it without nuance

**Gate 3 — Timeline confirmation** (5 minutes):
- "Can you complete one 10,000-word herb guide manuscript (first draft) within 14 calendar days?"
- Pass: Yes, with specific availability
- Fail: Hedged, conditional, or mentions major competing commitments

### Compensation Structure

- Flat rate: $400–550 per complete bundle manuscript (10,000 words, first draft)
- Revision round included: up to 2 hours revision per bundle
- Botanical accuracy review by owner before final payment
- Payment upon delivery of approved draft (no upfront payment)
- Timeline: 14 days per bundle, maximum 2 concurrent bundles

### Pre-Shortlist (build during Phase 2 observation window, June 1–14)

Identify 3–5 candidates across sourcing channels. Do not send outreach. Log candidates in `author-outreach-tracking.md` (systems-resilience template). Activate outreach June 21 if Scenario B or C is confirmed.
