## Part 3: Review Evaluation Framework

### 3.1 Rubric for RH Feedback Quality

**Accept & integrate** (substantive review):
- ✅ Nomenclature corrections (if any errors identified)
- ✅ Missing contraindications or interactions flagged
- ✅ Evidence-tier adjustments (recommending upgrade from Level 3 to Level 2, etc.)
- ✅ Clinical protocol suggestions (dosing, duration, safety parameter refinements)
- ✅ Practitioner-specific language requests (clinical jargon, abbreviation standardization)

**Acknowledge but optional integrate** (minor feedback):
- ⭕ General praise ("Great work on the Goldenseal guide")
- ⭕ Formatting suggestions (appendix structure, footer notes)
- ⭕ Cross-reference additions
- ⭕ Future edition requests ("Would love to see X plant in Phase 4")

**Reject** (feedback outside scope):
- ❌ Pricing/business model criticism
- ❌ Marketing strategy suggestions
- ❌ Unsubstantiated claims ("This contradicts my 20-year practice" without citation)
- ❌ Completely new content requests for Phase 3 (defer to Phase 4)

1. ✅ At least 1 RH review received by June 21
