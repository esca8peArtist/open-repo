## JOB 3: Recipe & Preparation Guide Writer (Writer #2)

**Title**: Recipe & Preparation Guide Writer for Herbal Medicine

**Project type**: Fixed-price, one-time contract  
**Contract length**: 6 weeks (July 1 – August 10, 2026)  
**Budget**: $[RATE_HERE] for full project  
**Estimated hours**: 60–75 hours

**Project overview**

Seedwarden publishes medicinal herb guides (Etsy). We need a writer to create recipes, preparation instructions, and dosage guides for three bundles: Respiratory Health, Immunity Support, and Digestive Support. This is practical, consumer-facing content (teas, tinctures, syrups, infusions).

**Your deliverables**

- Three preparation guide sections, each 2,000–2,400 words
  - Respiratory bundle: 8–10 recipes (teas, steam inhalation, honey syrups, cough drops, salves)
  - Immunity bundle: 8–10 recipes (teas, tinctures, elder syrup, tonics, gummies, capsule guides)
  - Digestive bundle: 8–10 recipes (digestive bitters, teas, infused oils, fermented tonics, dosage guides)
- Delivery schedule: 1 section per week (Week 2–4)
- Format: Google Doc or Word (.docx)
- Tone: Step-by-step instructions for non-specialists; encouraging and accessible

**Content requirements**

- Each recipe entry: 150–250 words including ingredients, equipment, method, storage, dosage, safety notes
- Ingredient measurements: Both metric (grams, ml) and US standard (tablespoons, cups, ounces)
- Dosage guidance: Age-appropriate recommendations (adults, children 6+, pregnancy considerations where applicable)

- Visual format: Include a prep time, total time, yield, and difficulty level for each recipe

**Style guidelines**

- Clear, jargon-free language (define terms like "infusion," "maceration," "decoction" on first use)
- Encouraging tone (suitable for someone trying herbs for the first time)
- Consistent formatting: Ingredients in bullet points, method in numbered steps

**Your qualifications**
