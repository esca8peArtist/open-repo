# Track B Go-Live Sequencing
## June 22 – December 2026 Month-by-Month Activation

**Prepared**: May 21, 2026
**Sequencing baseline**: June 22 US Etsy launch (Option C solo — 3 bundles by July 13, consistent across all scope options)
**Independence**: All sequences are independent of Track A blockers. No step requires Etsy account verification resolution to begin.

---

## Overview

The post-launch activation follows a strict margin-to-complexity sequencing: the highest-margin, lowest-complexity channels activate first (practitioner direct), followed by medium-complexity channels (clinics, Amazon KDP), followed by high-complexity physical channels (co-ops, distributors) only after demand is validated. Geographic expansion mirrors this logic: Canada activates first (near-zero compliance cost), UK second, EU deferred.

Each phase includes a go/no-go checkpoint before the next phase activates. The checkpoints are revenue-denominated, not time-denominated: if the revenue threshold is not met, the next phase does not open regardless of calendar date.

### September 1: Amazon KDP Live

**Trigger**: Phase 1C go signal met (monthly gross ≥$2,000, ≥10 reviews).

**Actions**:
- Upload epub-formatted Women's Health, Respiratory Health, Sleep and Nervines bundles to Amazon KDP.
- Set price at $22 (matching Etsy), 35% royalty (no KDP Select — no exclusivity restriction).
- Confirm listings on Amazon.com, Amazon.ca, Amazon.co.uk automatically (KDP international distribution).
- Monitor first 30-day Amazon sales. Expected: 10–25 sales across three marketplaces in Month 1.

### September 2026: Physical Product Evaluation

**Actions**:
- Obtain POD pricing from Lulu.com and IngramSpark for 50-page saddle-stitch guide at 20+ unit quantities.
- Acquire 5 UPC barcodes from GS1 US ($30 each, or $150 total for one barcode per bundle title). This is an infrastructure investment for the co-op channel — do not order until POD pricing is confirmed and at least one co-op has expressed interest.
- Research 5 pilot co-op targets (Wedge Co-op Minneapolis, Willy Street Madison, Honest Weight Albany, PCC Markets Seattle, Rainbow Grocery SF) for October outreach.

### October 2026: Co-Op Pilot + UK Outreach Evaluation

**UK outreach trigger**: US+Canada combined gross ≥$5,000 by September 30 (per TRACK_B_GEOGRAPHIC_EXPANSION_ANALYSIS.md go signal).

**Actions (if UK trigger met)**:
- Research NIMH (National Institute of Medical Herbalists) online directory — 800–1,000 registered practitioners.
- Adapt practitioner outreach template for UK audience (reference BHP alignment, NIMH credential recognition).
- Send 25 UK outreach emails (October 1–7). Target: 5–10 UK affiliates confirmed by October 31.

**Co-op pilot (October)**:
- Send co-op outreach emails to 5 pilot stores (use printed sample or high-resolution digital sample for first impression).
- Proposal: 20-unit consignment at $14.30 wholesale price (35% discount from $24.99 retail). Net $10.37 per unit to Seedwarden.
- If POD supplier confirmed: place first 100-unit POD order (~$450–$550 COGS) to fulfill pilot co-op orders.

**Phase 2 targets (September–October)**:
- Amazon KDP: 25–60 sales across three markets (US, Canada, UK)
- Canada affiliate confirmations: 8–15
