**Revenue model at steady state** (12 months post-launch): 5 bundles × $20–22 average price × estimated 20–35 sales/bundle/month = $2,000–$3,850/month gross. Break-even per bundle: 13–16 sales (includes production time at $25/hour equivalent).

### Per-Species Word Targets and Structure

Each species section follows a standard 4–5 section structure:

1. **Identification** (250–350 words): Common names, botanical name, plant family, visual distinguishing features, growth habit, seasonal appearance
2. **Cultivation** (350–450 words): Hardiness zones, soil requirements, light requirements, propagation (seed/division/cuttings), grow timeline from propagule to harvest-ready, yield per plant
3. **Traditional Use & Constituents** (400–500 words): Historical use in herbal traditions (Western, Ayurvedic, TCM as applicable), active constituents, primary therapeutic claims, preparation methods (infusion, decoction, tincture, salve)
4. **Contraindications & Safety** (150–250 words): Drug interactions, pregnancy/lactation status, overdose signs, populations to avoid, storage recommendations
5. **Conservation Sidebar** (150–200 words, select species): CITES status, United Plant Savers At-Risk list membership, ethical sourcing, cultivation-as-conservation angle (FGV-verified farms, native population context)

**Species with conservation sidebars** (mandatory in these bundles):
- Goldenseal (Immunity): CITES Appendix II, FGV farm partnerships, cultivated-stock recovery pathway
- Black Cohosh (Women's Health): United Plant Savers At-Risk, wild population decline + cultivation recovery
- Echinacea (Respiratory + Immunity): Prairie ecosystem restoration through cultivated propagation
