# Phase 3 Contractor Hiring Timeline

**Objective**: Hire 6 contractors across 6 roles (photographer, writer 1, writer 2, writer 3 / clinical herbalist writer, herbalist consultant, logistics coordinator) by July 6, 2026. All contracts signed and 25% deposits sent by July 1–6.

**Critical constraint**: First deliverables are due July 5–8. Zero buffer between hire date and production start. Any delay in hiring cascades directly into bundle launch dates.

**Date reference**: All dates are offset from "Approval Day" (AD), defined as the day the user confirms the Q3 hiring start. If approval comes June 29, all dates below are as written. If approval is later, shift all dates by the corresponding number of days.

## WEEK 1: Portfolio Audit and Reference Checks (July 1 – July 6)

### Portfolio Audit — 5-Point Deep Review Per Candidate

Run for all tier-1 candidates after offers are sent. Complete within the 48-hour offer window.

**Photographer portfolio audit** (review 5 most recent projects):
- [ ] Minimum 3 projects show botanical, food, or natural product flat-lay (not portraits, not events)
- [ ] Image resolution appears at or above 1600px width (professional standard)
- [ ] White balance is consistent within each project's image set
- [ ] Props and composition match Seedwarden's warm, natural aesthetic (reference LIFESTYLE_PHOTOGRAPHY_STRATEGY.md for examples)
- [ ] No harsh shadows or dark dramatic backgrounds in botanical close-ups

**Writer 1 portfolio audit** (review 5 most recent articles):
- [ ] Minimum 3 articles reference plant names with scientific terminology
- [ ] At least 1 article discusses contraindications or safety considerations
- [ ] Tone is educational, not marketing hyperbole or dry academic
- [ ] At least 1 article exceeds 2,500 words (confirms long-form capacity for 3,600-word bundles)
- [ ] No unqualified health claims in article sample (checked for "cures" or "prevents" language)

**Writer 2 portfolio audit** (review 5 most recent recipe or prep guides):
- [ ] At least 3 include step-by-step numbered method instructions
- [ ] At least 2 include dosage or serving guidance
- [ ] Language is accessible to non-specialists (no undefined jargon)
- [ ] Measurements use both metric and US standard (or explicitly state adaptability)
- [ ] Format could support photographic illustration (visual description present)

**Writer 3 / Clinical Herbalist Writer audit** (professional background verification):
- [ ] AHG or NAHA credential verified via directory search OR professional profile confirms 5+ years clinical practice
- [ ] Published clinical work verified (link resolves to actual article, book, or clinical resource)
- [ ] Drug interaction experience confirmed (NatMed Pro, Micromedex, or equivalent named)
- [ ] Evidence-grading or research-citation approach visible in sample work

**Herbalist Consultant audit** (professional background verification):
- [ ] Same credential/experience verification as Writer 3 audit above
- [ ] At least 1 review or editorial project described or evidenced
- [ ] Conservation awareness (CITES, UpS) detectable in portfolio or stated in application

**Logistics Coordinator audit** (portfolio review):
- [ ] At least 2 coordination or operations projects described with scope, team size, and deliverable type
- [ ] At least 1 email marketing campaign mentioned with platform name
- [ ] Etsy or e-commerce coordination described (any platform)
- [ ] Communication tone in application is professional, clear, and brief

### Reference Check Protocol

Run one reference check per tier-1 candidate (July 2, targeting 5-minute async email or brief call).

**Reference request email template**:

> Subject: Quick reference check for [CANDIDATE NAME] — Seedwarden (5 min)
>
> Hi [REFERENCE NAME],
>
> I'm checking a reference for [CANDIDATE NAME], who has applied for a [ROLE] project with Seedwarden. Could you answer 3 quick questions by reply email?
>
> 1. Quality: On a scale of 1–10, how would you rate [CANDIDATE]'s work quality?
> 2. Reliability: Did [CANDIDATE] deliver on time? Any missed deadlines or quality issues?
> 3. Communication: How responsive was [CANDIDATE] to feedback during the project?
>
> Thank you — a reply by [DATE, 24 hours from send] is ideal.

**Document responses** (one sentence per reference, log in WORKLOG.md):
- Example: "Photography client — 9/10 quality, on-time, responded to feedback within 4 hours."
- Example: "Editor — 8/10 writing, requested 2 revisions on herbal accuracy, delivered cleanly."

## WEEK 1: Offer and Contract Process (July 1 – July 6)

### Offer Email Content (Send to All Tier-1 Candidates July 1, 12pm ET)

Each offer email must include:
1. Role and project scope (2 sentences max)
2. Deliverables summary (bullet list, 3–5 items)
3. Rate and payment schedule (4 milestones for most roles, 3 milestones for herbalist)
4. Key dates: Contract signature deadline, first deliverable, final deliverable
5. Reply deadline: 48 hours from send time
6. Closing question: "Any questions before I send the contract?"

### Contract Signature Sequence

1. Send offer email (July 1, 12pm ET — all 6 roles)
2. Receive acceptance reply within 48 hours (deadline: July 3, 12pm ET)
3. Generate contract from Google Docs template or HelloSign (fill: candidate name, rate, deliverables, key dates, 4 payment milestones)
4. Send contract via DocuSign or HelloSign (July 5, 9am ET — all acceptances)
5. 24-hour signature reminder if not signed by July 5, 6pm ET
6. Hard signature deadline: July 6, 12pm ET
7. Send deposit (25% of total contract) within 1 hour of signature

### Payment Trigger Checklist (Per Candidate, On Offer Acceptance)

- [ ] Candidate confirms July 1 start availability (yes/no recorded)
- [ ] Candidate provides payment method (Venmo, PayPal, Zelle, ACH, or other)
- [ ] Final rate confirmed in acceptance reply ("We're offering $[X] flat for [deliverables scope]")

### Tier-2 and Tier-3 Contingency

**If tier-1 candidate rejects offer within 48 hours**:
1. Log rejection in PHASE_3_CONTRACTOR_SUCCESS_DASHBOARD.md (reason if provided)
2. Send tier-2 offer immediately (new 48-hour window starts)
3. If tier-2 also rejects: Send tier-3 offer (tier-3 candidates from PHASE_3_CONTRACTOR_SELECTION_SCORECARD.md)

**If all tiers reject by July 4, 12pm ET**:
- Photographer: Cross-post to Thumbtack; accept score-6.0+ candidate
- Writer 1: Consider splitting content scope between Writers 2 and 3 (see PHASE_3_SOLO_FALLBACK_ARCHITECTURE.md)
- Writer 2: Reduce recipe count per bundle from 8–10 to 5–6; absorb gap in production sprint
- Writer 3 / Clinical: Defer clinical sections to Week 3; internal herbalist review acceptable short-term fallback
- Herbalist Consultant: Internal review by Writer 3 if that role is filled; defer formal consultant review to final pass
- Coordinator: Absorb weekly check-in tasks internally; hire last-minute admin on a 1-week Upwork posting if needed

### First Deliverable Confirmation (End of Kickoff Call)

Each contractor must confirm verbally by the end of their kickoff call:
- Their first deliverable and due date
- Any blockers that could affect the first delivery (equipment issues, schedule conflicts, materials needed)
- Preferred communication method for check-ins (email, Slack, Upwork messages)

## Hiring Completion Criteria

By end of Week 1 (July 6, 12:00pm ET), all six of the following must be true:

- [ ] All 6 contractor offers accepted
- [ ] All 6 contracts signed (DocuSign confirmation received)
- [ ] All 6 deposits sent (25% of contract value, payment receipt logged)
- [ ] All 6 onboarding kits delivered via email
- [ ] All 6 kickoff calls scheduled (and completed by July 8)
- [ ] All 6 contractors confirm their first deliverable dates verbally in kickoff call

**Red flags — escalate immediately if any of these occur**:
- Any contractor declines offer after verbal acceptance (indicates unreliable candidate — escalate to tier 2 immediately, do not wait 24 hours)
- Any contractor cannot confirm July 1 start during contract negotiation
- Any reference check returns a reliability score below 7/10 or mentions missed deadlines
- Any contractor is unreachable for more than 24 hours after offer is sent
