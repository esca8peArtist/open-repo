moment without needing additional photography.

**Canva workflow tips**:
- Access Brand Kit: left sidebar > Brand Kit — colors and fonts are one-click from here
- Duplicate a design: in Canva home, right-click the design thumbnail > Duplicate
- Full-bleed image: insert image > resize to cover entire zone > right-click > "Set image as background" (alternative: resize manually to fill the zone without padding)
- Export all at once: File > Download > select all pages if you built 3 pages in one document
