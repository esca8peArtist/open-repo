## TikTok Repurpose Notes

All Instagram Reels can be repurposed to TikTok without modification IF:
- The video was exported without an Instagram watermark (use CapCut export or original source file)
- The caption is rewritten to remove "link in bio" (TikTok suppresses external link references in captions — replace with "link in profile")
- Hashtags are adjusted: add TikTok-native herbalist hashtags (#HerbalTok, #PlantTok, #GardenTok)

**Do not**: Cross-post directly from Instagram to TikTok using Meta's built-in share tool — this adds a watermark that TikTok's algorithm depresses.
