## Section 4: Risk Mitigation

### If photography session cannot happen before July 1
- Publish Women's Health bundle on Etsy with slots 1, 2, 3, and 5 filled (cover, interior preview, Wikimedia botanical photo, infographic).
- Leave slot 4 empty temporarily — Etsy allows publishing with fewer than 5 images.
- Note in WORKLOG.md: slot 4 placeholder date, actual shoot date when completed.
- Update slot 4 within 2 weeks of launch. Conversion impact of missing slot 4 is minimal in the first two weeks (low traffic volume).
- **Do not delay the launch** for the photography session. The listing can go live without it.

### If Phase 2 gate conditions are not met at July 11 check
- Do not publish Etsy listings. Continue building all templates, Kit automation, and social content.
- Re-check gate conditions at Phase 2 Week 10 (approximately August 8).
- Zero work wasted — all assets built before gate confirmation are still usable after.
- Notify the Phase 3 email waitlist: "Launching later this summer" — one short email, no explanation needed beyond "we're taking extra time to get it right."

### If the Black Cohosh Conservation Guide PDF is not ready before Lead Magnet A page goes live
- Use a 2-page version: just the UpS At-Risk status summary and three sourcing contacts. Can be written in 30 minutes.
- The full 6-page version can replace it after the launch window.
- Do not delay the Kit landing page for a polished PDF — subscribers who sign up first get the shorter version, then receive the expanded version as a follow-up.

### If Kit Herbalist Funnel Sequence is not built before launch
- Send Email 1 manually as a Broadcast to all new `herbalist` tag subscribers at the end of each week.
- This is a 1–3 week temporary workaround, not a permanent solution.
- The Sequence must be live by Week 3 post-launch — the Day 14 first offer email (Email 5) is critical for first-window conversions.

### If Etsy account has issues at launch
- Phase 3 cannot use Track B's independence from Etsy (unlike Phase 2, which had the zone card lead magnet as an Etsy-independent product). Medicinal herb guides are Etsy products.
- Contingency: Create a Gumroad listing as a 48-hour backup. Gumroad allows digital product sales with immediate PDF delivery. Price matching Etsy. Swap Etsy links for Gumroad links in the launch email.
- Gumroad account setup: 15 minutes. Use wanka95@gmail.com.
