# Phase 3 Canva Mockup Brief — Medicinal Herbs Product Line

**Prepared**: May 9, 2026
**How to use**: Each section is a self-contained design brief. Open Canva, follow the brief for that deliverable. All hex codes, font specs, and dimension requirements are listed within each brief. Complete the Brand Kit update (Section 0) before starting any other deliverable.

---

## Section 0: Canva Brand Kit — Medicinal Herbs Secondary Palette

Before building any Phase 3 template, add the Medicinal Herbs color palette to the Canva Brand Kit.

**Steps**: Log in to canva.com > Brand Hub > Brand Kit > [Your Seedwarden Kit] > Colors > Add Palette > Name: "Medicinal Herbs"

Add these five hex codes in order:

| Swatch Name | Hex Code | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Herb Brown (primary accent) | #6B4F35 | Section headers, card borders, CTA buttons |
| Herb Sage (secondary accent) | #8A9E6E | Botanical illustration accent, species name text |
| Herb Cream (background) | #F5EFE0 | Card backgrounds, email header backgrounds |
| Herb Ink (dark text) | #2E2A24 | Body text on all medicinal herbs materials |
| Herb Alert (conservation callout) | #B85C38 | UpS At-Risk species callouts, conservation sidebar borders |

**Font stack**: No new fonts required. Use Playfair Display (headers), Lato Regular (body), Lato Italic (Latin binomials). All three are already in the Phase 2 Brand Kit.

**Bundle 3 — Immunity Support Herbs Guide**
- Title: "Immunity Support Herbs Guide"

- Conservation badge: YES, double badge: "UpS At-Risk + CITES II Species Included."
- Color treatment: deep forest greens and browns, highest Herb Brown (#6B4F35) saturation of all five covers.

**Bundle 4 — Sleep and Nervines Herbs Guide**
- Title: "Sleep and Nervines Herbs Guide"

- Color palette: cooler, more muted. Reduce Herb Brown saturation; increase white space. Restful visual tone.

**Bundle 5 — Digestive Support Herbs Guide**
- Title: "Digestive Support Herbs Guide"

- Conservation badge: NONE.
- Color treatment: warm spring palette — more yellow-green. Increase Herb Sage (#8A9E6E); reduce brown tones.
- Forager crossover note: add small "Forager-to-Herbalist Bridge Guide" tag in Herb Sage, bottom center.

## Deliverable 2: Etsy Listing Slot Images (Slides 2–5, per bundle)

Slot 1 is the bundle cover (Deliverable 1). The following specs cover the remaining four image slots required per Etsy listing.

**Dimension**: 2400 × 2400 px (Etsy square). Also save each as 1600 × 2400 px (Pinterest 2:3 crop) in a separate file.

**Slot 2 — Interior Page Preview**
Show a mock interior page spread. Use the Canva document template for the guide's interior (see Deliverable 5 below for the zone card template, which uses the same interior style). Populate with the most visually compelling species from the bundle (Black Cohosh for Women's Health; Elderberry for Respiratory; Goldenseal for Immunity; Passionflower for Sleep; Dandelion for Digestive). Must be legible at thumbnail size. Text must show: species Latin binomial, cultivation section header, one visible table or list.

**Slot 3 — Species Close-Up Botanical Photo**
Single high-quality botanical photo of the anchor species in its growing context. Source from the Phase 3 stock image list (stock-image-lists/phase-3-botanical-stock-list.md). Minimum 2400 × 2400 px source image. No text overlay on this slide — pure photography. Canva crop and export only.

**Slot 4 — Lifestyle Flat-Lay**
Per the PHASE_3_LAUNCH_READINESS.md Section 3 photography spec. This image must be shot, not stock-sourced (except Category 3/practitioner images). Placeholder: if the photo shoot has not happened, create a Canva placeholder at 2400 × 2400 px with Herb Cream background, center text "PHOTO PENDING — [bundle name] flat-lay" in Herb Brown. Do not upload a placeholder to Etsy. Hold the slot until the actual photo is available.

**Slot 5 — Infographic or Bundle Value Comparison**
Canva-built graphic. Format: three columns. Column 1: "What you get" — list each species in the bundle (bullet list, Lato Regular 15pt, Herb Ink). Column 2: "For who" — one-line descriptor per audience segment (herbalists, NPs/RNs, students, gift buyers). Column 3: "Price" — "$22" (or applicable price) in Playfair Display Bold 48pt, Herb Brown. Background: Herb Cream. Top header band: Herb Brown, Herb Cream text. Bottom: FTC disclaimer in 9pt Lato.

## Deliverable 4: Medicinal Herb Zone Card Template

**Purpose**: Canva template for the 32 medicinal herb zone cards (per PHASE_3_LAUNCH_READINESS.md Section 2). This is a template — once built, it is duplicated 32 times and populated with species content.

**Dimension**: 1500 × 1050 px (5 × 3.5 inches at 300 DPI — standard postcard/zone card size, printable at home or Vistaprint). Export each final card as PDF (300 DPI) for Etsy delivery.

**Template front side**:
- Background: Herb Cream (#F5EFE0), full bleed.
- Top bar: 80 px, Herb Brown (#6B4F35). Contains: zone designation (e.g., "Medicinal Zone A — USDA Zones 3–5") in Lato Regular 14pt, Herb Cream.
- Central illustration zone: 500 × 500 px, centered. Botanical illustration placeholder (replace per species pair). Caption below illustration: Species Pair common names in Playfair Display 20pt, Herb Ink.
- Left-side micro-badge: Herb Alert (#B85C38) tag if species is UpS At-Risk. Text: "At-Risk" in Lato Bold 10pt, white.
- Bottom bar: 60 px, Herb Brown. Contains: Seedwarden logo (small, left-aligned) + "Full guides at [Etsy shop URL]" in Lato Regular 10pt, Herb Cream.

**Template back side** (second page in Canva):
- Background: white (#FFFFFF) for print cost efficiency.
- Species 1 block (left half):
  - Common name: Playfair Display Bold 16pt, Herb Ink.
  - Latin binomial: Lato Italic 13pt, Herb Sage.
  - 5-row table (Lato Regular 11pt): Planting window | Sun/shade | Soil preference | Harvest timing | Conservation note (UpS status if applicable) | Contraindication flag if applicable.
- Species 2 block (right half): Same structure.
- Center divider: 1 px Herb Sage (#8A9E6E) vertical rule.
- Footer: FTC disclaimer in Lato Regular 8pt, Herb Ink at 50%. "For educational purposes only. Not medical advice."

## Deliverable 6: Instagram Carousel Template (5-slide format)

**Purpose**: Phase 3 educational carousel format for species deep-dives and bundle promotions. Each carousel is 5 slides.

**Dimension**: 1080 × 1080 px per slide (Instagram square).

**Slide 1 — Hook/Cover**:
- Background: Herb Brown (#6B4F35), full bleed.
- Central botanical illustration or photo (circular crop, 700 px diameter).
- Headline: Playfair Display Bold 40pt, Herb Cream. Max 2 lines.
- Swipe prompt: Lato Regular 14pt, Herb Sage. "Swipe to learn more →"

**Slide 2 — Identification**:
- Background: Herb Cream (#F5EFE0).
- Left column (480 px): Botanical photo of species.
- Right column (580 px): Species name (Playfair Display Bold 26pt, Herb Ink) + Latin binomial (Lato Italic 16pt, Herb Sage) + 3-bullet ID key (Lato Regular 14pt, Herb Ink). Conservation badge if applicable.

**Slide 3 — Cultivation Key**:
- Background: white (#FFFFFF).
- 4-icon grid (2×2). Each cell: Canva stock icon (sun, shovel, thermometer, calendar) + label (Lato Bold 13pt, Herb Ink) + value (Lato Regular 13pt, Herb Brown). Data: Sun requirement, Planting zone, Planting window, Harvest timing.

**Slide 4 — Traditional Use**:
- Background: Herb Cream (#F5EFE0).
- Pull quote: Playfair Display Italic 24pt, Herb Brown. 2–3 sentences from the guide's traditional use section. Quote marks in Herb Sage, oversized (80pt).
- Attribution line: Lato Regular 12pt, Herb Ink at 60%. "Traditional use context — educational only. See guide for sources."

**Slide 5 — CTA / Sourcing Note**:
- Background: Herb Brown (#6B4F35).
- Headline: Lato Bold 20pt, Herb Cream. "Prefer cultivated stock — here's why:"
- 2-sentence sourcing note: Lato Regular 15pt, Herb Cream at 90%.
- CTA: "Get the full guide — Link in bio" in Lato Bold 16pt, Herb Sage.
- Seedwarden logo: bottom center, Herb Cream, 100 × 40 px.
