## Section 2: 90-Day Milestone Checkpoints

### Checkpoint 1 — July 31 (Month 1 Complete)

Pull these numbers and answer each question:

1. **Gate question — Revenue**: Gross medicinal herbs revenue above $400? If yes, continue full Phase 3 execution. If no, investigate which channel is underperforming (Etsy SEO? Email list size? Social traffic?).
2. **Gate question — Email list**: `herbalist` tag count above 75? If below 50, add 2 additional Pinterest pins per week pointing to Lead Magnet A, and increase Instagram posts to 3/week.
3. **Gate question — Conversion**: Women's Health bundle conversion rate above 1.5%? If below 1%, run the Etsy listing A/B test (see PHASE_3_LAUNCH_READINESS.md Section 5.3).
4. **Decision point**: Begin preparing the Respiratory Health Herbs Guide content (October 2026 launch) if Month 1 metrics are at or above target. If below target, delay Respiratory content prep by 4 weeks and focus on optimizing Women's Health bundle first.

---

### Checkpoint 2 — October 1 (Month 3 Complete)

1. **Gate question — Revenue**: Gross medicinal herbs revenue above $1,500? This is the Phase 3 Medicinal Herbs pivot trigger threshold from phase-3-profitability-model.csv (Conservative scenario projects $2,860 in Month 3). Falling below $1,500 requires review of the profitability model assumptions.
2. **Gate question — Practitioner attach rate**: Above 5%? If not, this affects the long-term revenue model significantly (practitioner bulk is the mechanism behind the $4,000–$6,000/month projection).
3. **Gate question — Respiratory bundle**: Is October 2026 launch on track? Have guide content, cover design, and Etsy listing copy been completed?
4. **Gate question — Review count**: At least 5 reviews on the Women's Health bundle? Reviews are the primary driver of Etsy organic traffic growth. If below 5 reviews at Month 3, send a review request broadcast email to all Women's Health buyers.

---

### Checkpoint 3 — December 31 (Month 6)

1. **Revenue target**: Total medicinal herbs gross revenue above $3,000/month (December). This is the Month 6 success metric from phase-3-medicinal-herbs-strategy.md Part 7.
2. **Bundle live count**: At least 2 bundles live (Women's Health + Respiratory; ideally 3 if Immunity launches in November on schedule).
3. **Practitioner revenue**: Practitioner bulk generating at least $500/month. If not, assess whether direct outreach to ND/NP organizations has begun.
4. **12-month trajectory**: Is the medicinal herbs channel on a trajectory toward 20%+ of total Seedwarden revenue by Month 12 (April 2027)? If not, assess: which bundle has the highest conversion rate and double down on its social and email content.

## Section 3: Analytics Spreadsheet Build Spec

**File to create**: `phase-3-analytics-dashboard.csv` in `projects/seedwarden/analytics/`

**Sheet structure** (tabs):

**Tab 1 — Weekly KPI Log**:
Columns: `week_ending_date`, `gross_revenue_MH`, `bundle_units_WH`, `bundle_units_RH`, `bundle_units_IM`, `bundle_units_SN`, `bundle_units_DS`, `practitioner_license_units`, `etsy_listing_views`, `etsy_conversion_rate`, `kit_herbalist_tag_count`, `new_subscribers_this_week`, `email1_open_rate`, `email5_click_rate`, `instagram_saves_avg`, `pinterest_monthly_views`, `pinterest_link_clicks`

**Tab 2 — LTV Tracker (Herbalist Cohort)**:
Extension of `phase-2-ltv-tracker-phase1-baseline.csv`. Add rows for herbalist cohort buyers.
Columns: `buyer_id`, `cohort` (value: Herbalist), `first_product`, `first_purchase_date`, `practitioner_license_purchased` (Y/N + date), `white_label_inquiry` (Y/N + date), `ltv_30d`, `ltv_60d`, `ltv_90d`, `ltv_12mo`, `email_funnel_stage`

**Tab 3 — Kit Email Performance**:
Columns: `email_number`, `email_name`, `send_date_or_delay`, `subscribers_sent`, `open_rate`, `click_rate`, `unsubscribes`, `alert_flag` (formula: open_rate < threshold → "REVIEW")

Formula for alert column:
- Email 1: =IF(C2<0.40,"REVIEW EMAIL 1","OK")
- Email 5: =IF(C6<0.25,"REVIEW EMAIL 5","OK")
- All others: =IF(C_row<0.18,"REVIEW","OK")

**Tab 4 — Etsy Listing Performance**:
Columns: `listing_id`, `listing_name`, `launch_date`, `weekly_views`, `weekly_orders`, `conversion_rate` (formula: orders/views), `review_count`, `avg_star_rating`, `revenue_this_week`, `cumulative_revenue`

**Tab 5 — B2B Outreach Log**:
Columns: `outreach_date`, `organization`, `contact_name`, `email_sent`, `response_received` (Y/N), `response_date`, `outcome` (Partnership / Declined / No Response / Follow-up pending), `notes`

## Section 4: Success Metrics — Final Definitions

These are the binding success criteria for Phase 3. All metrics are measurable from existing data sources.

### 90-Day Success Criteria (October 1, 2026)

- Total Phase 3 medicinal herbs gross revenue: above $1,500 (minimum), $2,500 (target)
- Women's Health bundle Etsy conversion rate: above 1.5%
- Women's Health bundle review count: 5 or more, average 4.8 stars or higher
- Kit `herbalist` tag count: above 200 subscribers
- Practitioner license sales: at least 3 units (confirms B2B revenue path is open)
- Email 1 open rate: above 40% (sustained through Month 3)
- Email 5 click rate: above 8% (confirms offer-message fit)

### 6-Month Success Criteria (December 31, 2026)

- Monthly gross revenue from medicinal herbs: above $3,000
- At least 2 bundles live on Etsy (Women's Health + Respiratory minimum; 3 preferred)
- Practitioner license monthly revenue: above $500
- Email list `herbalist` segment: above 500 subscribers
- Repeat purchase rate (herbalist cohort): at least 15% have purchased 2+ products
- At least 1 B2B partnership inquiry received (AHG, school, ND org)

### 12-Month Success Criteria (April 30, 2027)

- Monthly gross revenue from medicinal herbs: above $4,000 (minimum), $6,000 (target)
- All 5 bundles live on Etsy
- Full library bundle live ($72 — after all 5 bundles have at least 1 review each)
- Practitioner license monthly revenue: above $1,600 (3+ units/month at $130+)
- At least 1 active white-label store partnership
- Medicinal herbs channel representing 20%+ of total Seedwarden gross revenue
- Email list `herbalist` segment: above 1,000 subscribers (Phase 4 gate condition)

## Section 6: Contingency Triggers

| Scenario | Trigger Condition | Response |
|---|---|---|
| Slow start — Etsy | Women's Health bundle below 10 unit sales by July 31 | Review listing SEO, check image slots all filled, run 7-day 15% price test ($19 vs. $22) |
| Email underperformance | Email 5 click rate below 5% at Month 2 | Test alternate Email 5 subject line, test price anchor ($22 vs. $19 launch price) |
| Low review count | Fewer than 3 reviews by October 1 | Activate review request email (Section 5). Consider offering $2 Etsy coupon to previous buyers who leave honest reviews (FTC-compliant practice). |
| No practitioner sales | Zero practitioner license sales by Month 2 | Add practitioner framing to Instagram bio. Run targeted Email 8 early (Day 45 instead of Day 60). Begin ND/NP association outreach. |
| Social traffic not converting | Pinterest drives views but zero Kit sign-ups after 4 weeks | Change pin destination from lead magnet landing page to direct Etsy listing (test which converts better). |
| Phase 3 launch gate fails | Phase 2 forager cohort below 20% at August 1 check | Continue Phase 3 preparation but delay bundle launches to September 2026. Zero-cost to delay. |
