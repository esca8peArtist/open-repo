## ROLE 1: Botanical Product Photographer (Flat-Lay and Lifestyle)

**Upwork job title**: Botanical Product Photographer — Medicinal Herb Guides (Flat-Lay, Lifestyle, 3-Session Contract)

**Project type**: Fixed-price, one-time contract
**Contract length**: 6 weeks (July 1 – August 10, 2026)
**Budget**: $[RATE_HERE] for full project (see rate range notes at end of document)
**Estimated hours**: 40–50 hours total (3 photo sessions + editing + delivery)

**Project overview**

Seedwarden publishes digital medicinal herb guides sold on Etsy. We are producing five bundles (Respiratory Health, Immunity Support, Digestive Support, Sleep Support, Women's Health) launching July through August 2026. We need high-quality botanical product photography for three core bundles: Respiratory Health, Immunity Support, and Digestive Support.

**Your deliverables**

- 5 edited, print-ready images per bundle (15 images total across 3 bundles)
- Each set of 5 images includes: (1) top-down flat-lay with guide mockup and herbs, (2) 45-degree flat-lay with props, (3) lifestyle close-up of herb specimens, (4) styled composition showing preparation context (tea, tincture, mortar), (5) detail shot of guide cover text with herbs
- Resolution: 2000×2000px minimum, sRGB color space, JPG format
- Delivery: 1 set of 5 images per week for 3 consecutive weeks (Session 1 due July 5, Session 2 due July 12, Session 3 due July 24)
- File naming: [bundle-slug]-[type]-[sequence].jpg (e.g., respiratory-flatlay-01.jpg, immunity-lifestyle-02.jpg)

**What we provide**

- Dried herb specimens for each bundle (Echinacea, Elderberry, Ginger, Turmeric, Astragalus, Marshmallow, Peppermint, and 5–8 others per session)
- Guide mockup PDFs showing cover layout and text (for use as props in flat-lay)
- Style reference board (3–5 images representing the warm, natural aesthetic we're targeting)
- Prop suggestions list (tea infuser, mortar and pestle, small glass bottles, natural wood surfaces, linen cloth)
- Ship-to address if you need herbs delivered (or you may source locally from a natural food store)

**Style requirements**

- Warm, diffuse natural light (no harsh flash, no cold artificial light)
- Herb specimens clearly identifiable (not wilted, not blurry, not obscured by props)
- Guide cover text legible in at least 2 of the 5 images per set
- Props enhance the composition without overwhelming the subject
- Consistent white balance and tone across all 15 images (the final bundle must look cohesive)
- Natural surfaces preferred: wood, stone, linen, slate, ceramic (no glossy or plastic surfaces)

**Technical specifications**

- Camera: 18MP minimum (smartphone acceptable if you have a strong flat-lay portfolio demonstrating quality)
- Lens: 35mm–50mm macro or portrait focal length preferred
- Editing: Light adjustment, color correction, minor cleanup only (no heavy retouching or compositing)
- Delivery format: High-resolution JPG (required) and RAW or Lightroom catalog (optional but appreciated)
- Color space: Must be sRGB (not Adobe RGB or ProPhoto)

**Required qualifications**

- Portfolio showing 3 or more flat-lay or lifestyle product photography projects (botanical, food, cosmetic, or natural products)
- Demonstrated understanding of color consistency and white balance across a set
- Familiarity with file naming conventions and resolution requirements for print-quality digital products
- Availability to start July 1 and deliver on July 5, July 12, and July 24 (firm dates — no exceptions)

**Payment schedule**

- Milestone 1 (25%): On contract signature (by July 1)
- Milestone 2 (25%): On Session 1 approval (July 5 delivery)
- Milestone 3 (25%): On Session 2 approval (July 12 delivery)
- Milestone 4 (25%): On Session 3 final handoff approval (July 24 delivery)

**Work-for-hire notice**

This is a work-for-hire contract. All deliverables are owned by Seedwarden upon delivery and final payment. Seedwarden may use images commercially (Etsy listings, email marketing, social media, future product materials) without additional attribution or licensing fees. You retain no ongoing rights to the images after final payment is received.

## ROLE 2: Wellness Content Writer — Medicinal Herb Educational Guides (Writer 1)

**Upwork job title**: Wellness Content Writer — Medicinal Herb Educational Guides (3 Bundles, FTC-Compliant)

**Project type**: Fixed-price, one-time contract
**Contract length**: 6 weeks (July 1 – August 10, 2026)
**Budget**: $[RATE_HERE] for full project
**Estimated hours**: 80–100 hours

**Project overview**

Seedwarden publishes digital medicinal herb guides on Etsy, targeting home herbalists and wellness-curious consumers. We need an experienced wellness writer to author three comprehensive educational bundles: Respiratory Health, Immunity Support, and Digestive Support. This is evidence-based educational content for a general audience, not marketing copy and not clinical documentation.

**Your deliverables**

- Three manuscripts, each 3,600–3,800 words, structured as species-by-species educational guides
  - Respiratory Health (3,600 words): 7–8 species including Echinacea, Ginger, Licorice Root, Marshmallow Root, Thyme, Elecampane, Mullein, Coltsfoot
  - Immunity Support (3,800 words): 8–9 species including Elderberry, Astragalus, Goldenseal, Shiitake, Reishi, Andrographis, Ginseng, Ashwagandha — plus mandatory CITES legal sidebar on Goldenseal (verbatim language provided)
  - Digestive Support (3,600 words): 7–8 species including Ginger, Turmeric, Fennel, Peppermint, Chamomile, Slippery Elm, Bitter Herbs overview, Probiotic context
- Delivery schedule: One manuscript per week (Respiratory by July 8, Immunity by July 15, Digestive by August 3)
- Format: Google Docs with tracked changes enabled for revision rounds
- Tone: Educational, accessible, herbalist-level depth (not academic journals, not marketing language)

**Content requirements**

- Each species entry: 350–450 words covering documented history, active compounds, primary traditional and modern uses, contraindications and drug interactions, and quality sourcing notes
- Latin binomials required on first mention of every species (e.g., Echinacea purpurea, not just "echinacea")
- Contraindication panels must cite primary source databases (NatMed Pro, MSK Integrative Medicine, or peer-reviewed equivalents)
- FTC compliance: All therapeutic claims must include evidence-tier framing ("Research suggests...", "Traditionally used for...", "May support..."). No unqualified claims ("prevents," "cures," "treats" without qualifier). You confirm FTC compliance at submission.
- Mandatory conservation sidebars: UpS At-Risk flags for Black Cohosh, Echinacea angustifolia, and any species on the UpS monitoring list
- Mandatory legal inserts:
  - Goldenseal CITES section in Immunity bundle (200 words minimum, verbatim CITES language will be provided)
  - Thyroid warning in Ashwagandha section (Immunity bundle)
  - Sourcing and sustainability guidance sidebar in all three bundles

**Style expectations**

- Accessible to readers with no prior herbal background; herbalist-level detail appropriate when clearly explained
- Warm, conversational tone — informative, not dry; encouraging, not hyperbolic
- Consistent formatting: species name in bold on first mention, contraindications in a clearly separated panel, citations in [Author, Year] or [Source name] format
- No placeholder text ([TBD], [INSERT], etc.) in any submitted manuscript

**Required qualifications**

- 3 or more published articles on herbal medicine, wellness, or natural health (link to published work required in application)
- Working knowledge of medicinal herb contraindications and drug interactions
- Familiarity with FTC compliance requirements for health and wellness content
- Comfortable with 2–3 revision rounds per manuscript incorporating editor and herbalist reviewer feedback
- Availability to start July 1 and deliver on the July 8, July 15, and August 3 deadlines

**Payment schedule**

- Milestone 1 (25%): On contract signature (by July 1)
- Milestone 2 (25%): On Respiratory draft approval (by July 10 if minor revisions needed)
- Milestone 3 (25%): On Immunity draft approval (by July 17)
- Milestone 4 (25%): On Digestive final draft plus all revisions complete (by August 10)

**Work-for-hire notice**

This is a work-for-hire contract. All content is owned by Seedwarden upon delivery and final payment, and may be published digitally (Etsy, website, social media, email sequences) without additional attribution unless separately negotiated in writing. You may not republish this content elsewhere without written consent.

## ROLE 3: Recipe and Preparation Guide Writer — Herbal Medicine (Writer 2)

**Upwork job title**: Recipe and Prep Guide Writer — Medicinal Herb Guides (Teas, Tinctures, Syrups, Dosage Guides)

**Project type**: Fixed-price, one-time contract
**Contract length**: 6 weeks (July 1 – August 10, 2026)
**Budget**: $[RATE_HERE] for full project
**Estimated hours**: 60–75 hours

**Project overview**

Seedwarden publishes medicinal herb guides on Etsy for home herbalists. We need a writer to create practical preparation guides — recipes, dosage instructions, step-by-step methods — for three bundles: Respiratory Health, Immunity Support, and Digestive Support. This is consumer-facing practical content (teas, tinctures, syrups, infusions) for readers who may be trying herbal preparations for the first time.

**Your deliverables**

- Three preparation guide sections, each 2,000–2,400 words
  - Respiratory bundle: 8–10 recipes (teas, steam inhalation, honey syrups, cough drops, salves, poultices)
  - Immunity bundle: 8–10 recipes (teas, tinctures, elder syrup, immune tonics, mushroom gummies, capsule preparation guides)
  - Digestive bundle: 8–10 recipes (digestive bitters, herbal teas, infused oils, fermented tonics, probiotic preparation, dosage guides)
- Delivery schedule: One section per week (Respiratory by July 8, Immunity by July 15, Digestive by August 3)
- Format: Google Docs with template structure applied (ingredients list, method steps, yield, storage, dosage, safety note)

**Content requirements per recipe**

- Ingredients list: Both metric (grams, ml) and US standard (tablespoons, cups, ounces, teaspoons)
- Method: Numbered steps clear enough for someone who has never made a herbal preparation
- Preparation metadata: Prep time, total time, yield (cups, doses, or pieces), difficulty level (beginner / intermediate)
- Dosage guidance: Adults (standard dose), children 6+ (age-appropriate reduction), pregnancy and breastfeeding (note if contraindicated or if adjustments apply)
- Storage and shelf-life: Realistic timeframes (herbal tea: 24 hours; tincture: 3–5 years; syrup: 6 months refrigerated; salve: 12 months)
- Equipment alternatives: Acknowledge both specialized equipment (Instant Pot, dehydrator, French press) and common-kitchen alternatives (pot, jar, fine-mesh strainer)
- Safety disclaimer on every recipe: FTC-compliant language ("This preparation is intended to support general wellness and is not a substitute for professional medical advice.")
- Photography note: Brief description of the finished product's appearance (color, texture, consistency) to guide the photography partner

**Style expectations**

- Clear, jargon-free language — define terms like "infusion," "maceration," "decoction," and "macerate" on first use in each bundle
- Encouraging and accessible tone appropriate for first-time herbalists
- Consistent formatting across all recipes: bullet-point ingredients, numbered method, formatted metadata block
- No unqualified health claims in recipe introductions or headings

**Required qualifications**

- 3 or more published recipes or preparation guides (herbal medicine, food, or natural products) — link in application
- Comfortable with herbal dosage ranges, food safety, and preparation methods
- Experience writing step-by-step instructions for non-expert audiences
- Availability July 1 – August 10

**Payment schedule**

- Milestone 1 (25%): On contract signature (by July 1)
- Milestone 2 (25%): On Respiratory recipes approval (by July 10)
- Milestone 3 (25%): On Immunity recipes approval (by July 17)
- Milestone 4 (25%): On Digestive recipes plus all revisions complete (by August 10)

**Work-for-hire notice**

This is a work-for-hire contract. All content is owned by Seedwarden upon delivery and final payment and may be used commercially without additional attribution. No republishing without written consent.
