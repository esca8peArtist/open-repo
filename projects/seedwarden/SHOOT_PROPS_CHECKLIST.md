## Sourcing Run Plan (May 5-9)

Combine into one trip — most items come from the same two store types:

**Stop 1 — Garden center** (one trip): terracotta pot, trowel, gardening gloves,
grow tray, potted herb, any seeds needed for germination tray.

**Stop 2 — Grocery store** (can be combined with routine shopping): fresh herb sprig
(buy morning of shoot), hot peppers (morning of May 11), garlic, red food coloring if
needed, mason jars if not on hand.

**Stop 3 — Fabric or craft store** (if linen backdrop needed): 1 yard dark linen from
JoAnn or Hobby Lobby. Can be skipped if a dark cloth is already on hand.

**Online (if needed)**: Kraft seed envelopes are faster to order than find locally — a
10-pack from Amazon ships in 1-2 days. Order by May 7 at the latest for delivery before
May 10.
