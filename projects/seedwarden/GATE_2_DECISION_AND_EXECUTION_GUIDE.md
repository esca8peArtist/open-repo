## Decision Summary

**Decision**: Start the 30-day free trial of Canva Pro on May 19.

**Why this decision**:
- The Brand Kit color limit is the decisive constraint
- Seedwarden's Brand Kit requires 10 colors (6 brand + 4 zone bands)
- **Free tier**: Limited to 1 kit with 3 colors max → requires manual hex-code entry for every design element (inefficient, error-prone)
- **Pro tier**: Unlimited colors per kit + 141M premium assets + Magic Resize → zone cards, pins, carousels all producible at speed
- **Cost**: $15/month ($0 during trial)
- **Trial length**: 30 days (no charge if cancelled anytime before day 30)
- **Zone card deadline**: May 25 (before any payment required)
- **Decision point**: May 24 evening (review production quality, decide to continue or cancel before day 30)

**Risk level**: Zero (trial is free, cancellable anytime). The trial window covers all May 24-25 zone card production with 5+ days of buffer before payment is required.

**Location**: canva.com (web browser)

### Pre-Execution Checklist

- [ ] Have your email `wanka95@gmail.com` accessible

### Step 1: Activate Canva Pro (Free Trial)
1. Go to [canva.com/canva-pro](https://www.canva.com/canva-pro/)
2. Click "Start free trial"
3. Log in with email `wanka95@gmail.com` (or create account if first time)
4. Add a credit card or PayPal (REQUIRED to activate trial, but you will NOT be charged if cancelled before day 30)
5. Confirm trial activation — Canva will show "Trial active until [date 30 days from today]"

### Step 2: Create Brand Kit
1. In the Canva sidebar, click "Brand Hub"
2. Click "Create a Brand Kit"
3. Name it: `Seedwarden`
4. Click "Create"

### Step 3: Add 6 Brand Colors
In the Brand Kit, click "Add a color" and enter each hex code exactly:

| Color Name | Hex Code |
|---|---|
| Deep Forest Green | #143b28 |
| Deep Ink Green | #1A3A2A |
| Warm Cream | #F5EDD6 |
| Parchment | #EDE0C4 |
| Sage | #8FA882 |
| Burnt Sienna | #A0522D |

**Tips**:
- Canva will show a preview circle next to each hex code
- Click "Save" after each color
- If you paste a hex code and it doesn't recognize it, try removing the `#` and typing it fresh

### Step 4: Add 4 Zone Band Colors (Optional — can skip if you prefer minimal Brand Kit)
These are used only in zone cards. If you want them in the Kit for one-click access, add them:

| Zone Group | Hex Code | Zones |
|---|---|---|
| Cool band | #3D6B8A | Zones 3, 4 |
| Temperate band | #2D5016 | Zones 5, 6 |
| Warm band | #C9943A | Zones 7, 8 |
| Hot band | #A0522D | Zones 9, 10 |

**Note**: The "Hot band" color (#A0522D) is the same as "Burnt Sienna" (already added in Step 3), so when you add it here Canva will show it as a duplicate. That's fine — it's correct.

### Step 5: Add 3 Fonts
Click "Add a font" and search by name (all are free in Canva):

| Role | Font Name |
|---|---|
| Heading | Playfair Display |
| Body | Lato |
| Accent | Cormorant Garamond |

**If Lato is unavailable**: Substitute Source Sans 3 (same role, visually identical)

### Step 6: Upload Logo
1. In Brand Kit > **Logos** section, click "Upload a logo"
2. Select `seedwarden_logo_1.png` from your computer
3. Canva stores it for use in all designs (no re-upload needed)

### Step 7: Save Brand Kit
1. Click "Save" or "Publish" (button may vary)
2. In the Brand Kit view, click the "Share" button
3. Copy the share link (looks like `canva.com/design/DADxxxxxx/share`)
4. **Paste this link into the next section** so it's recorded

## Canva Trial Management

**Important reminders:**

1. **No payment during trial**: Your credit card will NOT be charged during the 30-day trial, even if you use Pro features every day.

2. **Cancel anytime**: If you decide Pro isn't worth $15/month after seeing the zone cards, go to Canva > Settings > Billing > Cancel Plan. This can be done anytime before day 30, including May 24 evening after zone cards are exported.

3. **Access after cancellation**: Even if you cancel on day 1, you keep Pro access for the full 30 days. So if you cancel May 21, you can still use all Pro features through June 20.

4. **Continue at day 30**: If zone cards look great and you want to continue for Phase 2 social media production (Pinterest pins, Instagram carousels), the subscription continues automatically. You can switch to annual billing ($10/month) to save $60/year once you're confident in the platform.
