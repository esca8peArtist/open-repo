CTA: "Link in bio — every listing now has 5 images." (OR if shop not live: 
"Link in bio to get notified when we open — a community of people growing their own food.")

**Reel specifications**:
- Duration: 30–45 seconds
- Format: 1080×1920px (vertical)
- Export: MP4 H.264 codec, 60 FPS
- Platform: Upload to Instagram, cross-post natively to TikTok (separate upload, not linked)

**Algorithm context**: 
Per `track-b-independent-launch-roadmap.md` Part 1, Condition 5:
- Origin story posts with emotional resonance drive higher completion rates (good signal)
- Higher completion rate = algorithm recommends account more broadly
- Product promotion posts typically perform poorly on zero-follower accounts (trust context missing)
- This is not aesthetic preference — it is platform mechanics

- [ ] Same video, upload separately to TikTok (different platform, different algorithm)

**Success criteria**:

### Risk 2: Phase 1 Etsy Shop Remains Blocked
**Status as of 2026-04-29**: Account verification pending, no ETA

**If** shop not live by Day 14 from Track B launch:
- Maintain Kit email capture as link-in-bio (✅ already planned for Option 3b)
- Per `track-b-independent-launch-roadmap.md` Part 4, this has a ~3-week credibility window before "opening soon" becomes a concern

**If** shop not live by Day 21:
- Evaluate Gumroad (gumroad.com) or Payhip (payhip.com) as immediate alternative
- Both support digital product sales with no verification hold
- Setup time: 15 minutes
- **Trigger**: Signal 1 in `track-b-independent-launch-roadmap.md` Part 5 (500+ followers before shop live)

### Risk 3: Lifestyle Photography Unavailable for Week 3+ Content
**Current status**: Week 1–2 content uses existing 2400×2400px mockup images ✅ sufficient

**If** lifestyle photos not ready by Week 3:
- Continue using mockup images through Week 4 (per concurrent-track-execution-plan.md Risk 2)
- Trigger full photography sprint when Etsy listing reaches 50+ views (conversion signal)
- Mockup-based content performs slightly lower in saves but identical in reach

### Risk 4: Limited Time Availability Mid-Execution
**If** time becomes constrained after launch:
- **Priority 1**: Keep Etsy updated (Track A) — zero revenue without this
- **Priority 2**: Maintain Pinterest batch pins (Track B) — evergreen traffic, no daily active management required after batching
- **Priority 3**: Pause Instagram/TikTok daily Reels (highest time cost, lowest evergreen ROI)
- **Trigger**: If social posting drops below 1 pin per week for 2+ consecutive weeks, shift to Pinterest-only mode
- **Escalation**: If both tracks stall for 2+ weeks, trigger contingency review and reset decisions
