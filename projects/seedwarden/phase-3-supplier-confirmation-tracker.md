## 7. Goldenseal Decision Tree (Hard Deadline: June 8)

Goldenseal is the single highest-stakes sourcing decision in Phase 3. CITES Appendix II designation limits wild trade; only cultivated stock is appropriate for guide citation. Follow this tree in order.

**Step 1 (May 20–22)**: Email Strictly Medicinal Seeds and Prairie Moon Nursery. Ask: (1) Do you have cultivated Goldenseal rhizome divisions available for June shipping? (2) What is your current lead time? (3) Can plants ordered today arrive by July 13?

**Step 2 (May 22–25)**: Evaluate responses.
- If either supplier confirms availability with July 13 delivery: place order by May 25. Log tracking number in the order status table above.
- If neither supplier can confirm July 13 delivery: move to Step 3.

**Step 3 (May 25 – June 7)**: Activate Wikimedia CC photo path.
- Email media@ncbg.unc.edu (NC Botanical Garden) for educational use photo permission (1–2 photos with full institutional attribution).
- Email media@mobot.org (Missouri Botanical Garden) for the same request.
- Search Wikimedia Commons: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Special:Search?search=Hydrastis+canadensis+habit — identify 3–5 CC-BY-SA images. Log URLs and license details in WORKLOG.md per project protocol.

**Step 4 (June 8 EOD)**: Hard deadline.
- If order confirmed with tracking: record in order status table. Live specimen path is active.
- If order not placed: send final confirmation email to NC Botanical Garden. Log in WORKLOG.md: "Goldenseal sourcing path: Wikimedia CC — decision confirmed June 8. No live specimen ordered." This decision has zero impact on writing or launch date.
