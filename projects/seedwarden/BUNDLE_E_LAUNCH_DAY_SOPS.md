# Bundle E — Launch Day Standard Operating Procedures

**Purpose**: Minute-by-minute operational guide for May 19 launch day. Open this document at 06:00 EST on May 19. Read each block in order. Do not skip ahead.

**Assumptions**:
- All guides are complete and QA-passed
- Etsy listing is in Draft status, ready to activate
- All 5 Kit emails are scheduled (Email 1 scheduled for 9:00 AM EST)
- All 7 social media posts are scheduled (Post 1 at 10:30 AM EST)
- Press release was distributed May 18 EOD

**Definition of success for this day**: Email 1 sent and confirmed delivered, Etsy listing is active and accessible to buyers, Post 1 is published and visible. Everything else is amplification.

## Launch Window (May 19, 08:00 - 20:00 EST)

### 08:00 EST — Activate Etsy Listing

This is the single most important action of launch day. Everything else is automated; this one is manual.

1. Log in to Etsy Shop Manager
2. Open Bundle E listing
3. Change status from "Draft" to "Active"
4. Click "Publish" or "Save and publish"
5. Etsy will confirm "Listing is now active"
6. Open the listing URL in an incognito browser window — confirm you see the full listing with purchase button, price $24.99, and "Instant download" notation
7. Record the exact activation time: ___:___ EST

**If Etsy throws an error on activation**:
- Most common error: "Your shop is not set up to accept payments." Go to Etsy Shop Manager > Finances > Payment account and confirm bank details are on file.
- Second most common: "This listing has no digital files." Re-upload the 5 PDFs, then try activating again.
- If listing activation fails and cannot be resolved within 30 minutes: Delay Kit Email 1 by 1 hour. Edit the Kit broadcast schedule to 10:00 AM EST instead of 9:00 AM. This gives you time to fix Etsy before the email drives traffic to a broken link.

### 09:00 EST — Email 1 Send (Automated)

Email 1 sends automatically via Kit at 9:00 AM EST if it was correctly scheduled.

**At 09:05 EST**:
- [ ] Open Kit Broadcasts — confirm Email 1 shows "Sending" or "Sent" status (not "Draft" or "Paused")
- [ ] If Email 1 shows "Draft": Click "Send" immediately. This sends to all subscribers now. A 5-minute delay from 9:00 to 9:05 does not matter.
- [ ] If Email 1 shows "Paused": Click "Resume" — it will send to remaining subscribers immediately.

**At 09:10 EST**:
- [ ] Check your own email inbox for a copy of Email 1 (you should be a subscriber on your own list)
- [ ] Confirm: subject line correct, body text complete, Etsy link is live and clickable, "From" name shows "Anya" or "Seedwarden"

### 10:00 EST — Pre-Social Check

- [ ] Confirm Etsy listing is still active (refresh the public URL)
- [ ] Check Kit > Broadcasts > Email 1: note current "Opened" count. Record: ___ opens at 10:00 AM.
- [ ] This is early data — open rates accumulate over 24 hours. A 10% open rate at 10 AM is not alarming.

### 10:30 EST — Social Post 1 Publishes (Automated)

Post 1 (Instagram Reels + TikTok) publishes automatically if correctly scheduled.

**At 10:35 EST**:
- [ ] Open Instagram — confirm Post 1 is visible on the profile grid
- [ ] Confirm "Link in bio" still points to Etsy listing
- [ ] Check TikTok — confirm Post 1 is visible (TikTok sometimes delays by 1-2 minutes)
- [ ] If either platform shows an error: manually post the content now. Caption and image are saved in `bundle-e-launch-backup/`. Manual posting takes 3-5 minutes per platform.

**At 10:40 EST — First Response Monitoring**:
- [ ] Check Instagram notifications for any comments on Post 1
- [ ] Check TikTok notifications
- [ ] Standard response protocol for comments: Thank the commenter, answer any specific questions briefly, direct questions about purchase to "link in bio." Do not promise features or content not in the guides.

### 12:00 EST — Midday Analytics Check

Record these numbers in your tracking spreadsheet (see Section: Metrics Tracking below):

| Metric | Target | Actual |
|---|---|---|
| Email 1 open rate | 25-30% | ___ |
| Email 1 click rate | 3-8% | ___ |
| Etsy listing views | 10-30 in first 3 hours | ___ |
| Instagram Post 1 reach | 50-200 (if account is <1,000 followers) | ___ |
| TikTok Post 1 views | 50-500 (depends on account) | ___ |
| Etsy conversions | 0-2 in first 3 hours (normal for early hours) | ___ |

**If email open rate is below 15% at noon**: This is below target but not a crisis. Email open rates typically peak 2-4 hours after send, not immediately. Check again at 2:00 PM before taking action.

**If Etsy listing has 0 views at noon**: 
1. Confirm the listing is still Active (not accidentally reverted to Draft)
2. Confirm the listing URL in Email 1 is correct
3. Check if the Email 1 click count in Kit matches (if 20 people clicked but Etsy shows 0 views, there may be a URL mismatch)

### 14:00 EST — Real-Time Response Playbook

If a media outlet contacts you between 08:00 and 20:00:

**Scenario A: A blogger or journalist requests an interview**
- Response time: Same day, within 2 hours
- Response: "Happy to talk — what's your timeline? I can do a 15-minute call anytime this week. Please email [your email] to schedule."
- Do not commit to a call on launch day itself unless you have clear time available
- Note the outlet and contact in your press response log

**Scenario B: A large account shares your post (organic amplification)**
- Action: Comment on their share ("Thank you for sharing — happy to answer any questions about the bundle")
- Do not message them asking for further promotion (this reads as spammy)
- If they have questions or want to feature the content, respond within 1 hour

**Scenario C: A negative comment or misinformation in social comments**
- Assess: Is it a factual error about plant safety? If yes, respond factually and publicly ("Actually, [correct information]. The guide covers this in the safety notes section.")
- Do not delete factual corrections or concerns — address them
- If a comment is harassing or clearly bad-faith: Hide the comment rather than deleting (hiding avoids triggering conflict)

## Post-Launch Window (May 19, 20:00 — May 22)

### Daily Monitoring Protocol (May 20-22)

**Each morning (08:00-08:30 EST)**:
1. Open Etsy Shop Manager — record listing views, favorites, and conversions (sales)
2. Open Kit Broadcasts — record Email 1 final open rate and click rate. Record any new sales tied to email click (Etsy may not directly attribute, but timing correlation is useful)
3. Open social scheduling tool — confirm remaining posts are still scheduled
4. Check email inbox for any press responses or customer questions
5. Record all metrics in the tracking spreadsheet

**Time required daily**: 30 minutes maximum. This is monitoring, not action.

### Email Follow-Up Sequence

Emails 2-5 are scheduled. Do not alter the schedule unless:
- An email contains a critical error (wrong link, wrong subject line)
- The campaign is significantly outperforming and you want to add a bonus send

Email 2 (May 22) is the Garlic Mustard species spotlight. It is timed to coincide with the peak of Garlic Mustard season — this timing is intentional and the email should send on schedule.

### Social Media Amplification (May 20-25)

Beyond the scheduled posts, do these manual actions once daily (10-15 minutes each):
1. Respond to all new comments on any post with at least a brief reply
2. If someone shares or reposts your content: thank them in comments
3. Search hashtags used in your posts (#invasivespecies, #foraging, #permaculture) and comment genuinely on 3-5 related posts — this signals algorithmic relevance, not spam
4. Story re-post: if Instagram is being used, post one Story per day (May 20-25) showing something from the bundle — a recipe, a species ID tip, a seasonal photo. Stories are low-effort and keep the algorithm active.

## Contingency Escalation

### If Day 1 Metrics Are Below Target

**Below-target signal**: Fewer than 5 Etsy sales and email click rate below 2% by May 20 EOD.

**Step 1 — Diagnose before acting**:
- Is the Etsy link in Email 1 working? (Test it yourself)
- Did Email 1 actually send? (Check Kit Broadcasts for open count — if opens are 0, the email failed to send)
- Is the Etsy listing still Active? (Check Shop Manager)

**Step 2 — If everything is working technically but conversions are low**:
- This is normal for Day 1 of a new product. Do not change pricing or messaging within the first 48 hours. Insufficient data.
- Continue with the scheduled email sequence — Emails 2 and 3 often drive more conversions than Email 1 because they provide more specific content

**Step 3 — If metrics remain below target after Email 3 (May 24)**:

| Signal | Response |
|---|---|
| Email open rate >20% but 0 sales | Etsy listing may have trust issues. Add 1-2 more photos or a short "About the Author" section. The content is reaching people but not converting. |
| Email open rate <15% | List may not be well-matched to this product. Check if subject line is in spam. Try resending Email 1 with subject line B. |
| Etsy views <20 total after 5 days | SEO or listing visibility issue. Add 2-3 more specific tags ("invasive plant foraging", "knotweed harvest guide"). |
| Social reach <100 after 5 posts | Platform algorithm issue or low follower count. Shift effort to manual engagement (see amplification protocol above). |

### Emergency Delay Protocol (If Guides Are Not Ready by May 19)

If for any reason guides are not upload-ready by May 19 morning:

1. Do not send Email 1 — change Kit Email 1 schedule to May 20, 9:00 AM
2. Post a brief Instagram Story: "Bundle E launches tomorrow — guides are in final QA. Pre-order opens at 9 AM EST tomorrow."
3. Fix whatever is blocking guide readiness (refer to BUNDLE_E_QA_CHECKLIST.md escalation protocol)
4. Proceed with May 20 launch — this is the acceptable delay scenario documented in BUNDLE_E_GUIDE_WRITING_SPRINT_PLAN.md Risk 3

A 1-day delay costs nothing in revenue. Launching with a broken link or incomplete guide costs credibility.

After the 5-day campaign window closes:

1. Do not delete or deactivate the Etsy listing — keep it live as an ongoing product
2. After May 25, remove the "Pre-Order Pricing" language from the Etsy description and Kit emails (or let the scheduled reminder email mention the price change)
3. Update the Etsy listing price from $24.99 to $28.00 on May 26 (per BUNDLE_E_PUBLICATION_PACKAGE.md campaign plan)
4. Export the Kit campaign analytics to a CSV for the Phase 2 retrospective
5. Document any guide errors discovered during customer feedback — fix and re-upload the corrected files before the Phase 2 May 30 launch

**Transition to Phase 2 (May 26-30)**:
- Bundle E becomes a permanent catalogue item
- Phase 2 launch on May 30 will drive additional traffic to Bundle E as a cross-sell alongside new Phase 2 bundles
- The email subscriber growth from May 19-25 feeds into the Phase 2 launch list
