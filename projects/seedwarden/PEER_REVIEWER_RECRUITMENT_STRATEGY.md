## Why Peer Review is Non-Negotiable

The practitioner market applies a binary credibility filter on first scan. A single sentence in the Etsy product description — "Reviewed by [Name], RH (AHG), [specialty]" — changes the conversion outcome for the $120–$150 practitioner tier. Without it, cold outreach to credentialed RH and ND buyers risks active negative signal ("Is this PLR content?"). With it, the friction collapses.

This is not a marketing nice-to-have. It is the minimum credibility requirement for the practitioner segment to convert. Per HERBALIST_NETWORK_ECOSYSTEM_MAPPING.md Section 7.3: "Pre-launch credibility action (required before June 22): One RH (AHG)-level practitioner review of the Women's Health bundle."

---

## Reviewer Tier Requirements

The contraindication depth target is 4–5 on a 1–5 scale, defined as:

| Depth Level | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | General cautions only ("consult your healthcare provider") |
| 2 | Pregnancy/lactation classification; major adverse effects |
| 3 | Drug-herb interaction classes named; population-specific cautions |
| 4 | Specific interactions with mechanism, named drug classes, clinical significance rating |
| 5 | Full pharmacological interaction review including CYP450 metabolism pathways, case report citations |

Target reviewers are those qualified to assess at Depth 4–5. This requires either:
- Registered Herbalist (AHG) credential with clinical practice focus
- Naturopathic Doctor (ND) with botanical medicine emphasis
- PhD in phytochemistry, pharmacology, or ethnobotany with clinical herbalism expertise

## Deliverable Requirements from Reviewer

The reviewer is asked to provide:

1. A written email confirmation (minimum one sentence) stating:
   - That they reviewed the guide for botanical nomenclature accuracy
   - That the contraindication content is clinically appropriate for practitioner use
   - Their name and credential as they wish it to appear in the listing

2. Any corrections or required changes flagged before June 21 (Seedwarden will make corrections within 24 hours of notification)

3. Optional: A 1–2 sentence pull-quote suitable for the Etsy listing and outreach emails

The reviewer is **not** asked to:
- Write new content
- Provide a formal literature review
- Make legal or liability representations
