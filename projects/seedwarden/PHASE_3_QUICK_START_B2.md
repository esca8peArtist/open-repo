## Contractor Contingency (If They Miss Deadline)

### Scenario: Contractor Delivers July 6 Instead of July 5

**Impact**: You lose 1 day of revision time.
**Recovery**: You can still deliver by July 9 if you:
- Skim July 6 manuscript for major errors (1 hour)
- Do quick fix-pass July 7 (2 hours)
- Upload to Etsy July 8 (no further revision)
- Launch July 9 (as planned)

**Total overhead**: +3 hours. Still manageable.

### Scenario: Contractor Delivers July 7 or Later

**Impact**: You lose all revision time. Zero buffer.
**Options**:
1. **Publish unrevised** (not recommended, risk quality)
2. **Push launch to July 12–13** (miss some launch window, but safer)
3. **You finish Immunity/Digestive yourself** (10 hours July 7–8, if possible)

**Prevention**: Contractor confirmation + daily check-ins. If they're behind by July 3, escalate immediately.
