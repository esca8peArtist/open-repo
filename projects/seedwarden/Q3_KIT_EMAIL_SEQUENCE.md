**The respiratory decoction (stronger, more effective):**

A decoction is a long simmer. Here's how:

1. Add 1 teaspoon of dried root (Elecampane, Licorice) or 2 teaspoons of dried leaf (Thyme, Coltsfoot) to a pot with 2 cups of cold water.
2. Bring to a simmer (not a rolling boil—gentle).
3. Simmer 20 minutes.
4. Strain. Drink 1 cup 2–3 times daily.

That 20-minute simmer releases the deep medicine from these plants.

- **Elecampane root**: Always decoct (20 min)

- **Coltsfoot leaf**: Can infuse (15 min) or add to decoction
- **Ginger rhizome**: Can infuse (10–15 min) or decoct (10 min)

1 teaspoon Elecampane root + 1 teaspoon Ginger rhizome + ½ teaspoon Licorice root = one dose.
