## Section 2: Primary Launch Audience

**Primary audience (highest-value, launch-day focus)**:

The Companion Planting newsletter audience and any existing Kit subscribers are the primary
launch audience. These are warm contacts who already know Seedwarden and have opted in.
The 12:00pm broadcast email reaches them first — before any social traffic. This is intentional.

**Audience size at launch (projected)**:
- Kit subscribers (pre-launch): 0–50 (depends on Zone Quick-Start Card pre-launch sign-ups
  from Gate 1–3 social bio traffic in May 13–29 window)
- Existing buyers from Phase 1 (47 customers): reachable by Etsy messaging if Kit list is thin
- Social followers at launch: 0–200 depending on Gate 1 timing and content posted before May 30

**Audience behavior by platform on launch day**:

Email subscribers (12:00pm broadcast): Warm audience. Already know the brand. Will click
through to Etsy listings. Target 35%+ open rate, 8%+ click rate. These are the buyers most
likely to purchase on launch day.

Instagram/TikTok (2:00pm posts): Cold-to-warm depending on whether Gate 1 was completed in
Week 1 or Week 3. If accounts are new, expect low organic reach (algorithm hasn't calibrated).
Primary goal on Day 1: follower additions and profile saves. A purchase from social on launch
day is a signal event, not an expectation.

Pinterest (3:30pm pins): Coldest audience, longest discovery cycle. Pinterest pin impressions
on Day 1 are low (2–24 hour indexing lag). Do not interpret Day 1 Pinterest data as a signal.
Pinterest performance is a 30-day metric.

Etsy organic (10:00am–12:00pm window): Highest-quality signal. Any buyer who finds the listing
through Etsy organic search before the email broadcast arrived without any push. Record these
buyers in WORKLOG.md — they represent real search demand.

## Section 4: Day 1 Success Metrics

**Philosophy**: Day 1 is about confirming the launch worked, not about hitting revenue targets.
Revenue is a Week 1 metric. Day 1 metrics confirm the infrastructure fired correctly and the
audience engaged.

### Must-Hit (Launch Confirmed Working)

| Metric | Target | Platform | If Below Target |
|---|---|---|---|
| Etsy listings live | All Phase 2 drafts published | Etsy Shop Manager | Publish any missed listings immediately |
| Kit broadcast delivered | >80% delivery rate | Kit > Broadcasts | Remove hard bounces; check spam filters |
| Kit broadcast sent on time | Before 12:30pm | Kit > Broadcasts | If not sent by 12:30pm: click "Send Now" immediately |
| Social posts live | Instagram + TikTok by 2:15pm; Pinterest by 3:45pm | Buffer | Post manually from `phase-2-social-content-calendar-60day.md` Day 30 |
| Zone card delivery test passing | Zone 5 test email receives PDF within 60 seconds | Kit / Google Drive | Run diagnosis: check Drive sharing permissions; check Kit Email 1 URL |

### Signal Metrics (Calibration, Not Pass/Fail)

| Metric | Strong Signal | Acceptable | Weak Signal | Action |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Etsy views (Day 1) | 50+ | 20–49 | <20 | <20: confirm listings are indexed by searching keywords in Etsy search |
| Etsy orders (Day 1) | 3+ | 1–2 | 0 | 0: normal — email cohort drives orders Days 1–3; check again at Day 3 |
| Kit open rate | >40% | 25–39% | <25% | <25%: review subject line; test a resend on Day 3 to non-openers |
| Kit click rate | >10% | 5–9% | <5% | <5%: review email body CTA language and Etsy listing URL |
| Instagram reach (Day 1 post) | >500 | 100–499 | <100 | <100 on zero-follower account: normal; check video completion rate |
| New Kit subscribers (Day 1) | 10+ | 3–9 | <3 | <3: bio links may not be updating; verify links in all 3 social bios |
| New followers across platforms | 20+ combined | 5–19 | <5 | <5: likely posting schedule issue; verify posts are live |

### Revenue Target (Week 1, Not Day 1)

| Timeframe | Conservative | Target | Stretch |
|---|---|---|---|
| Day 1 | $0–$75 | $75–$200 | $200+ |
| Day 3 | $100–$200 | $200–$400 | $400+ |
| Day 7 | $300–$500 | $500–$900 | $900–$1,200 |

Revenue targets from `PHASE_2_SOCIAL_GROWTH_STRATEGY.md` 30-day projection: $3,500–$4,500
for the first 30 days from Phase 2 social activation. Day 1 is approximately 5–10% of that.
