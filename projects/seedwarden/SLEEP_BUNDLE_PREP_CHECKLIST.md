Run this audit when Valerian and Passionflower sections arrive. Any item failing = send revision email immediately.

#### Valerian Section (800w target)
- [ ] "Second-year root harvest only" — planting expectation set explicitly

#### Passionflower Section (700w target)
- [ ] MAOI antidepressant interaction warning (highest-severity in bundle): "Do not combine with MAOI antidepressants" — drug class named
- [ ] Zone 6–10 perennial / Zone 4–5 annual cultivation distinction included

- [ ] Maypop fruit forager connection (Southeastern US wild edibles crossover) included

#### Lemon Balm Section (500w target)
- [ ] TSH binding inhibition warning: "Thyroid conditions or medication users: consult a qualified healthcare provider before therapeutic use"

#### Lavender Section — Condensed (500w target)
- [ ] Linalool and linalyl acetate research framing is FTC-compliant ("studied in laboratory and clinical settings for anxiolytic properties" — not "proven to treat anxiety")
- [ ] Aromatherapy preparation instructions specific to sleep: sachet, eye pillow, sleep spray (distinct from Women's Health topical salve)

### 2.3 FTC Compliance Audit

Run this audit after all sections received. Pass/fail on each item:

- [ ] No claims using "cures," "prevents," "treats," "heals" without evidence-tier framing
- [ ] All therapeutic references use: "traditionally used for," "may support," "has been studied for," "evidence suggests"
- [ ] Every health claim has a citation (NatMed Pro, MSK Integrative Medicine, PubMed, German Commission E, or equivalent)
- [ ] Contraindication section reads in clinical tone (not marketing tone)
- [ ] Preparation methods describe process only — no dosage claims
- [ ] Valerian sedative warning is unambiguous: "Do not combine with prescription sedatives, benzodiazepines, or anesthetics"
- [ ] Passionflower MAOI warning names drug class: "MAOI antidepressants (e.g., phenelzine, tranylcypromine)"
- [ ] Lemon Balm thyroid warning directs to practitioner: "consult a qualified healthcare provider before therapeutic use if you have thyroid conditions or take thyroid medications"
- [ ] Disclaimer on first page: "This guide is for educational purposes only. Not intended as medical advice. Consult a qualified healthcare provider before use."
