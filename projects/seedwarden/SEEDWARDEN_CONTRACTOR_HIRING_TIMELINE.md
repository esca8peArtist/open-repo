## Phase 2: Scoring (Day 1)

### Scoring Rubric — 4 Dimensions

Score every remaining applicant across 4 dimensions using the 5-point scale below. Calculate weighted overall score.

| Dimension | Weight | What to Assess |
|---|---|---|
| Portfolio Quality | 40% | Does prior work match the subject matter and quality standard required? Use role-specific audit checklists below. |
| Timeline Fit | 30% | Can they start July 1 and commit to all delivery dates? This is a hard requirement — score 1 if unavailable. |
| Communication Clarity | 15% | Response time, clarity and completeness of application, relevant questions asked. |
| Price Reasonableness | 15% | Does proposed rate fall within posted budget ±10%? See rate negotiation rules below. |

### 5-Point Scoring Scale

**Portfolio Quality:**

| Score | Criteria |
|---|---|
| 5 | Prior work directly matches subject matter and quality level (e.g., photographer with 5+ recent botanical flat-lay portfolios; writer with 5+ published herbal medicine articles using Latin binomials and contraindications) |
| 4 | Meets requirements; clear capability with minor subject-matter gaps |
| 3 | General capability but limited subject-matter match; acceptable with caveats |
| 2 | Significant gap between prior work and project requirements |
| 1 | Auto-reject criteria met (see role-specific checklist below) |

**Timeline Fit:**

| Score | Criteria |
|---|---|
| 5 | July 1 start confirmed; all delivery dates confirmed; no conflicts |
| 4 | July 1 start confirmed; minor uncertainty on one delivery date (resolvable in kickoff call) |
| 3 | July 1 start likely; one delivery date in question (moderate risk) |
| 2 | Unclear start date or confirmed conflict with a delivery date |
| 1 | Cannot confirm July 1 start or has confirmed conflict with multiple delivery dates |

**Communication Clarity:**

| Score | Criteria |
|---|---|
| 5 | Applied within 12 hours; asked 1–2 specific role-relevant questions; all links work |
| 4 | Applied within 24 hours; application complete and professional |
| 3 | Applied within 48 hours; application adequate but generic |
| 2 | Application incomplete (missing link or confirmation) or clarity issues |
| 1 | No response to outreach; obviously templated application with no role-specific content |

**Price Reasonableness:**

| Score | Criteria |
|---|---|
| 5 | Proposed rate at or below posted budget |
| 4 | Proposed rate 1–5% above posted budget |
| 3 | Proposed rate 6–10% above posted budget (borderline; negotiate before offer) |
| 2 | Proposed rate 11–15% above posted budget (decline or hard-negotiate) |
| 1 | Proposed rate more than 15% above posted budget |

### Overall Score Calculation

**Overall Score** = (Portfolio × 0.40) + (Timeline × 0.30) + (Communication × 0.15) + (Price × 0.15)

Maximum possible: 5.0

### Decision Matrix (GO / WATCH / NO-GO)

| Score | Decision | Label | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| 4.0–5.0 | GO | **Tier 1** | Extend offer within 4 hours of scoring. Meets all criteria. |
| 3.0–3.9 | WATCH | **Tier 2** | Conduct 10-minute screening call to resolve scoring gap. If resolved, extend offer. Hold as backup if Tier 1 available. |
| Below 3.0 | NO-GO | **Reject** | Do not extend offer. No follow-up needed. |

### Worked Scoring Examples

**Photographer — GO example:**
- Portfolio Quality: 4 (3 botanical flat-lay portfolios; professional resolution; minor prop style differences)
- Timeline Fit: 5 (July 1 confirmed; all 5 shoots confirmed)
- Communication Clarity: 4 (replied within 18 hours; complete application; asked about goldenseal prop sourcing)
- Price Reasonableness: 5 ($1,380 proposed against $1,400 budget)

Overall: (4 × 0.40) + (5 × 0.30) + (4 × 0.15) + (5 × 0.15) = 1.60 + 1.50 + 0.60 + 0.75 = **4.45 — GO (Tier 1)**

**Writer — NO-GO example:**
- Portfolio Quality: 3 (3 wellness articles; basic contraindication coverage; no articles above 2,000 words)
- Timeline Fit: 2 (says "likely available" but no confirmation)
- Communication Clarity: 4 (clean application; asked reasonable question about word count)
- Price Reasonableness: 2 (proposed $1,300 against $950 budget — 37% over)

Overall: (3 × 0.40) + (2 × 0.30) + (4 × 0.15) + (2 × 0.15) = 1.20 + 0.60 + 0.60 + 0.30 = **2.70 — NO-GO (Reject)**

## Phase 3: Portfolio Audit Checklists

Run on every Tier 1 candidate before sending offer. Require minimum 5 portfolio samples for all roles.

### Photographer (5 recent projects)

- [ ] At least 3 samples show botanical, food, or natural-product flat-lay (not portraiture or events)
- [ ] Resolution appears professional (ask for source dimensions if unclear from display quality)
- [ ] White balance is consistent within each project set — no visible color drift between frames in a series
- [ ] Composition and props fit Seedwarden's warm, accessible, naturalist aesthetic
- [ ] No harsh shadows across central subjects in botanical close-ups

**Auto-reject**: Work is exclusively portraiture, architecture, or event photography with zero product or flat-lay samples.

### Writer #1 — Women's Health & Immunity (5 published articles)

- [ ] At least 3 articles cover women's health, herbal medicine, or integrative wellness
- [ ] At least 2 articles use Latin binomials for plant species mentioned
- [ ] At least 1 article addresses contraindications or drug interactions for an herb or supplement
- [ ] FTC-compliant language visible: "may support," "research suggests," "traditionally used" — not "cures" or "prevents" without evidence qualifier
- [ ] At least 1 article exceeds 2,000 words (demonstrates capacity for 3,600-word manuscript)

**Auto-reject**: Articles make unqualified health claims without evidence-tier framing, or articles cite no sources.

### Writer #2 — Respiratory & Sleep (5 published articles)

- [ ] At least 3 articles cover herbal medicine, natural health, or evidence-based wellness
- [ ] At least 1 article addresses drug interactions or safety for an herb
- [ ] Uses primary sources or named databases (NatMed Pro, Cochrane, Examine.com, or equivalent)
- [ ] Tone is educational and accessible (not academic-only or marketing-heavy)
- [ ] At least 1 article exceeds 2,500 words

**Auto-reject**: Blog content only with no evidence citations; articles recommending supplements as replacements for medication.

### Writer #3 — Digestive & Practitioner (5 published articles)

- [ ] At least 3 articles cover clinical nutrition, herbal medicine, or integrative health
- [ ] At least 1 article uses primary database citations (named database or journal)
- [ ] Demonstrates ability to write in both clinical format (table-based) and accessible educational format
- [ ] At least 1 article covers drug interactions or safety for food or supplement
- [ ] At least 1 article exceeds 2,000 words

**Auto-reject**: Content exclusively consumer-facing blog; no clinical or research-oriented writing.

### Herbalist Consultant (credential + 3 professional references or publications)

- [ ] Credential verified: AHG Professional Member via ahg.memberclicks.net (public directory), NAHA equivalent, or documentation of 5+ years clinical practice
- [ ] At least 3 professional projects or publications listed (monographs, consulting references, continuing education delivery, or peer-reviewed publications)
- [ ] Drug interaction knowledge demonstrated in profile (mentions NatMed Pro, Micromedex, MSK, or clinical databases)
- [ ] CITES or UpS familiarity visible: membership, At-Risk list language, or conservation language in published work

**Auto-reject**: Self-described "herbalist" with no verifiable credential and no professional project history.

### Logistics Coordinator (3–5 professional references or projects)

- [ ] At least 2 projects show email marketing platform management (Kit, ConvertKit, Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, or equivalent)
- [ ] At least 1 project shows Etsy or e-commerce coordination (uploading listings, managing digital product files)
- [ ] Evidence of managing concurrent timelines (multi-contractor coordination or VA with multiple simultaneous clients)
- [ ] Communication samples show professional, concise tone
- [ ] Stated availability matches Monday morning ET window for check-in sends

**Auto-reject**: No email marketing platform experience, or only buyer-side Etsy experience.

## Phase 5: Trial Task (Optional — Day 2–4)

For any role where the portfolio leaves ambiguity and the candidate is borderline Tier 1/2, issue a paid trial task.

**Trial task budget**: $50–$100 per role
**Trial task turnaround**: 2–3 days maximum (must complete before offer window closes)

**Trial tasks by role:**

- Photographer: Deliver 1 flat-lay image of any herb or botanical subject using the specified resolution, file naming, and style direction. This is a paid test; you own the result regardless of outcome.
- Writer #1/#2/#3: Write a 350-word species entry for echinacea (*Echinacea purpurea*) following the content format described in the job posting (history, constituents, uses, contraindications, Latin binomials). FTC compliance required.
- Herbalist: Provide a 200-word review note for a sample passage (provided); flag any accuracy issues, cite at least one primary source.
- Coordinator: Draft a Monday check-in email for a hypothetical contractor using the format: "Expected last week: [X]. Delivered: [Y]. Due this week: [Z]. Blockers: [none/describe]."

Candidates who do not complete the trial task within 3 days are deprioritized. The trial task result replaces the Portfolio Quality score if it is a clearer signal.

## Phase 6: Offers and Contract Signature (Day 2–6)

### Offer Process

1. Send offer email to all Tier 1 candidates simultaneously (Day 2 target, Day 3 latest)
2. Set 48-hour offer expiry: state explicitly in the email ("This offer expires [DATE] at [TIME] ET")
3. Mid-window check (24 hours after send): identify any unacknowledged offers; send one follow-up ("Just checking in — let me know if you have questions.")
4. Offer deadline: Tier 1 candidates who have not replied move to Tier 2 queue
5. Send Tier 2 offers immediately upon Tier 1 rejection (new 48-hour window)
6. Selection complete when all 6 roles have confirmed acceptances

### Rate Negotiation Rules

- Rates within posted budget: accept immediately
- Rates 1–10% above posted budget: counter with posted budget; accept if candidate holds at ≤10%
- Rates 11–15% above posted budget: counter with original budget and a scope-reduction option (e.g., reduce manuscript word count by 200 words); accept counter if candidate meets within 10%
- Rates more than 15% above posted budget: decline and move to Tier 2

### Contract Signature Sequence

1. Generate contract using Google Docs template (fields: name, role, rate, deliverables, payment schedule, key dates, work-for-hire clause)
2. Send via DocuSign or HelloSign on Day 7 (July 1 in ideal timeline)
3. Signature deadline: same day sent (target: signed within 4 hours)
4. Upon signature: send 25% deposit immediately via agreed payment method (Venmo, PayPal, Zelle, or ACH)
5. Log hire date, contract rate, and deposit sent date in SEEDWARDEN_CONTRACTOR_TRACKING_SHEET_TEMPLATE.csv

## Contingency Triggers

### If fewer than 3 qualified applicants per role

Activate within 48 hours of posting close:

| Role | Contingency Action |
|---|---|
| Photographer | Re-post on Thumbtack in addition to Upwork; expand geography (accept US-remote with prop shipping); raise posted budget by 10% |
| Writers | Split scope: offer 1-bundle contracts to 2 separate writers instead of 2-bundle contract to 1; contact prior-session writers (Rebecca Lexa, Arianna Collins from PHASE_3_CONTRACTOR_SELECTION_SCORECARD.md) |
| Herbalist | Defer to 1-week post-hire (herbalist review begins after first manuscripts arrive; not blocking Day 1); expand to NAHA and university extension network |
| Coordinator | Absorb internally for Week 1 only (manual check-ins); re-post with hourly rate and reduced scope |

### If Tier 1 rejects and Tier 2 also rejects

1. Reduce scope: for writers, offer 1 manuscript instead of 2 (adjust rate to 55% of original)
2. Extend timeline: for roles not on critical path, allow 3-day extension on first delivery
3. For photographer: accept backup licensing (Wikimedia Commons CC-licensed images) for first bundle only; continue hiring for second bundle

### If no qualified candidate exists for herbalist by July 4

- Internal review: writer self-flags mandatory content items (CITES, UpS, drug interactions) using the provided reference list
- Hire 2-bundle review-only herbalist (scoped to Immunity and Digestive only — highest risk bundles)
- Document deviation in WORKLOG.md
