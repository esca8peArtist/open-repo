## Section 2: Decision Checklist — Three Points Required by May 30

### Decision 1: Sprint Scope (Due May 30)

**Context**: The Phase 3 medicinal herbs line will eventually include 5 bundles (Women's Health, Respiratory, Immunity, Sleep, Digestive). The question is which bundles execute in the June 22–July 13 sprint, and whether this will be one writer or two.

**Three Options**:

| Option | Scope | Timeline | Effort | Risk | Recommendation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **A** | All 5 bundles | June 29, July 6, July 13, July 20, Aug 3 | 56–66 hrs (adjusted for overlaps) | Tight writing schedule; no float on critical path | If confident in 5–6 hrs/day for 11 days |
| **B** | 5 bundles, two writers | Parallel write (5 bundles complete by July 8) | 28–33 hrs per writer | Requires coordinating two writers; overhead | If second writer available and briefed now |
| **C** | 3-bundle priority launch: Women's Health, Respiratory, Sleep | June 29, July 6, July 13 | 20–24 hrs writing | Safe schedule; July 20 for Immunity; Aug for Digestive | **RECOMMENDED** ← Lowest risk, highest quality |

**Why Option C is Recommended**:

1. **Critical path is writing, not design or photography** (writes are 56–66 hours; Canva is 12.5 hours). Option C focuses all effort on getting the three highest-demand bundles (Women's Health, Respiratory, Sleep) live by July 13 with time for refinement and testing.

2. **Immunity and Digestive bundles defer to July 20 and August 3 at zero cost**. Both bundles use established species and templates. No timeline pressure.

3. **Three-bundle sequence matches Etsy search seasonality**:
   - Women's Health (July 1): Women's health concerns year-round; summer peak for menopausal buyers
   - Respiratory (July 6): Starts building toward fall cold/flu season
   - Sleep (July 13): Summer sleep anxiety, outdoor noise avoidance — both peak July

4. **Goldenseal dependency is removed** from the critical path. Option C launches Women's Health and Respiratory without Goldenseal photography (Goldenseal is Immunity bundle only). Goldenseal ordering can slip to June 15 or use the CC-photo backup without delaying any July launch.

5. **Quality advantage**: Three-bundle sprint allows 3–5 hours per bundle for writing + 2 hours per bundle for editing/refinement. Five-bundle sprint compresses to 2–2.5 hours per bundle for writing — material quality drop.

**Action needed**: User selects Option A, B, or C and confirms by May 30. **Recommendation: Confirm Option C.**
