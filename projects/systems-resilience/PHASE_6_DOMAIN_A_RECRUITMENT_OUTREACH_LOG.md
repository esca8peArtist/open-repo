## Decision Criteria

### Strong Interest = Confirmed

A candidate qualifies as "confirmed interested" when all three conditions are met:

1. **Explicit availability**: Candidate confirms 20–22 hours/week available June 10 — July 10 (not "I think I can" — explicit "yes, I'm available")
2. **Writing sample reviewed**: Orchestrator has reviewed at least one writing sample demonstrating practitioner voice (not purely academic) at 1,500+ words
3. **Scope understood**: Candidate can articulate in their own words what Domain A addresses and why the three core research questions matter

A candidate who meets 2 of 3 by June 2 can be given a June 3 extension for the remaining condition. A candidate who meets none of the three by June 3 EOD is not confirmed regardless of enthusiasm level expressed.

### Compensation Negotiation Parameters

- Floor: $2,500 (Tier 3 organizer with strong practitioner voice but limited academic depth)
- Standard: $3,000–4,000 (Tier 1 or 2 candidate with confirmed writing sample quality)
- Premium: $4,000–5,000 (Tier 1 academic with public profile and demonstrated practitioner writing)
- Payment schedule: 20% on signed agreement / 30% at June 29 draft milestone / 30% at July 10 draft milestone / 20% on final acceptance
- Alternatives: Co-authorship credit, equity/revenue share — refer to template options in PHASE_6_AUTHOR_RECRUITMENT_TEMPLATES.md
