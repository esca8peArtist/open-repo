## Scenario B: Two-Domain Parallel Selection

If user selects **two domains** to research in parallel.

**Rationale**:
- Economic structures shape governance design (economically fragile systems need different governance than economically stable ones)
- Governance structures shape economic possibilities (centralized governance enables different economic models than distributed governance)
- Source bottleneck staggered: Economic (75% ready) completes weeks 2–4, creates context for Governance (65% ready) weeks 3–6
- Both authors provide checks and balances on scope (each domain is adjacent enough to catch the other drifting)

**Timeline**: 12 weeks (June 1 – August 23)

```

  - User decision + both authors onboarded
  - Source library compilation for both domains (parallel)
  - Scope boundary-setting for both (+1 hour cross-domain check)
  - Status: TWO AUTHORS READY FOR PRODUCTION by June 7

Weeks 2–4 (June 8–28):
  - AUTHOR A (Economic): Production weeks 1–3 (complementary currencies, commons governance, cooperative models)
  - AUTHOR B (Governance): Research weeks 1–3 (source compilation, failure modes research, federation model study)
  - Deliverable A: 6,000–8,000 words (outline complete, sections drafted)
  - Deliverable B: 0–1,000 words (outline complete, research ongoing)
  - Cross-check: Authors sync June 21 (30 min) to align on economic-governance interface

Week 5 (June 29–July 5):
  - AUTHOR A (Economic): Production week 4 (inter-community trade, Zone 5 implications)
  - AUTHOR B (Governance): Production week 2 (federation models section, full draft)
  - Deliverable A: 10,000–12,000 words cumulative
  - Deliverable B: 5,000–7,000 words cumulative
  - Integration point: Economic inter-community trade informs governance federation models

Weeks 6–8 (July 6–26):
  - AUTHOR A (Economic): Finalization (templates, case studies, final draft)
  - AUTHOR B (Governance): Full production (accountability, learning mechanisms, Zone 5 implications)
  - Deliverable A: 40,000–45,000 words (95%+ complete) by July 13

  - AUTHOR B (Governance): Finalization (templates, governance charters, final draft)
```

- Author coordination risk: MEDIUM (requires disciplined communication)
- Timeline risk: MEDIUM (both authors on critical path; one slip delays both)

**Confidence**: 85% on-time delivery for August 23 target

## Critical Path Analysis: What Determines Phase 6 Completion

### Single-Domain Critical Path

**Bottleneck domain**: Whichever domain is selected.

**Bottleneck activity**:
- Economic: Source library completion (June 5–8)
- Skills: Learning curve data research (June 8–15)
- Governance: Governance failure case studies (June 8–15)

**Solution**: Anticipate bottleneck activity in Week 1 planning; pre-assign research hours.

---

### Two-Domain Critical Path

**Bottleneck domain**: Governance Scaling (if paired with Economic) or neither (if paired Skills + Economic)

**Bottleneck activities** (if Economic + Governance pair):
1. Economic source library (June 5–8) → unlocks Economic production
2. Economic production (June 8–28) → creates context for Governance understanding of economic-governance linkage
3. Governance source library (June 8–15) → unlocks Governance production (delayed start acceptable)
4. Governance production (June 15–August 9) → determines Phase 6 completion date

**Critical path sequence**:
```
Economic sources (June 5–8) 
  → Economic production (June 8–July 13) [CRITICAL, determines July 13 delivery]
  → Governance sources (June 8–15) [overlaps, acceptable delay]
  → Governance production (June 15–August 9) [delayed start OK; Aug 9 is hard deadline]
  → Integration (Aug 10–23) [depends on both above]
```

**Solution**: Governance author begins sourcing immediately (June 1) while Economic author is onboarding, to offset the later start.

---

### Three-Domain Critical Path (If All in Parallel)

**Bottleneck**: Governance Scaling (longest, 13 weeks, highest scope risk)

**Critical path**:
```
All sources (June 1–15) 
  → Economic production (June 15–July 13) [11 weeks total, completes first]
  → Skills production (June 15–August 8) [12 weeks total, completes second]
  → Governance production (June 15–August 29) [13 weeks total, completes last and determines Phase 6 date]
  → Cross-review (Aug 20–30) [overlaps Governance production; can review Economic and Skills in parallel]
  → Integration (Aug 25–September 1) [depends on Governance completion]
```

**Solution**: If all three domains, staff Governance with strongest author and give it weekly scope audits (not optional).
