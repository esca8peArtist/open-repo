## Section 2: Contingency Paths by Option

**If Printer Unavailable (June 1-4 for Phase 4)**:
→ Use Office Depot in-store color copying ($0.25/page x 500 pages = $125 vs. $20-30 from print shop); 1-day turnaround

**If Peer Reviewer Unavailable (June 1-15)**:
→ Backup reviewer list identified by June 1; activate by June 8 if primary non-responsive

**If Phase 6 Author Unavailable (June 10-15)**:
→ Reduce scope 20%; defer non-critical sections to Phase 7

**If Microgrid Data Outdated (June 1-15)**:
→ Contact 3 microgrid vendors (Schweitzer, Siemens, Schneider) June 1-5 for latest spec sheets; incorporate June 10-14 editorial pass

### Option B (Unified Publication)

**If Publication Slips (June 14-15 deadline)**:
→ Acceptable slip: June 16 (1 day)
→ Not acceptable slip: June 17+ (conflicts with governance ratification assembly June 15)
→ Contingency: Publish digital-first June 15 early AM; physical copies June 16-17

**If Zone 5 Audit Not Complete (June 12 deadline)**:
→ Priority: Heating decision (critical for Phase 4 ratification)
→ Defer: Preservation, water winterization (secondary)
→ Approach: Abbreviated audit June 12; full audit post-publication June 20-25

---

### Option C (Rolling Publication)

**If Week 1-3 Publication Slips**:
→ Maintain consecutive weekly cycle; subsequent weeks slip by 1 week each
→ No catch-up possible; extend final publication from July 4 to July 11

**If Phase 6 Author Outline Rework Extends Past July 10**:
→ Activate backup author July 10; original author transitions to peer review/integration

**If Governance Coherence Risk Triggers**:
→ Escalate to single ratification assembly June 30 (after Phase 5 complete)

## Section 3: Bulk Purchasing & Cost Optimization

### Option A Bulk Purchasing Strategy

**Printing consolidation** (Phase 4 + Phase 5 Wave 1+2 + cross-project):
- Combined: 5,000-7,000 pages across governance, Phase 5 Wave 1+2 distribution, seedwarden/manufacturing-farm
- Local print shop bulk rate: $0.04-0.06/page (vs. Office Depot $0.15/page)
- **Savings**: (0.15 - 0.05) x 6,000 = $600 savings

**Coffee/food consolidation** (Phase 4 assembly + Phase 6 planning meetings):
- Combined need: 300+ servings across June-July
- Costco bulk coffee ($100 + coffee station) + potluck signup
- **Savings vs. café catering**: $400-600

**Total Option A bulk purchasing savings**: $1,000-1,200

### Option B Bulk Purchasing Strategy

**Printing consolidation** (larger single order for unified publication):
- Combined: 7,500+ pages (150 copies x 100 pages for Phase 5 distribution + Phase 6 interim)
- Bulk rate: $0.035-0.05/page
- **Savings**: 30% vs. per-page retail

**Food consolidation** (single 4-5 hour event, larger scale):
- Single event for 150-200 people (vs. 3 events in Option A)
- Bulk catering vs. multiple coffee services
- **Savings**: 15-20% per person due to economy of scale

**Total Option B bulk purchasing savings**: $1,500-2,000

### Option C Bulk Purchasing Strategy (Not Recommended)

**Food fragmentation** (6 weekly assemblies):
- Lower efficiency than consolidated events
- **Cost premium vs. Option A**: +$200-300

**Total Option C bulk purchasing penalty**: $500-800 (higher cost vs. Options A/B)
