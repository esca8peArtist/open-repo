## Scoring Guide (Project Lead Use Only — Not for Authors)

### Tier Assignment Matrix

| Section 1 Score | Section 4.1 (all checked) | Section 5 Anticipated Challenge | Assigned Tier |

| 16–20 | Yes | Low-stakes (formatting, tools) | **Tier A** — skip Section 2b extended tutorial and T+3 check-in |

| Any | No (missing confirmations) | — | Hold — resolve before tier assignment |

### Onboarding Step Adjustments by Tier

**Tier A authors:**
- Receive scope document + annotated bibliography without additional scaffolding notes
- No T+3 check-in unless they post a question
- Peer review protocol sent but not required to be reviewed before the June 24 window
- Publication checklist self-certification taken at face value

**Tier B authors:**
- Receive scope document + brief introductory note explaining key scope constraints for their domain
- T+3 check-in scheduled as a light post: "How is the outline going? Any early scope questions?"
- Peer review protocol primer sent with the T+7 checkpoint reminder
- Publication checklist review includes a project lead spot-check of Section 1 (citation quality) before final submission

**Tier C authors:**
- Scope consultation on June 10 (15-minute async exchange to narrow the scope and confirm the author understands what they are writing)
- Target word count reduced to 2,500–3,500 words; sections reduced accordingly
- T+3 check-in mandatory: confirm author has started outline and is not stuck
- Peer review pairing: pair with a Tier A or Tier B author who can model strong structured feedback
- Project lead performs light editorial pass at T+14 (full draft) rather than waiting for peer review to catch structural issues

### Red Flags to Resolve Before June 10

| Flag | Resolution |
|------|-----------|
| Section 4.1 not all checked | Direct conversation before June 10; if commitment cannot be confirmed, release the slot and move to secondary candidate list |
| Internet access listed as "limited or intermittent" | Review contingency offline onboarding document (JUNE_5_15_CONTINGENCY_OFFLINE_ONBOARDING.md); confirm author can work in offline-first mode |
| Section 3.1 response time: "irregular — batch by week" | Author must agree to daily check of platform messages during the sprint, even if brief. This is a hard requirement. |
| Conflict listed in June 24–27 peer review window | Adjust peer review window by 1–2 days if needed; notify peer reviewer of the adjustment |
| Section 5.3 Zone 5 familiarity: "limited" | Note in scope document; add a Zone 5 calibration question to the T+7 checkpoint review |
