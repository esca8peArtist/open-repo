### Peer Review Routing Email (June 11 — sent by orchestrator to reviewer)

```
Subject: Phase 6 [Domain] — peer review draft — June 11 2026

Hi [REVIEWER NAME],

Thank you for confirming your availability for this review. You are reviewing
the Phase 6 [DOMAIN] research document. The author is [AUTHOR NAME].

WHAT IS ATTACHED:
- Current draft ([N] words, sections [X-Y] complete, sections [Z] marked IN PROGRESS)
- Domain scope summary (1 paragraph)
- Author's three questions for you
- Citation list (informal format; formatting is not a review criterion)

REVIEW CRITERIA — please structure your feedback around these four areas:
1. FACTUAL ACCURACY: Are there any factual claims in the document that require
   correction or stronger sourcing?
2. CITATION DEPTH: Are there sections with claims that are not adequately sourced?
   Flag specific claims and suggest the type of source needed.
3. COMMUNITY CONTEXT (Zone 5 / practitioner applicability): Does each major section
   ground its analysis in specific community contexts? Flag any sections that are
   too abstract or that would benefit from a concrete practitioner example.
4. PRACTITIONER VOICE: Does the document read as practitioner guidance rather than
   academic analysis? Flag any sections that feel overly theoretical.

FEEDBACK FORMAT:
Please return your feedback using the structure below. Separate mandatory items
(factual corrections, critical citation gaps) from advisory items (framing
suggestions, additional sources, voice notes).

RETURN DEADLINE: June 13 17:00 UTC (48 hours from now)

Your author contact for clarifying questions: [AUTHOR EMAIL/CHANNEL]

Thank you again for your time on this.

[ORCHESTRATOR NAME]
```
