|------|-------------|-----------------|----------------------|

## Section 9: Contact Verification and Go/No-Go Criteria

For each candidate, before outreach confirm:

1. **Email current**: Institutional email confirmed via faculty page, organization website, or LinkedIn (within 6 months)
2. **No known unavailability**: No sabbatical, extended leave, or major deadline conflict (check lab pages, social media, recent activity)
3. **Domain fit confirmed**: Public work aligns with domain requirements (not just title match)
4. **Tier pre-assessment**: Profile supports the estimated tier — do not place a candidate in Tier A if recent publications are all 2018 or earlier with no intervening practitioner work

**Go/No-Go Decision per Candidate (June 14)**:

| Criterion | Go | No-Go |
|-----------|-----|-------|
| Contact verified | Active email confirmed | Bounced or more than 12 months inactive |
| Availability confirmed | 8+ hrs/week (Tier A), 4–8 hrs/week (Tier B/C) verbally confirmed | "Not sure" or no response by June 12 |
| Domain fit score | ≥ 14 for Tier B assignment; ≥ 20 for Tier A | Score below 14 on rubric; D1 or D2 = 1 |
| Tier certainty | Pre-assessment consistent with rubric score within 3 points | Pre-assessment diverges 4+ points from rubric score (re-assess before assigning) |
| Commitment certainty | "Yes" or "Yes, with conditions" (conditions resolvable) | "Maybe" or no reply |
