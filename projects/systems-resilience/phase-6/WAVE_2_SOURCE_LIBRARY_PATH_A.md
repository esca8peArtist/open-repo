## Topic A-1: Community-Scale Food Preservation and Storage

### Government and University Extension (Highest Authority)

- [PRIORITY] Survival Stronghold 2026: Home Canning and Fermentation — https://survivalstronghold.com/home-canning-fermentation-food-storage/ — practical 2026 framing from resilience perspective
