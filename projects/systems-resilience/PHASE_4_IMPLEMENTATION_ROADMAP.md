## Contingency Plans

### **If Agent Falls Behind Schedule**
- **Trigger**: Agent checkpoint missed by 3+ days
- **Action**: Reduce scope (combine sections, drop subsections, focus on 3 of 6 sections)
- **Fallback**: Deliver in phases (Sections 1-3 by deadline, Sections 4-6 two weeks later)
- **Never compromise**: Source quality, Zone 5 specificity, case study inclusion

### **If Research Reveals Insufficient Sources**
- **Trigger**: Fewer than 15 sources found for a topic (vs planned 25–35)
- **Action**: Escalate to orchestrator; consider topic deferral or hybrid approach
- **Alternative**: Rely on gray literature (gray box research: homesteading blogs, community reports, gray-literature databases)

### **If Institutional Demand Changes May 21 – June 1**
- **Trigger**: May 21 synthesis = WEAK, but user decides to proceed with Phase 4 anyway
- **Action**: Reduce to Option B (Technology Repair only) to minimize effort
- **Communicate**: Adjust agent assignments, rescope Wave 2+ topics

### **If Phase 4 Approval Comes Late (After June 5)**
- **Trigger**: User hasn't decided on Phase 4 by June 5
- **Action**: Compress timeline (parallel agents instead of sequential; accept reduced source depth if needed)
- **Fallback**: Defer Option A to November 2026, proceed with Option B July 1
