# Wave 2 Author Onboarding Readiness & Contingency Activation

**Purpose**: Wave 2 (June 10–July 10) requires dedicated author for psychological guide + conflict resolution framework. Author outreach began May 22 (pre-decision). This document stages: (a) successful onboarding path if author accepts, (b) self-execution contingency if author declines, (c) volunteer pairing options if author can only partially commit.

**Key decision gate**: May 30 user decision on Wave 2 scope determines author requirements. This playbook assumes **Wave 2 parallel or hybrid sequencing** (author needed June 1–9 onboarding, June 10–July 10 production).

**Timeline**:
- May 22: Author outreach (preliminary brief)
- May 30: User Wave 2 decision (scope confirmation)
- June 1–9: Author onboarding sprint (if author accepts)
- June 10–July 10: Writing production (author active, orchestrator supporting)
- July 10: Author delivers draft
- July 10–15: Peer review + revision
- July 15: Joint publication with Phase 5 Wave 1

### Contingency Path B: Self-Execute Fallback (Compressed Timeline)

**Trigger**: No co-author available by May 31 EOD, AND primary author declined OR can only do <50% of workload.

**Decision**: Orchestrator completes writing with support from volunteer peer reviewers. Compressed timeline but achievable before July 10 deadline.

**Scope Options**:

#### Option B1: Psychological Guide Only (Defer Conflict Resolution)
- Author declines; conflict resolution is deferred to Wave 3 (August)
- Orchestrator writes Psychological guide June 10–July 5 (50 hours)
- Peer review June 5–10
- Delivery July 10 on schedule
- Impact: Wave 2 incomplete but core is delivered

#### Option B2: Both Guides (Parallel Peer Reviewer Support)
- Orchestrator core writing + 2–3 volunteer peer reviewers on co-authoring basis
- Co-reviewers contribute 5–10 hours each (section co-writing + refinement)
- Orchestrator manages + synthesizes
- Timeline: June 10–July 5 writing, June 5–10 peer review + revision
- Delivery July 10
- Impact: Higher quality (peer input) but requires volunteer recruitment

#### Option B3: Scaled-Back Scope (Both Guides, 6–8K words each vs. 8–10K)
- Full two guides but shorter + tighter
- Orchestrator writes core sections (40 hours)
- Peer reviewers fill gaps + refine tone (10–15 hours total)
- Timeline: June 10–July 5 writing, June 5–10 review
- Delivery July 10
- Impact: Slightly narrower scope but avoids 3-week delay

**Workload Estimate by Option**:

| Option | Primary Author | Orchestrator Hours | Co-Reviewer Hours | Timeline | Quality |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **B1: Psychological only** | 0% (declines) | 50 | 10 (volunteer peer review) | June 10–July 10 | Good |
| **B2: Both guides, peer-co-authored** | 0% | 60 | 15–20 (co-author sections) | June 10–July 10 | Very good |
| **B3: Both guides, scaled-back** | 0% | 40 | 10–15 (refinement only) | June 10–July 10 | Good |

**Decision tree**:
- ✅ **Co-author available by May 31**: Proceed with co-author path (Option A)
- ⏸️ **No co-author, but time allows compression**: Self-execute with peer support (Options B1/B2/B3)
- 🔴 **Neither author nor time**: Defer Wave 2 to August (7-week delay)

## Part 3: Partial Commitment Scenario (Author Can Do 50% + Co-Author Needed)

### Trigger
- May 30: Primary author says "I can do Conflict Resolution (25 hours) but not Psychological Guide (30 hours)"
- OR author says "I can do 10 hours/week (40 hours total) instead of full 55 hours"

### Solution: Hybrid Co-Author Model

**Workload split**:
- Primary author: Assigned guide (Conflict Resolution OR Psychological)
- Co-author: Complementary guide OR co-write assigned guide
- Orchestrator: Managing, synthesis, peer review coordination

**Example split for Conflict Resolution + Psychological**:
- **Author 1** (Primary, 25 hours): Conflict Resolution guide deep-write
- **Author 2** (Co-author, 25 hours): Psychological guide deep-write
- **Orchestrator** (30 hours): Synthesis, integration, peer review management, publication prep

**Result**: Both guides completed, both authors at 50% capacity, orchestrator ensures integration.

**Timeline**: Same June 10–July 10 (no delay). Cost: +Co-author payment (typically $[X] for 25-hour commitment).
