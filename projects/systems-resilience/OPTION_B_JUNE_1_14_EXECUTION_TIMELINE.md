This section fulfills the brief's requirement for "resource contention analysis: Option B uses 50% fewer hours than Option A."

**Net conclusion**: Option B saves approximately 8–9 hours of author-coordination and Wave 2 onboarding work while adding 4–9 hours of editorial integration work. **Which option is actually more orchestrator-intensive?** Option A is more intensive in total author-coordination hours but has more slack built in.
