### What Phase 3 Did Not Cover

Phase 3 documents what needs to happen at community scale. It does not bridge the gap between a community that has read these documents and a community that has activated them operationally. Four gaps remain:

1. **Household implementation layer** (8-25 people): How does a household or extended family translate Phase 2-3 frameworks into daily coordination? The household scale is documented only for 3-6 people; the 8-25 person range is the most common unit for intentional community planning and is currently served by interpolation, not a direct document.

2. **Economic coordination**: Phase 3 governance and food systems document what to allocate and how to decide; they do not document how a community coordinates exchange when cash and supply chains fail. This is Phase 6 Domain A territory.

3. **Cross-domain failure cascade mapping**: If communications fail, which governance decisions become impossible? If food systems fail, what is the sequenced cascade into security deterioration? Phase 3 identifies domain interdependencies; the cascade maps (`cross-domain-failure-cascade-maps.md`) were produced separately and are not integrated into community operational guides.

4. **Education and knowledge transmission**: Phase 3 has no document for how communities develop and transfer the skills the other Phase 3 documents assume people have. This is the load-bearing gap that Phase 6 Domain C addresses.

### The Phase 4 Activation Role

Phase 4 is not new research. It is the activation layer — governance synthesis, household structure activation, and the Zone 5 operational reality-check that translate existing Phase 1-5 documents into operational community structures.

**Phase 4 activates on June 1 regardless of which Phase 5 publication option is selected.** The three Phase 4 option files (PHASE_4_ACTIVATION_OPTION_A.md, B, C) are staged and waiting. The specific Phase 4 activities (community governance workshop, household charter ratification, domain coordinator assignment) align with the same Option A/B/C timing as Phase 5 because they depend on publication timing to schedule community engagement.

### Phase 4 Bridge: Option C (Rolling — Not Recommended)

**Trigger**: Weekly thematic pairs starting May 30, through July 4.

**Phase 4 activation**: Technically June 1, but the rolling release creates a fragmented activation problem. Each weekly pair introduces new domain content; the governance structure cannot be ratified until the community has context on what it is governing.

**Phase 4 problem under Option C**: Governance ratification is the irreversible step. A community that ratifies its charter on June 1 using only the Week 1 documents (Implementation Playbook + Microgrids) is ratifying without the Conflict Resolution, Psychological Support, Veterinary Care, or Wave 3 operational documents. The governance structure is therefore incomplete at ratification and must be amended with each weekly pair — a structurally unstable pattern.

**If Option C is selected despite this analysis**, the Phase 4 recommendation is:
- Week 1 (June 1-7): Run governance pre-assessment only; no charter ratification
- Week 3 (June 13-20): Run governance workshop after Week 1+2 pairs are in community hands
- Week 6 (July 4+): Charter ratification after full corpus is available

This delays Phase 4 governance ratification to July, which is the primary reason Option C scores poorly on Phase 4 activation feasibility.

## Part 7: What Happens If No Decision Is Made by May 31

**Option A default activation**: If no decision is received by May 31, 23:59 UTC, Option A is the standing recommendation and can begin June 1 06:00 UTC without explicit confirmation. The Orchestrator should log "Option A default — no user decision received by deadline" in PHASE_4_EXECUTION_LOG.md and PHASE_5_EXECUTION_LOG.md, then proceed with the June 1 morning activation sprint described in Part 5.

**The only decision that cannot default**: Phase 6 domain selection. This is acceptable (2-day slip) but the orchestrator cannot proceed without knowing which domains are selected.
