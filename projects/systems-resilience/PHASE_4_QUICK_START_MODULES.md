## Overview: Why Phase 4 Modules Now?

Phase 5 research (66,400 words) addresses **community-scale systems resilience**. Once published, readers will immediately ask:

1. **"How do we actually govern ourselves?"** (governance framework extension)
2. **"How do we apply this to our household?"** (individual/household scaling)
3. **"How does this work in our region?"** (Zone 5 Midwest specifics)

These three questions are **not dependent on Phase 5 publication path** (Option A, B, or C). They are **independent research tracks** that can begin June 1 while Phase 5 is being published/distributed.
