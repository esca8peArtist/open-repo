# Wave 1 Peer Reviewer Candidates

**Usage note**: This document pre-identifies peer reviewers for two distinct purposes:
1. **Phase 5 Wave 1+2 quality validation** (before June 5 publication) — optional but recommended given the documents have not had external review
2. **Phase 6 Wave 1 domain peer review** (June 11–13 window) — required by the Wave 1 execution checklist; each Phase 6 domain must have one confirmed reviewer before T+9 (June 10)

Do not contact any candidate until the orchestrator authorizes outreach (June 1–2).

The project has produced practitioner-focused documents covering energy systems, food and water operations, governance, healthcare, and psychological support — 66,000 words across 12 documents, now entering a Phase 6 expansion on [DOMAIN].

>
> The review window is June 11–13, 2026. Would you be available?
>
> I am happy to share a sample document so you can assess fit before committing. Thank you for your time.
>
