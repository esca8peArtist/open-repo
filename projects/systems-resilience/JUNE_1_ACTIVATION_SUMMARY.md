## Decision 2: Phase 6 Domain Selection

The three recommended Week 1 launch domains are fully analyzed in `PHASE_6_SCOPE_AND_READINESS.md`. Each is 20 hours, independent of the others, and independent of Phase 6 pre-research (Farm Equipment, Meshtastic).

**Domain A — Community Economic Resilience (20 hours, High value, High citability)**
The single largest gap in the current corpus. Phase 5 produces food, water, and energy — but says nothing about how communities coordinate exchange when cash and supply chains fail. Covers mutual credit, labor hours, gift economies, cooperative structures, community currencies. Elinor Ostrom commons governance, Transition Towns, Vermont Time Banks, Totnes Pound as documented cases.

**Domain C — Skills Development and Knowledge Transmission (20 hours, High value, Medium-high citability)**
The enabler layer that makes Phase 5 usable. Phase 5 documents procedures; this domain builds the human capacity to execute them and survive the loss of any single expert. Covers adult apprenticeship, skill inventory systems, knowledge preservation across departure or death of key community members. Extends the educational governance document (Phase 5 Wave 3) from institutional structure to operational competency.

**Domain D — Governance Evolution and Scaling (20 hours, High value, High citability)**
Extends the Phase 5 conflict resolution and community playbook documents with the constitutional dimension: what breaks when a community of 20 scales to 50, 100, 200 — and how do you design governance that survives that growth. Sociocracy, deliberative democracy, Mondragon cooperative evolution, kibbutz governance failure analysis. No replication of Phase 5 content — this is the next unanswered question for any community that implements Phase 5.

**User input needed**: Confirm Week 1 launch selection (all three A+C+D recommended, or subset). If Week 1 is A+C+D, confirm whether Domains E, F, I (Week 2) should begin immediately after Week 1 documents are complete, or hold for user review between waves.
