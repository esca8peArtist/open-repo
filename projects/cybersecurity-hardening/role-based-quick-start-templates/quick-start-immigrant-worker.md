# Protecting Yourself from Immigration Surveillance
**A one-page guide for immigrant communities**

---

## What ICE Uses to Find People

ICE uses a computer system that collects your address from medical records, driver's license data, and social media. It gives each address a score — the higher the score, the more likely ICE will show up there. You can lower that score.

---

## The Four Most Important Actions

**1. Remove your address from data broker websites** (takes 45 minutes, free)

These websites sell your information to ICE. Ask them to remove you:
- Spokeo.com — search your name, then click "Remove my listing"
- Whitepages.com — go to whitepages.com/suppression_requests
- BeenVerified.com — go to beenverified.com/opt-out
- FastPeopleSearch.com — search your name, then use the opt-out link at the bottom

Repeat this for each family member living at your address.

**2. Turn off location on your phone** (takes 5 minutes)

On iPhone: Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > turn Off
On Android: Settings > Location > turn Off

Also: Remove your home address from Google Maps and Apple Maps if it appears as a saved location.

**3. Lock down your social media** (takes 10 minutes)

- Set your Facebook profile to Friends Only (not Public)
- Remove any photos that show your home address, your neighborhood, or your workplace
- Turn off location on any photos you post (on iPhone: Settings > Photos > turn off location access)

**4. Know what ICE cannot do at your door**

- ICE cannot enter your home without a warrant signed by a judge
- An ICE deportation order is NOT a warrant
- You have the right to say "I do not consent to a search" through the door without opening it
- You do not have to answer questions about your immigration status

---

## If ICE Contacts You or Your Family

1. Do not open the door unless they show a warrant signed by a federal judge (not just an ICE order)
2. Say through the door: "I am exercising my right to remain silent. I do not consent to a search."
3. Call a lawyer immediately — save this number: **ILRC Hotline: 1-800-898-7180**
4. Power off your phone. Put it in a bag or another room. Location data cannot be collected from a powered-off phone.
