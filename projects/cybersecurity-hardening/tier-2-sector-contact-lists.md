## Sector C: Researcher Communities

**Outreach approach**: Peer-to-peer, not top-down. Invite critique, not endorsement. Treat any correction as a contribution. Do not blast generically — find specific researchers who have published on surveillance tech, data broker ecosystems, or immigration enforcement technology and reference their work. Conference submissions are higher-leverage than email blasts.

**Volume**: 10 venues and community channels.
