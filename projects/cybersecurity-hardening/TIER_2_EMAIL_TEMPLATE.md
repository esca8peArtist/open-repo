# Tier 2 Email Templates — Sector-Specific Outreach

**How to use**: Select the template that matches your target organization's sector (Journalist, Whistleblower, Financial, or Activist). Replace {{bracketed}} placeholders with the recipient's name, organization, and specific details. Copy the subject line and body into your email client. Keep the signature block professional and brief.

**Key principle**: Each template leads with the organization's institutional mandate or primary concern before introducing the playbook. This framing significantly increases engagement and follow-up interest compared to leading with the corpus itself.

## Final Reminders

- **Personalization matters**: A generic email to a named recipient gets lower response than a template. Use the organization-specific variants and fill in {{bracketed}} fields accurately.
- **Lead with their mandate, not your corpus**: Every template leads with what the organization cares about, then positions the playbook as a tool for their existing work.
- **Include a specific ask**: Don't end with "let me know if you're interested." End with "would your {{team}} benefit from X?" Specificity increases reply rate significantly.
- **Keep follow-ups brief**: If they don't reply in 7 days, a one-sentence follow-up is better than a full re-send.
- **Offer customization**: Organizations are more likely to engage if they know you can adapt the material for their specific context.

All templates are copy-paste ready. Fill in {{placeholders}}, adjust the signature to match your preferred contact method, and send.
