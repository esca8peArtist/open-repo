## Missing Contact Details — Flags for User

The following gaps require user research before Day 1 sends (30–45 minutes, May 31):

1. **Named individuals for Senate committee staff**: Senate staff counsel and legislative staff are rarely named publicly. The user must use LinkedIn, published committee reports, or direct committee contact to identify named individuals before the Day 1 send. Sending to "Attention: Staff counsel" is an acceptable fallback if no named individual is findable, but named outreach converts at significantly higher rates.

2. **Calendly link**: The per-contact personalization is complete, but the Calendly booking link is not yet set up (item 3 in the Day 0 checklist). This must be created before Day 1 sends — it belongs in the follow-up offer at the bottom of each email.

3. **Personal connections**: If the user has any existing professional relationships with Wyden's or Markey's office specifically, that connection should be made explicit in the email ("I've followed your office's work on [X] — "). This is the highest-value personalization and cannot be supplied by the research agent.
