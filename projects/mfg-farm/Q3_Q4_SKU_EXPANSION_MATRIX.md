# Q3-Q4 SKU Expansion Analysis — 15 Candidate Products

## Executive Summary

Phase 1 has validated the mfg-farm unit economics: FDM printing on a single Bambu P1S delivers 30-40% gross margins on desk/organization products at $15-50 price points. Supplier costs are locked (eSUN PLA+ at $10-12/kg, PETG at $15-17/kg). This document identifies 15 SKU candidates across three material categories (PLA+, PETG, specialty) designed to maximize printer utilization while targeting customer pain points validated through Etsy market research.

**Key findings:**
- Cable management extensions: 5 candidates identified (highest demand signal, lowest design complexity)
- Storage/organization systems: 4 candidates (seasonal peaks in January + July)
- Specialty material (resin + TPU): 3 candidates (higher margins, lower volume baseline)
- Hobby/gift items: 3 candidates (gift-season peaks, impulse-buy price points)

**Portfolio composition targets:**
- 60% from cable/desk categories (ModRun buyer overlap, predictable demand)
- 25% from storage systems (seasonal amplification in spring/fall)
- 15% from specialty/gift items (margin upside, brand differentiation)
