# Phase 2 Q3-Q4 SKU Expansion — Executive Summary

## The Opportunity

Phase 1 has validated the core unit economics of the mfg-farm: FDM printing on a single Bambu P1S delivers 30-40% gross margins on desk/home organization products at $15-50 retail price points. With proven supplier relationships (eSUN PLA+ at $10-13/kg), established manufacturing workflow, and a modular CAD platform, Phase 2 can expand rapidly across 15 new product candidates without reinventing supply chain or production processes.

**Phase 2 targets:**
- Launch 15 new SKUs across 4 material categories (PLA+, PETG, resin, TPU)
- Distributed across 4 waves (July → October 2026)
- Revenue target: $21,200 gross (July-December 2026), $15,000+ net after COGS
- Printer utilization: Single printer through August; 2nd printer decision gate at Aug 15

## Risk Assessment & Contingencies

### Low Risk (Proceed Confidently)
- **Wave 1 SKUs:** All 4 products have proven demand signals (150-300 Etsy listings); simple CAD; validated supplier chain
- **Material sourcing:** PLA+ and PETG suppliers confirmed at volume pricing; magnets pre-ordered
- **Printer capacity:** Single printer can sustain 20-25 units/week on Wave 1-2 without constraint through August

### Medium Risk (Monitor, Mitigate)
- **Demand cliff (Wave 1):** If <15 units by July 31, Wave 2 launch delayed; impacts timeline but not viability
- **Magnet stock-out:** 2,000 units ordered by June 24; if delayed past July 15, use Amazon Prime emergency sourcing at +$0.20/unit cost
- **Design flaws (tolerances):** Cable Tray snap-fit, Monitor Riser load-bearing, and Drawer Dividers snap-fit require test-print validation; if tolerance issues found, design revision needed (3-5 days)

### Higher Risk (Decision Gates Required)
- **2nd Printer ROI:** Only proceed if Wave 1-2 combined sustain >50 units/week by mid-August (otherwise capex is unrecovered)
- **Specialty materials (resin, TPU):** Require equipment investment ($12K-18K for resin printer); only fund if Phase 2 cash flow validates (>$10K net by August)
- **Seasonal demand assumption:** Q4 gift season may underperform for desk/organization (less seasonal than home decor); monitor June-Sept traction to validate

### Contingency Playbook
- **Filament stock-out:** Activate Amazon Prime backup (3-5 day delivery)
- **Printer failure:** Use Craftcloud service bureau at $6-15/part for emergency orders; accept margin hit to preserve customer goodwill
- **Demand collapse:** Pause Wave 3-4; optimize Wave 1 (pricing test, Etsy Ads, photography refresh)
- **Quality issues:** Initiate design review; offer free replacements to existing customers; revise STL if tolerance flaw confirmed
