# Amazon Handmade Application Template — ModRun

**Critical timing note**: The Amazon Handmade approval process takes 2–5 weeks based on current review queue times (seller reports, mid-2026). Apply immediately after the test print passes — do not wait until Etsy Phase 1 is complete. The approval timeline means you need to be in the queue now to have access ready when you want to list at Month 2–3.

## Section 1: Original Design Certification Language

Amazon Handmade requires that sellers attest their products are genuinely handmade or handcrafted by the seller, not manufactured by a third party and resold. For 3D printed original designs, this certification holds fully and should be expressed clearly in the application.

**Boilerplate certification statement (use in the application's "About Your Products" section):**

---

I am the original designer and sole manufacturer of all products submitted in this application. All designs were created entirely by me using CadQuery (parametric CAD software) with no third-party design files, licensed assets, or AI-generated geometry. Each product exists as a unique STL file stored in my local design repository.

All products are manufactured to order on a Bambu P1S FDM 3D printer located at my home workshop address. I am the sole operator. No contract manufacturing, drop-shipping, or third-party fulfillment is involved in production. I source raw material (PLA+ filament) from US-based distributors and perform all steps — design, print, quality check, package, and ship — myself.

I attest that no product in this application infringes on any third-party trademark, copyright, or patent. All product names, designs, and geometries are original. The ModRun cable management system was designed from scratch with no reference to existing commercial products.

---

**Checklist: what Amazon reviews to verify this claim**:
- [ ] Photos of the production workspace (printer, filament, in-progress prints)
- [ ] Photo of the seller working with the product
- [ ] Description of tools used (CAD software name, printer model)
- [ ] Statement on team size (must be fewer than 20 people; solo = clearly qualifies)
- [ ] Any trademark search results or design records (optional but strengthens claim)

## Section 3: Photography Specifications — Amazon Handmade

Amazon's image requirements are stricter than Etsy's. The main image must comply exactly or the listing will be suppressed. No exceptions.

### Main Image Requirements (Slot 1)

| Requirement | Specification | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Background | Pure white, RGB 255/255/255 | Off-white and light gray trigger suppression — verify with color picker |
| Product fill | ≥85% of the image frame | Product must be large relative to the frame; not centered with large white borders |
| Image content | Product only | No text, logos, watermarks, props, accessories not included with the product |
| Minimum resolution | 1,000 pixels on longest side | 2,000+ recommended to enable Amazon's zoom function |
| File formats | JPEG, PNG, or TIFF | No GIF, BMP, or WebP |
| File size | Under 10MB | Compress without quality loss using Squoosh or TinyPNG |
| Aspect ratio | 1:1 (square) recommended | Allowed: between 3:4 and 4:3; square is safest for all placement types |

**How to verify pure white**: In Canva, use the color picker on the background of your image. If it does not read as R255, G255, B255, use Remove.bg to replace the background, then export on a white (#ffffff) canvas.

### Supplemental Image Requirements (Slots 2–9)

- No background restriction — lifestyle, infographic, and contextual backgrounds all permitted
- Same minimum resolution: 1,000 pixels (2,000+ preferred)
- No text overlay on the main image; text callouts are permitted on supplemental images (slots 2–9)
- Videos: Amazon allows one product video per ASIN; MP4, MOV, or AVI; up to 5 minutes; 1,920×1,080 minimum

**Recommended supplemental image order for the cable clip listing:**

| Slot | Content |
|---|---|
| 2 | In-use lifestyle — cables routed on a clean desk |
| 3 | Feature callout infographic — snap arm labeled, channel widths labeled, desk thickness compatibility |
| 4 | Before/after transformation — tangled vs. managed |
| 5 | Installation steps — 3-panel composite: clip on desk edge, cable inserted, cable routed |
| 6 | Size guide — which cable diameters fit each channel width |
| 7 | Color options flat-lay |
| 8 | Pack contents — three clips laid out |
| 9 | Scale reference — clip next to quarter coin |

## Section 4: Brand Story Template

Amazon Handmade asks sellers to provide a brand story — a 2–3 paragraph description of the maker, design philosophy, and quality commitment. This appears on the Handmade listing and the seller's Amazon Handmade storefront.

**Pre-written brand story (customize bracketed fields):**

---

### Paragraph 1 — Maker Background

[YOUR SHOP NAME] is a one-person design and manufacturing studio based in [YOUR CITY, STATE]. I design functional accessories for home offices, gaming setups, and workshops — and I manufacture every product myself on a Bambu P1S 3D printer.

I started this project because I was frustrated with the cable management options available. Mass-market clips were either too generic to actually fit my desk, or they used adhesive that left residue, or they were sold only in bulk packs when I needed three specific sizes. I designed the ModRun cable clip in CadQuery to solve exactly my problem, printed a prototype, and it worked better than anything I could buy.

---

### Paragraph 2 — Design Philosophy

Every product I sell is something I use myself. I design in CadQuery, a parametric CAD tool that lets me build geometry from measurements rather than guessing at shapes. Before a product is listed, it has been test-printed at least once, verified for fit and function, and photographed in actual use. If the test print doesn't perform the way the design intended, it doesn't get listed.

I don't keep inventory. Every order triggers a new print. That means the part you receive was printed within the last 48 hours, not sitting in a warehouse for six months. It also means I can accommodate custom requests — different colors, thickness adjustments, special configurations — without minimum order quantities.

---

### Paragraph 3 — Quality Commitment

All materials are purchased from US-based distributors. I use PLA+ filament from [FILL IN: supplier, e.g., Polar Filament, eSUN] — a high-quality PLA+ formulation with better layer adhesion and impact resistance than standard PLA. Print settings are dialed in for each product: 0.20mm layer height, 3-wall perimeters, 220–225°C for optimal strength.

Every part is inspected before packaging: I check the snap-fit mechanism, verify the channel dimensions, and confirm the mounting geometry is clean. Packaging is minimal — a poly mailer with a business card — because the product is the product, not the box it comes in. If something arrives damaged or doesn't fit as described, I'll make it right.

## Section 5: Shipping Policy Template

Amazon Handmade allows sellers to set their own processing time and shipping policies within certain minimums. Fill in the specific values for the cable clip.

**Processing time:**

- Listed processing time: **3–5 business days**
- Actual ship time: **1–2 business days** (underpromise to trigger "faster than expected" reviews)
- This gap is intentional — Amazon calculates on-time delivery from the listed processing window, not actual ship date. Shipping in 1–2 days against a 3–5 day promise builds review quality and Star Seller eligibility metrics.

**Shipping method:**

| Order Type | Carrier | Service | Expected Transit |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cable clip 3-pack (under 1 oz parts) | USPS | First Class Package | 2–5 business days |
| Bundle with rail section (4–6 oz) | USPS | Priority Mail | 1–3 business days |
| Heavy bundle (8+ oz) | USPS | Priority Mail | 1–3 business days |

**Handling for small 3D printed parts:**

- All parts are individually inspected before packaging
- Wrapped in tissue paper to prevent surface scratching during transit
- Packaged in poly mailers for clips and small hooks; rigid padded mailers for larger items
- No foam peanuts (environmentally inconsistent with PLA+ positioning)
- Every shipment includes a printed card with shop contact and review request

**Return policy for Amazon Handmade:**

- 30-day return window required by Amazon policy (mandatory; cannot offer shorter window)
- Personalized orders: mark as non-returnable unless defective (enable in Seller Central under "Custom Products")
- Defective product: full replacement shipped within 3 business days of return confirmation
- "Not as described" returns: accept and refund within 24 hours to maintain seller metrics

## Section 6: Price and Quantity Fields — FBA vs FBM Cost Comparison

### Per-Unit Economics on $28.99 Sale Price

Source: PHASE_2_TRACK_3_MARKET_EXPANSION_RESEARCH.md and MULTI_CHANNEL_SALES_ARCHITECTURE.md. These are the researched numbers for a $28.99 bundle sale (starter bundle: 3-pack clips + rail section).

| Cost Element | Etsy (FBM) | Amazon FBA | Amazon Handmade (FBM) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Sale price | $28.99 | $28.99 | $28.99 |
| Platform referral / transaction fee | $1.88 (6.5%) | $4.35 (15%) | $4.35 (15%) |
| Payment processing | $1.12 (3% + $0.25) | Included in referral | Included in referral |
| Listing fee | $0.20 | $0 | $0 |
| FBA fulfillment fee (5 oz, incl. 3.5% surcharge) | — | $3.41 | — |
| **Total platform fees** | **$3.20 (11%)** | **$7.76 (26.8%)** | **$4.35 (15%)** |
| **Net to seller (before COGS)** | **$25.79** | **$21.23** | **$24.64** |
| COGS (material + machine + packaging) | $0.81 | $0.81 | $0.81 |
| **Net profit per unit** | **$24.98** | **$20.42** | **$23.83** |

**Key insight**: Etsy generates $4.56 more per unit than Amazon FBA on the same $28.99 product. This is the margin penalty you accept in exchange for Amazon Prime visibility, 310M buyer reach, and Prime delivery credibility.

**The correct frame is additive revenue, not substitution.** Running both channels at 50 units/month each generates $(50 × $24.98) + (50 × $20.42) = $2,270 combined, versus $1,249 on Etsy alone. The per-unit margin penalty is offset by additive volume from a non-overlapping buyer base.

### FBA Initial Inventory Requirement

To launch Amazon FBA, you must ship forward inventory to Amazon warehouses before orders arrive. For the cable clip launch:

| Item | Value |
|---|---|
| Recommended first batch | 50 units |
| Estimated COGS for 50 units | $40.50 (50 × $0.81) |
| FBA inbound shipping cost (to warehouse) | ~$15–25 (depends on warehouse location assigned) |
| Inbound placement fee (New Selection Program exemption) | $0 (first 100 units per new ASIN exempt) |
| Total forward capital required | ~$55–75 |
| Additional: PPC advertising budget (first 30 days) | $100–200 recommended |
| **Total FBA launch capital** | **~$155–275** |

### Quantity Limits

- Initial FBA batch: 50 units (validates demand without aged inventory risk)
- Reorder trigger: When remaining FBA inventory drops to 15 units (30-day supply at ~0.5 units/day initial velocity)
- IPI exemption: New sellers receive unlimited storage capacity for first 39 weeks — no capacity constraint at this volume

## Section 7: Application Timing — 5–7 Week Review Window

**Apply immediately after the test print passes.** Do not wait for Etsy Phase 1 to be complete. The Amazon Handmade approval process runs independently of your Etsy operations — there is no cost to having approval sitting ready.

**Timeline:**

| Week | Action |
|---|---|
| Test print passes (current blocker) | Submit Amazon Handmade application the same day |
| Week 1–2 after submission | Application under review (no action required) |
| Week 3–4 | Amazon may request additional photos or information (respond within 48 hours) |
| Week 5–7 | Approval received (typical range; can be faster or slower) |
| Week 8 (concurrent with Month 2 Etsy) | Create Amazon Handmade listings; photograph main images to Amazon spec |
| Week 10 | Ship first FBA batch to warehouse; set up Sponsored Products campaign |

**What delays approval:**
- Vague application responses (be specific: name the printer model, the software, the materials)
- Missing workspace photos (photograph the Bambu P1S with filament and a work-in-progress print before applying)
- Products that don't clearly fit one of the 13 approved categories

**What accelerates approval:**
- Clear, detailed answers with specific tool and material names
- 3–5 high-quality workspace photos included
- Professional seller account already active before submitting
