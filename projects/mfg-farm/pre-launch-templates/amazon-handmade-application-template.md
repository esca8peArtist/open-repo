# Amazon Handmade Application Template — ModRun Cable Clip

**Critical path note**: Amazon Handmade application review takes 2–4 weeks (manual human
review). Every day you delay applying is a day later you can list on Amazon. Apply within
48 hours of test print PASS — you do not need finished product photos to apply, only
workspace and process photos.

**Net target**: $21.23/sale after 15% Amazon referral fee, at $28.99 list price
(FBA self-fulfill variant). FBA fulfillment fee adds ~$3.41 for small standard weight,
bringing FBA net to ~$17.82/sale at $28.99.

---

## Part 1: Account Prerequisites

Complete these in order before accessing the Handmade application.

### Step 1: Amazon Professional Seller Account

Navigate to: https://sell.amazon.com → "Start selling" → "Professional" plan

Required information:
- Legal name (your full name or registered LLC name)
- Business address
- Bank account for disbursements
- Credit card for Amazon charges
- Government-issued ID (SSN for individual; EIN for LLC)
- Phone number for two-step verification

**Time**: 30–45 minutes to apply; 1–3 business days for identity verification.

**Cost**: $39.99/month for Professional plan. Amazon Handmade approval waives this fee from
Month 2 onward — budget for one month only.

### Step 2: Two-Step Verification

Mandatory. Use an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy) rather than SMS —
Amazon has been locking SMS-authenticated accounts more frequently in 2026.

## Part 4: Photography Specifications for Application

Amazon requires 3–5 process photos demonstrating your production setup.
Take these photos immediately after test print — you will need them for the application.

### Photo 1 — Printer + active print (most important)

What to show: Bambu P1S with a cable clip visible on the print bed, mid-print
(or freshly finished, bed not yet cooled). The printer should be clean and clearly
operational.

Requirements:
- Background: simple — a wall, workbench surface, or neutral background
- No Amazon trademarks, competitor logos, or distracting clutter in frame
- Resolution: minimum 1600×1200px; JPEG or PNG

### Photo 2 — Quality inspection in progress

What to show: Finished clips laid out on a flat surface with calipers measuring
one clip. This is the most direct proof of handmade quality control.

Requirements:
- Calipers clearly visible and readable
- 2–4 clips visible (not a production run of hundreds — small batch signals artisan)
- Clean, well-lit surface

### Photo 3 — Packaged order ready for shipment

What to show: A kraft paper mailer or small box with the ModRun clip inside,
tape applied, label affixed (can be blank label if you haven't configured shipping yet).

Requirements:
- Shows the end-to-end process from production to shipment
- Mailer should look professional — neat tape, straight label

### Optional Photo 4 — Design process screenshot

What to show: CadQuery or CAD software open on a screen with the cable clip
parametric model visible.

Requirements:
- Must clearly show the design software interface, not just a rendered image
- Optional but strengthens the original-design narrative

Amazon Handmade sellers who self-fulfill set their own handling times.
Use these settings in Seller Central > Shipping Settings:

```
Handling time: 3 business days (we ship in 1–2; the gap creates positive
              "faster than expected" feedback)
Carrier: USPS
Service: Priority Mail or Ground Advantage (both trackable)
Default transit time: 5–7 business days
Return window: 30 days (Amazon Handmade mandatory minimum)
Return address: [FILL: your home address]
```

**Personalized items return policy**: Amazon's custom products policy (updated 2025)
allows sellers to mark custom/personalized items as non-returnable unless defective.
Enable this in Seller Central: Inventory > Manage Listings > [listing] > Edit >
scroll to "Personalization" > check "This item is customized for individual buyers."

## Part 9: Post-Approval — First 30 Days on Amazon

### Review accumulation (critical — Amazon's cold-start problem)

- After each confirmed delivery, use the "Request a Review" button in Seller
  Central > Orders > Order ID > "Request a Review" (sends Amazon's templated
  email; most effective tool allowed)
- Do NOT send third-party review request emails — Amazon prohibits solicitation
  outside the "Request a Review" button
- Enroll in Amazon Vine once 30+ reviews are accumulated (Vine seeds additional
  reviews from vetted reviewers; requires Brand Registry)

### Sponsored Products (PPC — start Day 1)

Amazon organic ranking for new sellers with zero reviews is extremely limited.
PPC is nearly mandatory for the first 60 days.

Minimum viable PPC setup:
```
Campaign type: Sponsored Products — Auto-targeting
Daily budget: $3.00 (start here; increase if ACoS < 30%)
Bid: automatic (let Amazon set bids initially)
Duration: indefinite (turn off when organic ranking established)
Target ACoS: under 30% (means you're spending <$0.30 to generate $1.00 in
             attributed sales)
```

After 14 days, switch to manual targeting with the keywords that generated
the most clicks in the auto campaign.
