## Section 6: Image Checklist

Minimum 5 images required before publishing. This is not negotiable — listing with fewer
than 5 images performs meaningfully worse in the 14-day recency window.

### Image 1 — Hero lifestyle shot (CRITICAL — determines CTR)

- Product: ModRun clip actively holding a cable on a real desk surface
- Composition: Clip in sharp focus; cable visibly threaded; desk background clean and minimal
- Background: White, light gray, or natural wood — no clutter
- Lighting: Soft natural window light; no harsh shadows; no flash
- Resolution: Minimum 2000×2000px (Etsy displays at 1000×1000; higher resolution = better
  zoom on desktop)
- Aspect ratio: Square (1:1)
- What NOT to do: Plain white-background product photo without context; the clip is too
  small to fill a frame without a desk surface for scale and context

### Image 2 — Snap-arm mechanism close-up

- Shows the snap-arm channel with a cable mid-insertion
- Demonstrates the engineering precision; builds buyer confidence
- Focal length: macro or 50-100mm equivalent; clip and cable in crisp focus
- Frame: fill 60-70% of the frame with the mechanism

### Image 3 — Before/after cable organization

- Left half: cables loose and tangled on a desk surface
- Right half: same desk, cables routed cleanly through ModRun clips
- Dividing line: subtle center line or diagonal fade
- Emotional hook: the transformation sells the product more than any text

### Image 4 — Color/variant flat-lay

- All available colors displayed in a clean flat-lay overhead shot
- Each clip at the same angle; consistent spacing
- White or light gray background (poster board is fine)
- Label colors using text overlay if the shades are subtle

### Image 5 — "Complete the system" cross-sell shot

- ModRun clips + rail section shown together as a system
- Caption or text overlay: "ModRun Rail sold separately — complete your desk system"
- This drives cross-sells and communicates the modular value proposition

### Optional Image 6 — Size reference

- Clip next to a common object (coin, pencil) for scale
- Buyers frequently ask about dimensions; a scale photo reduces questions and returns

### Video (strong recommendation — 7-second minimum)

- Etsy listings with video receive a ranking lift in categories with under 10% video
  penetration (3D printed desk accessories is well under 10%)
- Shoot: clip snapping onto desk edge (3 sec) → cable threading into channel (2 sec) →
  cable held taut and released cleanly (2 sec) = 7 seconds total
- No audio required (Etsy strips audio); no editing required; vertical or square format
- Max file size: 100MB; formats: MP4 or MOV

## Section 8: Pricing Calculator (Working Math)

Use this to verify pricing for any new SKU before listing.

### COGS components per unit (Phase 1, single printer)

| Component | Cost | Notes |
|-----------|------|-------|
| Filament (PLA+) | $0.14–0.18 | ~10-13g per clip at $13.50/kg (Cost_Model_May2026_Validation.md) |
| Electricity | $0.02–0.04 | ~20-min print per clip at $0.12/kWh |
| Packaging (per order) | $0.40–0.60 | Bubble mailer + tape |
| USPS Ground Advantage | $4.75–5.00 | Commercial rate via Pirateship, under 8 oz |
| Labor (QC + pack) | $0.50–1.00 | ~3-5 min/order at opportunity cost |
| **COGS per order (4-pack)** | **~$6.85–7.80** | Total, including shipping |

### Net calculation formula

```
Net per sale = List price − Etsy fees − COGS

Etsy fees = (List price × 0.065) + (List price × 0.03) + $0.25 + $0.20
           = List price × 0.095 + $0.45

Example at $28.99 list:
  Etsy fees = $28.99 × 0.095 + $0.45 = $2.75 + $0.45 = $3.20
  COGS (12-pack + rail order) ≈ $4.50 (materials + packaging + shipping)
  Labor ≈ $1.00
  Net = $28.99 − $3.20 − $4.50 − $1.00 = $20.29 (70% gross margin)
```

### Margin by price point

| List Price | Etsy Fees | Shipping + Mat | Net | Gross Margin |
|------------|-----------|----------------|-----|--------------|
| $12.99 (4-pack) | $1.68 | $5.75 | $5.56 | 43% |
| $19.99 (8-pack) | $2.45 | $6.00 | $11.54 | 58% |
| $28.99 (12-pack+rail) | $3.20 | $6.50 | $19.29 | 67% |
| $38.99 (system bundle) | $4.25 | $7.00 | $27.74 | 71% |

Target margin floor: 60%. The 4-pack at $12.99 is a review-acquisition tool, not a profit
center. Transition buyers to the 8-pack and above as quickly as reviews allow.
