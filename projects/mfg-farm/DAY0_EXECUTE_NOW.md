## Day 5–14: The Recency Window (highest leverage period)

Etsy gives new listings a 14–21 day recency boost. Impressions during this window are "loaned" to measure click-through and conversion signals. A listing that converts at 2%+ during this window earns sustained organic promotion. This is the most important window of the listing's lifecycle.

**Daily actions during the recency window:**
- Respond to every message within 2 hours (9 AM–6 PM)
- Check Etsy Stats daily: views, favorites, conversion rate
- Day 5 check: if CTR < 2%, swap the primary photo. Use the in-context lifestyle shot (not a white-background product shot)
- Day 10 check: if no sales yet, reduce price by $1.50 for a 5-day test
- Day 14 check: if conversion < 1.5%, create a new listing (do not edit this listing's URL — the LQS is tied to it)
