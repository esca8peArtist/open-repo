## 1. Listing Title

**Primary recommendation (88 characters):**
```
Desk Headphone Hook with Cable Manager | 3D Printed Clamp | Custom Color
```

## 2. Listing Description

```
CABLE-MANAGING HEADPHONE HOOK — DESIGNED FOR DESK SETUP ENTHUSIASTS

Keep your headphones off the desk and your headset cable actually organized. Unlike standard hooks, this one includes a built-in cable-wrap post so you're not left with loose cable coiling on your desk.

— Installs in seconds — no screwdriver, no assembly. Just press onto your desk edge.
— Built-in rubber pads protect your desk surface (no scratches, no slipping).
— Works with desk tops 12mm, 25mm, or 40mm thick (select your size at checkout).
— 55mm arm reach holds full-size gaming headsets and studio headphones.
— Printed to order in the USA from PLA+ filament.

HOW IT WORKS
The C-clamp slides over your desk edge and grips with friction — the rubber pads do the holding work. The hook arm angles slightly upward so your headset stays put. The cable-wrap post keeps your cable coiled neatly when your headset is hanging.

WHAT'S IN THE BOX
• 1× 3D printed headphone hook (with pre-installed rubber pads)
• 1× thank-you card

CHOOSING YOUR SIZE
— Thin desk (12mm): IKEA desk tops, plywood shelves, particle board shelves
— Standard (25mm): IKEA LINNMON, BEKANT, most home/office desk tops (most common — if unsure, choose this)
— Thick desk (40mm): Solid wood, butcher-block, standing desk bamboo tops

MATERIALS & PRINT SPECS
• PLA+ filament (biodegradable, rigid, indoor-use rated)
• 0.20mm layer height, 25% infill — optimized for strength at minimal weight
• Self-adhesive silicone rubber pads included (desk protection + grip)

COLOR OPTIONS
Select your color in the "Add your personalization" field at checkout. Available: Matte Black, Matte White, Space Gray, Deep Blue, Terracotta. Other colors available — just describe in notes and I'll confirm availability.

DIMENSIONS
• Hook arm reach: 55mm from desk edge
• Clamp body: 38mm wide × 28mm deep
• Total hook height above desk: approx. 65mm at arm tip

PART OF THE ModRun DESK ECOSYSTEM
This hook pairs with the ModRun cable management rail (sold separately). Same aesthetic, same material, designed for the same desk setup. See my shop for the full ModRun system.

PROCESSING & SHIPPING
Printed to order — processing time 3–5 business days. Ships via USPS First Class in a padded poly mailer.

Questions? Message me — I'm happy to help you choose the right size.
```

| SKU | Listing Price | Shipping | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Standard single hook | $12.99 | Buyer pays ($3.50–$4.00) | Launch price; test $14.99 after 50 reviews |
| Bundle 3-pack | $32.99 | Buyer pays ($4.00–$4.50) | Personalization: "List 3 thicknesses + 3 colors" |
| Dual-hook (Month 2) | $19.99 | Buyer pays | After dual-hook STL designed |
| Wall-mount (Month 2) | $9.99 | Buyer pays | Entry tier |

**Rationale for charging shipping separately (vs. free shipping):** At $12.99 and 60g shipping weight, absorbing First Class ($3.75) would require raising price to $16.74 for equivalent net. At launch without reviews, $16.74 is above dominant competitor ($14.99 with 1,254 reviews). Charge shipping separately and compete at $12.99. Once reviews accumulate, test free shipping at $15.99–$16.99.
