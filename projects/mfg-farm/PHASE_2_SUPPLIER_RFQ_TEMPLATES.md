I am building a production 3D print farm for a cable management product line sold on Etsy and Amazon. My business name is ModRun Design.

I am currently running one Bambu P1S and am planning to expand to a 4-printer cluster in June 2026, with a target of 8 printers by Q4 2026.

I am writing to:

1. Request pricing for 3 additional P1S units (base model and/or Combo with AMS 2 Pro — please quote both configurations)
2. Confirm current in-stock lead time
3. Understand whether volume pricing is available for a multi-tranche procurement plan (10+ units total across June, July, and August)
4. Inquire about net-30 payment terms for qualified business accounts
5. Confirm tax-exempt account setup process

Context for your team: This is a production commercial print farm, not a hobbyist setup. We run PLA+ cable management clips on a production duty cycle (16 hours per day). Our expansion to 4 printers in June is the first of three planned tranches.

Could you send a quote and confirm lead time for 3× P1S base and 3× P1S Combo (with AMS 2 Pro) by June 5?

Best regards,
[NAME]
ModRun Design
[EMAIL]
[PHONE]

---

**Key negotiation points:**
- Frame as multi-tranche buyer (10+ units total) to improve volume discount leverage
- Net-30 requires business documentation — have LLC registration or DBA documents ready
- If no response in 48 hours, submit again via the live chat on bambulab.com
- Volume discount is entirely at rep discretion; 5–8% is realistic; 15% requires larger commitment

**Typical response timeline:** 1–2 business days via portal; same-day via live chat.

- MMU3 multi-material add-on: $299

**Why Prusa is contingency-only for ModRun:**
- 15–25% slower than Bambu P1S at equivalent print quality (150–200 mm/s vs. 200–250 mm/s)
- $400 more per unit than P1S at $399
- No Bambu Farm Manager integration — requires separate OctoPrint monitoring stack, fragmenting the software environment
- Fully open-source, user-serviceable, printable spare parts — the primary advantage if Bambu hardware supply becomes a systemic risk

**When to activate Prusa sourcing:**
- Bambu P1S becomes unavailable or is discontinued without a successor model
- A tariff event makes Chinese-manufactured hardware unreliable to procure
- Phase 3+ (5+ printers) where long-term repairability justifies the price premium

**No RFQ required for contingency position.** Monitor pro.prusa3d.com for enterprise pricing; initiate contact if any of the above triggers materialize.
