# Bambu Lab Farm Supplier Contacts — ModRun Phase 2

**Lead finding:** Bambu Lab operates a formal B2B/corporate sales channel (bambulab.com/en/corporate-sales) that accepts purchase orders for multi-unit orders and offers tax-exempt account setup. For 10+ printers, the three highest-value channels are: (1) Bambu Lab direct B2B portal for PO terms and volume intent, (2) MatterHackers (the only US distributor with documented print farm bundles and 2-year extended warranty coverage), and (3) Dynamism (Chicago-based reseller with enterprise support program and quote-based pricing for multi-unit orders). Supplier outreach can begin immediately — no test print result is required before initiating quote requests and lead time confirmations.

### Program Notes

Bambu Lab's corporate sales program accepts formal purchase orders directly. This is the appropriate route for 10+ printer acquisitions when you want to document a business procurement for accounting purposes or require an invoice format compatible with net-30 terms. The portal also handles tax-exempt account status, which is relevant if ModRun's workshop space qualifies as a business entity.

Bambu Lab does not publish bulk pricing tiers publicly. The B2B inquiry form connects you to a sales representative within 1–2 business days. Frame the inquiry as: "Production print farm build-out, planning 10 P1S units across 2 procurement tranches (June and Q3 2026), seeking volume pricing and lead time confirmation."

**Current P1S Pricing (May 2026):**
- P1S (base, no AMS): $399 (sale pricing, was $699 MSRP)
- P1S Combo (with AMS 2 Pro): $649–$750 depending on retailer and sale
- Additional AMS 2 Pro unit: ~$286

**Estimated 10-printer capital at current pricing:**
- 10x P1S Combo: ~$6,490–$7,500 (before any volume discount)
- 10x P1S base (add AMS separately if needed): ~$3,990
- Volume discount expectation: 5–15% off list for 10+ unit orders — not guaranteed, negotiated per inquiry

**Payment terms:** Credit card standard on US store. Net-30 available through B2B channel for qualified business accounts; requires business documentation.

**Lead time:** In-stock P1S models ship within 3–7 business days. During demand spikes (post-new-model launch, back-to-school), expect 2–3 week delays. Phase 2 procurement should initiate by June 1 to receive printers by June 20–30.

## Section 5: SimplyPrint Integration Checklist

SimplyPrint (simplyprint.io) is the recommended cross-brand farm management platform for mixed Bambu Lab fleets. Bambu Farm Manager is free and Bambu-native, but SimplyPrint adds AI failure detection, camera monitoring, and broader compatibility if the farm ever includes non-Bambu printers.

**SimplyPrint Pricing (2026):**

| Plan | Monthly Cost | Printers |
|---|---|---|
| Free | $0 | Up to 2 printers |
| Basic | $5.39/month | Up to 5 printers |
| Pro | $8.99/month | Up to 10 printers |
| Print Farm | $31.49/month | Unlimited printers |

**For a 10-printer ModRun build-out: SimplyPrint Print Farm plan at $31.49/month.**

### SimplyPrint + Bambu Lab P1S Setup Checklist

The following steps are required before adding a P1S to SimplyPrint:

- [ ] Update P1S firmware to current stable release via Bambu Handy or Bambu Studio
- [ ] Unbind the P1S from Bambu Handy app (Settings > Account > Unbind)
- [ ] Disable LAN mode on P1S (Settings > Network > LAN mode off)
- [ ] Note printer serial number (back panel or Bambu Studio)
- [ ] In SimplyPrint: Add printer > Select Bambu Lab > P1S > Enter serial
- [ ] Confirm AMS detection in SimplyPrint dashboard (AMS mapping required for multi-color jobs)
- [ ] Configure print start options: First layer inspection, camera monitoring, failure detection sensitivity
- [ ] Test print with camera view active to confirm failure detection calibration
- [ ] Set up job queue: Import .3MF file, assign to printer or farm queue
- [ ] Configure filament profiles per spool loaded in AMS

**Bambu Farm Manager (free alternative):** For a pure-Bambu farm (all P1S or P1S + P1P), Bambu Farm Manager (cloud-free, Windows 10+, launched May 2025) handles fleet monitoring over local LAN with no subscription. Use Farm Manager for basic job dispatch; add SimplyPrint if AI failure detection or cross-brand compatibility is needed.
