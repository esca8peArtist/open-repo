## Glossary of Key Terms

**SKU:** Stock keeping unit (individual product)
**Wave:** Launch group (Wave 1 = 4 products July 1; Wave 2 = 4 products Aug 15; etc.)
**FDM:** Fused deposition modeling (extrusion-based 3D printing; Bambu P1S)
**COGS:** Cost of goods sold (material + hardware + labor)
**AOV:** Average order value (revenue per customer)
**Etsy fees:** Transaction fee (6.5%) + listing fee ($0.20) + payment processing (~3%)
**Gross margin %:** (Retail price - COGS) / Retail price × 100
**Net margin:** Gross margin minus Etsy fees and fulfillment costs
**Demand signal:** Proof of customer interest (Etsy listings, reviews, download counts, price points)
**Safety stock:** Minimum on-hand inventory to prevent stock-outs
**Reorder point:** Inventory level that triggers a new print run
