## Part 2: Etsy Listing Copy & SEO

### Primary Listing Title (140 characters max, SEO-critical)

**Title:** `Parametric Desk Clamp Headphone Hook | Cable Management | 3D Printed`

**Analysis:**
- Includes key search terms: "desk clamp," "headphone hook," "cable management," "3D printed"
- Demonstrates material (3D printed) and key feature (cable management)
- Length: 65 characters (well under limit)
- **Why this works:** Buyers searching "desk headphone hook" or "cable management desk" will surface this listing; "clamp" distinguishes from under-desk hangers

**Alternative titles (test A/B after launch):**
- `Desk Clamp Headphone Hanger | Parametric 3D Printed | Cable Wrap Post`
- `3D Printed Headphone Hook | Adjustable Desk Clamp | Cable Organizer`

### Listing Description

**Intro (first 2–3 sentences — hook the reader):**

> The Headphone Hook is a precision-engineered desk accessory designed for gaming setups, home offices, and creative workstations. Unlike basic under-desk hangers, this clamp grips your desk firmly without tools, supports any headphones up to 500g, and includes a cable-wrap post to keep your audio cables organized. Printed in durable PLA+ and finished with protective rubber pads.

**Section 1: What You Get**

> **DESIGN FEATURES:**
> - Parametric desk clamp (grips 12mm–40mm desk thicknesses) — no screws, no tools required
> - Integrated cable-wrap post for 4–8mm audio cables
> - Rounded hook arm (55mm reach) with gentle 8° upward angle — headphones stay on
> - Rubber protective pads on both clamp jaws (desk protection, no slipping)
> - Clean, minimalist aesthetic that matches modern desk setups
> 
> **MATERIAL & PRINTING:**
> - 100% Original design (CadQuery parametric, not adapted from existing models)
> - Printed in PLA+ — durable, food-safe resin, environmentally friendlier than ABS
> - 22–30g per unit depending on desk thickness
> - Print time: ~25 minutes on professional-grade 3D printer
> - No supports required; smooth print finish on all surfaces
>
> **BUNDLE OPPORTUNITY:**
> Pair this hook with our ModRun cable management system for a complete desk cable solution. Both products share the same minimalist aesthetic and work perfectly together. Use code CABLE15 for 15% off when purchasing both.

**Section 2: How It Works**

> **INSTALLATION (no tools required):**
> 1. Choose your desk thickness variant (12mm for thin desks like some IKEA models, 25mm for most home/office desks, 40mm for standing desks or thick worktops)
> 2. Open the clamp and slide it over your desk edge
> 3. The jaws grip firmly from above and below — position where desired
> 4. Hang headphones on the hook arm; wrap cable around the post to prevent slack
> 5. Rubber pads protect both your desk and the headphones
> 
> **HOW THE CLAMP GRIPS:**
> The upper jaw (desk-contact side) has a rubber pad. The lower jaw (underside) has a second rubber pad. Together, they create a non-slip grip that won't damage your desk finish and won't slide sideways when you hang/remove headphones.
> 
> **WHY THE CABLE POST:**
> Most headphone hooks are missing a cable management solution. The integrated 8mm-diameter post lets you loop excess cable to prevent tangling, slack, and that messy look. The post top is rounded to prevent cable abrasion.

**Section 3: Technical Specifications**

> **DIMENSIONS & WEIGHT:**
> - Total width: 38mm
> - Clamp reach: 28mm (upper jaw), 22mm (lower jaw overhang)
> - Hook arm reach: 55mm horizontal from clamp body
> - Hook arm height: 25mm above desk surface
> - Weight: 22–30g (depending on desk thickness variant)
> 
> **DESK THICKNESS OPTIONS:**
> - **12mm:** For thin desk tops (some IKEA models, laminate surfaces)
> - **25mm:** For standard home/office desks (most common; 18–30mm thickness)
> - **40mm:** For standing desks, thick wood worktops, or specialty desks (30–45mm thickness)
> 
> **MAXIMUM LOAD:**
> - Designed for headphones up to ~500g (most over-ear headphones)
> - Cable post rated for 4–8mm cables only (not designed for thick bundles)
> - Clamp is not rated for continuous pulling force; install firmly and test stability before loading

**Section 4: Why This Design**

> **ORIGINAL DESIGN — NOT 3D PRINTED FROM A FREE MODEL:**
> We designed this hook from scratch using parametric CAD, printed it ourselves, and validated it through real-world testing. Every unit is printed in-house on professional equipment and quality-checked before shipping. This design is our intellectual property; Etsy June 2025 compliance verified.
> 
> **DIFFERENTIATION:**
> This is the only desk clamp headphone hook on Etsy with an integrated cable-wrap post. Competitors offer either a basic under-desk hanger (no clamp, no cable organization) OR a monitor-mount hook (limited desk compatibility). We combined the best of both: a parametric clamp that works on any desk thickness, plus cable management built in.
> 
> **PERFECT PAIRING WITH MODRUN:**
> If you own our ModRun cable management system, this hook completes the ecosystem. ModRun routes all your cables; this hook organizes audio cables specifically. Both share the same minimalist design language.

**Section 5: Shipping & Returns**

> **SHIPPING:**
> Each unit is carefully wrapped in bubble wrap and shipped via USPS First Class in a padded poly mailer. Typical delivery: 3–7 business days to most US locations.
> 
> **RETURNS & REPLACEMENTS:**
> If your hook arrives damaged or doesn't fit your desk (wrong thickness variant), contact us within 7 days of delivery. We'll diagnose the issue and send a replacement immediately at no cost. No returns needed for wrong-variant orders — we'll resend the correct size.
> 
> **GUARANTEE:**
> We stand behind our designs. If the clamp doesn't grip your desk or the cable post breaks under normal use within 30 days, we'll replace it free.

**Section 6: Care & Tuning**

> **FIRST-TIME SETUP:**
> When your hook arrives, test the clamp on your desk. If the grip is too tight or too loose, contact us — we can tune the tolerance on your next order. FDM (3D printing) tolerances vary slightly by printer; we've calibrated ours for standard desk thicknesses, but minor adjustments are sometimes needed.
> 
> **CARING FOR YOUR HOOK:**
> - Clean with a dry cloth or gentle soap and water
> - The rubber pads protect your desk and may leave a light residue; clean with rubbing alcohol if needed
> - Don't hang objects heavier than 500g on the hook arm
> - The cable post is not designed for yanking cables; use it as a gentle cable loop, not a storage solution for bundled cables
> - PLA+ is durable but not heat-resistant; keep away from hot environments (> 60°C)

**Section 7: The Story Behind This Design**

> We created this hook because we got tired of basic desk organization. Headphone hangers that screw into your desk leave holes. Monitor-mount hooks only work if you have a monitor arm. Cable clutter turns a nice setup into a mess. So we designed something parametric: a clamp that works on any desk, sized for any headphones, with integrated cable management. Three years of desk setup iteration led here.
>
> All our products start with a problem we're solving for ourselves. This one is here because we needed it.

## Part 3: Photography Brief

### Photo Session Setup (2–3 hours)

**Location:**
- User's desk or well-lit workspace with natural light from window
- Clean, organized desk aesthetic (not cluttered)
- Neutral background (wall, or blurred background for lifestyle shots)

**Lighting:**
- Natural window light (best) or 2 desk lamps positioned 45° from subject
- Avoid harsh shadows across the clamp or hook arm
- Reflector or white poster board to fill shadows if needed

**Camera / Phone:**
- Phone with good camera (iPhone 12+ or equivalent Android flagship)
- Tripod for stability
- Clean lens before shooting

### Required Shots (14 total images for Etsy gallery)

#### Group 1: Product Shots (white/clean background)

**1. Main product shot (top-down angle)**
- Clamp body centered, hook arm pointing toward camera
- Hook in "at rest" position (not loaded with headphones)
- Rubber pads visible on jaw surfaces
- Cable post visible rising from hook arm
- Clean white/light background (poster board or lightbox)
- Sharp focus on entire hook (use macro mode if available)

**2. Side view (hook arm profile)**
- Shows the hook arm angle, cable post position, and overall assembly
- Hook arm horizontal and slightly upward tilt visible
- Clamp jaw open (not clamped on anything) so interior geometry visible
- Same white background

**3. Close-up: Clamp jaw detail**
- Macro shot of one jaw showing rubber pad pocket and pad itself
- Clear focus on the pad recess and Bumpon foot
- Shows scale (can include coin or ruler for reference)
- White background

**4. Close-up: Cable post detail**
- Macro shot of cable post showing rounded top, 8mm diameter, and connection to hook arm
- Post tip should be in sharp focus
- Shows the post's ability to hold cable loops

**5. Transparent/cutaway view (optional but high-impact)**
- Hook placed on translucent acrylic stand showing internal geometry
- Cable post vertical position and clamp jaw profile visible
- Advanced shot; skip if not feasible

#### Group 2: Lifestyle Shots (real desk context)

**6. Hero lifestyle shot: Hook on desk with headphones**
- Hook installed on a real desk (25mm thickness preferred)
- Headphones hung on hook (over-ear preferred; shows typical use)
- Cable looped around the cable post
- Desk should look organized and professional (desk lamp, keyboard, monitor, ModRun cables in background if available)
- Natural lighting from window or soft desk lamp
- Photo should make the viewer want the hook (aspirational, not clinical)

**7. Ecosystem shot: Hook + ModRun together**
- Hook installed on desk
- ModRun cables routed nearby
- Both products in same frame showing aesthetic match
- Implies: "These products work together"

**8. Desk variant 1: Thin desk setup (12mm)**
- Hook installed on IKEA desk or thin desk top
- Shows the thin-desk use case
- Indicates that hook fits thin desks too

**9. Desk variant 2: Thick/standing desk setup (40mm)**
- Hook installed on thick wooden desk or standing desk
- Shows the thick-desk use case
- Reassures standing-desk buyers that clamp will fit

**10. Installation sequence: Three-frame carousel (optional)**
- Frame 1: Clamp open, approaching desk edge
- Frame 2: Clamp closing on desk
- Frame 3: Hook installed, ready to use

#### Group 3: Comparison & Context Shots

**11. Before/after: Without hook vs. with hook**
- Left half: Desk setup with headphones lying on desk (messy)
- Right half: Same desk with hook installed (clean)
- Emphasizes the organizational benefit

**12. Desk setup aesthetic: Hook in context**
- Overhead view of entire desk with hook installed
- Shows how hook integrates into overall workspace aesthetic
- Include mouse, keyboard, monitor, monitor stand for context

**13. Cable management focus**
- Close-up: Cable looped around the post, showing slack prevention
- Shows the "cable wrapping" feature in action

**14. Sizing/comparison shot (optional)**
- Hook next to common objects (coin, pen, tape measure) for scale
- Helps buyer visualize actual size

### Photo Editing (post-processing)

**Minimum editing (Snapseed or built-in phone editor):**
- Crop: Frame subject to fill 80% of image, leaving 20% for whitespace
- Brightness: +10–20% for indoor shots (don't overexpose)
- Contrast: +5–10% to make product pop
- Saturation: Leave at default or -5% (slightly muted color looks premium)
- Sharpness: +10% if blurry; leave at default if sharp
- **Do NOT:** Heavy filters, HDR, artificial color shifts

**File format & size:**
- Export as JPEG, 72 DPI, 2000×2000px minimum (Etsy requires min. 1000×1000px)
- File size: 500KB–2MB per image
- Filename: `headphone-hook-01-main.jpg`, `headphone-hook-02-side.jpg`, etc.

## Part 6: Post-Launch Monitoring Dashboard

### Key Metrics (Track Weekly)

| Metric | Target | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| **Views/week** | 50–100 | Indicates Etsy algorithm visibility |
| **Favorites/week** | 5–10 | Indicates purchase intent |

| **Customer Q&A** | <2 hrs response time | Etsy rewards fast, helpful responses |
| **Inventory turns** | 1–1.5x per week | At 20 units/week, inventory should move quickly |

### Customer Feedback Focus Areas

**Questions to monitor in Q&A:**
- "Will this fit my desk?" → Indicates need for clearer thickness guidance (add photos of each variant on different desk types)
- "Does the cable post hold thick cables?" → Indicates need for material strength clarification (add spec that post is for 4–8mm cables, not thick bundles)
- "How do I install this?" → Indicates installation steps need to be clearer (consider adding photo carousel installation guide)
- "What color options?" → Indicates demand for variants (start planning color SKUs if >3 asks)

**Review sentiment to track:**
- Positive: "Fits perfectly," "Great quality," "Love the cable post," "Matches my ModRun"
- Negative (watch for): "Too tight," "Too loose," "Broke on arrival," "Cable post too thin," "Confusing desk-thickness sizing"

### Profitability Dashboard

| Metric | Healthy Target | Warning Sign |
|--------|---|---|
| **Weekly net profit** | $200+ (at 15+ units/week) | <$100/week = low traction |
| **Customer acquisition cost** | <$2 per sale (organic Etsy) | >$5/sale = affordability issue |
| **Return rate** | <2% | >5% = potential design flaw |
