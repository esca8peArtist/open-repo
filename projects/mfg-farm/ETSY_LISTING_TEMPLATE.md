# Etsy Listing Template — ModRun Cable Clip

---

## 1. Title Formula

**Pattern**: `[Primary keyword phrase] — [Key differentiator] | [Use-case modifier]`

- 60–80 characters maximum (Etsy's 2026 NLP soft cap; over 70 chars triggers mobile demotion)
- Primary keyword phrase must appear in the first 40 characters, intact
- Do not invert phrase order ("Clips Cable Management" instead of "Cable Management Clips")
- Occasion and gift language belongs in tags, not the title

**Pre-filled for ModRun cable clip (3-pack, C-01):**

```
Cable Management Clips — 3D Printed Modular | Desk Wire Organizer
```

(66 characters; "Cable Management Clips" in first 40)

**For future SKUs, substitute:**

| Field | Replace With |
|---|---|
| Primary keyword phrase | The exact noun-first phrase buyers type for that product |
| Key differentiator | The single feature competitors don't have |
| Use-case modifier | Who or where this product is used |

## 5. Price Field — Margin Math Embedded

**Pre-filled for ModRun cable clip 3-pack (C-01) at 20 units/week volume:**

| Input Variable | Value | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Filament cost per gram (PLA+, bulk rate) | $0.022/g | $18.99/kg + 15% scrap buffer |
| Print weight (3-pack total) | 14g | COMMODITY_PRODUCT_LIBRARY_Q3_2026.md |
| Material cost | $0.31 | 14g × $0.022 |
| Machine time (P1S depreciation + electricity) | 0.55 hrs | Library: 0.55 hrs print time |
| Machine cost per hour | $0.28/hr | $0.08 depreciation + $0.03 electricity + wear parts |
| Machine cost per unit | $0.15 | 0.55 × $0.28 |
| Packaging (mailer + tissue + card + tape) | $0.35 | Standard packaging kit |
| Subtotal COGS (before platform fees) | **$0.81** | |
| Etsy transaction fee (6.5%) on $12.99 | $0.84 | |
| Etsy payment processing (3% + $0.25) | $0.64 | |
| Etsy listing fee (amortized) | $0.20 | Per unit sold |
| Total Etsy fees | **$1.68** | 12.9% of sale price |
| Subtotal costs | $2.49 | COGS + fees |
| **Floor price** (0% net margin) | **$2.49** | Do not sell below this |
| **Target price (30% net margin)** | **~$9.99** | Floor ÷ 0.70 = ~$3.56; then price must cover shipping too |
| **Launch price (free shipping, 82.8% gross margin)** | **$12.99** | Includes ~$4.50 shipping cost absorbed; net margin $10.75 |
| Post-review price (after 15+ reviews) | **$14.99** | Net margin $12.75 (85.1%) |

**Price decision rule**: Never launch above $16.99 without 15+ reviews. The Etsy algorithm penalizes new listings with above-market prices during the 14–21 day recency window. Use the floor + velocity math, not competitor prices alone, to set the number.

## 6. Photo Checklist (References LISTING_PHOTOS_GUIDE.md 10-Shot List)

Complete all 10 shots before listing. Use the priority sequence: Stage 1 before listing; Stages 2–4 within the first month.

| Shot | Description | Stage | Required Before Listing? |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Hero — white background, product at 45°, fills 85% of frame | Stage 1 | YES — launch blocker |
| 2 | Lifestyle hero — cable inserted, mounted on desk surface in soft focus | Stage 1 | YES — launch blocker |
| 3 | 3/4 angle view showing channel opening, snap arm, and base | Stage 3 | No |
| 4 | In-use — clip mounted, cable routed through, full installation context | Stage 2 | No |
| 5 | Snap-fit detail macro (f/8–f/11; side lighting; snap arm engaged) | Stage 2 | No |
| 6 | Scale reference — clip next to US quarter or 30cm ruler | Stage 3 | No |
| 7 | Color variants flat-lay — all available colors overhead 90° | Stage 1 | YES — launch blocker |
| 8 | 3-pack bundle contents — three clips + packaging, overhead | Stage 3 | No |
| 9 | Before/after — same desk section: tangled cables left, clipped right | Stage 2 | No |
| 10 | Full desk context — 3–4 clips visible in natural positions | Stage 3 | No |
| Video | 15 seconds max: cable loose → snap to desk edge → cable routed | Stage 4 | No (but provides ranking lift) |

**Amazon main image rule**: Shot 1 must be pure white (RGB 255,255,255) to be Amazon-compliant. Test with Canva color picker — if the background reads as light gray rather than 255/255/255, reshoot or use Remove.bg to replace the background.

**Minimum viable set for launch**: Shots 1, 2, 7. These three answer "what is it," "how does it work," and "what color do I want?"

## 8. Shop "About" Section — First-Launch Copy

This copy goes in the Etsy shop "About" section. It is read by the Etsy algorithm as a trust and relevancy signal, and by buyers who want to verify that the shop is active and legitimate before purchasing.

**Pre-written copy — customize bracketed fields:**

---

### The ModRun Story

[YOUR SHOP NAME] designs and manufactures functional accessories for home offices, gaming setups, and workshops — all printed to order in the USA.

Every product starts as a problem: cables that slide off a desk, headphones with nowhere to go, toolboxes where you can never find the right bit. We design the solution in CAD, test it on a Bambu P1S, and make it available to order the same day we confirm it works.

**Why 3D printed?**

Because injection-molded products are designed for mass appeal and manufactured in batches of 10,000. 3D printing lets us make exactly what a home office actually needs — a modular clip that fits your specific desk edge, in your specific color — with no minimum order quantity and no warehouse full of parts nobody asked for.

**What we make**

Our flagship product is the ModRun cable management system: a clip-and-rail system that mounts to any desk edge without adhesive, drilling, or tools, and organizes USB-C, power, and headphone cables into a clean managed path. The same clip system works for standing desks and sitting desks.

We also make under-desk headphone hooks, magnetic workshop labels, and a growing catalog of desk accessories — all designed to work together aesthetically and practically.

**Made to order**

We print every order after you place it. Standard processing is 3–5 business days, but most orders ship within 24–48 hours. If you have a specific size, color, or configuration need, message us before ordering — we can usually accommodate it.
