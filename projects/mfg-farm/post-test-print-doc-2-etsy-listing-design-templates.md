## 4. Pricing Template — Cost Breakdown & Margin Model

### COGS Calculation (Per Unit)

Use this formula to set your price. Adjust {{VARIABLES}} based on your actual costs.

```
MATERIAL COST:
- Filament per unit: {{FILAMENT_GRAMS}} grams
- Filament cost: {{COST_PER_KG}}/kg (wholesale rate)
- Material cost per unit: ({{FILAMENT_GRAMS}} / 1000) × {{COST_PER_KG}} = ${{MATERIAL_COST}}

LABOR COST:
- Time per clip: {{LABOR_MINUTES}} minutes (includes design, printing setup, QC)
- Your labor rate: ${{LABOR_RATE}}/hour (what you value your time at)
- Labor per unit: ({{LABOR_MINUTES}} / 60) × ${{LABOR_RATE}} = ${{LABOR_COST}}

PACKAGING & SHIPPING:
- Mailer/box: ${{PACKAGING_COST}}
- Shipping label: ${{LABEL_COST}} (at volume via Pirate Ship)
- Packaging per unit: ${{TOTAL_PACKAGING}}

OVERHEAD (electricity, rent, equipment depreciation):
- Monthly overhead: ${{MONTHLY_OVERHEAD}} (estimate or actual)
- Units per month: {{UNITS_PER_MONTH}}
- Overhead per unit: ${{MONTHLY_OVERHEAD}} / {{UNITS_PER_MONTH}} = ${{OVERHEAD_PER_UNIT}}

GROSS COST PER UNIT:
${{MATERIAL_COST}} + ${{LABOR_COST}} + ${{TOTAL_PACKAGING}} + ${{OVERHEAD_PER_UNIT}} = ${{GROSS_COGS}}

PLATFORM & PAYMENT FEES:
- Etsy transaction fee: 6.5% of sale price
- Payment processing: 3% + $0.25
- Blended fee: ~9.75% of sale price (or use 10% for simplicity)

TARGET MARGIN:
- Target net margin: {{TARGET_MARGIN_PERCENT}}% (recommend 65%+ for 3D printing)
- Required sale price to hit target: ${{GROSS_COGS}} / (1 - ({{TARGET_MARGIN_PERCENT}} / 100) - 0.10)

RECOMMENDED RETAIL PRICE: ${{FINAL_PRICE}}
```

---

### Example Calculation (Cable Clip, Single Unit)

```
MATERIAL: 5 grams × $12/kg = $0.06
LABOR: 3 minutes × $20/hour = $1.00
PACKAGING: $0.40 (mailer + label)
OVERHEAD: $50/month ÷ 100 units = $0.50

GROSS COGS: $1.96

REQUIRED PRICE for 65% net margin with 10% fees:
$1.96 / (1 - 0.65 - 0.10) = $1.96 / 0.25 = $7.84

ROUND UP TO: $7.99 or $8.99 (depending on perceived value)

ACTUAL MATH AT $7.99 sale price:
- Etsy fees (10%): -$0.80
- COGS: -$1.96
- NET PROFIT: $7.99 - $0.80 - $1.96 = $5.23 per unit (65% margin) ✓
```

## 6. Photo Positioning Guide — Etsy Algorithm Favors First Photo Heavily

Etsy's algorithm ranks listings partly on engagement with the primary (first) photo. If your first photo has a low click-through rate, your entire listing gets lower placement. This is critical.

### Photo 1: Hero Lifestyle Shot (MOST CRITICAL)

**What it should show**:
- ModRun clip on a real desk, holding a real cable
- The clip should be the clear focal point
- Cable should show clearly threaded through the snap-fit channel
- Background: clean, minimal, neutral (white, light gray, or natural wood)
- Desk surface should look premium/aspirational (not cluttered, not cheap-looking)

**Why this matters**:
- Etsy shows this photo in search results at thumbnail size
- Low-quality or unclear photos = low click-through = low algorithmic boost
- This photo is where you win or lose the click

**Photo specifications**:
- Resolution: 1000×1000px minimum (Etsy recommendation)
- Aspect ratio: Square (1:1) for consistency across all photos
- Lighting: Soft natural light from a window (north-facing, no harsh shadows)
- Depth of field: f/4–f/8 (cable and clip in focus, slight background blur)
- Color grading: Neutral white balance (no warm/cool tint)

**Example composition**:
```
FOREGROUND: ModRun black clip snapping cable in place
MIDGROUND: Cable laid across clean light-gray desk
BACKGROUND: Subtle (blurred), natural wood or white wall
PROPS: Minimal (maybe one other desk item like a keyboard to show scale, but don't clutter)
```

---

### Photo 2: Product Detail Shot (Zoom on Snap-Fit Mechanism)

**What it should show**:
- Close-up of the snap-fit channel
- Cable actually inside the channel, mid-snap
- Shows the engineering/precision of the design
- Background: same neutral color for consistency

**Why it matters**:
- Builds trust in the design ("this looks engineered, not crude")
- Answers the question "How does it actually hold the cable?"

**Specifications**:
- Resolution: 1000×1000px
- Focal length: 50–100mm macro or close-focusing lens
- Depth of field: f/5.6–f/8 (cable and snap-fit sharp, edges slightly soft)
- Light: Same soft natural light as Photo 1

---

### Photo 3: Installation Sequence (Before/After)

**What it should show**:
- Left half: Cables loose/messy on desk (the "before")
- Right half: Same desk with ModRun clips installed, organized (the "after")
- Emotional hook: Clean → Mess solved

**Why it matters**:
- Shows the transformation and value proposition
- Buyers envision their own desk transformation

**Composition**:
```
LEFT SIDE: Desk cables tangled, lying loose, messy appearance
RIGHT SIDE: Same desk, same angle, cables routed through ModRun clips, organized
DIVIDING LINE: Subtle vertical line or arrow pointing to "after"
```

---

### Photo 4: Color Variant Display (If Offering Multiple Colors)

**What it should show**:
- Flat-lay view of ModRun clips in all available colors (black, white, gray, custom)
- All clips shown at same angle for easy comparison
- Clean background (white or light gray)
- Minimal props (maybe one cable to show scale)

**Why it matters**:
- Buyers who want a specific color see immediately what's available
- Color options are a key conversion factor (buyers love choice)

**Flat-lay specifications**:
- Camera directly overhead (90° angle to surface)
- Even lighting across all clips (use bounce boards if needed)
- White or light gray background (poster board works)
- Each clip clearly visible, no overlapping

### Photo 5: In-Context Variant (Optional But Recommended)

**What it should show**:
- ModRun clips in a real workspace (office desk, gaming rig, home office, etc.)

## 7. FAQ/QA Section (Copy-Paste Answers)

Etsy allows sellers to add a FAQ section and also manage buyer questions via the Q&A feature. Pre-write answers to common questions so you can paste responses quickly.

### Pre-Written FAQ Answers (Copy-Paste Ready)

**Q: What material are these made from?**

A: ModRun clips are printed in food-safe PLA+ plastic (a toughened version of standard PLA) or PETG depending on the product tier. Look at your listing variant — it will specify which material you're ordering.

PLA+ is rated to 60°C ambient temperature and is best for indoor use. PETG is more heat- and impact-resistant, rated to 80°C, and is recommended if your desk is in a warm environment or gets direct sunlight.

---

**Q: Will these scratch my cables or desk surface?**

A: No. ModRun cable channels are smooth-walled with no sharp edges — the geometry is specifically designed to avoid abrasion. The snap-fit mechanism applies pressure gently, not aggressively.

If you're mounting the rail directly to a desk surface you want to protect, I recommend putting a thin foam mounting tape (like 3M Command strips) between the rail base and the desk. The clip itself has no sharp burrs or rough transitions.

---

**Q: Can I get a custom color?**

A: Yes. Black, white, and gray are my standard in-stock colors and ship on the 3–5 day timeline.

Other colors (blue, red, green, translucent, or matte finishes) are available as custom orders. Message me with your color request before ordering and I'll confirm availability and lead time — usually 1 extra business day for a custom color. Color availability depends on current filament stock.

---

**Q: How do I install the ModRun clip?**

A: Installation is simple and requires no special tools:

1. Choose your mounting surface (desk edge, rail, or wall)
2. If mounting to a surface: use M3 hardware (I include hardware for Pro tier; standard tier is adhesive or command strips only)
3. Flush-mount the clip to the surface
4. Press your cable into the snap-fit channel until you hear a click
5. Done

To remove a clip: Press the small engagement tab on the side and slide the clip out. The cable pops free cleanly.

The installation guide with photos is included in the product listing images. Message me if you have any questions.

---

**Q: What if my order arrives damaged?**

A: Contact me within 14 days of delivery with a photo showing the damage. I will send a replacement at no cost or issue a full refund — your choice. You do not need to return the damaged item.

I aim to respond to all messages within 2 hours during business hours (9 AM–6 PM Central, Monday–Friday).

---

**Q: Can I order in bulk for an office or studio?**

A: Yes. Orders of 20+ units qualify for a bulk discount. Message me with:
- Quantity
- Product (single clip, 3-pack, etc.)
- Color preferences
- Any custom requirements

I'll create a custom listing with adjusted pricing. Typical bulk lead time is 7–10 business days depending on order size. Corporate invoicing is available on request.

---

**Q: How long will these last?**

A: PLA+ parts are stable at indoor temperatures indefinitely. The mechanical snap engagement is tested for 100+ engage/disengage cycles before shipping. For a cable that's set once and left in place, there's no practical wear-out mechanism.

PETG Pro tier parts are rated for more demanding environments (higher ambient temperature, outdoor-adjacent spaces, more frequent cable swaps).

Avoid leaving PLA clips in direct sunlight for extended periods — UV exposure will cause discoloration over time, though it doesn't affect structural performance for indoor use.

---

**Q: Do these work with {{CABLE_TYPE}}?**

A: ModRun clips work with any cable 4–12mm in diameter. This includes:
- USB-C, USB-A, USB Mini, USB Micro
- 3.5mm audio jacks
- HDMI, DisplayPort, Thunderbolt
- Power cables (up to ~10mm diameter)
- Ethernet (both straight and coiled)

If you have an unusual cable type, message me with dimensions and I'll confirm compatibility before you order.

---

**Q: Can I combine orders for a discount?**

A: I don't offer multi-item discounts on standard orders, but my bundle packages (3-pack, 5-pack) are already discounted compared to single-clip pricing.

If you're ordering 20+ clips for an office installation, reach out about bulk pricing — see the FAQ about bulk orders above.

---

**Q: How long does shipping actually take?**

A: 
- **Processing**: 1–2 business days (I print after you order)
- **Shipping**: 5–8 business days via USPS (depending on your location and mail volume)
- **Total**: Plan for 7–10 business days from order to delivery

Tracking is included. You'll receive a shipping notification email with your USPS tracking number as soon as I drop the package off.

If you need something by a specific date, message me before ordering and I'll let you know if I can guarantee it.

---

**Q: What if I need this faster?**

A: I can often accommodate rush orders for an extra fee. Message me with your target delivery date and I'll confirm whether I can print and ship faster, and quote any rush fee if applicable.

Standard lead time is 3–5 business days of printing + 5–8 days shipping = 8–13 days total. If you need it in 4–5 days, I can probably do it by printing overnight and using Priority Mail or Express shipping.

### Tips for Managing Q&A Section

1. **Enable Q&A early** — Customers can ask questions before you even have sales. Answer every question within 24 hours; it signals reliability.

## Decision Thresholds — When to Act

### If <50 views by Day 3

**Root cause**: Photos not appealing, tags not matching buyer search intent, or listing just not indexed yet

**Action**:
1. Check: Is your listing visible when you search "cable management clip"? If no, give Etsy 24 more hours.
2. If visible: Swap primary photo to the in-context lifestyle shot (Photo 3). Often the most compelling.
3. Check Etsy Ads: If you're paying for ads and getting zero clicks, the issue is definitely the photo.
4. If still <50 views by Day 5: Rewrite the description with more keywords. Add "3D printed" to the first sentence if it's not already there.

---

### If 50+ views but 0 sales by Day 7

**Root cause**: Buyers are interested (clicks prove that) but price, description, or reviews not convincing them to buy

**Action**:
1. Price check: Are you priced 20%+ higher than competitors? If yes, reduce by $1-2 for 1 week A/B test.
2. Description check: Is your value proposition clear in the first 2 sentences? If not, clarify and republish.
3. Review check: You have zero reviews. This is normal for new shops. Don't panic. First 10-20 reviews take time.
4. Photo check: Do all photos load correctly? Test by viewing listing on mobile phone.

---

### If CTR <2% (few clicks despite views)

**Root cause**: Primary photo isn't compelling enough to click

**Action**:
1. Swap primary photo for Photo 3 (before/after installation)
2. Wait 48 hours; monitor CTR

## Etsy Ads Configuration

### Budget: Start at $1/day

**Why $1/day**:
- Low commitment (tests the water)
- Generates 50-100 impressions/day at typical Etsy CPC rates
- Reveals which tags drive real clicks vs. false impressions
- Total spend: $30/month, recoverable with 5-10 sales at $7-15 each

**Setup**:
1. Go to Shop Manager → Marketing → Etsy Ads
2. Create campaign: "ModRun Cable Clips Launch"
3. Select listings to advertise: All of them (get data on all SKUs)
4. Set daily budget: $1.00
5. Monitor daily: Impressions should start within 24 hours

**Read Data**:
- **Impressions**: Number of times your ads were shown
- **Clicks**: Number of clicks to your listing
- **CTR**: Clicks ÷ Impressions (aim for 2-5%)
- **CPC**: Cost per click (Etsy sets this; typically $0.05-0.20 for new shops)
- **Conversions**: Sales from ad clicks (track which ads drive sales)

## Key Principles (Don't Violate These)

1. **The first photo is 50% of your CTR** — Invest time here. Test 2-3 variations if needed.

2. **Tags drive impressions; quality drives conversions** — Good tags get you views. Good description/price/reviews get you sales.

3. **Title + first 150 words of description appear in search** — Every word counts. No fluff.

4. **New shops have zero reviews** — This is normal. Don't compare yourself to 5-year-old sellers. Your job is to get to 20 reviews; then your listing becomes visible to more buyers.

5. **Price is not your friend at launch** — You have no reviews. A lower price ($7.99 vs. $9.99) dramatically increases trial likelihood. Once you have 50+ reviews, you can raise price.

6. **Monitor daily for 7 days, then weekly** — First week is noisy. Trends emerge by Week 2.
