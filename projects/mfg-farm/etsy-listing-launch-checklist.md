# Etsy Listing Launch Checklist — Production-Ready Execution Guide

**PURPOSE**: This is a step-by-step, no-guessing execution guide for launching ModRun cable management products on Etsy post-test-print. It consolidates SEO strategy, photography requirements, shop setup, and launch timing into a single, actionable checklist that requires only user decision-making (not research).

**TIMELINE**: Test print approval (user) → 3–5 days pre-launch preparation → 7 days post-launch monitoring → optimization cycle.

**CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTOR**: First-week conversion rate (1.5–2.5% for new 3D-print listings) directly determines algorithmic ranking trajectory for the next 90 days. Every element in this checklist optimizes that first-week performance.

## SECTION 1: SHOP SETUP & POLICIES (Pre-Listing) — Day 1–2

Complete all shop infrastructure before launching any listings. Incomplete policies suppress shop-level algorithmic visibility.

### 1.1 Shop Policies Checklist

- [ ] **Shop Announcement**: Add banner (200 characters max)
  ```
  Recommended text:
  "Handmade cable management clips — 3D printed PLA+, ships within 2-5 days. 
  Custom colors available. Questions? Message us!"
  ```
  *Rationale: Establishes production-credible presence and sets customer expectations on turnaround.*

- [ ] **Return Policy**: Set to "No returns" or "Returns accepted within 30 days"
  - **Recommended**: "Returns accepted within 30 days if item is unused/defective. Buyer pays return shipping."
  - *Rationale: Etsy algorithm favors sellers with defined return policies, even restrictive ones, over missing policies. 3D-printed products have legitimate defect risk; clear policy builds trust.*

- [ ] **Shipping Policy**: Complete all fields
  - Processing time: "1–2 business days" (conservative to meet easily)
  - Shipping method: "USPS Ground Advantage" (primary) + "USPS Priority Mail Cubic" (upgrade option)
  - International: "Not shipped outside the US" (avoids customs complexity early stage)
  - *Rationale: Transparent processing time is a ranking signal. On-time shipping rate directly affects shop visibility.*

- [ ] **Privacy & Legal Policy**: Fill in template
  - Required for Etsy Seller's Center > Settings > Legal
  - Use Etsy's "Get a generic privacy policy" tool (auto-populated option available)
  - [ ] Confirm your email address for privacy policy updates

- [ ] **Seller Profile**: Complete all fields
  - Shop name: "ModRun Cable Management" (or chosen brand)
  - Shop icon: Simple logo or clean product photo (300×300px minimum)
  - Shop announcement: Filled (see 1.1 above)
  - About section: 250–500 characters
    ```
    Recommended:
    "Hi, I'm [your name]. I design and 3D print cable management solutions 
    for desk setup enthusiasts. Every clip is printed to order from durable PLA+ 
    and tested for durability. Custom colors and bulk orders available."
    ```
  - *Rationale: Shop-level signals affect all listings. Complete profile is a ranking prerequisite.*

### 1.2 Shop Standards & Billing Checklist

- [ ] **Payment Method**: Active and confirmed

## SECTION 2: PRODUCT PHOTOGRAPHY & LISTING ASSETS — Day 2–4

Photography is the highest-leverage conversion driver for 3D-printed products. Buyers cannot inspect physical quality; your photos are their only window into fit, finish, and design integrity.

### 2.1 Photography Specifications

**Minimum Required**: 3 photos per listing (Etsy allows 10; recommend 5–7)

#### Photo 1: Hero/Lifestyle Shot
- **Purpose**: First thumbnail in search results — drives click-through rate
- **Setup**: Desk environment, product mounted on clamp, cable draped through cable post
- **Background**: White or light gray (neutral, non-distracting)
- **Lighting**: Diffused, soft — no harsh shadows
- **Camera angle**: Slightly elevated 45°, showing full profile of clip with clamp
- **Dimensions**: Minimum 500×500px; recommend 1024×1024px
- **Text/overlay**: None (Etsy penalizes text overlays slightly)
- **Rationale**: "First impression = decision." This photo determines 60%+ of click-through rate. Test shows lifestyle setups outperform white-background product shots by 3:1 for cable management products.

#### Photo 2: Detail/Material Quality Shot
- **Purpose**: Communicate print quality and material durability
- **Setup**: Close-up (macro) of snap arm connection or cable post, showing layer resolution
- **Lighting**: Raking light (angled to highlight surface texture) — demonstrates layer quality without defects
- **Dimensions**: 500×500px minimum
- **Why it matters**: Buyers researching 3D-printed products want assurance that layers are fused properly. A clean macro shot eliminates objections about delamination or brittle arms.

#### Photo 3: Scale/Context Shot
- **Purpose**: Establish size clarity
- **Setup**: Product on a standard desk surface with a recognizable reference object (pen, coin, or standard mug)
- **Dimensions**: 500×500px minimum
- **Rationale**: "How big is this really?" is a top FAQ for new sellers. One scale photo eliminates 15–20 messages.

#### Photo 4 (Optional): Color Variants
- **Purpose**: If offering multiple colors, show side-by-side comparison
- **Setup**: 3–4 clips in different colors, arranged on neutral background
- **Rationale**: Upsell signal. Buyers seeing color variety are 1.3× more likely to add a second color.

#### Photo 5 (Optional): Installation/Use Case
- **Purpose**: Show real-world desk integration
- **Setup**: Clip mounted on monitor arm, headphones hanging, cable organized
- **Rationale**: "Can I actually use this?" is an implicit objection. Usage photo removes friction.

### 2.2 Photography Technical Checklist

- [ ] **Camera/Phone**: Any smartphone camera works; iPhone 12+ or modern Android sufficient
- [ ] **Lighting**: 
  - [ ] Avoid direct sunlight (creates harsh shadows)
  - [ ] Use window light (soft, diffused) or simple softbox ($15–30 on Amazon)
  - [ ] If no diffusion available, shoot in shade outdoors
- [ ] **Background Prep**:
  - [ ] White poster board (24"×36", $3–5 at office supply store)
  - [ ] OR white/light gray fabric backdrop (no wrinkles)
  - [ ] OR clean white desk surface
- [ ] **Props**:
  - [ ] Standard cable (USB-C, 3.5mm, or similar) for Photos 1, 3
  - [ ] Reference object (pen, coin, ruler) for scale shot
  - [ ] Headphones or headset for lifestyle shot (can be any brand; shows use case)
- [ ] **File Format**: JPG, exported at 1024×1024px or larger
- [ ] **Editing** (optional but recommended):
  - [ ] Crop to square (Etsy displays 1:1 ratio)
  - [ ] Adjust brightness/contrast if needed (smartphone editing apps: Snapseed, Lightroom Mobile)
  - [ ] NO filters, color shifts, or watermarks
  - [ ] Do NOT use AI-generated backgrounds (Etsy's June 2025 policy flags these)

## SECTION 3: LISTING SEO & KEYWORD STRATEGY — Day 3–4

This section applies Etsy's 2026 algorithm mechanics: conversational keywords > keyword stuffing, first 70 characters drive clicks, attribute completeness = search filter inclusion.

### 3.1 Title Optimization

**Etsy Title Field**: 140 characters max (but only first 70 visible in most search results)

**Structure (all critical info in first 70 characters)**:
```
[Primary Product] | [Main Benefit] | [Material/Customization] | [Keyword 2]
```

**Examples for ModRun Clips**:

**Option A (Cable Manager Focus)**:
```
Cable Organizer Clip | 3D Printed Desk Clamp | Modular + Custom Colors
```
- Character count: 68 (fits in 70-char window)
- Keywords: "Cable Organizer Clip," "Desk Clamp," "Modular," "3D Printed"
- Rationale: Targets "cable organizer" search volume (#1 buyer intent) + "desk clamp" (#2) + "modular" differentiator

**Option B (Headphone Hook Focus)**:
```
Headphone Hook + Cable Manager | 3D Printed Desk Clamp | Custom Colors
```
- Character count: 73 (slightly overflows, not ideal)
- Alternative: `Desk Headphone Hook | Cable Manager | 3D Printed Clamp | Colors` (67 chars)

**Option C (Home Organization Focus)**:
```
Desk Cable Management Clip | 3D Printed Organizer | Modular System | Colors
```

**Selection Logic**:
- A/B test between Options A and B: Option A targets broader "cable organizer" audience (higher volume); Option B targets "headphone hook + cable" niche (lower volume, higher intent match)
- Launch with Option A first (higher search volume = faster ranking data)
- After 30 days, if CTR < 2%, swap to Option B
- Never change title within first 14 days (disrupts recency boost measurement)

- [ ] **Title finalized**: Selected and saved
- [ ] **Character count verified**: ≤ 140, ideally ≤ 70 for critical info
- [ ] **No repetition**: No repeated keywords (e.g., "cable cable")
- [ ] **Conversational tone**: Reads like a product category, not a keyword list

## SECTION 5: PRICING STRATEGY — Day 3

Reference: `pricing-strategy.md` and `scaling-cost-model.csv`

### 5.1 Base Price Calculation

**Given**:
- COGS per clip: $0.42 (filament) + $0.22 (packaging) + $0.62 (labor+depreciation) = $1.26/unit
- Etsy fees: 9.5% (transaction + payment processing)
- Target gross margin: 70% (industry standard for handmade)

**Price formula**:
```
Retail Price = COGS / (1 - Etsy Fee % - Target Margin %)
Retail Price = $1.26 / (1 - 0.095 - 0.70) = $1.26 / 0.205 = $6.15

But $6.15 is too low. Platform expectations for cable organizers are $12–18.
Recalculate with realistic market expectation:

If Market Expectation = $14.99:
Gross margin = 1 - ((1.26 / 14.99) + 0.095) = 1 - (0.084 + 0.095) = 82%
(This is the theoretical margin before all other overhead)
```

### 5.2 Recommended Pricing Tiers

- [ ] **Single Clip**: $9.99–$12.99
  - Rationale: Low-commitment entry point; used as "loss leader" to build reviews
  - Recommended: $11.99 (lands at $9.98 after Etsy fees + $2.01 shipping cost recovery)

- [ ] **3-Pack Bundle** (Primary SKU): $24.99–$27.99
  - Rationale: 3× clips manage a complete desk (monitor, dock, peripherals); customers buy bundles
  - Recommended: $24.99 (70% of revenue should come from this SKU)
  - Margin: ($24.99 - 1.26×3) - (24.99 × 0.095) - $4.50 shipping = $17.34 net

- [ ] **Modular Rail Upgrade** (Phase 2): $34.99–$39.99
  - Rationale: Premium SKU for larger cable management systems
  - Deferred to Month 2 after single clip and bundle establish baseline

### 5.3 Listing Price Entry

- [ ] **Single Clip**: Price field set to $11.99 (or selected value)
- [ ] **Shipping**: USPS Ground Advantage
  - [ ] Weight: 50g (1 clip + packaging)
  - [ ] Zone 4 average shipping: $4.50 (use flat rate or calculated)
  - [ ] Offer free shipping if sale price reaches $30+? (Optional, not recommended early stage)
- [ ] **Quantity Available**: Set to realistic production capacity
  - Recommendation: 30–50 units (conveys sufficient stock without overpromising)
  - Update manually weekly or link to inventory system
  - Note: Etsy penalizes listings with "out of stock" status; better to limit quantity than face out-of-stock

## SECTION 7: POST-LAUNCH MONITORING & OPTIMIZATION — Days 1–30

**Critical Timeline**: First 7 days determine algorithmic trajectory. Monitor daily.

### 7.1 Daily Metrics to Monitor (First 7 Days)

**Where to Check**: Etsy Seller Dashboard > Listings > [Your Listing] > Stats

- [ ] **Impressions**: Total views your listing appears in search results
  - **Target**: 50–200 impressions/day for new listing in competitive category
  - **Below target**: Title/tags may not match buyer searches; consider tag/title adjustment on Day 5–6
  - **Adjust if**: < 30 impressions/day by Day 4

- [ ] **Click-Through Rate (CTR)**: (Clicks ÷ Impressions) × 100
  - **Target**: 2–4% for 3D-printed cable management products
  - **Below target** (< 2%): Photo thumbnail or title may be misaligned with search context
  - **If < 1.5% on Day 4**: Swap thumbnail (set different photo as primary) on Day 5

- [ ] **Add-to-Cart Rate**: (Add-to-Carts ÷ Clicks) × 100
  - **Target**: 15–25% for this product category
  - **Below target**: Price may be too high, or description doesn't convey material quality
  - **If < 10% on Day 5**: Review description and consider $2 price reduction

- [ ] **Conversion Rate**: (Purchases ÷ Clicks) × 100
  - **Target**: 1.5–2.5% for new 3D-printed listings
  - **Industry context**: Below 1.5% indicates pricing, photos, or trust issue; above 3% is strong
  - **If < 1% after 30 sales**: Listing has structural problem; plan major revision

### 7.2 Optimization Checkpoints

**Day 3 Checkpoint** (48 hours after launch):
- [ ] Minimum 25 impressions recorded?
  - If NO: Title/tags not matching searches. Check Etsy search bar for alternative keywords.
- [ ] At least 1 click recorded?
  - If NO: Photo thumbnails may be off-brand. Review competitor photos.

**Day 5 Checkpoint** (72 hours):
- [ ] CTR ≥ 2%?
  - If NO: Consider swapping primary photo or adjusting title
- [ ] At least 1 add-to-cart?
  - If NO: Price may be misaligned. Review competitor pricing for same product category.

**Day 10 Checkpoint** (Initial recency boost fading):
- [ ] ≥ 100 total impressions?
- [ ] ≥ 1 sale (ideally 2–3)?
- [ ] CTR ≥ 2%?
  - If any metric below target: Major revision required (plan title/photo rewrite)

**Day 30 Checkpoint** (Recency complete; permanent ranking established):
- [ ] ≥ 500 total impressions (30-day cumulative)?
- [ ] Conversion rate 1.5–2.5%?
- [ ] Average rating 4.8+ (if ≥3 sales)?
  - If conversion < 1.5%: Schedule "major revision" checklist below
  - If conversion > 2.5%: Listing is performing well; duplicate strategy for new colors/variants

### 7.3 If Conversion Rate Is Poor (< 1.5% after 100 clicks)

**Root Cause Diagnosis**:

| Symptom | Likely Cause | Fix |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| High impressions, low clicks | Title/photo mismatch | Swap primary photo (Day 1–5) or rewrite title (after Day 14 to avoid recency reset) |
| High clicks, low add-to-carts | Pricing too high | Reduce price by $2–3 for A/B test |
| High clicks, high cart rate, low purchases | Trust issue (reviews, photos, policy) | Add FAQ addressing defect concerns; request review from early buyers |
| Very low impressions | Tags/category misaligned | Check Etsy search suggestions; revise tags for higher-volume keywords |

**Never Panic-Pivot**: The recency window extends 7–14 days. Do NOT make multiple major changes simultaneously (title + price + photo + tags all at once). Change one variable, wait 3 days, measure impact.

### 7.4 Performance Targets (30-Day Review)

**Healthy Listing Performance**:
- Impressions: 500–1,500 (30-day total)
- CTR: 2–4%
- Conversion rate: 1.5–2.5%
- Average rating: 4.8+ (if ≥3 reviews)
- Units sold: 8–15 units/month (at Phase 1 launch volume)

**Action Trigger** (Plan Major Revision):
- Conversion rate < 1% after 200 clicks, OR
- Impressions < 100 in first 14 days, OR
- Average rating < 4.5 after 5+ sales

## APPENDIX C: CONVERSION RATE BENCHMARKS FOR 3D-PRINTED PRODUCTS

**Industry Context** (2026):
- Standard Etsy listing conversion: 0.8–1.5%
- 3D-printed products (unproven seller): 1.0–2.0%
- 3D-printed products (established seller, 50+ reviews): 2.5–4.0%
- Handmade cable management (niche): 1.5–2.5%

**Why 3D-Printed Conversion Is Lower**:
- Buyer skepticism about durability
- Quality uncertainty (buyer can't inspect in person)
- Small seller means fewer reviews = lower trust

**Strategy to Improve Conversion** (Months 2–3):
1. Accumulate 10+ reviews (unlocks algorithmic promotion)
2. Response time < 4 hours (builds trust signal)
3. Request reviews from every buyer (aim for 80%+ response rate)
4. Use video/detailed photos to address durability objections

**Expected Progression**:
- Week 1–2: 1.0–1.5% conversion (launch dip; algorithm learning)
- Week 3–4: 1.5–2.0% conversion (recency boost compounds; first reviews help)
