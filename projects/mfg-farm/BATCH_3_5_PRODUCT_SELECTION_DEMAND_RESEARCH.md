## Section 2: Design Time Estimates & CadQuery Feasibility

### Design Methodology: CadQuery Parametric System
All designs will use the CadQuery library (Python-based CAD) with timestamped git commits for Etsy original-design verification. Each design follows the ModRun pattern: parametric generators + tolerance tuning + test prints.

| Batch | Product | Design Scope | CadQuery Complexity | Estimate | Feasibility |
|-------|---------|---|---|---|---|
| 2 | Headphone Hook | Clamp body + cable-wrap post, 3 parameters (desk thickness, clamp width, post height) | Low–Medium | 2–3 hrs | ✅ HIGH — direct analog to ModRun rail clamps |
| 3 | Magnetic Bin Labels | Parametric tile (edge radius, magnet pocket tolerance, text field cutout) | Low | 1.5–2 hrs | ✅ HIGH — simple 2D profile + pocket extrusion |
| 4 | Garden Plant Markers | Stake geometry + emboss text (buyer-provided), ASA material handling | Low–Medium | 2–3 hrs | ✅ HIGH — single parametric profile, text field via Etsy customization (not CAD) |
| 5 | Pegboard Hook System | Parametric hook generator (J-hook variants, embossed text, modular assembly) | Medium | 4–5 hrs | ✅ MEDIUM — multi-part assembly, tolerance-critical holes |
| 6 | Monitor Riser Legs | Parametric leg variant generator (height + shelf thickness) + cable channel option | Medium | 5–6 hrs | ✅ MEDIUM — multi-parameter system, load-bearing tolerance critical |

**Critical path:** Design creation itself is not a blocker. All five products can be designed in parallel (total 14–19 hrs development), but manufacturing validation (test prints, tolerance tuning) follows serially: 1 week per product × 5 products = 5 weeks start-to-finish validation pipeline. This is absorbed by the post-test-print ramp schedule.
