## Part 1: Executive Decision Summary

Nine domains are in various states of Phase 2 readiness as of June 3, 2026. This document synthesizes the sequencing logic across all of them into a single decision framework. It answers four questions for each domain: (1) when does it distribute, (2) what can run in parallel with it, (3) which audiences does it reach, and (4) what would delay or break the timeline.

The most urgent action is not a research decision — it is a distribution decision. Domain 59 (Economic Precarity) and Domain 56 (Civil Service Politicization) both have June legislative windows that are either open now or closing. Neither requires additional research. ## Part 8: Synthesis Integration Points

### What Synthesis Requires

Phase 2 synthesis is the document that demonstrates the convergence of Phase 2 domains — the argument that domains 48, 49, 50, 51, 56, 57, 58, and 59, taken together, reveal a coordinated democratic dismantlement architecture rather than a collection of individual policy failures.

For synthesis to be analytically defensible, the following must be true before synthesis begins:
1. All primary research documents are complete and have passed quality gates
2. At least Tier 1 distribution has occurred for all domains — synthesis cites engagement signals, not just research production
3. Cross-domain integration sections in each domain document are accurate — synthesis depends on these connections being real, not asserted
