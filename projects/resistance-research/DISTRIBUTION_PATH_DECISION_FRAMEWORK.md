**Reversibility**: ✅ **Fully reversible**. Path A→A+37 can be executed immediately after initial Tier 1 outreach (Day 3–4), routing Domain 37 specifically to 12 election-protection organizations with messaging keyed to litigation dockets. No sunk cost or time conflict.
