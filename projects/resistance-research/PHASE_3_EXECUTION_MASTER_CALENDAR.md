# Phase 3 Execution Master Calendar

**Week-by-week execution plan for Domain K and Domain H research production, November 4, 2026 through January 3, 2027. Covers resource allocation, zone sequencing, parallel execution strategy, critical path items, and contingency buffer windows.**

## Executive Summary

Phase 3 research execution spans exactly 9 weeks from Election Day (November 4, 2026) through Congress seating (January 3, 2027). The calendar is organized around two key regulatory deadlines:

- **Senate Judiciary Recess (December 14-25)**: Domain K distribution must complete December 12-13 to reach Judiciary Committee staff before recess shutdown
- **Senate Finance Recess (December 22-Jan 1)**: Domain H distribution must complete December 20-27 to reach Finance Committee staff before year-end recess

Total baseline capacity required: **40-60 research hours over 9 weeks (4.4-6.7 hours/week average), with front-loaded weeks 1-2 (verification) and back-loaded weeks 6-9 (gist preparation and distribution strategy).**

Research infrastructure (sources, expert contacts, preliminary findings) is production-ready. The calendar governs quality verification, source currency validation, gist preparation, and coalition contact calibration.

- Allocate 4-6 hours for gap remediation
- Decision tree:
  - **Gap is material** (changes legislative strategy): revise relevant section, delay Gist launch to Dec 15
