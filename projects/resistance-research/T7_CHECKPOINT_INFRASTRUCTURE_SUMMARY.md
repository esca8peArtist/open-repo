## Signal Scoring At-a-Glance

| Signal | Score | Definition | Example |
|--------|-------|-----------|---------|
| **S (STRONG)** | 4-5 | Named decision-maker, specific action | "Can we set up a call?" / "Forwarding to our team" |
| **M (MODERATE)** | 3 | Generic acknowledgment + interest | "Very relevant to our work, thank you" |
| **W (WEAK)** | 1-2 | Auto-reply, no engagement | "We received your message" |
| **B (BOUNCE)** | 0 | Hard bounce or OOO | Action: Re-verify address, resend 48h |
| **-- (NO REPLY)** | 0 | Normal at T+7 | Recount June 30 (Day 14) |
