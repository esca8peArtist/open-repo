# Domain M — July 1-15 Activation Sequence
## Direct Democracy Infrastructure Defense Distribution Plan

**When to use this file**: July 1 or after. Domain M Phase 2 acceleration is approved and the July 1-15 distribution window is active regardless of whether Domain 51 Path A activates. Domain M runs on its own track: the November 3, 2026 ballot measures in Missouri, North Dakota, South Dakota, and Utah are time-sensitive and do not depend on Domain 51's status.

1. **Introduction** (300w): The four-state coordinated attack; November 3 deadline; why this is authoritarian infrastructure playbook, not isolated state policy
2. **What the supermajority requirements do** (600w): Missouri (congressional district approval = structural impossibility for progressive measures), North Dakota (60% threshold), South Dakota (60%), Utah (60% for tax measures)

5. **The coordinated nature** (400w): 100+ bills in 15+ states; BISC's documentation of the pattern; connection to ALEC and state legislative infrastructure
6. **Opposition campaigns and their needs** (400w): Missourians for Fair Governance; what the research synthesis provides that campaign organizations do not yet have
7. **Reform and resistance pathways** (300w): What BISC, Democracy Docket, and Common Cause are doing; how this brief connects to their work
8. **Conclusion** (200w): Call to action; July-October advocacy window
