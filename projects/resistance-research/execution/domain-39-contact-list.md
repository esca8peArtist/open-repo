# Domain 39 Contact List
## Healthcare Access as Democratic Infrastructure — June 1, 2026 Distribution

18 high-leverage contacts across 5 categories. June 1 is the HHS interim final rule deadline on OBBBA work requirements — the highest-leverage advocacy moment of 2026 for this domain. Send Tier 1 (5 contacts) on June 1; Tier 2 (7 contacts) June 2-5; Tier 3 (6 contacts) June 6-12.

**The democratic frame**: Domain 39 argues that healthcare access is civic infrastructure, not merely social welfare. The five pathways (rural hospital closures → turnout decline; Medicaid/NVRA; medical debt; maternal mortality; disability disenfranchisement) are simultaneously health crises and democracy crises. This framing gives each organization a new audience and a new coalition partner.
