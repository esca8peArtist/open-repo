## Follow-Up and Final Reminder Protocol

**Day 7-10 post-Wave 1 send (May 15-18)**: Check for responses from Category A contacts. If a Category A organization has responded substantively, reply within 24 hours. If no response from a high-priority Category A contact (Drug Policy Alliance, NORML), send one follow-up with the subject line: `[Organization name] — following up, May 28 deadline is [X days away]`

**May 21 — Final reminder wave**: One week before the deadline. Send a final abbreviated reminder (2-3 sentences, link to domain, deadline date) to all contacts that have not responded. Subject line: `One week to DEA hearing participation deadline (May 28) — Domain 42 briefing still available`

**Hard stop**: Do not send any new Domain 42 outreach after **May 21**. Organizations receiving the domain after May 21 will not have time to file a participation notice. Post-deadline distribution pivots to the secondary audiences that care about the democratic design analysis for longer-term policy purposes (law school clinics, general Substack audience).
