# Domain 1 Gist Staging — Voting Rights and Elections

**Why a standalone Domain 1 Gist**: The six existing canonical Gists (main proposal, exec summary, litigation tracker, and three trackers) serve the general distribution. ## Section 3: Public Zone vs. Private Zone — What Goes Where

### Public Zone (in the Gist — visible to anyone with the URL)

Include everything:
- Zone A header with CC 4.0 license statement
- Zone B domain-context block with advocacy windows and baseline metrics
- Full Domain 1 body: problem definition, international comparisons, legislative and litigation pathways, implementation timeline
- Sources section with all primary citations
- Zone D footer with cross-links to other framework documents

Do NOT include in the Gist:
- Your personal email address in Zone A (use "reply to the email you received this through" in Zone D instead, or leave contact field blank)
- Any internal research notes, session logs, or draft commentary that appears in the source file but is not final research
- Any `{{placeholder}}` text that was intended for email templates, not for the domain document itself

### Private Zone (keep in email cover notes; do not paste into Gist)

- Contact-specific personalization (Weiser framing vs. Elias framing)
- Path-specific language `[PATH A]` / `[PATH A+37]` / `[PATH B]`
