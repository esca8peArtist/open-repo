## Troubleshooting

### If the Gist URL ends up wrong in emails already sent

If you realize after sending that the Gist URL in the emails was the placeholder hash (not the actual Gist):
1. Do not send a correction email immediately — wait to see if recipients click it (they will get a 404).
2. If any recipient reports the link is broken, reply promptly with: "Apologies — the link in my initial message had a formatting error. The correct URL is [ACTUAL_URL]. The document is live and accessible."
3. The social media posts can be corrected before posting — check all post text before publishing.

### If an email bounces

**Hard bounce** (delivery failure, address invalid):
1. Note the bounced address in your send log.
2. Go to the organization's website and find the current contact email (comms@, info@, or specific staff).
3. Re-send within 2 hours to the new address.
4. If Partnership for Public Service bounces: try info@ourpublicservice.org as fallback, or use their website contact form.

**Soft bounce** (mailbox full, temporary failure):
1. Wait 4 hours, then re-send.
2. If soft bounce persists, treat as contact form submission and use the organization's website form.

**If 3+ emails bounce**: Stop the send sequence. This likely indicates a spam filter trigger — possibly the Gist URL being flagged. Verify the Gist is publicly accessible and not marked as spam by GitHub. Then re-send from a different email client or address.

### If the Protect Democracy contact form errors

If the form returns an error on submission:
1. Wait 20 minutes and try again (forms occasionally have brief outages).
2. If the error persists, check protectdemocracy.org for an alternate contact method (some organizations list press@, info@, or specific staff).
3. As a fallback, use info@protectdemocracy.org (not verified as live — check their site first).

### If synthesis at 19:00 UTC is imminent and sends are incomplete

**If sends are not complete by 18:30 UTC**:
1. Stop sending. Send any remaining emails after the synthesis completes (post-19:00 UTC).
2. Synthesis runs on its own schedule; your pending sends will not affect it.
3. Resume sends May 29 morning if any Tier 1 contacts remain unsent.

### If you miss May 28 entirely
