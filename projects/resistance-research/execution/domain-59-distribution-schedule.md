# Domain 59 Distribution Schedule

---

## Overview

Three-wave distribution spanning June 2-10. Rationale for sequencing:

- **Research organizations first (Wave 1)**: CBPP and ITEP have the longest decision cycles and highest analytical standards. Engaging them first establishes a credibility baseline; if either responds positively, subsequent organizations can be shown that Domain 59 has traction in the research community.
- **Advocacy hybrid organizations second (Wave 2)**: NWLC and MomsRising operate faster action cycles and can move to member communication or Senate contact within days of receiving the material. Staggering them one day apart avoids batch-send appearance.
- **Labor federation third (Wave 3)**: AFL-CIO June 2026 convention creates availability constraint; June 9-10 is the post-convention sweet spot. AFL-CIO acts on longer timescales than MomsRising but has direct relationships with Senate Finance members.

Total campaign window: June 2 through June 10 (sends); monitoring through July 1 (post-markup assessment).
