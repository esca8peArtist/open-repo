## Path A — Immediate 35-Domain Distribution

**Launch date**: May 4, 2026 (Day 5 after confirmation)
**Contact count**: 125 across 3 tiers / 21 days
**Key advantage**: Single coordinated launch event. Full analytical coherence visible to all recipients on Day 1. FISA/Section 702 hook still active in first week of May.
**Key tradeoff**: Domain 37 reaches election protection organizations in a general distribution package alongside Substack readers and academics. No strategic sequencing for the most time-sensitive content.
**Best for**: User who values unified launch over targeted election-protection sequencing; user with direct existing relationships with Democracy Docket, Campaign Legal Center, or ACLU Voting Rights Project staff.
**Roadmap**: `path-a-execution-roadmap.md` (740 lines, fully operational)

---

## Path A+37 — 35-Domain Hybrid (Recommended)

**Launch date**: April 30 Phase 1; May 12 Phase 2 (Domain 37 targeted wave)
**Contact count**: 125+ Phase 1; 7-16 targeted Phase 2 election protection orgs
**Key advantage**: Captures April 30 launch timing AND gives Domain 37 strategic sequencing. Election protection organizations receive Domain 37 with Phase 1 credibility established, framed to their specific litigation dockets, before the May 30 DOJ consent decree window. The two-phase structure is explained to contacts in Phase 1 outreach.
**Key tradeoff**: Two-phase operation requires monitoring Phase 1 for response signals before Phase 2 personalizes Domain 37 outreach. Slightly more operational overhead.
**Best for**: User who wants maximum strategic impact on the 2026 election protection ecosystem and can commit 2 additional hours of effort for Phase 2 coordination.
**Why this is the recommended path**: The May 30 DOJ consent decree window, August 7 NVRA quiet period, and targeted Democracy Docket / Campaign Legal Center framing are all captured. Phase 1 and Phase 2 both have clear execution protocols.
**Roadmap**: `path-a-37-execution-roadmap.md` (800 lines, 8-week timeline with decision points)

## Path B — Domain Updates First

**Launch date**: May 12 (optimistic) to May 20 (maximum holdoff)
**Contact count**: Same as Path A/A+37 — delayed
**Key advantage**: Domains 1, 21, 25, 33 updated with April-May 2026 developments before launch. Iran WPR outcome integrated. Potentially a more empirically complete document on launch day.
**Key tradeoffs**:
- Spring legislative session advocacy windows in AZ, OH, WI adjournments begin closing in May
- FISA/Section 702 attention hook is missed entirely (most relevant to Brennan Center and EFF the week of the vote)
- 79-87 days to NVRA August 7 deadline vs. 95 days for immediate launch — compresses the 6-8 week institutional adoption cycle
- Current domains are current through April 27-29 and are production-ready; additional research is enhancement, not prerequisite
**Hard triggers for forcing distribution early** (regardless of research status): Trump v. Slaughter SCOTUS decision arrives; major ICE/DHS enforcement escalation; any reconciliation vote on SAVE Act or OBBBA Medicaid
**Best for**: User who prioritizes document completeness over advocacy timing windows, or who wants to integrate the post-May 1 Iran WPR outcome before launch.
**Day 7 checkpoint**: Path B includes a mandatory decision point on May 6. At that checkpoint, the orchestrator assesses whether findings justify continuing, pivoting to Path A+37, or forcing distribution.
**Roadmap**: `path-b-continuation-roadmap.md` (482 lines, Day 7 checkpoint decision tree)
