# Phase 3 Research Team Readiness Checklist
## November 3 Pre-Launch Verification (Domain K + Domain H)

**Purpose:** Single-reference operational checklist to verify that all systems, people, communications, and infrastructure are ready for Phase 3 research launch at 00:00 UTC November 4, 2026. Each checklist item includes a verification procedure, responsible party identification, and escalation action if the item is incomplete by November 3 EOD.

**Scope:** Researchers, infrastructure, communications, source access, expert contacts, budgets, contingency frameworks, and backup systems for the November 4 – December 20 parallel research execution of Domains K (Federal Judiciary Restructuring) and H (Constitutional Resilience Architecture).

**How to use this checklist:** 
- Print or display on November 1. Work through items daily November 1-3.
- For each item, complete the verification step by the date specified.
- Check the box if verification PASSES. If verification FAILS or is INCOMPLETE, escalate immediately to the escalation contact specified.
- If all items are checked by November 3 EOD, Phase 3 is cleared to launch on November 4 at 00:00 UTC.
