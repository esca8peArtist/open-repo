## Monitoring: When to Check SCOTUSblog

The Court releases opinions on opinion days — typically Monday and Thursday mornings during the final weeks of the term. All OT2025 opinions will be released by late June 2026.

**Daily check protocol** (June 15 – July 10):
- Check SCOTUSblog "orders and opinions" page each morning: https://www.supremecourt.gov/opinions/slipopinion/25
- Check SCOTUSblog live blog on Thursdays (typically the busiest opinion day): https://www.scotusblog.com
- On any opinion day: first check whether Trump v. Barbara is listed; SCOTUS typically releases multiple opinions per day in the final term weeks

**If ruling drops on a Friday or non-standard day**: Same protocol applies — the document update and Tier 1 send can execute within 4 hours regardless of day of week.
