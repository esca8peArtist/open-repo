## At a Glance

- **22 policy domains** spanning democratic structure, material conditions, rights, and justice
- **Every reform** has been implemented somewhere, has documented evidence, and has a plausible U.S. adoption pathway
- **160+ movement case studies** inform the theory of change
- **International benchmarks** from 40+ countries ground each domain in comparative evidence
- **Fiscal analysis**: $650B-1,000B/year investment, largely offset by internal revenue mechanisms; the status quo costs more
- **Three implementation phases**: immediate (executive action + state initiatives), medium-term (federal legislation), long-term (constitutional amendments + national scaling)
