## If You Cannot Find an Email Address

Attempt in this order:
1. Institutional webpage (most reliable)
2. Standard email format for that institution (e.g., first.last@nyu.edu, firstlastinitial@brennancenter.org)
3. X/Twitter DM as a fallback only if institutional email is completely unavailable — send a brief DM asking for a better contact point; do not pitch the framework via DM
4. LinkedIn message as last resort

Do not send to an unverified email address. A bounce creates a deliverability flag for your account. If you cannot verify a contact's email, skip them and proceed with the remaining 4 contacts; they can be added to Batch 2 once verified.
