## Scoring Rubric (from Item 96)

- **Urgency (1-10)**: Presence of immovable legislative, litigation, or organizational deadlines between Nov 4, 2026 and March 31, 2027. Score of 10 = hard deadline that collapses advocacy window if missed. Score of 5 = meaningful but deferrable. Score of 2 = evergreen.
- **Movement Leverage (1-10)**: Coalition activation potential — breadth and density of organized constituencies that can absorb and act on analysis post-election.
- **Feasibility (1-10)**: Estimated research hours relative to Phase 2 baseline (28.5 hrs/domain). Score of 10 = under 20 hours. Score of 5 = 25-35 hours. Score of 2 = 45+ hours.
- **Strategic Impact (1-10)**: Multiplier effect — does completing this domain enable or amplify other Phase 3 domains or Phase 2 coalitions?
- **Composite** = ((Urgency × 1.5) + (Movement Leverage × 1.2) + Feasibility + (Strategic Impact × 1.3)) / 5
