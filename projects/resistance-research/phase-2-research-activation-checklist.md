### Decision 1: Domain Sequencing — Simultaneous or Staggered?

**Question**: Are all four domains launching distribution simultaneously (June 1), or staggered by policy window?

**Option A — Staggered (recommended default)**:
- Domain 59: June 1 (HHS rule anchor)
- Domain 56: May 26 – June 7 (H.R. 492 window opens June 1)

**Option B — Front-loaded (aggressive)**:

This maximizes early momentum but creates contact overlap risk (organizations on multiple lists receive 2–3 documents in one week). Requires more intensive cover email differentiation.

**Default**: Option A (staggered). User should confirm or override.

### Decision 2: Phase 1 / Phase 2 Parallel Execution
