# Domain H Expert Contact Framework
## Constitutional Resilience Architecture — Phase 3 Research Infrastructure

**Purpose:** Unified Domain H-specific expert contact reference with 32 contacts, tiered by engagement likelihood and organized for Phase 3 activation. Consolidates contacts from the Domain H production document (Zone 4 coalition section, 6 experts + 16 organizations) and the combined K+H framework (PHASE_3_EXPERT_CONTACT_FRAMEWORK.md), adding 10 additional contacts identified through Session 3777 and prior research threads.

**Engagement Strategy Overview:**
- July-August outreach (Tier 4 data verification): Secure accuracy verification before Phase 3 content is finalized
- September outreach (Tier 1 warm contacts): Re-activate existing relationships; secure fall schedule slots before they fill
- October outreach (Tier 1 cold, coalition organizations): Coordinate November 4 launch logistics
- November outreach (Tier 2 constitutional scholars): Research brief distribution, congressional testimony pipeline
- November-December outreach (Tier 3 media): Translation and amplification of November research launch
- January-February 2027 outreach (Tier 4 international submission): Congressional window activation, international documentation

**Critical sequencing rule:** Contact Domain K's Tier 1 coalition organizations (Fix the Court, Brennan Center, ACS) BEFORE contacting Domain H's Tier 2 scholars. Domain K must distribute December 12-20; Domain H follows by 10-14 days. If Domain H contacts reach scholars before Domain K has established credibility in that network, both documents underperform.

## TIER DEFINITIONS

**Tier 1 — Anchor Organizations and Warm Contacts (6 contacts):** Coalition partners and warm re-activations with existing relationships. These contacts will engage with minimum friction. September-October 2026 activation.

**Tier 2 — Constitutional Scholars (12 contacts):** Primary academic voices who move legal and policy discourse. Engagement converts research into credibility with law school, bar, and congressional audiences. October-November 2026 activation.

**Tier 3 — Media and Secondary Distribution (8 contacts):** Journalists and podcast hosts who translate research for broader audiences. Lower conversion probability than Tier 2; worth attempting for amplification. November-December 2026 activation.

**Tier 4 — International and Data Verification (6 contacts):** Accuracy verification, data permission, and international submission. July-September 2026 for pre-launch verification; January-February 2027 for post-launch international documentation submissions.
