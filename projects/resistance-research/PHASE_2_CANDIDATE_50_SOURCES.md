## Romer v. Evans — Core Legal Authority

The constitutional anchor for the ballot measure challenge argument. Access via:
- SCOTUS opinion: 517 U.S. 620 (1996) — search Cornell LII: https://www.law.cornell.edu/supct/html/94-1039.ZO.html
- Key holding: An amendment to Colorado's constitution that prohibited any governmental entity from enacting anti-discrimination protections for LGBTQ+ people was unconstitutional under equal protection. "A bare ... desire to harm a politically unpopular group cannot constitute a legitimate governmental interest." Kennedy majority (6-3).
- Application to 2026 measures: Colorado specifically — the state with Romer precedent most directly applicable. Lambda Legal's Colorado challenge will likely invoke Romer directly.
