## Most Important Finding

**The January 3, 2027 congressional seating is the immovable deadline organizing the entire Phase 3 execution calendar -- and it now gates three non-deferrable domains, not two.**

The Item 93 timeline identified Domain K and Domain H as non-deferrable load-bearing domains for January 3. Item 89's expanded analysis adds a third: **Domain 50-Update (OBBBA Medicaid Implementation)**. The January 1, 2027 OBBBA Medicaid work requirement effective date creates a lame-duck session window for amendment (November 4 - January 3) that collapses at the same moment as the congressional seating window. Domain 50-Update must be in distribution by December 15 -- before OBBBA takes effect on January 1 -- for organizations running navigator and enrollment assistance campaigns and for any lame-duck legislative advocacy. After January 1, the domain converts from prevention advocacy to post-implementation analysis, which retains value but loses the lame-duck amendment window.

**Four dates anchor the expanded timeline:**

- **November 4, 2026**: Midterm election day. Triggers post-election research window. Domain 37a deploys within 10 days if certification challenges materialize. Domains 49 and K research begins.
- **December 15, 2026**: Domain 50-Update distribution deadline. Before OBBBA Medicaid work requirements take effect January 1.
- **December 31, 2026**: Domain 49 redistricting analysis must publish before post-election litigation filing deadlines.
- **January 3, 2027**: 120th Congress seated. Domain K and Domain H must be in Tier 1 distribution. Committee assignments harden January 10-17; after January 17, the blank-slate window for shaping incoming member priorities closes.
