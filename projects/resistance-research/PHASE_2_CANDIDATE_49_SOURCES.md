## Source Gaps (Fill During Production)

The following gaps were identified during this staging pass and require active research:

1. **EPA EJ grant cancellation authoritative dollar figure**: Runbook notes $300M–$500M range. During production, find the most authoritative single source with explicit documentation. Options: EPA budget rescission notice in Federal Register; OMB rescission memo; GAO report if available. Use whichever is most authoritative, note caveat if only range is available.

2. **Title VI complaint processing time**: The "10+ years average" figure needs a primary source. Options: GAO report on EPA Title VI enforcement; EPA ECHO system historical data; law review article on Title VI and environmental permitting. This is empirical anchor #3 per the research runbook.

3. **D.C. Circuit case dockets**: Search CourtListener and PACER for current D.C. Circuit cases challenging (1) the Endangerment Finding rescission and (2) the NEPA regulation rescission. Record case names, docket numbers, and status.

4. **State environmental justice acts**: Seven states have enacted EJ frameworks (identify which; compare strongest statutory model to the now-eliminated federal infrastructure). Likely sources: National Academy for State Health Policy, NCSL, state-specific environmental law publications.

5. **Earthjustice active cases**: Confirm D.C. Circuit docket directly with earthjustice.org/cases (website search) or communications@earthjustice.org if needed.
