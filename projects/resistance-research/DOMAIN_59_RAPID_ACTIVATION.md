### Coalition Collaboration Opportunities to Watch

At 48 hours, scan replies specifically for these opportunities — they are worth prioritizing over generic responses:

1. **Request for adapted materials** — Any organization asking for a "member-facing version," "one-page summary," or "legislative brief format." These are Phase 2 activation signals. Respond within 24 hours. This means the organization is moving from information consumer to distribution partner.

2. **Referral to a named colleague** — "I've forwarded this to [Name] in our policy/advocacy team." The named person becomes your direct follow-up target. Email them directly with a brief 2-sentence context note referencing the original email.

3. **Coalition meeting invitation** — Any invitation to discuss or present. Accept immediately. These are rare but represent the highest-value engagement outcome.

4. **Publication inquiry** — Any organization asking about citation terms, reproduction rights, or co-publication. CC Attribution 4.0 allows all of these. Reply within 12 hours; this is time-sensitive.
