## How to Use This Matrix

This matrix lists 3-5 Tier 2 contacts per domain — organizations NOT in Wave 1-2 sends that are the next-highest-priority targets for Phase 2 Tier 2 activation. For each contact, an engagement probability estimate is provided with the specific reasoning behind it.

**Do not activate Tier 2 contacts before the T+7 checkpoint** (June 23-25). The signal from Wave 1 contacts determines whether to activate Tier 2 at all, and which domains to prioritize.

**Personalization strategy**:
- Wave 1 respondents (STRONG or MODERATE reply) → warm handoff language: "Following up on your [date] engagement with this research..."
- No Wave 1 reply → new-contact cold outreach with shortened ask: "One-page memo, 15-min read" framing
- Coalition overlap (org works with Wave 1 respondent) → cite mutual connection: "Colleagues at [Wave 1 org] have engaged with this research..."

## Engagement Probability Model — Criteria Documentation

The following criteria determine each probability estimate. This ensures estimates are traceable, not guessed.

### Criterion A: Wave 1 Reply Status

| Wave 1 Status | Effect on Tier 2 Probability |
|---------------|------------------------------|
| STRONG reply from same-domain Wave 1 contact | +20-30 percentage points vs. cold baseline |
| MODERATE reply from same-domain Wave 1 contact | +10-15 percentage points |
| No reply but delivery confirmed | No adjustment (cold baseline) |
| Coalition partner (different org) replied STRONG | +10-20 percentage points (warm coalition reference) |

### Criterion B: Deadline Proximity

| Deadline Type | Effect |
|---------------|--------|
| Active Senate/legislative deadline <30 days | +15-25 percentage points |
| State ballot measure window <60 days | +10-15 percentage points |
| No active external deadline | No adjustment |

### Criterion C: Coalition Overlap

| Coalition Relationship | Effect |
|------------------------|--------|
| Org cites Wave 1 respondent in their own publications | +10-15 percentage points |
| Org co-publishes with Wave 1 respondent | +15-20 percentage points |
| Org is known adversary or competitor of Wave 1 respondent | -10 to -20 percentage points |
| No documented relationship | No adjustment |

### Criterion D: Research Citation (Cold Baseline Adjuster)

| Citation Status | Cold Baseline |
|-----------------|---------------|
| Organization's research is primary source in the domain | 55-70% cold baseline |
| Organization's research is cited (not primary) | 40-55% cold baseline |
| No direct citation; policy overlap only | 25-40% cold baseline |
| Adjacent field; weak overlap | 15-25% cold baseline |
