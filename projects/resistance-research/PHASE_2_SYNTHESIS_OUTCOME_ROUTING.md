## The Core Routing Logic

May 28 synthesis classification determines Phase 2a activation (Domains 56/39/58/57/59 — governed by SYNTHESIS_OUTCOME_DECISION_TREE.md). Phase 2b is a downstream layer: it activates based on Phase 2a's trajectory, not independently. The four rules:

1. **STRONG outcome**: Phase 2b activates same-day (May 28 evening). All four domains (37/42/51/54) sequence May 29–June 9.
2. **MODERATE outcome**: Phase 2b activates with Domain 51 leading on May 31. Domains 37, 42, 54 follow at 2–3 day intervals. Domain 54 requires June 10 gate assessment.
3. **WEAK outcome**: Phase 2b is held. Only activates if Batch 2 produces signal by June 5. If no Batch 2 signal, Phase 2b delays to August 1.
4. **TOO_EARLY outcome**: Phase 2b waits for May 25/28 reclassification. Follow TOO_EARLY path in SYNTHESIS_OUTCOME_DECISION_TREE.md Section 4, then return to this document once reclassified.

## Why Domain 51 Leads Under MODERATE

This is the most consequential sequencing decision in Phase 2b, and it warrants explicit justification.

Under MODERATE, the instinct might be to lead with Domain 37 because it is the most immediately urgent (2026 midterms) or Domain 54 because it has the Virginia June 1 urgency hook. Domain 51 leads instead for three reasons:

**First**: Campaign Legal Center has the broadest cross-domain institutional reach of any Phase 2b Tier 1 contact. Their work spans elections (Domain 37), dark money (Domain 51), and congressional advocacy (DISCLOSE Act). A Score 3+ reply from them validates the framework to the election protection, campaign finance reform, and legislative advocacy sectors simultaneously — three of the four Phase 2b domain constituencies in a single reply.

**Second**: Domain 51's "connective tissue" framing — that Citizens United's architecture is the mechanism enabling every sector-specific corporate capture in the framework — creates immediate cross-domain resonance. A contact receiving Domain 51 who already knows about Domain 42 (pharma regulatory capture) or Domain 54 (prison industrial complex funding) will immediately see how they connect. This cross-domain recognition signal is the fastest path to Tier 3 promotion.

**Third**: Under MODERATE, we have one confirmed engaged contact. Domain 51's opening paragraph is the easiest to personalize with a single confirmed engagement as social proof — because Domain 51 argues that all of the specific sector captures the recipient already knows about share a common mechanism. "You've engaged with [Domain X] — Domain 51 explains the structural architecture that makes that capture possible" is a natural and credible opening.

Domains 37 and 54 have more acute external deadlines (midterms, Virginia), but those deadlines are months away. The June 2–6 distribution window gives organizations 4–5 months to act on Domain 37 before the November election, and 5 months to act on Domain 54 before the Virginia constitutional amendment vote. The connective tissue argument in Domain 51 accelerates their engagement with everything that follows.
