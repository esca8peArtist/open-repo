### Signal thresholds

**Green — proceed to Tier 2 expansion**: 2+ substantive responses by June 9 AND at least one organization explicitly citing the democratic design framing in markup-related advocacy. If green, send Tier 2 contacts (EPI, National Housing Law Project, Demos, National Partnership for Women and Families, CAP, SEIU) the week of June 9–13.

**Yellow — targeted follow-up**: 1 substantive response, or confirmed Gist views exceeding 15 without direct response. Send a single follow-up to CBPP and MomsRising (highest-probability responders) on June 10. Do not follow up with the others yet.

**Red — reassess before Tier 2**: Zero responses by June 15 and Gist views under 10. Hold Tier 2 and review whether the framing or targeting needs adjustment. Red does not mean the research failed — it may mean the Senate Finance markup window moved faster than anticipated.

### Secondary engagement signals (measure by June 15)

- Meeting request from any organization (highest-value — treat as priority response)
- Coalition interest expressed (cross-org coordination offer)
- Gist views exceeding 10 by June 15 (organic spread signal)
- Any public testimony, statement, or advocacy referencing the democratic design framing
