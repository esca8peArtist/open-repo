## Section 3: Phase 3 Start Date Decision

### Option A: Begin Domain K Research Immediately (June-July 2026)

**Case for**: Domain H is complete. The October-November 2026 window requires Domain K to be complete by December 1, 2026 at the latest (allowing 33 days for distribution before January 3, 2027 seating). Starting Domain K in July 2026 gives 5 months — far more comfortable than a November sprint. Also prevents Phase 2 carryover pile-up (Domains G, 37a, and 31x are already Phase 2 obligations competing for November attention).

**Case against**: Phase 3 was designed to launch post-midterm because midterm results determine which Phase 3 domains have legislative traction. Starting Domain K before November 3 means writing about political feasibility without knowing the Congressional composition. The section on "which reform proposals survive a Republican-controlled Senate" vs. "which survive a Democratic-controlled House" cannot be calibrated until election night.

**Recommendation**: Hybrid — begin Domain K research on the non-electoral sections (Section 1: structural capture analysis; Section 2: term limits constitutional analysis; Section 3: international comparison) before November 3. Leave Section 4 (political feasibility per chamber composition) as a post-election addition. This produces a 70% complete document by November 3 that can be finished within 10 days of election results.

### Option B: Hold All Phase 3 Research for November 4, 2026

**Case for**: The Phase 3 strategic roadmap specifies November 4 launch. Holding allows Phase 2 completion (Domains G, 37a, 31x) to proceed without resource competition. User bandwidth is finite.

**Case against**: Domain H is already complete — it will sit unreleased for 5 months with no distribution occurring. The January 3, 2027 deadline is served better by having Domain K complete in October than in December. The PHASE_3_EXECUTION_TIMELINE.md already acknowledged the November capacity crunch (40-42 hours required in four weeks). Producing Domain K in October reduces that crunch significantly.

**Recommended decision**: Option A hybrid — begin Domain K Sections 1-3 as an autonomous session in July-August 2026, before midterms. Hold Section 4 (political feasibility) for post-election completion. Total pre-election hours: approximately 12-15 of the estimated 16-20 total.
