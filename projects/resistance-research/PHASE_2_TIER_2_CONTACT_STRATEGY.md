# Phase 2 Tier 2 Contact Strategy

*Created May 23, 2026. This document maps secondary contact networks (Tier 2 backup contacts) for each Phase 2 domain (56–59). Use if Tier 1 response rate falls below 40% within T+7 of first Tier 1 send. Activation is contingency-based: normal execution awaits May 25 synthesis outcome; this file pre-stages rapid activation triggers and contact lists.*

---

## Activation Protocol

**Monitor these Tier 1 response metrics** (Days 3–10 post-send):
- **Positive signals** (→ continue Tier 1): Substantive replies from 40%+ of Tier 1 contacts; any public citations; forward shares; meeting requests
- **Weak signals** (→ activate Tier 2): Replies from <40% of Tier 1; mostly acknowledgment-only; no public amplification
- **Non-response** (→ escalate): Zero replies after T+10; activate full Tier 2 + media outreach simultaneously

**Tier 2 activation window**: Days 7–14 post-Tier-1-send (May 28-June 4 for STRONG outcome; May 25-June 1 for TOO_EARLY contingency)

**Success threshold for Tier 2**: 25%+ substantive response rate within T+14 of Tier 2 send
