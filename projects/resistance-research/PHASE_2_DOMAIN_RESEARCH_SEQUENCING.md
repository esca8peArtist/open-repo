### Domain Classification Framework

**Depth Tiers**:
- **Tier 1 "Canonical"**: 5–7 days research, 40+ citations. Foundational domains that unlock multiple downstream domains.
- **Tier 2 "Substantive"**: 2–3 days research, 20–35 citations. Supporting domains with focused scope, clear advocacy windows.
- **Tier 3 "Foundational Brief"**: 1 day research, 10–15 citations. Landscape-establishing domains, narrower scope, targeted audiences.

**Advocacy Windows**:
- **ASAP** (≤May 31): Immediate window closing; time-critical deadlines
- **JUNE** (June 1–30): Summer legislative windows, early planning phase for fall
- **SUMMER** (July 1–Aug 31): Pre-election cycle; ballot measure research integration
- **EXTENDED** (Sept 1+): Post-election prep, longer-arc institutional analysis

**Impact Categories**:
- **LEVERAGE**: High-impact domains enabling multiple downstream domains (e.g., voting rights underpins election security, ballot measure suppression, etc.)
- **URGENT**: Time-critical advocacy windows; must sequence within specific deadline
- **SUPPORTING**: Specialized domains serving specific constituencies; lower urgency
- **FOUNDATIONAL**: Landscape-establishing domains; can be sequenced more flexibly
