Democracy and election law scholars are actively looking for synthesis documents that bridge their specialized work to systemic reform proposals.

## Pillar 2: Think Tanks — Policy and Legal Analysis

Think tanks have communications infrastructure the proposal lacks — policy briefs, op-ed placement, testimony pipelines, legislative staff relationships. A policy analyst at Brookings, EPI, or CAP who cites the proposal in their own work extends its reach to audiences the proposal cannot reach directly. Framing: offer the research as a resource they can use and cite, not compete with.
