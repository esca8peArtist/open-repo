## Notes for Executor

1. **Personalization is critical.** Do not send form emails. For each contact, reference a specific recent publication, policy brief, or legislative initiative they've worked on. Increase open rate from 10% (generic) to 40%+ (personalized).

2. **Two-stage send strategy**: Tier 1 first (May 22-24) establishes credibility and early adoption signals. Tier 2 (May 30-June 2) builds on those signals. "Already cited by [Brookings/Carnegie/Congress]" is more persuasive in Tier 2 outreach.

3. **Customizable attachments**: The email templates have bracketed customization points. Spend 5 minutes per email personalizing the angle to that contact's known interests.

4. **Follow-up cadence**: 1-week follow-up for non-responders (may have missed first email or require reminder). 2-week follow-up only if explicitly requested.

5. **Gist management**: Once published, do not edit. If errors are found, publish a v1.1 Gist and note the version in outreach. Changing published content breaks links and credibility.

6. **Success is slow.** A 25-40% email open rate is standard for cold policy outreach. A 10-15% "adoption signal" response rate is excellent. Plan for June 30 measurement, not May 30 results.
