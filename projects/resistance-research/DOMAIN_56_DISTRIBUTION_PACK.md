What to do if problems arise before Day 7.

- 1-2 bounces: Log in Contacts sheet, use fallback contact method (see Section 2 of pre-testing checklist), resend
- 3+ bounces: CONTACT_VERIFY determination — run the Day 7 CONTACT_VERIFY branch immediately (do not wait for Day 7)

- No action required at Day 3 unless you have bounces.
- NTEU, AFGE, GAP (Tier 1 contacts) may still reply from earlier sends — these count toward Day 7 metrics.

- Try the fallback: CREW — if citizensforethics.org/contact is down, try any staff email from their team page
