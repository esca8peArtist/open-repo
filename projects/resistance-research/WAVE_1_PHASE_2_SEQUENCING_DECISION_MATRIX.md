### 3.4 Failure Recovery (Score Below 3)

**Signal interpretation**: Wave 1 has experienced significant engagement failure — zero or near-zero engagement across all metrics with confirmed delivery. This is the lowest-probability outcome for a targeted 5-contact send to organizations whose primary mandate is campaign finance reform. Before concluding failure, the infrastructure diagnostic must be complete.

**Immediate actions (within 2 hours of FAILURE score determination)**:
1. Complete full infrastructure diagnostic per DOMAIN_51_JUNE_16_DECISION_LOGIC.md Section 3.4
2. If delivery confirmed and Gist accessible: content failure mode. Move to failure recovery protocol.
3. If delivery or Gist problem found: fix infrastructure, extend monitoring window by 7 days before classifying as engagement failure. The send effectively starts over on the re-delivery date.

**Failure recovery protocol (content failure confirmed)**:
- Modification 1 — Stakeholder substitution: Replace national directors with state-level campaign finance reform organizations. State Common Cause chapters with active ballot campaign or DISCLOSE Act engagement. State-level campaign finance reform orgs (Montana I-194 coalition, Michigan Clean Elections, New Mexico campaign finance reform orgs from DOMAIN_51_CONTINGENCY_SB_299_FALLBACK.md) are Tier B alternatives with mission alignment and faster engagement cycles.
- Modification 2 — Framing revision: Shift from "comprehensive dark money architecture analysis" to single-hook pitch. For CLC: "research specifically on FEC enforcement shutdown — 200+ days without quorum action." For Issue One: "AI PAC proliferation following Fairshake template — new data for DISCLOSE Act hearings." Single-hook pitches have higher opening rates with high-volume-inbox contacts than framework overview emails.
- Modification 3 — Channel shift: Submit a shortened Domain 51 analysis to Just Security or Lawfare. A published piece creates credibility with all five original contacts without requiring them to engage with an unsolicited email. One published article citing the FEC enforcement shutdown data or the AI PAC proliferation analysis creates search-visible credibility that converts cold outreach to warm.

**Phase 2 domains under FAILURE**: Domain 57 (August 10, fixed) and Domain 50 (August 1, hard stop) proceed as planned. Domain 48 preparation begins July 1 (independent contact pool). Domain 49 deferred to July 15 preparation start.

## Section 4: Edge Cases and Override Conditions

**Score 5 override at any time**: Any contact sending an explicit adoption signal (Score 5) upgrades the Wave 1 classification to STRONG regardless of composite score. Immediate STRONG path activation.

**Split constituency signal**: If Tier A contacts (CLC, Issue One) show STRONG engagement but California contacts (Type B) show zero engagement, apply STRONG sequencing to all Phase 2 domains. California ballot campaign engagement is desirable but not required for Phase 2 batch activation. The core Domain 51 distribution has succeeded if the national democracy reform organizations engage substantively.

**Trump v. Barbara SCOTUS ruling**: Domain 58 (Tribal Sovereignty) activates within 48 hours of any confirmed ruling, independent of Wave 1 signal level. This is a rapid-response track that does not interact with Wave 1 signal classification.

**Domain 51 Wave 2 California sends (if deferred)**: If June 11–12 California sends were deferred for any reason, they can still be sent June 16–20 without materially affecting the July 1 ballot campaign window. A June 16–20 send gives California contacts 10–12 days before the July 1 soft deadline, which is the minimum viable lead time for campaign-framing integration. Do not defer California sends past June 20.

**Day 30 checkpoint upgrade trigger**: If the Day 7 determination is MODERATE but the Day 30 (July 9) composite score shows STRONG engagement trajectory (cumulative Bitly clicks 50+, 3+ Score 3 replies, 1+ adoption signals), upgrade to STRONG path at that point. STRONG activation triggers Domain 49/50 parallel track immediately and activates Domain 57 expanded contact list for the August 10 send.
