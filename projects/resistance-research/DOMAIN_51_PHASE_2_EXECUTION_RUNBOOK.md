**Q: Can I send both emails at the same time?**
A: No. The 90-minute spacing is deliberate — reduces spam filter flags and gives recipients time to read Email 1 context before Email 2 lands.

**Q: What if I get an out-of-office auto-reply?**
A: Good sign — email was delivered. Log the auto-reply, continue monitoring for substantive responses. Out-of-office does NOT count as a bounce.

**Q: My email client doesn't timestamp — how do I log send times?**
A: Check your email sent folder for "date sent" field. If unavailable: note the local time and convert to UTC. Approximate timestamps (within 30 min) are acceptable.

**Q: Is there a backup date if June 30 is too busy?**
A: No. June 30, 23:59 UTC is the hard deadline. If you cannot send by then, contingency activates immediately on July 1. Plan accordingly.

**Q: What if I'm not in UTC timezone?**
