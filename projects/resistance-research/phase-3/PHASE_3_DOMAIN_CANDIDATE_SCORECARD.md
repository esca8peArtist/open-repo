# Phase 3 Domain Candidate Scorecard

*Scoped: June 6, 2026. Phase 3 launch: November 2026. Planning deadline: June 25, 2026.*

---

## Scoring Methodology

**Urgency (1-10)**: Presence of immovable external deadlines — congressional term starts, legislative windows, judicial calendars, campaign finance law windows. Score 10 = hard date where missing the window forfeits 12+ months of leverage. Score 1 = no time-sensitive driver.

**Movement Leverage (1-10)**: Cross-coalition activation potential — how many existing Phase 1-2 audience sectors can immediately operationalize this domain's findings. Score 10 = plug directly into 4+ active coalition networks with existing Phase 2 relationships. Score 1 = narrow audience, specialist-only.

**Feasibility (1-10)**: Research effort relative to impact. Score 10 = source-rich, well-documented, researchable in under 25 hours. Score 7-9 = 25-35 hours. Score 4-6 = 35-50 hours. Score 1-3 = requires primary data collection or 60+ hours.

**Phase 2 calibration**: 7 domains / 200 hours = 28.5 hrs average. Phase 3 targeting 8-10 domains across Nov 2026-March 2027 = 228-285 estimated hours at same rate. Feasibility scores reflect this baseline.
