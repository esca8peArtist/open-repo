The following sections are the most broadly accessible and highest-impact for recipients who will decide in the first 90 seconds whether to read further:

This single paragraph is the most morally and analytically powerful element in the document.

The Virginia, Ohio, Oregon, and Vermont measures are the positive democratic mobilization counterpart to the anti-trans wave. This section makes the document useful for mobilization as well as defense.
