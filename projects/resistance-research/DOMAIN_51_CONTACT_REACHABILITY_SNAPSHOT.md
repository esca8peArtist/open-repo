## Executive Assistant Routing Paths — Tier 1

For Domain 51's highest-priority contacts, the following EA routing paths have been documented:

### CLC (Adav Noti / Erin Chlopak / Brendan Fischer)
- CLC has approximately 40 staff and uses direct staff email routing rather than a central EA system.
- For campaign finance research: send directly to Erin Chlopak (echlopak@campaignlegalcenter.org) rather than routing through Noti's office.
- If Chlopak is the target and there is no response by Day 7, a secondary send to info@campaignlegal.org with subject "Attn: Campaign Finance Research Team" is documented in prior execution checklists as an appropriate fallback.

### Issue One (Nick Penniman)
- Penniman is high-profile (TIME100, Washingtonian 500) and receives substantive correspondence through info@issueone.org, which routes to his policy team.
- For media-adjacent questions: Cory Combs (ccombs@issueone.org) is the documented media routing contact.
- For research-to-research framing: use info@issueone.org with "For your research team" in the subject line (documented in prior email templates; this routing language is specifically tested in Domain 51 templates).

### Common Cause California (Darius Kemp)
- Kemp is the sole executive-level contact for California operations. No EA documented for a state affiliate of this size.
- Campaign mode (June 2026) means Kemp's personal inbox at dkemp@commoncause.org is preferable to the ca@commoncause.org general inbox — less volume, faster routing.
- If dkemp@commoncause.org is unavailable, ca@commoncause.org is the fallback; do not email both simultaneously.
