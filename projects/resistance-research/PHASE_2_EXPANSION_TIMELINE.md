## Part 4: Engagement Thresholds That Trigger Next Domain

These are the specific thresholds that constitute "proceed" vs. "pause" for each inter-wave gap. Any threshold below is a go/no-go criterion, not a soft target.

### Domain 51 → Domain 37 (Synthesis Point: June 1–3)

**Go condition**: At least 1 substantive reply (Score 3+ on the QRP scale) from Domain 51 contacts within 3 days of May 31 send. OR: No reply but Gist views delta 10+ over 3 days.

**No-go condition**: Zero replies + Gist delta 0–2. Action: Review Domain 51 subject lines and opening paragraph before Domain 37 sends. Add one sentence of Domain 51 urgency framing to Domain 37 templates.

**Default**: Proceed regardless unless WEAK outcome and Batch 2 uplift has not triggered.

### Domain 37 → Domain 42 Phase 2b (Synthesis Point: June 3–5)

**Go condition**: Domain 51 has at least 1 reply and Domain 37 has sent without confirmed delivery problem (inbox test). Academic audience for Domain 42 has different expectations — the go/no-go here is delivery quality, not reply rate.

**No-go condition**: Domain 37 delivery self-test lands in spam. Action: Fix delivery before Domain 42 academic sends (academic spam filters are strict).

**Default**: Proceed regardless.

### Domain 42 Phase 2b → Domain 54 (Synthesis Point: June 5–7)

**Go condition**: Virginia King v. O'Bannon June 1 compliance status is known. If Virginia state is in compliance, use compliance news as hook in Domain 54 Virginia-focused templates. If Virginia state is not in compliance, this creates an even stronger urgency hook.

**No-go condition**: None — Domain 54 proceeds regardless of Virginia compliance status. The status affects framing, not timing.

### Phase 2b Full Send → Tier 2 Expansion (June 10–15 Gate)

**Go condition (Tier 2 full activation)**: Combined Phase 2b engagement rate 30%+ (at least 4 substantive replies across all four domains from 15+ combined contacts). Proceed to Tier 2 expansion in Week 6.

**Conditional activation**: Combined engagement 15–29% (2–3 substantive replies). Proceed with Tier 2 for the strongest-performing domain only.

**Hold**: Combined engagement <15% (0–1 substantive reply). Hold Tier 2 expansion; prioritize Domain 57/59 research under Phase 2a instead. Revisit Phase 2b Tier 2 at August 1 gate.
