## Contingency: If Batch 1/2 Feedback Recommends Significant Deletions

**Low-probability scenario**: One of Batch 1/2 contacts identifies a factual error or misleading analysis that should be removed/revised.

**Response protocol**:
- [ ] Flag domain for Orchestrator review (not user responsibility)
- [ ] Research the disputed claim with additional sources
- [ ] If error confirmed: Correct or delete problematic analysis before public launch
- [ ] If analysis defensible: Document disagreement and note in "Research Partners" acknowledgment: "[Contact name] noted concerns about [section]; our analysis differs on [specific point] for these reasons: [sourcing]"
- [ ] Maintain evidentiary standard: No unsourced claims, no speculation

**This keeps institutional credibility high even in presence of disagreement.**
