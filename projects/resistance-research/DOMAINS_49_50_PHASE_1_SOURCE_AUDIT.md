*Completed July 4, 2026. Audits all sources in DOMAINS_49_50_EXPANDED_SOURCE_INDEX.md against live URL verification and known access barriers.*

---

## Audit Methodology

Sources were audited in three tiers:
1. **Direct URL verification** — searched for the document at its indexed URL and confirmed the hosting domain is live and resolves to correct content.
2. **Paywall/access classification** — government, academic institution (.edu), nonprofit (.org), and major law firm client alerts are open-access by default. Academic journals and commercial publishers flagged for paywall risk.
3. **Mirror availability** — where original documents have been removed (EPA.gov content, EJSCREEN), mirror locations are identified and status confirmed.
