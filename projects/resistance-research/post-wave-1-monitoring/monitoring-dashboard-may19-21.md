**Scoring reference** (quick lookup):
- Score 1: "Thanks, will read" — delivery confirmed, zero engagement quality
- Score 2: "This is interesting" — general positive, no domain specificity
- Score 3: Domain-specific question, methodological critique, specific content reference — 1 Quality Reply Point
- Score 4: Asks how to use, names a colleague/org to receive materials, specific brief request — 2 Quality Reply Points
- Score 5: Cites in filing/brief/testimony, formal collaboration offer — STRONG OVERRIDE (stop, classify STRONG)
