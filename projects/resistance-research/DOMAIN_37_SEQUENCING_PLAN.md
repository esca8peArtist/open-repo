# Domain 37 Sequencing Plan — Path A+Hybrid

*Built April 27, 2026 (Session 522). This document covers the targeted second-wave distribution of Domain 37 (Federal Executive Interference in the 2026 Midterms) specifically to election-protection organizations. It assumes Phase A general distribution is already in flight (see PHASE_A_POSTING_SCHEDULE.md). The 2-week delay after Phase A launch allows Phase A momentum to establish credibility before Domain 37 outreach goes to election-protection specialists.*

---

## Why Sequence Domain 37 Separately

Domain 37 is the most time-sensitive and highest-stakes domain in the framework. Its advocacy windows are tied to specific 2026 dates (May 30, June 30, August 7, September, October). Organizations that receive it need time to act within those windows. But Domain 37 has the highest credibility threshold — election-protection organizations receive political research constantly, and what separates material they engage with from material they discard is institutional credibility and demonstrated rigor.

The sequencing logic:

1. **Phase A launch (Weeks 1-4)** establishes credibility: Substack readers, Reddit engagement, academic and policy organization responses, media pickup
2. **Week 6-8**: Domain 37 outreach goes to election-protection organizations, citing Phase A momentum
3. The election-protection orgs receive Domain 37 as the targeted research it is — not as a cold pitch from an unknown source, but as the election domain from a research project that law school clinics and policy organizations have already engaged with

This sequencing also allows the May 30 advocacy window to still be actionable — if Phase A launches in the first week of May 2026, the Domain 37 outreach reaches election-protection organizations by late May, before the first advocacy window closes.

## What Domain 37 Outreach Should NOT Do

- Do not send Domain 37 to general audiences before the Phase A Substack serialization includes it (Post 16 in the Phase A2 sequence per DISTRIBUTION_PHASE_ORDER.md). The Phase A general audience serialization and the Phase B election-protection targeted outreach are parallel tracks — neither is withheld from the other, but the election-protection outreach goes to organizations that can act on it before general audiences receive the serialized version.

- Do not frame Domain 37 outreach as partisan. The document is explicitly analytical — it documents federal interference mechanisms using government documents, court filings, and investigative journalism. Frame it as: "documented interference mechanisms" and "advocacy windows," not "the administration is cheating."

- Do not send Domain 37 to Republican election officials or election administration offices without careful review. The document is nonpartisan analytically, but its subject matter (federal interference in elections) will be read as political by partisan election administration officials. Limit Phase B contacts to civil society organizations and legal networks.
