- Is the "what goes wrong without this domain" argument specific? **Yes**: Without Domain 48, the framework documents prosecutorial weaponization (Domain 29) and racial injustice (Domain 22) but has no standalone analysis of how criminal justice contact converts into aggregate civic exclusion as a structural democratic design problem. The framework's criminal justice coverage is incomplete without a domain that addresses scale, mechanism, and feedback loop.

**Domain 51 Questions**:

Sector-specific captures are documented individually; the meta-architecture is documented in Domain 51.
- Does the democratic design angle generate different policy implications? **Yes**: The corporate capture-as-architectural-design-failure frame generates a reform argument for architectural redesign (disclosure, public matching, constitutional amendment) rather than anti-corruption enforcement — a structurally different policy pathway.
- Is the "what goes wrong without this domain" argument specific? **Yes**: Without Domain 51, the framework documents 50 sector-specific captures without providing the structural explanation for how coordinated cross-sector capture is possible simultaneously.
