# Domain K Expert Contact Framework
## Federal Judiciary Restructuring — Phase 3 Research Infrastructure

**Purpose:** Unified Domain K-specific expert contact reference with 33 contacts, tiered by engagement likelihood and organized for Phase 3 activation. Consolidates contacts from three prior documents into a single operational framework with updated engagement strategy, timing windows, and outreach sequencing.

**Engagement Strategy Overview:**
- September outreach (Tier 1 primary experts): Secure feedback slots in fall schedules before they fill
- October outreach (coalition organizations): Coordinate November 4 launch logistics
- October-November outreach (academic scholars): Research brief distribution, testimony pipeline
- November-December outreach (media): Translation and amplification of November research launch

**Critical sequencing rule:** Contact Tier 1 coalition organizations (Fix the Court, Brennan Center, ACS) BEFORE contacting Tier 2 scholars. The warm intro chains route through Tier 1 contacts. Cold-contacting Tier 2 scholars without coalition organizational context reduces conversion rates substantially.

---

## TIER DEFINITIONS

**Tier 1 — Primary Experts (5-8 contacts):** Coalition partners and anchor organizations who will definitely engage. These are organizations with existing stakes in Domain K material, established policy functions related to judicial reform, and infrastructure to convert research engagement into legislative impact. Contact September–October 2026.

**Tier 2 — Secondary Experts (8-12 contacts):** Constitutional scholars and senior practitioners who likely have time and interest. These are the academic voices that move legal and media discourse; engagement converts research into credibility with law school and bar audiences. Contact October–November 2026.

**Tier 3 — Tertiary Contacts (10-15 contacts):** Media contacts, international specialists, and secondary advocacy organizations. Worth attempting; lower conversion probability. Contact November–December 2026, after Tier 1 and Tier 2 are engaged.
