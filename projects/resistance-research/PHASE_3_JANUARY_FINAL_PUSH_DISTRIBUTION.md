# Phase 3 January Final Push and Distribution

**Purpose**: Govern the final 3-day research completion, proofread, and distribution execution window from January 1–3, 2027. This document assumes Domain K is fully distributed (December 12–13 send complete) and Domain H base document is distributed (December 20–21 send complete). The January window covers: Domain H Extensions A/B/C integration completion, final proofread, updated Gist publication, congressional briefing scheduling confirmation, and post-January 3 tracking activation.

## Section 2: Final QA Checklist

Run this checklist on January 1. All items must be checked before January 2 distribution. If any item fails, do not distribute until resolved — except where noted.

### Domain K Final QA

| Item | Check | Pass Standard | Fail Action |
|------|-------|---------------|-------------|
| Zone K-1 bills: primary source accuracy | Verify 5 of 8 bills by opening the Congress.gov bill text directly | Bill number, title, sponsor, and committee referral match what is in the Zone K-1 text | Correct the Zone K-1 entry; do not distribute with inaccuracy |
| Zone K-2 constitutional argument: source attribution | Verify Fix the Court's statutory term limits analysis is cited as the source, not paraphrased without attribution | Citation is explicit: "Fix the Court [year] analysis argues..." | Add explicit attribution |
| Zone K-3 international precedent: citation accuracy | Verify Germany section cites the Bundesverfassungsgericht term structure from a primary or authoritative secondary source (not from an American law review that cites another law review) | Citation traces to a German government source, official court document, or peer-reviewed comparative law publication | Replace secondary-of-secondary citation with primary or authoritative secondary |
| Zone K-4 executive summary: plain-language test | Read the 500-word executive summary to a person who has not read the research. They must be able to tell you (a) what the structural problem is, (b) what the three most achievable reforms are, (c) why January 2027 matters | Non-specialist identifies all three elements without prompting | Revise executive summary until test passes |
| Active links: spot-check | Open 10 links at random from the full Domain K document | All 10 links return the target page or document | Replace dead links; do not distribute with broken links in Zone K-4 or executive summary (lower zones: flag and note in cover email) |
| No placeholder language | Search document for: "TODO", "VERIFY", "TBD", "ADD CITE", "[", "]" (in non-citation context) | Zero instances | Remove all placeholder language; if a placeholder reflects a genuine gap, either fill the gap or delete the claim |
| Cross-references to Domain H | Count Domain K cross-references to Domain H | At least 3 explicit cross-references (one in Zone K-2 on amendment pathway; one in Zone K-3 on Germany/Ireland; one in Zone K-4 on distribution) | Add missing cross-references |
